## 4. VOCABULARIO + FRASES POR TEMA (19:10–19:28, 18 min)

**Setup (2 min):** "Each of you, take your envelope topic from yesterday. We're going to build vocabulary specific to YOUR topic. Not generic. Specific. **5 power words + 3 sentences using them.** ENGLISH ONLY."

**Cómo funciona:**

**Round 1 — solo (8 min):** "8 minutes. Alone. Find 5 words specific to your topic. They must be:
- B1+ tier (not basic) — ej: 'breathtaking', 'heritage', 'distinctive', not just 'beautiful'
- Useful for YOUR topic specifically
- You can use phone for translation only — NOT for AI generation
Then write 3 full sentences using at least 3 of those words."

Caminas en silencio. NO ayudas con palabras. Si preguntan: *"Use simpler English. Or describe what you mean."*

**Round 2 — comparten en parejas (5 min):** "Pairs. Read your 5 words to your partner. Partner says: ARE these words B2-tier? Help each other upgrade if a word is too simple."

**Round 3 — banco común en tablero (3 min):** Cada estudiante dice UNA palabra de su lista. Las escribes en el tablero. Banco común para el grupo.

> **English Points:** +2 a quien aporte 3 o más palabras genuinamente B2-tier (las que tú reconoces como avanzadas).

**Estudiantes abren video en sus celulares. Subtítulos ENCENDIDOS.**

**PASO 1 — ESCUCHAR (3 min).** Di: "Just listen. No repeat. No read. Just LISTEN." Reproducen. Boca cerrada.

**PASO 2 — CON TEXTO (3 min).** Di: "Again. Subtitles ON. Repeat IMMEDIATELY after the speaker." Reproducen y repiten.

**PASO 3 — SIN TEXTO (3 min).** Di: "Again. Subtitles OFF. Phone face down after pressing play." Escuchan y repiten sin leer.

**PASO 4 — OJOS CERRADOS (3 min).** Di: "Last time. Eyes closed. Maximum concentration." Reproducen, ojos cerrados, repiten.

**PASO 5 — ROLE PLAY DE PIE EN PAREJAS (2 min).** Di: "Phones DOWN. Stand up. Pairs. Repeat from memory what you remember. One says a phrase, the other says the next."
