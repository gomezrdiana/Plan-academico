## 8. 📝 PROTOCOLO TRANSCRIPCIÓN A MANO + REGISTRO ESCRITO formal→casual (19:21–19:33, 12 min) — SENTADAS + voz alta

**OBJETIVO PARA LA ESTUDIANTE:**
> *"This is the new core skill. You will turn your OWN spoken English into clean written text — by hand, here. You don't invent content. You already said it in Phase 2. We just put it on the page and fix it. This is how your Final script is born — from your mouth, not the internet."*

### Parte A — El protocolo de transcripción (7 min)

**Tablero — PROTOCOLO (5 pasos):**

```
HAND-TRANSCRIPTION PROTOCOL — from speech to text

STEP 1 — RECALL: bring back ONE of your Phase 2 audios on
         your topic (listen once OR recall it). NO copying
         any AI/internet text. By hand only.
STEP 2 — DUMP: write it down as you said it — fast, messy,
         in pencil/pen. Get the SPOKEN words on paper.
STEP 3 — CLEAN: remove fillers (um, like, you know),
         fix spelling + verb agreement + tense.
STEP 4 — UPGRADE: where a word is weak, swap for a B2 word
         (Set #1-#6). Keep at least 1 anchor + 1 connector
         + 1 deliberate paraphrase VISIBLE in the text.
STEP 5 — READ-CHECK: you will read it ALOUD later and adjust
         what doesn't sound like real, clean English.
```

Modelado (3 min): tú tomas 1 frase hablada típica con muletillas ("so like, Colombian food is um, really good you know, because...") y la transcribes EN VIVO en el tablero pasando por Steps 2→3→4 hasta texto B2 limpio. Coro lee la versión final.

### Parte B — Registro escrito formal→casual (5 min)

**Tablero:**

```
WRITTEN REGISTER — formal -> casual ON THE PAGE

• A spoken paraphrase becomes a written sentence with
  proper punctuation — not "...,you know,..." but
  "; in other words, ..."
• Spoken anchor "well, what I'd say is" -> written
  opener "It is worth noting that..."
• Then ONE casual re-say IN WRITING for the reader:
  "Put simply, ..." (the bridge — now on paper)
```

Drill (2 min): tú das 1 oración hablada informal; 2 estudiantes dicen en voz alta cómo la ESCRIBIRÍAN en registro B2 + 1 re-say casual escrito ("Put simply, ...").

> Inserción #2 Frase del Día parafraseándose: *"In other words — to be just, albeit briefly — what I mean is, what you already said is, when all is said and done, what you now put on the page."*
