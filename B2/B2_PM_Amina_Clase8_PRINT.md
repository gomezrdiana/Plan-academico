## 8. 🆕 Q&A HANDLING — 5 PATTERNS + DRILL HOSTIL (19:18–19:32, 14 min) — SENTADAS

**OBJETIVO PARA LA ESTUDIANTE:**
> *"The most critical skill for your Final. After your monologue, you face Q&A live for 3-5 minutes. 5 patterns to handle ANY question — even hostile ones."*

### Concepto (4 min)

**Tablero (escribir ANTES de clase):**

```
5 Q&A HANDLING PATTERNS

1. CLARIFY THE QUESTION (when unclear):
   "Just to make sure I understand — are you asking about X or Y?"
   "Could you rephrase that? I want to give you a precise answer."

2. BUY TIME (when you need to think):
   "That's an excellent question. Let me think for a moment."
   "I'd like to take a second to give that the answer it deserves."

3. ACKNOWLEDGE + REDIRECT (when challenged):
   "That's a fair concern. From my perspective, however..."
   "I understand why you'd say that — at the same time, the data shows..."

4. ADMIT LIMITS (when you don't know):
   "Honestly, I don't have complete data on that — what I can tell you is..."
   "That goes beyond what I researched, but my best estimate would be..."

5. BRIDGE TO YOUR MESSAGE (always do this):
   "And that's exactly why my main point matters: ..."
   "Which brings me back to the central argument: ..."
```

Lee cada pattern + modela 1 ejemplo en voz alta.

### Drill — Preguntas hostiles (10 min)

5 preguntas hostiles. Cada estudiante responde UNA usando 1 pattern.

**Banco de preguntas hostiles:**

1. *"Isn't your whole argument based on cherry-picked data?"*
2. *"What makes you qualified to say that?"*
3. *"That's been disproven recently — what's your response?"*
4. *"Can you give me a specific number, not just a general claim?"*
5. *"I disagree completely. Convince me otherwise."*

**Procedimiento (2 min por estudiante):**
- Tú lanzas la pregunta hostil
- Estudiante usa 1 de los 5 patterns + responde (90 seg)
- Cohorte vota: ¿qué pattern usó? ¿Lo manejó con composure?
- +5 EP si manejó bien

> Si una estudiante se traba, espera 10 seg silencio. Si sigue trabada: *"Use Pattern 2. Buy time first."* Deja que retome.
