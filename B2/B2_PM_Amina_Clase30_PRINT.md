- If topic is *Colombian food*: *"You argued arepa is central. If someone said arepa is just regional comfort food, not cultural identity — where would you push back, and where would you concede?"* (B2 asks defend + concede + Set #23 hold).
- If topic is *Colombian music & dance*: *"You named cumbia as the gateway genre. If someone said vallenato is actually more accessible to a foreigner — would you maintain your choice or update it, and why?"* (B2 asks maintain or revise).
