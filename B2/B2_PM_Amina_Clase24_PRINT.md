## 11. PILLAR 3 CHUNK NO PAPER — 45 min — STAND UP / MIXED

> *"This is 45 minutes for ONE chunk: PILLAR 3 — your third argument pillar, ~60 seconds, from memory, no paper. Five minutes to extract from the script. Ten minutes alone walking and rehearsing. Twelve minutes with a partner who listens and gives one landed/one shaky. Fifteen minutes in the mini-audience where each of you says PILLAR 3 standing, mouth on, paper off. By 20:16, the third pillar lives in your mouth. Tomorrow CTA. Monday we start chaining."*

### Setup (1 min)

- Scripts open on each student's table (ONLY for the 5-min extraction).
- Stopwatch VISIBLE only for you.
- MEMORIZATION NOTES sheet on your knee.
- Clear space: students can walk during individual rehearsal.

### Extraction (5 min) — SIT

- Each student opens HER physical script (returned post-C3).
- Locates PILLAR 3.
- Silent reading 3-4 times.
- **You circulate:** verify each correctly identified PILLAR 3 (not P2 or CTA). If anyone doubted, help her mark it on the paper.
- At 4 min: *"30 seconds left. Last silent read."*
- At 5 min: *"Close. Paper face down. Stand up."*

### Individual rehearsal (10 min) — STAND UP / WALKING

- Each student walks the room reciting PILLAR 3 from memory, low-mid voice.
- ~60 sec per pass. 4-5 passes in 10 min.
- **You observe (DO NOT interrupt):** who peeks? who stays at 35 sec? who sustains 60 sec fluid?
- If anyone stalls 3+ times in a row: 1 peek authorized, then close script and restart from top.
- At 9 min: *"30 seconds. One more pass."*
- At 10 min: *"Stop. Find your partner."*

### Pairs rehearsal (12 min) — STAND UP / SIT

- 5 students → 2 pairs + 1 trio rotating (5 min per round, rotation at 6 min so trio rotates).
- **Protocol:**
  - Student A says PILLAR 3 from memory (~60 sec). Student B listens WITHOUT paper, WITHOUT notes.
  - B returns: 1 thing that LANDED + 1 thing that felt SHAKY (NO advice, NO "I would have said").
  - Roles switch.
  - At 6 min: rotate pairs.
- **You circulate:** verify feedback is "landed/shaky", not advice; verify NO paper.

### Mini-audience (15 min) — 1 STANDING / 4 SITTING OBSERVING

- **Pre-assigned order** (see pre-flight). ~3 min per student (includes setup and brief applause).
- **Per student:**
  - ~20 sec: setup (speaker settles STANDING at front, breathes, "go" said by you).
  - ~60 sec: PILLAR 3 from memory, NO paper, projected voice.
  - ~10 sec: group thanks (short applause), next speaker settles.
- **NO Q&A today** (Q&A is for full monologues, not isolated chunks).
- **You note in internal sheet per student** (raw text, no grade — feeds Phase 4 tracking, NOT communicated):

```

REAL TIME PILLAR 3 CHUNK: ___ sec

Said from memory without peek? (yes / 1 peek / multiple)
Sustained ~60 sec or cut short? (___ sec if cut)
Internal pillar connection fluid or broken?
Pronunciation clear of Sets #9-#17 words that appeared?
Body-close intonation (vs. mid-pillar)?
Internal structure sustained (topic + support + closing)?
Felt like autonomous piece or tried to "jump" to CTA?

INTERNAL NOTE (not communicated):
```

(NO grade. NO "passes / fails." SOLELY raw observation. Feeds Phase 4 tracking, not C4.)
