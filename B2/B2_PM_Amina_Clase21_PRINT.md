## 10. HOOK CHUNK NO PAPER — 45 min — STAND UP

> *"This is the first time you say your HOOK without the paper in your hand. Three stages — alone, in pairs, in front of the group. The HOOK content is closed; today is the LIFT — the moment your voice carries the architecture instead of the page carrying it for you. I coach form, not content. If you forget mid-HOOK: breathe, pivot, rebuild. That IS the practice."*

### Setup (1 min)

- 5 STANDING. Bags to one side — scripts INSIDE the bags.
- Your INTERNAL copy of the 5 HOOKs in your folder (NOT shown — silent reference for pronunciation, rhythm, pause).
- Stopwatch VISIBLE for you, NOT for students.
- 4 corners + center defined for Stage 1 (each finds her space to rehearse alone).

### STAGE 1 — INDIVIDUAL SILENT + WHISPER + SPOKEN (12 min) — STAND UP

```
STAGE 1 — INDIVIDUAL (12 min):

Minute 1: SILENT READ (optional refresh) — each one
  takes HER personal HOOK copy, reads 1x silently,
  CLOSES and stores in bag.

Minutes 2-4: IN-HEAD REHEARSAL — repeat HOOK mentally
  several times, sentence by sentence. No lip movement.

Minutes 5-7: WHISPER — whisper the HOOK 2-3 times,
  listening to yourself. Adjust where memory drops.

Minutes 8-12: SPOKEN VOICE — say the HOOK in spoken
  voice (not projected yet) 3-4 times. If you trip,
  repeat ONLY the tripped piece, then from the top.
```

**Your role:** walk SILENTLY. NO interrupting. Note in personal notebook WHO trips WHERE, WHO already flows, WHO is glued to the memory of the text ("reading in the air" gestures = signal of non-internalization). DO NOT correct yet — Stage 2+3 are for correcting.

### STAGE 2 — PAIRS (15 min) — STAND UP

```
STAGE 2 — PAIRS (15 min, ~3 cycles per pair):

Pair up: 2+2+1 (the fifth rotates — pairs with you
  Amina or with another rotating).

PER CYCLE (~5 min):
  ~2 min: PARTNER A says HOOK aloud, NO paper.
          PARTNER B listens with NO paper either.
  ~1 min: PARTNER B gives ONE coaching note on FORM
          (accent / rhythm / pause / transition /
          filler). NOT content. NOT opinion.
  Then SWITCH.

ROTATE PAIRS every 2 cycles so each student hears
  at least 2 different HOOKs (NOT to copy — to TRAIN
  THE EAR to hear another's architecture).
```

**Your role:** rotate between pairs. Listen to 1-2 HOOKs per pair. Give 1 micro-correction per student (no more — this is practice, not clinic). If a pair drifts to content ("I would change this in your HOOK"), interrupt: *"Content is closed — only form. What did you HEAR in her cadence?"*

### STAGE 3 — MINI-AUDIENCE (15 min) — STAND UP

```
STAGE 3 — MINI-AUDIENCE (15 min, 5 students x ~3 min):

5 STUDENTS, one at a time, STANDING IN FRONT OF GROUP.
ZERO paper in ANY hand — all of them.

PER STUDENT (~3 min):
  ~15 sec: breathe, eye contact, begin when ready
  ~1 min : HOOK COMPLETE from memory, projected voice
  ~1 min : Amina coaches live: 2-3 micro-notes on FORM
           (accent, rhythm, pause, transition, filler)
  ~15 sec: student REPEATS only the corrected piece
           (not whole HOOK) — brief applause — next
```

**Your PHASE 4 DAY 1 OBSERVATION SHEET (internal, NOT communicated):**

```
STUDENT: ______________________________
HOOK TIME: ___ sec (target ~60 sec)

VOICE WITHOUT PAPER: fluid / light tremor / heavy tremor / collapse
HOOK MEMORY: 100% / 80% / 60% or less
ARCHITECTURE (anchor + opening marker): held / diluted
INTONATION: alive / flat
CADENCE: natural / rushed / dragged
EMPHASIS: there / no magnet word
FILLERS returning when paper removed: none / 1-2 / many
B2 PRONUNCIATION (vocab placed in HOOK): clean / 1-2 drops / many drops
HANDS BEHAVIOR: calm / searching for paper
IF MEMORY BLANK: how reacted — breathed + pivoted / froze / asked paper

CORRECTED PIECE TODAY (form, not content): _____________
READY FOR PILLAR 1 CHUNK (Cl 22 tomorrow): yes / almost / needs repetition
```

(NO numeric grade. NO "passes / fails." NO judgment on content. ONLY raw form observation. Feeds your work Cl 22-28; NOT communicated; NOT today's checkpoint.)
