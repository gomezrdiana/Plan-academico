## 10. PILLAR 1 CHUNK NO PAPER — 45 min — MIXED

> *"PILLAR 1 of your monologue, about one minute, by heart, said without paper four times — alone, with a partner, with the group. The text is closed since C3 — today is voice, not editing. Restart from the last topic chunk you remember if you stumble; do not start over from the first word. Mini-audience asks honest content questions only. I coach memory and articulation; I do NOT touch your content."*

### REFRESH — 30 sec silence — SIT

One instruction: *"Eyes closed or on the table. Feel — silently — how your HOOK from yesterday lands and hands off to your PILLAR 1 today. Don't say them. Don't even mouth them. Just FEEL the handoff for 30 seconds. Then we begin Pass 1."*

### PASS 1 — SILENT MEMORIZATION (10 min) — SIT

- Each student with her C3 script. PILLAR 1 only.
- Optional: 1 low-volume read-aloud in the first minute (warm-up). Then silence.
- Strategies visible on board: (a) read-cover-say; (b) memorize by topic chunks, not by words; (c) mark the forward connector that opens PILLAR 1 as a mental anchor.
- You walk silently. No verbal coaching. If someone raises a hand: short whisper.

### PASS 2 — ALONE NO PAPER (10 min) — STAND UP

- Paper face down or in pocket.
- Each student says PILLAR 1 entirely 3-4 times for herself (not in chorus, not for anyone else). Low-mid voice.
- If she stalls: *restart from last remembered topic chunk, NOT from the first word*.
- You walk. Note in personal notebook (with name): where she drops (which topic chunk lost / which connector forgotten / which B2 word stumbled). DO NOT interrupt unless someone freezes 10+ seconds.

### PASS 3 — PAIRS (12 min) — STAND UP

- 2 pairs + 1 trio (cohort of 5).
- 2 rounds: in each, A says PILLAR 1 to B without paper; B listens and at the end says (a) which forward connector opened PILLAR 1, (b) which B2 word she heard.
- Then swap. Total: 2 rounds × 2 sayings × ~3 min each + transitions.
- You circulate. DO NOT correct the speaker. If the listener missed the connector → SOFT question *"¿qué connector quería entrar al principio?"* (aim at self-awareness, not text correction).

### PASS 4 — MINI-AUDIENCE (12 min) — 1 STANDING / 4 SITTING OBSERVING

- 5 students × ~2 min each (~1 min PILLAR 1 + ~30-60 sec content Q&A from group).
- Rules: hands down, paper down, 1-2 honest questions about CONTENT (NOT delivery, NOT advice, NOT "I would have said").
- You note in personal notebook: real time, where restart if any, clarity of forward connector, cleanest B2 word, most visible hesitation.
- If someone freezes completely (more than 15 sec): short coaching *"start from your last topic chunk"* — DO NOT give her the next word.

### Close (after 5th) — 30 sec

> *"PILLAR 1 is in your voice. Tomorrow (Cl 23) we do PILLAR 2 the same way. Friday we do PILLAR 3. Next week we chain. The script stays closed."*
