## CONTEXTO DE HOY

**Módulo 22: GERUNDIOS CON VERBOS DOBLES** — verbo + gerundio (-ing) o + infinitivo (to + verb), depende del primer verbo:

```
Verbos que llevan GERUNDIO (-ing) después:
- LOVE       I love SWIMMING.        (no "love to swim")
- HATE       She hates WAITING.
- ENJOY      We enjoy COOKING.
- FINISH     I finished EATING.
- STOP       He stopped SMOKING.
- MIND       I don't mind WAITING.
- AVOID      They avoid TALKING about it.
- KEEP       Keep TRYING.

Verbos que llevan INFINITIVO (to + verbo) después:
- WANT       I want TO TRAVEL.
- NEED       She needs TO STUDY.
- DECIDE     They decided TO MOVE.
- PLAN       We plan TO VISIT.
- HOPE       I hope TO SEE you.
- LEARN      He learned TO COOK.
- PROMISE    She promised TO HELP.
- AGREE      They agreed TO MEET.

⚠️ NO HAY REGLA UNIVERSAL — hay que aprender qué verbo lleva qué.
```

**Conexión con módulos previos:**
- M20: Gerundio como sujeto ("Swimming is fun")
- M21: Gerundio después de preposición ("good AT cooking")
- **M22 (HOY): Gerundio después de algunos verbos ("love swimming")**
- M23 (próxima): Phrasal Verbs No Separables

**VIRTUD HOY:** FORTALEZA — DÍA 5 (último día — coraje para perseverar en lo que amas Y aceptar lo que no controlas).

## 3. LIBRO: MÓDULO 22 — GERUNDIOS CON VERBOS DOBLES (00:13–00:28, 15 min)

**MÁXIMO 15 MINUTOS.**

**CONCEPTO CLAVE — escribe en el tablero (NO lo borres):**

```
TWO-VERB STRUCTURES

VERB + GERUND (-ing):
- LOVE / HATE / ENJOY / FINISH / STOP / MIND / AVOID / KEEP

  I LOVE swimming.       She HATES waiting.
  We ENJOY cooking.      I FINISHED eating.

VERB + INFINITIVE (to + verb):
- WANT / NEED / DECIDE / PLAN / HOPE / LEARN / PROMISE / AGREE

  I WANT to travel.      She NEEDS to study.
  We PLAN to visit.      I HOPE to see you.

⚠️ NO RULE — memorize which verb takes which.

CONTRAST:
- I love swimming.  ✓     I love TO swim.  ✗ (love takes -ing)
- I want to travel. ✓     I want traveling. ✗ (want takes to + verb)
```

**Setup (1 min):** "Open Module 22. Read with partner. 5 minutes."

**5 min:** Leen. Camina **EN SILENCIO**.

**9 min:** Te explican (tú NO):
1. Lista los 8 verbos que llevan GERUNDIO. → love, hate, enjoy, finish, stop, mind, avoid, keep.
2. Lista los 8 que llevan INFINITIVO. → want, need, decide, plan, hope, learn, promise, agree.
3. ¿Hay regla? → NO. Memoria.
4. ¿Ejemplo de cada lista?
5. ¿Diferencia entre "I love swimming" y "I want to swim"? → Estructura: love + ING vs want + to + verbo.
