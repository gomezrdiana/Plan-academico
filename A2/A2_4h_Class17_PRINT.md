## ⚠️ NOTA SOBRE EL CONTENIDO DE LOS MÓDULOS

**M37 oficial del libro (línea 21245):** "What Do You Look Like?" — vocabulario de descripción física.

Vocabulario base:
- Pelo: long hair, short hair, curly, straight, blonde, brunette, bald
- Cuerpo: tall, short, thin, slim, average, heavy-set
- Rostro: oval face, round face, square jaw, dimples, freckles
- Ojos: blue eyes, brown eyes, green eyes
- Otros: beard, mustache, glasses, scar

Estructura clave:
> *"What does she look like?"* → *"She is [adjective], she has [feature]."*
> *"He is tall and thin. He has short brown hair and blue eyes."*

**M38 oficial del libro (línea 21693):** "Order of Adjectives" — el orden correcto cuando hay múltiples adjetivos.

Orden canónico (OSAS COMP):
> **O**pinion + **S**ize + **A**ge + **S**hape + **C**olor + **O**rigin + **M**aterial + **P**urpose + NOUN

Ejemplos:
- *"A beautiful small old round red Italian leather wallet"* (todos los slots)
- *"A nice young tall woman"* (Opinion + Age + Size)
- *"A long curly blonde hairstyle"* (Shape/Length + Style + Color)

**Conexión M37 → M38:** M37 introduce las palabras descriptivas. M38 enseña a USARLAS en orden correcto cuando hay múltiples.

## 10. LIBRO M38 — ORDER OF ADJECTIVES (01:39–02:04, 25 min) — SENTADO

**OBJETIVO PARA EL ESTUDIANTE:**
> *"Module 38 — when you use MORE THAN ONE adjective, order matters. 'A red small old car' sounds wrong. 'A small old red car' sounds right. There's a rule."*

### Concepto (10 min)

**Tablero — escribe OSAS COMP grande:**

```
OPINION + SIZE + AGE + SHAPE + COLOR + ORIGIN + MATERIAL + PURPOSE + NOUN
   1        2     3     4       5       6         7         8        N

Memory: O.S.A.S.C.O.M.P.

Examples broken down:
- "A beautiful (opinion) small (size) old (age) round (shape)
   red (color) Italian (origin) leather (material) handbag"
- "A nice young tall woman"  (Opinion + Age + Size)
- "Cute little blue eyes"    (Opinion + Size + Color)
```

### Ejercicios del libro (10 min)

**Libro M38 ejercicios + Speed Drill propio:**

(5 min) Tablero con frases desordenadas. Cohorte ordena:

```
1. "red / big / car" → "big red car" (Size + Color)
2. "Italian / old / leather / shoes" → "old Italian leather shoes" (Age + Origin + Material)
3. "small / brown / cute / dog" → "cute small brown dog" (Opinion + Size + Color)
4. "tall / French / beautiful / woman" → "beautiful tall French woman" (Opinion + Size + Origin)
```

(5 min) Parejas: cada pareja construye 4 frases con 3 adjetivos en orden correcto. Pueden describir personas que conocen (M37) o cosas.

### Cierre (5 min)

> *"OSAS COMP is a rule that exists in your ear before your mind. With practice you stop calculating — you just feel it. Tomorrow more practice."*
