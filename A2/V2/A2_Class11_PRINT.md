## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT M13 ALREADY / STILL / UNTIL (~52 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board el titulo y las REGLAS del libro
   (M13, p.107-108), tal cual:

   ALREADY, STILL, AND UNTIL

   1) ALREADY -> "ya". The action happened or began BEFORE
      now (or another reference point). It is a frequency
      adverb like "often". Put it BEFORE the main verb (or
      AFTER "to be").
        "I already studied."  = Yo ya estudie.
        "I was already in Paris in 2019."
   2) STILL -> "todavia". The action is STILL happening now
      (or at the reference point). Same position as already.
        "I am still in Paris."
   3) UNTIL -> "hasta". The action STOPS at the specified
      point. Placed like in Spanish.
        "I was in Paris until 2019."
   4) All three work in PAST, PRESENT and FUTURE, plus their
      continuous versions.

2. SEÑALA las posiciones. DI: "ALREADY and STILL go before
   the main verb, or after 'to be'. UNTIL goes before the
   time/point where the action stops — just like Spanish
   'hasta'."

3. ESCRIBE EJEMPLOS del libro (p.109), tal cual:

      Richard already visited his mother.
      I lived in Canada until 2012.
      I still love my friends.
      Sebastian will study until March, 2022.
      Do you still like hamburgers?
      I won't go to bed until the morning.
      Are you still in class?
      We are still at the school, but we already finished
        the class.
      My friends were working until 7:30.
      Until when will you live in Colombia?

4. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "The phrase is
   the three words at work — 'a team ALREADY says thank you,
   STILL supports each other, stands together UNTIL the work
   is done.'"

5. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "I still am in class" (orden con to be)
   -> ESCRIBE "I am STILL in class." DI: "After 'to be',
   still/already go AFTER the verb: am still, is already."
   COHORTE repite 2 veces.

6. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "Already I studied" (already al inicio)
   -> TACHA, ESCRIBE "I ALREADY studied." DI: "Already goes
   BEFORE the main verb, not at the start." COHORTE repite
   2 veces.

7. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "I go to bed until morning" (afirmativo
   con until donde el ingles pide negativo) -> ESCRIBE
   "I won't go to bed UNTIL the morning." DI: "When the
   action does NOT happen before the point, English uses the
   negative + until." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

8. PRACTICA dirigida (oral): TU das la situacion, el grupo
   añade already / still / until:
        "The report — ___ finished?" (already)
        "She ___ working." (still)
        "We worked ___ 6:00." (until)
   8-10 vueltas, distintos estudiantes.

9. [VIRTUD] activation: cuando alguien reporte una tarea de
   equipo ya hecha, DI suave: "Good — who did it? Justice =
   thank the team. 'They have ALREADY helped.'"

10. PIDELES en parejas: "Student A asks about a task at work
    or home; Student B answers using ALREADY (done), STILL
    (in progress), or UNTIL (a stop point). Then switch.
    6 exchanges. Theme: a normal work day. 5 minutes."

11. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca en
    2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota errores con
    NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

12. ESCRIBE el uso en pasado/futuro continuo (p.108-119):
        "I was already living in Rome in 2011."
        "I was still working at three o'clock."
        "I will be living here until 2027."
    DI: "Same three words, any tense — even continuous."
    Practica 4-5 vueltas en coro.

13. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "Do you still liked hamburgers?"
    (still + verbo mal conjugado) -> TACHA, ESCRIBE "Do you
    still LIKE hamburgers?" DI: "Still does not change the
    verb form; the tense rules stay normal." COHORTE repite
    2 veces.

14. PIDELES en parejas round 2: "Now a 4-line TEAM REPORT:
    2 things ALREADY done, 1 STILL pending, 1 thing you will
    do UNTIL a deadline. Theme: a shared project at work.
    Thank one teammate. 5 minutes."

15. CIRCULA. CORRIGE cuando el mismo error aparezca 2 veces
    en parejas distintas.

16. CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo coloque already/still antes
    del verbo (o tras 'to be') y until antes del punto de
    parada. DI: "Stand up — we go to the simulation. Project
    status update at work."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 32 min y aun ponen already/still en mal lugar o confunden until, NO AVANCES al team report. Quedate en posicion de already/still + until hasta que salga limpio; deja el team report para tarea. Mejor LAND uno bien que rush dos shaky.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas usando already/still (antes del verbo / tras to be) + until (antes del punto) sin invertir.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: PROJECT STATUS UPDATE AT WORK (~36 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "PROJECT STATUS UPDATE AT WORK"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. One is a TEAM LEAD asking 'what
   is the status?' The other is a TEAM MEMBER reporting. In
   the whole conversation you must use, AT LEAST:
     - 3 'already' (tasks done: 'I have already sent it')
     - 3 'still' (in progress: 'I am still waiting')
     - 2 'until' (a stop/deadline: 'I will work until 6:00')
   And thank ONE teammate by name (justice). Then rotate."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This update IS the phrase. A
   fair team reports what is already done, what is still
   pending, and works together until it is finished."

4. ESCRIBE 2 mini-scenarios en board:

   SCENARIO 1 — Weekly project check-in
     Lead asks, member reports: "I already finished the
     budget." "I am still writing the report." "Maria
     already called the client — thank you, Maria." "We
     will review until 5:00."

   SCENARIO 2 — Shift handover at a store / clinic
     Outgoing asks, incoming reports: "I already restocked
     the shelves." "The delivery is still on the way." "I
     stayed until closing." "Carlos already cleaned the
     front — I owe him a thank you."

5. PIDELES round 1 (5 min): pareja escoge un scenario.
   Roles claros. El lead arranca con "What is the status?"

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces en
   la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven a parejas.

7. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, switch to
   Spanish, think 5 seconds, come back: 'I have already...',
   'I am still...', 'I will work until...'. Don't freeze."

8. ROTA parejas + cambia roles. Round 2 (5 min): cambien
   de scenario tambien.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante la simulacion: si alguien
   reporta el trabajo del equipo como solo suyo, DI suave:
   "Justice. Name the teammate. 'She has already helped.'
   Fair credit."

10. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces en
    parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu cuaderno.

11. OBSERVER COUNT (ligero): en round 2, asigna a 2
    estudiantes como observers — cuentan en silencio cuantos
    usos CORRECTOS de already/still/until oyeron en la pareja
    vecina + cuantos "thank you" al equipo. Reportan: "Pair
    X — 5 correct, 1 thank you." No correccion, solo conteo.

12. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente al
    grupo (un status update completo + un thank you). COHORTE
    cuenta cuantos already/still/until correctos cazaron.
    Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("What have you already done?" / "What is still pending?" / "Until when will you work?") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 2 rotaciones + UNA demo al frente + observer count reportado.
