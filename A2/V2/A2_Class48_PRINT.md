## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT CLUSTER 7 (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE 3 secciones en board grandes:

   A) REPORTED SPEECH       B) BECAUSE / SO / AND / BUT
   said vs told               BECAUSE = reason
     said (no object)           "I'm late BECAUSE
       "He SAID he was tired"     traffic was bad."
     told ME (always obj)     SO = result
       "He TOLD ME he was         "Traffic was bad SO
        tired"                     I'm late."
   back-shift ONE step       AND = addition
     I am   -> said WAS         "I sent it AND copied
     I will -> said WOULD        her."
     I can  -> said COULD     BUT = contrast inside
     I have -> said HAD         "I sent it BUT she
     imper. -> told ME TO        didn't reply."
              send

   C) ALTHOUGH / HOWEVER / EVEN THOUGH
   ALTHOUGH / EVEN THOUGH = clause connector
     "ALTHOUGH it rained, we went."  (comma)
     "We went, ALTHOUGH it rained."  (no comma needed)
     EVEN THOUGH = stronger ALTHOUGH
   HOWEVER = between two full sentences
     "It rained. HOWEVER, we went."
     "It rained; HOWEVER, we went."

2. SEÑALA seccion A. DI: "Two rules. SAID never takes
   an object. TOLD always takes TOLD ME. Then the verb
   steps back one tense — present to past, will to
   would, can to could, have to had."

3. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "Report
   what was said, back-shift one step. Discipline of
   the link."

4. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "He said me he was tired" ->
   TACHA "said me", ESCRIBE "He TOLD ME he was tired."
   COHORTE repite 2 veces.

5. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "She said that she is leaving" ->
   TACHA "is", ESCRIBE "She said that she WAS leaving."
   COHORTE repite 2 veces.

6. PIDELES en parejas: "5 direct quotes — convert to
   reported speech with said vs told and back-shift.
   Workplace situations: meeting, deadline, call,
   email, supervisor." 5 minutos.

7. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca
   en 2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota con
   NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

8. [VIRTUD] activation: si alguien suelta "He said me",
   DI suave: "Measure. One verb. Pick the link."

9. SEÑALA seccion B. DI: "Four connectors, four jobs.
   BECAUSE reason. SO result. AND adds. BUT contrast
   inside. One per sentence — never two."

10. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "I'm late because so traffic" ->
    TACHA "because so", ESCRIBE: "I'm late BECAUSE
    traffic was bad." Y debajo: "Traffic was bad SO
    I'm late." DI: "Pick ONE."

11. PIDELES en parejas: "6 sentences — 2 BECAUSE + 2
    SO + 1 AND + 1 BUT. Real work this week." 4 min.

12. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca
    2 veces.

13. SEÑALA seccion C. DI: "Three concessive connectors.
    ALTHOUGH and EVEN THOUGH open a clause — coma si
    la clause va primero. HOWEVER va entre full
    sentences — full stop o semicolon antes."

14. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "Although it was raining but we
    went" -> TACHA "but", ESCRIBE "ALTHOUGH it was
    raining, we went." DI: "Never ALTHOUGH y BUT
    juntos." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

15. [ERROR TIPICO #5] "It rained however we delivered"
    -> ESCRIBE "It rained. HOWEVER, we delivered."
    DI: "HOWEVER necesita full stop antes, coma
    despues." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

16. [ERROR TIPICO #6] "Even though, we went" -> ESCRIBE
    "EVEN THOUGH it was raining, we went." DI: "EVEN
    THOUGH necesita una clause." COHORTE repite 2
    veces.

17. PIDELES en parejas: "5 concessive sentences — 2
    ALTHOUGH + 1 EVEN THOUGH + 2 HOWEVER. Real
    workplace contrast." 5 minutos.

18. CIRCULA, CIERRA cuando 80% construya en las 3
    secciones sin titubear. DI: "Stand up — simulation."
```
