## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT M6 SCHEDULES (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board 2 COLUMNAS grandes:

   PRESENT SIMPLE                  PRESENT CONTINUOUS
   for TIMETABLES / SCHEDULES      for PERSONAL ARRANGEMENTS
   (objective, not yours)          (yours, fixed)

   "The train LEAVES at 7 PM"      "I'm LEAVING at 7 PM"
   "Class STARTS on Monday"        "I'm STARTING on Monday"
   "The store OPENS at 9"          "I'm OPENING the store at 9"
                                   (you work there)
   "The flight ARRIVES at 3 AM"    "I'm ARRIVING at 3 AM"
                                   (your trip)

   TIME MARKERS:
     AT + exact time (at 7 PM, at noon, at midnight, at night)
     ON + day / date (on Monday, on June 5th)
     IN + month / year (in June, in 2026)
     IN the morning / afternoon / evening

   SCHEDULE VERBS:
     leave / arrive / start / begin / end / finish
     open / close / depart / get to

2. SEÑALA columna izquierda. DI: "Present Simple for
   timetables. The schedule is objective. It is NOT yours.
   It's the train's, the flight's, the store's, the
   school's. So we use Present Simple — the form for
   facts that don't change."

3. SEÑALA columna derecha. DI: "Present Continuous for
   YOUR fixed plan. The time is yours. You decided it.
   You arranged it. So we use Present Continuous — the
   form for arrangements you confirmed."

4. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "This is what
   the phrase says — 'the train leaves at seven by the
   schedule, I leave at seven by my plan.' Two forms. Two
   thoughts. Prudence picks the form that fits."

5. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "The train is leaving at 7" (para
   schedule) -> TACHA "is leaving", ESCRIBE "The train
   LEAVES at 7." DI: "Whose time? The train's. Schedule.
   Present Simple." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

6. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "I leave at 7" (para plan personal
   con hora fijada) -> TACHA "leave", ESCRIBE "I'M LEAVING
   at 7." DI: "Whose time? Yours. Plan. Present Continuous."
   COHORTE repite 2 veces.

7. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "Class start at 9" -> TACHA "start",
   ESCRIBE "Class STARTS at 9." DI: "Third person — S.
   Callback to week 1." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

8. PIDELES en parejas: "5 SCHEDULE sentences with Present
   Simple. Train, flight, bus, class, store. Use AT / ON /
   IN. 4 minutes."

9. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca en
   2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota errores con
   NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

10. [VIRTUD] activation: si alguien dice "The flight is
    leaving" para el schedule de la aerolinea, DI suave:
    "Prudence. Whose time is this — yours or the schedule's?
    Pick the form that fits."

11. SEÑALA columna derecha otra vez. DI: "Now flip. SAME
    time. SAME action. But now YOU decided it. YOU arranged
    it. So now it's Present Continuous."

12. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "The meeting is on 9 AM" -> TACHA
    "on", ESCRIBE "The meeting is AT 9 AM." DI: "AT exact
    time. ON day. IN month." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

13. [ERROR TIPICO #5] "The store opens in Monday" -> TACHA
    "in", ESCRIBE "The store opens ON Monday." COHORTE
    repite 2 veces.

14. [ERROR TIPICO #6] "I'm flying at 8 AM" (cuando lee del
    schedule de la aerolinea, no SU vuelo) -> TACHA "I'm
    flying", ESCRIBE "The flight LEAVES at 8 AM" (the
    schedule) — OR — "I'M FLYING at 8 AM" (your trip).
    DI: "Whose time? Pick the form."

15. PIDELES en parejas: "Now MIX. 5 sentences total — 3
    SCHEDULES (Present Simple), 2 PERSONAL plans (Present
    Continuous). Same context — transportation. Force
    yourselves to switch forms. 5 minutes."

16. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando aparezca el mismo
    error 2 veces en parejas distintas.

17. CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo construya en las 2
    columnas sin titubear. DI: "Stand up — we go to the
    simulation. Travel and schedule info call."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 30 min y aun confunden los 2 formas, NO AVANCES al mix. Reduce a 1 sola columna (solo schedules) hoy, deja contraste para Cl 6 opener. Mejor LAND uno bien que rush dos shaky.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas construyendo en las 2 categorias sin preguntar cual usar.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: TRAVEL & SCHEDULE INFO CALL (~38 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "TRAVEL & SCHEDULE INFO CALL"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. One is the TRAVELER — calling
   the front desk or a transit info line. The other is the
   INFO PERSON — answering with schedules. You must use,
   in the whole conversation, AT LEAST:
     - 4 SCHEDULE statements (Present Simple: leaves /
       arrives / starts / opens / closes)
     - 3 PERSONAL plans (Present Continuous: I'm leaving /
       I'm arriving / I'm meeting)
     - 5 PREPOSITIONS (mix AT / ON / IN)
   Then rotate."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This call is the phrase in
   action. The train leaves at seven by the schedule, you
   leave at seven by your plan. Prudence picks the form
   that names the thought."

4. ESCRIBE 2 mini-scenarios en board:

   SCENARIO 1 — Calling the airport info line
     Traveler asks: flight schedules (leaves/arrives), gate
     opening, check-in time. Traveler states: own arrival
     plan, taxi time, meeting time after landing.

   SCENARIO 2 — Calling the train station / bus terminal
     Traveler asks: departure schedules, arrival times,
     ticket office hours. Traveler states: own travel plan,
     meeting at destination, return time.

5. PIDELES round 1 (5 min): pareja escoge un scenario.
   Roles claros. Traveler arranca.

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces en
   la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven a parejas.

7. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, switch to
   Spanish, think 5 seconds, come back to English. Don't
   freeze."

8. ROTA parejas + cambia roles. Round 2 (5 min): cambien
   de scenario tambien.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante la simulacion: si alguien
   suelta "the flight is leaving" en vez de "leaves" para
   el horario, DI suave: "Prudence. Whose time? Pick the
   form."

10. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces en
    parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu cuaderno.

11. OBSERVER COUNT (ligero): en round 2, asigna a 2
    estudiantes como observers — cuentan en silencio
    cuantos SCHEDULES y cuantos PLANS oyeron en la pareja
    vecina. Al final reportan: "Pair X — 4 schedules,
    2 plans." No correccion, solo conteo.

12. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente al
    grupo (traveler + info person, 3 preguntas + 3
    respuestas). COHORTE cuenta cuantos SCHEDULES y cuantos
    PLANS cazaron correctas. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("What time does the flight to Bogotá leave?" / "I'm meeting my brother at the airport at 3 PM — what time does Flight 2718 arrive?") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 2 rotaciones + UNA demo al frente + observer count reportado. Cierre con tiempo para Carta.

## BLOCK 4 — CIERRE + CARTA AL YO DEL FUTURO (~22 min)

```
1. ERROR PAPER RECAP (~3 min). ESCRIBE en board:
   "ERRORES DE HOY" + los 3 errores tipicos (anonimos):
     X "The flight is leaving at 8" (schedule)
                                  -> "The flight LEAVES at 8"
     X "Class start at 9"          -> "Class STARTS at 9"
     X "The store opens in Monday" -> "opens ON Monday"
   COHORTE repite las 3 correcciones 2 veces en coro.
   PARALELO: en TU cuaderno personal, los 3 errores
   literales con NOMBRES (para coordinacion).

2. [FRASE] close (~2 min). UN estudiante (al azar) dice
   la frase de memoria + LA PARAFRASEA en sus propias
   palabras. Aceptas cualquier parafraseo que mantenga
   "the schedule's time uses Present Simple, my plan uses
   Present Continuous, pick the form that names the
   thought."

3. [VIRTUD] cierre (~2 min). PREGUNTA: "What did you close
   clean this week with prudence?" — 2 respuestas maximo,
   rapido, sin discusion. Es la retrospectiva oral breve
   del bloque PRUDENCIA — la Carta abajo es el cierre
   escrito.

4. ============================================
   [OBLIGATORIO — NO se omite] CARTA AL YO DEL FUTURO
   (~12-13 min) — CIERRE ESCRITO DEL BLOQUE PRUDENCIA
   ============================================
   DI: "Last thing this week. Letter to Your Future Self.
   We meant to do this on day 1 — we do it now, closing
   the first virtue block."

   DEMUESTRA en una hoja propia (30 seg): saca una hoja
   de TU cuaderno, doblala en 3 (carta + sobre) — tercio
   de abajo arriba, tercio de arriba abajo (overlap),
   borde izq 1cm adentro, borde der 1cm adentro (lock).
   Nombre + fecha al frente.

   PIDELES: "Saquen una hoja de su cuaderno. Doblen
   igual."

   DI (voz lenta, calma): "You write a letter to
   yourselves. In English. You open it the last day of
   the cohort, Cl 55. Include:
     (1) what you commit to do this level,
     (2) what you want to be able to do at Cl 55 that you
         can't today,
     (3) one promise to yourself.
   About 150 words. Take 8 minutes."

   ESCRIBEN en silencio (8 min). SIENTATE. NO leas su
   contenido nunca. Si raise hand, te acercas en whisper:
   "Whatever is true. There is no correct."

   MIENTRAS escriben: en TU cuaderno personal, anota
   errores adicionales del dia con NOMBRES (para
   coordinacion).

   CUENTA: cuando termine de escribir el ultimo ->
   TODOS firman su sobre con su nombre afuera + lo
   cierran con cinta o doblez seguro. 30 seg.

   RECOGE los sobres en una pila dentro de TU cuaderno.
   CUENTA: sobres = estudiantes presentes. Lo entregas
   a coordinacion EL MISMO DIA en mano.

   [ALERTA]: si un estudiante no puede doblar el sobre
   -> hoja suelta con NOMBRE encima. Coordinacion
   archiva igual.

   [ALERTA]: si la sesion se atrasa y no hay tiempo para
   8 min escritura -> reducir a 5 min con 100 palabras,
   pero NUNCA omitir. La Carta es in-class only, jamas
   tarea casera.

5. HOMEWORK Cl 6 (~3 min). ESCRIBE en board mientras
   la dices:

      HOMEWORK CL 5 — DUE BEFORE CL 6, 7:00 PM
      TIME: 20-30 min (A2). NOT fragmented.

      (1) WRITING — 10 mixed sentences:
          - 5 SCHEDULES (Present Simple):
              "The train LEAVES at 6 AM."
              "The store OPENS on Monday at 9."
              "Class STARTS at 7 PM."
              "The flight ARRIVES in the morning."
              "The meeting BEGINS at noon."
          - 5 PERSONAL plans (Present Continuous, mix
            transportation + work + meetings):
              "I'M LEAVING at 6 AM."
              "I'M MEETING my boss at 9 AM tomorrow."
              "I'M FLYING to Bogotá on Friday."
              "I'M STARTING the new job in June."
              "I'M ARRIVING at 3 PM."
          Hand in on paper next class.

      (2) AUDIO eyes closed — 2 min — "My typical workday
          + tomorrow's plan." Use AT LEAST 3 SCHEDULES
          (when buses/store/work shift start) + 3 PERSONAL
          plans (when YOU leave / arrive / meet). Label:
          A2-5. Send to WhatsApp group before Cl 6.

      (3) LISTENING — 10 min YouTube — search "transportation
          schedules English" or "business hours English
          conversation". Note 5 TIMETABLE sentences with
          Present Simple. Paper.

      RULE: not fragmented. No "I didn't have time."

6. Cl 6 ANNOUNCE. DI: "Next class Cl 6: M7 — MIGHT. Modal
   for possibility. TEMPLANZA v1 day 1 OPENS the new
   virtue block. The cartas you wrote today live in
   coordination until the last day. See you next class —
   measured and clear."
```

**[ALERTA]** si un estudiante no anota la tarea -> DI: "Phone out. Photo of the board now." Nadie se va sin saberla. Si alguien protesta por la longitud: "20-30 min total, not fragmented. That is the rule."
**[ALERTA]** la Carta CIERRA con sobres sellados = estudiantes presentes. Si UN estudiante se va con su Carta en su propio cuaderno, el protocolo se rompe — recoge SIEMPRE, hoja suelta con nombre si no hubo doblez.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** cartas selladas + firmadas = estudiantes presentes. Bloque cerrado. Cohorte sale sabiendo tarea + frase parafraseada + 3 errores en board + announcement Cl 6 / TEMPLANZA hecho.
