## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT M5 FUTURE TRIPLICATE (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE 3 columnas en board grandes:

   WILL                   GOING TO              PRESENT CONTINUOUS
   spontaneous decision   pre-decided plan      fixed arrangement
   "I'll help you."       "I'm going to start." "I'm meeting Sarah."
   + predictions          + evidence-based      + with date/time/place
   "He'll come."          "It's going to rain." "We're flying Friday."
   + promises/offers      (clouds visible)      (already booked)

2. SEÑALA columna 1 (WILL). DI: "Three uses. Decision in
   THE moment — waiter asks, you answer: 'I'll have the
   chicken.' Prediction — 'He'll come, I'm sure.' Offer or
   promise — 'I'll help you with that.'"

3. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "Will for THE
   MOMENT — that is the first part of the phrase. You did
   not plan it. You decided as you spoke."

4. SEÑALA columna 2 (GOING TO). DI: "Two uses. Plan you
   already decided — 'I'm going to take a course next
   month.' Prediction based on what you SEE — 'Look at
   those clouds, it's going to rain.'"

5. SEÑALA columna 3 (PRESENT CONTINUOUS). DI: "One use at
   A2 level. Fixed arrangement — already on your calendar,
   with someone, somewhere, with a time. 'I'm meeting Sarah
   at 3 PM tomorrow.' 'We're flying to Bogota on Friday.'"

6. [ERROR TIPICO #1] ESCRIBES "I will take a course next
   month" (con plan ya decidido). TACHAS "will take",
   ESCRIBES "I'm going to take a course next month." DI:
   "Pre-decided plan = GOING TO." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

7. [ERROR TIPICO #2] ESCRIBES "I'm going to call you in
   5 minutes" (cuando recien decide en el momento). TACHAS
   "I'm going to call", ESCRIBES "I'll call you in 5
   minutes." DI: "Decision in the moment = WILL." COHORTE
   repite 2 veces.

8. [ERROR TIPICO #3] ESCRIBES "I think will rain." TACHAS
   y ESCRIBES "I think IT WILL rain." DI: "Will always
   needs a subject. No subject, no sentence." COHORTE
   repite 2 veces.

9. [VIRTUD] activation: si alguien titubea entre going-to
   y present continuous, DI suave: "Prudence. Which future
   fits this thought? Pick the form."

10. PIDELES en parejas: TRIGGER DRILL — tu lees la situacion,
    ellos producen la frase con el futuro correcto.
    Situaciones (di una a una, 5-6 total):

      a) "Waiter asks what you want for lunch."
         -> WILL ("I'll have the chicken.")
      b) "You enrolled in a course last week."
         -> GOING TO ("I'm going to take a course.")
      c) "You booked a flight for tomorrow morning."
         -> PRESENT CONT ("I'm flying tomorrow.")
      d) "Friend asks for help with a heavy box."
         -> WILL ("I'll help you.")
      e) "You see dark clouds in the sky."
         -> GOING TO ("It's going to rain.")
      f) "You have a meeting confirmed at 4 PM Friday."
         -> PRESENT CONT ("I'm meeting the team at 4 PM
            on Friday.")

    Parejas alternan: uno escucha, otro produce, intercambian.
    8 minutos.

11. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca en
    2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota errores con
    NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

12. [ERROR TIPICO #4] ESCRIBES "I'm gonna start next year."
    DI suave: "Gonna" es informal — speech only. Spelling
    = GOING TO. No "gonna" en escrito formal. NO TACHES,
    solo marca como aceptable en oral, no en paper.

13. [ERROR TIPICO #5] ESCRIBES "We will going to start
    next week." TACHAS la mezcla, ESCRIBES dos opciones:
    "We WILL START next week." / "We're GOING TO START
    next week." DI: "Pick one. Never mix two futures in
    one verb." COHORTE repite las 2 versiones 2 veces.

14. [ERROR TIPICO #6] ESCRIBES "I meet Sarah tomorrow at
    3 PM" (presente simple en vez de continuo para arreglo
    fijo). TACHAS "I meet", ESCRIBES "I'm meeting Sarah
    tomorrow at 3 PM." DI: "Fixed arrangement = present
    continuous. Not simple." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

15. PIDELES en parejas: cada uno produce UNA frase con
    cada futuro (3 frases por persona). El compañero
    marca cual futuro uso. 5 minutos.

16. CIRCULA, CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo construya en las
    3 formas sin titubear. DI: "Stand up — we go to the
    simulation."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 30 min y aun confunden WILL con GOING TO, NO AVANCES a present continuous como futuro. Reduce a 2 formas hoy (will + going to), deja present continuous para reforzar Cl 5. Mejor LAND 2 bien que rush 3 shaky.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas produciendo en las 3 formas sin pedir ayuda en el trigger drill.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: NEXT QUARTER WORKPLACE PLAN (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "NEXT QUARTER WORKPLACE PLAN"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. You are coworkers. You discuss
   what is happening at work in the next 3 months. Each
   pair must produce AT LEAST 3 sentences with WILL, 3 with
   GOING TO, and 3 with PRESENT CONTINUOUS. Observer in
   each pair marks which future each sentence uses and
   flags wrong picks."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This call is the phrase in
   action — pick the future that fits each thought. Will
   for the moment, going-to for the plan, present continuous
   for the arrangement. Prudence."

4. ESCRIBE 2 mini-scenarios en board:

   SCENARIO 1 — Quarterly planning meeting
     Coworkers discuss: a new project starting in 2 months,
     a client visit already on the calendar, ideas they
     just thought of for next quarter.

   SCENARIO 2 — End-of-quarter review
     Coworkers discuss: what they will do if a deadline
     slips (will), what they already decided to change
     (going to), what is already scheduled with the team
     (present cont).

5. ESCRIBE referencias rapidas en una esquina del board:

      WILL          GOING TO         PRESENT CONT
      moment        plan             arrangement
      "I'll do X"   "I'm going to    "I'm meeting Y
                    do X"             on Friday at 3"

6. PIDELES round 1 (12 min): trios — speaker A, speaker B,
   observer. Pareja A+B conversa con el scenario 1. Observer
   anota cada futuro usado en una columna: W / GT / PC.
   ROTA roles cada 4 min (A->B->observer->A).

7. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces en el
   mismo trio -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven al trio.

8. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, switch to
   Spanish, think 5 seconds, come back to English. Don't
   freeze."

9. ROTA trios. Round 2 (12 min): nuevo trio, scenario 2.
   Mismas reglas. Observer marca y al final reporta al
   grupo: "My trio produced X will, X going-to, X present
   continuous, and Y wrong picks."

10. [VIRTUD] activation durante la simulacion: si alguien
    suelta un futuro que no encaja con el pensamiento, DI
    suave: "Prudence. Which future fits this thought?
    Pick the form."

11. ROTA trios otra vez. Round 3 (10 min): formato libre,
    cualquier scenario. Push para que aparezcan los 3
    futuros en cada turno de habla.

12. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces en
    trios distintos. Anota con NOMBRE en tu cuaderno.

13. CIERRA con UN trio modelo: 90 segundos frente al grupo
    (coworker A + B + observer). COHORTE cuenta cuantos WILL
    / GOING TO / PRESENT CONT cazaron correctos. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si un trio se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("I just decided to redo the report — what future?" / "We already booked the room for Tuesday at 10 — what future?") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 3 rotaciones + UNA demo al frente + cada trio reportando conteo de los 3 futuros.

## BLOCK 4 — CIERRE (~15 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board: "ERRORES DE HOY"

2. ESCRIBE los 3 errores tipicos (anonimos):
     X "I will take a course next month" (decidido)
       -> "I'm going to take a course next month."
     X "I'm going to call you in 5 min" (recien decide)
       -> "I'll call you in 5 minutes."
     X "We will going to start"
       -> "We will start" / "We're going to start."

3. COHORTE repite las 3 correcciones 2 veces en coro.

4. [FRASE] close: UN estudiante (al azar) dice la frase de
   memoria + LA PARAFRASEA en sus propias palabras. Aceptas
   cualquier parafraseo que mantenga "will = moment,
   going-to = plan, present continuous = arrangement, pick
   the right one."

5. [VIRTUD] cierre: PREGUNTA: "Which future did you pick
   today that you would have said wrong yesterday?" — 2
   respuestas maximo, rapido, sin discusion.

6. ESCRIBE HOMEWORK en board (mientras la dices):

      HOMEWORK CL 5 — DUE BEFORE CL 5, 7:00 PM
      TIME: 20-30 min (A2). NOT fragmented.

      (1) WRITING — 10 mixed sentences:
          - 4 with WILL (moment / prediction / offer):
              "If you call, I ___ answer."
              "I think it ___ rain tomorrow."
          - 3 with GOING TO (pre-decided plan):
              "Next month I ___ start a new course."
              "Look at those clouds — it ___ rain soon."
          - 3 with PRESENT CONTINUOUS (fixed arrangement):
              "I ___ (meet) Sarah at 3 PM tomorrow."
              "We ___ (fly) to Bogota on Friday."
          Hand in on paper next class.

      (2) AUDIO eyes closed — 2 min — "My next month plan"
          Use AT LEAST 3 WILL, 3 GOING TO, 3 PRESENT CONT.
          Label: A2-4. Send to WhatsApp group before Cl 5.

      (3) LISTENING — 10 min YouTube — search "workplace
          planning English conversation". Note 3 sentences
          with WILL, 3 with GOING TO, 3 with PRESENT
          CONTINUOUS, well used. Paper.

      RULE: not fragmented. No "I didn't have time."

7. DI: "Next class Cl 5: M6 — Right on Time. Scheduled
   events. Prudence day 5 CIERRA the block. Tomorrow Cl 6
   opens TEMPERANCE. See you measured and prepared."
```

**[ALERTA]** si un estudiante no anota la tarea -> DI: "Phone out. Photo of the board now." Nadie se va sin saberla. Si alguien protesta por la longitud: "20-30 min total, not fragmented. That is the rule."
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** cohorte sale sabiendo la tarea + UN estudiante diciendo la frase de memoria + 3 errores en board.
