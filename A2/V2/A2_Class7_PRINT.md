## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT M8 THAT (~52 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board el titulo y las REGLAS del libro
   (M8, p.62-63), tal cual:

   THE RELATIVE PRONOUN "THAT" — joins two sentences

   1) Relative pronouns CONNECT two sentences to add info.
   2) "that" is about equal to Spanish "que".
   3) In simple sentences it's common & OK to OMIT "that":
        "I think that you're sick" = "I think you're sick."
   4) The component clauses can be negative or question
      normally:
        "Do you think that I am sick?"
        "I don't think that you are sick."
        "I think that you aren't sick."
   5) DIFFERENT from Spanish: if VERB 1 is PAST, VERB 2
      must ALSO be PAST.

2. ESCRIBE los 6 VERBOS del modulo (vocab, p.61) con su
   pasado irregular:
        know -> knew      think -> thought
        see  -> saw       hear  -> heard
        feel -> felt      say   -> said

3. ESCRIBE el contraste PRESENTE / PASADO del libro (p.63):

      PRESENT                         PAST
      I know (that) you live here.    I knew (that) you lived here.
      I think (that) you want it.     I thought (that) you wanted it.
      I see (that) you are happy.     I saw (that) you were happy.
      I hear (that) you like Nicole.  I heard (that) you liked Nicole.
      I feel (that) you are confused. I felt (that) you were confused.
      Mark says (that) you work.      Mark said (that) you were working.

4. SEÑALA la regla 5. DI: "Look — PRESENT left, PAST right.
   When verb 1 jumps to the past, verb 2 jumps too. 'are' ->
   'were'. 'live' -> 'lived'. Both sides move together.
   That is the balance — temperance."

5. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "The phrase
   shows it — 'I think THAT I stay calm; I know THAT I can
   wait.' One steady word joins the two ideas."

6. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "I thought that you ARE sick" -> TACHA
   "are", ESCRIBE "I thought that you WERE sick." DI: "Past
   with past. Verb 1 past -> verb 2 past." COHORTE repite
   2 veces.

7. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "I think that you ARE sick yesterday"
   -> mezcla -> ESCRIBE las 2 versiones limpias: "I think
   that you ARE sick" (now) / "I thought that you WERE sick"
   (past). DI: "Pick one timeline and keep it." COHORTE
   repite 2 veces.

8. [ERROR TIPICO #3] omitir el verbo o el sujeto en frase 2
   ("I think that is sick") -> ESCRIBE "I think that HE is
   sick." DI: "Clause 2 needs its own subject + verb."
   COHORTE repite 2 veces.

9. PRACTICA dirigida (oral, presente): TU das el comienzo,
   el grupo completa con "that":
        "I know that ___."   "I see that ___."
        "I think that ___."  "I hear that ___."
   8-10 vueltas, distintos estudiantes.

10. [VIRTUD] activation: si alguien rompe la concordancia,
    DI suave: "Temperance. Stay consistent. Past with past."

11. PIDELES en parejas: "Student A makes a PRESENT sentence
    with a verb + that ('I think that you...'). Student B
    repeats it in the PAST ('You thought that I...'). Use
    know/think/see/hear/feel/say. 6 exchanges, then switch.
    5 minutes."

12. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca en
    2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota errores con
    NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

13. ESCRIBE la regla 4 en accion (negativo / pregunta):
        "Do you think that Rome is beautiful?"
        "I don't think that Mary is here."
        "I think that you aren't ready."
    Practica 4-5 vueltas rapidas en coro.

14. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "I no think that..." -> TACHA, ESCRIBE
    "I DON'T think that..." DI: "Negative on verb 1 with
    don't/doesn't. Callback to week 1." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

15. PIDELES en parejas round 2: "Now MIX. 6 sentences total
    — 2 present, 2 past (keep both verbs past), 1 negative,
    1 question. Theme: what you think/know/hear about your
    job or your week. 5 minutes."

16. CIRCULA. CORRIGE cuando el mismo error aparezca 2 veces
    en parejas distintas.

17. CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo una dos frases con "that" y
    mantenga la concordancia de tiempos. DI: "Stand up — we
    go to the simulation. Workplace relay."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 32 min y aun rompen la concordancia pasado-pasado, NO AVANCES al mix. Quedate solo en PRESENTE ("I think that you are...") hasta que salga limpio; deja la concordancia de pasado para el opener de Cl 8. Mejor LAND uno bien que rush dos shaky.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas uniendo 2 frases con "that", concordancia presente-presente y pasado-pasado correcta.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: WORKPLACE RELAY (~36 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "WORKPLACE RELAY"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. One is the MESSENGER who relays
   what a third person (the boss, a client, a coworker)
   said or thinks. The other ASKS for the message. Every
   relay must use 'that'. You must use, in the whole
   conversation, AT LEAST:
     - 4 sentences with 'that' in the PRESENT
       ('The boss says that the meeting is at 3')
     - 3 sentences with 'that' in the PAST, both verbs past
       ('The client said that he wanted the report')
     - 1 NEGATIVE ('I don't think that we are late')
   Then rotate."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This relay is the phrase in
   action. You join two ideas calmly — 'She said THAT the
   plan changed.' One steady word, both sides consistent."

4. ESCRIBE 2 mini-scenarios en board:

   SCENARIO 1 — Relaying a manager's instructions
     Messenger relays: what the manager says about the
     schedule, what the manager said yesterday, what the
     team thinks. "The manager says that we start at 8."
     "She said that the client was happy."

   SCENARIO 2 — Passing on a client's message
     Messenger relays: what the client wants, what the
     client said on the phone, what you heard from
     reception. "The client says that he needs the file."
     "Reception said that he called at noon."

5. PIDELES round 1 (5 min): pareja escoge un scenario.
   Roles claros. El que pide el mensaje arranca.

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces en
   la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven a parejas.

7. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, switch to
   Spanish, think 5 seconds, come back to English with
   'that'. Don't freeze."

8. ROTA parejas + cambia roles. Round 2 (5 min): cambien
   de scenario tambien.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante la simulacion: si alguien
   mezcla presente y pasado, DI suave: "Temperance. Stay
   consistent. Past with past."

10. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces en
    parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu cuaderno.

11. OBSERVER COUNT (ligero): en round 2, asigna a 2
    estudiantes como observers — cuentan en silencio
    cuantas frases con "that" en PRESENTE y cuantas en
    PASADO oyeron en la pareja vecina. Reportan: "Pair X —
    4 present, 3 past." No correccion, solo conteo.

12. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente al
    grupo (3 relays presente + 3 relays pasado). COHORTE
    cuenta cuantos "that" con concordancia correcta
    cazaron. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("What did the boss say about the deadline?" / "What does the client think about the price?") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 2 rotaciones + UNA demo al frente + observer count reportado.
