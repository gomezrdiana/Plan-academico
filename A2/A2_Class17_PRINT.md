### ☐ 00:13–00:33 | LIBRO: MÓDULO 19 — PASSIVE VOICE IRREGULAR PARTICIPLES (20 min)

**⚠️ MÁXIMO 20 MINUTOS. NO TE PASES.**

**⚠️ CONCEPTO CLAVE — Passive con verbos IRREGULARES:**

En M18 viste pasiva con regulares (-ed). Hoy: pasiva con irregulares — el participio NO termina en -ed, hay que memorizarlo.

**Estructura misma:** OBJECT + BE + PAST PARTICIPLE (+ by + agent)

**Verbos irregulares clave (escribe en el tablero):**
```
INFINITIVE → PAST → PARTICIPLE        EJEMPLO PASIVA
go         → went → GONE              "It has GONE missing."
eat        → ate  → EATEN             "All the food has been EATEN."
take       → took → TAKEN             "My phone was TAKEN yesterday."
drive      → drove → DRIVEN           "The car has been DRIVEN."
write      → wrote → WRITTEN          "The book was WRITTEN by her."
speak      → spoke → SPOKEN           "Spanish is SPOKEN here."
break      → broke → BROKEN           "The window was BROKEN."
steal      → stole → STOLEN           "My wallet was STOLEN."
tell       → told  → TOLD             "The truth has been TOLD."
give       → gave  → GIVEN            "A prize was GIVEN to her."
```

Di: "Open your book to Module 19 — Passive Voice Irregular Participles. Read with your partner. 5 minutes."

**Primeros 5 min:** Leen en parejas. Camina **EN SILENCIO**. Si preguntan: "Read it again with your partner."

**Siguientes 15 min:** Siéntate con cada pareja. ELLOS te explican (tú NO explicas):

1. ¿Cuál es la diferencia entre regular e irregular en pasiva? → **El participio**. Regular: verbo + ED. Irregular: forma propia (gone, eaten, taken, etc.).
2. ¿La estructura cambia? → **NO**. Sigue siendo BE + participio.
3. ¿Cuándo se usa "by + agente"? → Cuando es importante quién hizo la acción.
4. ¿Cómo cambia el tiempo? → Solo cambia el verbo BE: present (am/is/are), past (was/were), perfect (has/have been), future (will be).

**Modelado de transformaciones — ACTIVA → PASIVA con irregulares:**

```
ACTIVE                              PASSIVE

"They speak Spanish here."          "Spanish IS SPOKEN here."

"She wrote the book in 2020."       "The book WAS WRITTEN in 2020."

"Someone has taken my phone."       "My phone HAS BEEN TAKEN."

"The chef made the meal."           "The meal WAS MADE by the chef."

"They drove the car for hours."     "The car WAS DRIVEN for hours."
```

Di cada par. La clase repite 2 veces.
