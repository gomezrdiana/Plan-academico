### Round 1 (~9 min) — Tenses + 3rd person -s (clusters 1 + 6)

```
2. DI: "Round 1. Tenses and third person -s. Standing.
   Fast chorus, then one fresh sentence per student."

3. CALL & RESPONSE chorus:
     "Past simple of GO"             -> "WENT"
     "Present perfect of SEND"       -> "HAS / HAVE SENT"
     "He / she / it + WORK"          -> "WORKS"
     "He doesn't WORKS / WORK?"      -> "He DOESN'T WORK"

4. PIDELES: "One fresh sentence each — past simple or
   present perfect, about real work this week. Go around
   the circle." 3 min.

5. [ERROR TIPICO #1] cross-cluster: "He work here since
   2020" -> TACHA "work", ESCRIBE "He HAS WORKED here
   since 2020." DI: "Two clusters in one fix — third
   person AND present perfect for 'since'."
```

### Round 2 (~9 min) — Comparatives + gerunds + phrasals (clusters 2 + 3)

```
6. DI: "Round 2. Comparatives, gerunds, phrasals. Sit
   at minute 4."

7. CALL & RESPONSE chorus:
     "COMP of FAST"              -> "FASTER than"
     "SUP of GOOD"               -> "THE BEST"
     "Gerund of MAKE"            -> "MAKING"
     "Phrasal: HAND _ report"    -> "HAND IN"
     "Phrasal: FILL _ form"      -> "FILL OUT"

8. PAREJAS (4 min): "3 sentences mixing comparative +
   phrasal — workplace context."

9. [ERROR TIPICO #2] cross-cluster: "More fast than the
   email" -> TACHA, ESCRIBE "FASTER than the email." DI:
   "One word, no two. Measured."

10. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA frase. DI: "Clean word,
    matched ending — on purpose."
```

### Round 3 (~9 min) — Questions + order + prep + articles (clusters 4 + 5)

```
11. DI: "Round 3. Questions, word order, prepositions,
    articles. Stand."

12. CALL & RESPONSE chorus:
     "Subject 'WHO' question"        -> "WHO SENT it?"  (no DID)
     "Object 'WHAT' question"        -> "WHAT DID you SEND?"  (with DID)
     "IN / ON / AT the office?"      -> "IN the office"
     "IN / ON / AT Monday?"          -> "ON Monday"
     "IN / ON / AT 9 AM?"            -> "AT 9 AM"
     "A / AN / THE office (first)"   -> "AN office"

13. SPEED LAP (3 min): cada estudiante UNA pregunta con
    Wh + UNA respuesta con preposicion correcta.

14. [ERROR TIPICO #3] cross-cluster: "Who did sent the
    email?" -> TACHA "did", ESCRIBE "WHO SENT the email?"
    DI: "Subject Wh — no DID. Disciplined link."
```

### Round 4 (~9 min) — There is/are + causative + reported + connectors (clusters 6 + 7)

```
15. DI: "Round 4. There is/are, causative, reported,
    connectors. Sit."

16. CALL & RESPONSE chorus:
     "there is / are 3 meetings"    -> "THERE ARE 3 meetings"
     "Causative: I had my car fix"  -> "I HAD my car FIXED"
     "Reported with TOLD ME"        -> "She TOLD ME she WOULD call"
     "BECAUSE / SO connector"       -> student gives one
     "ALTHOUGH / HOWEVER"           -> student gives one

17. PAREJAS (3 min): "Build 3 sentences combining
    reported + connector + causative. Real work
    situations."

18. [ERROR TIPICO #4] cross-cluster: "He said me he was
    busy because the client" -> TACHA "said me",
    ESCRIBE "He TOLD ME he was busy BECAUSE the client
    called." DI: "Right pair: TELL takes object, SAY
    doesn't."
```

### Round 5 (~9 min) — Conditionals + modals + bare verb (cluster 8)

```
19. DI: "Round 5. Conditionals and modals. Stand. Speed
    lap — one sentence per slot, go around the circle
    twice."

20. SLOTS (cada estudiante toma uno):
     1. Cond 1 (real future)     -> "IF I finish, I WILL leave."
     2. Cond 2 (hypothetical)    -> "IF I HAD time, I WOULD travel."
     3. SHOULD or MUST           -> "You MUST sign by Friday."
     4. MIGHT or COULD           -> "It MIGHT rain later."

21. [ERROR TIPICO #5] cross-cluster: "If I will have
    time, I can to leave" -> TACHA "will" + "to",
    ESCRIBE "IF I HAVE time, I CAN LEAVE." DI: "Two
    fixes: no WILL in IF clause, no TO after modal.
    Both temperance — measured pairs."

22. [VIRTUD] activation: cuando alguien recupere un
    patron sin titubear, DI: "That is temperance in
    action. Measured. Matched. Clean."

23. CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo construya en los 9
    clusters sin titubear. DI: "Stand up. Full Workplace

```

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: FULL WORKPLACE DAY (~45 min)

UNA simulacion continua, sin cambio de formato 45 min. UN coordinador maneja 4 micro-escenas back-to-back como si fueran UN solo dia de trabajo. Rota coordinador cada 2 estudiantes.

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "FULL WORKPLACE DAY — 4 scenes,
   1 day, 1 coordinator at a time"

2. DI las reglas: "Stand. ONE of you is the COORDINATOR
   for the day; the rest are colleagues / suppliers /
   clients / manager. We run 4 micro-scenes that follow
   each other as ONE workday. NO format change between
   scenes — flows as one day. Coordinator must use the
   9 clusters across the day, at least once each. Rotate
   coordinator every 2 estudiantes."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This is the phrase in action
   — one clean word, one matched ending, one disciplined
   link, one right pair — all on purpose, through one
   workday."

4. ESCRIBE en board (mientras lo dictas) los 4 momentos
   del dia:

     SCENE 1 — 09:00 MORNING EMAIL (~11 min)
       Coordinator dictates an email aloud to a colleague
       who "types." Forces:
         - past simple ("I FINISHED the audit")
         - connectors ("AND we shipped BECAUSE the
           deadline was Friday")
         - reported speech ("the client TOLD ME the
           data was ready")
       Audience listens; observer flags one cluster
       error at end.

     SCENE 2 — 11:00 MID-DAY CALL (~11 min)
       Coordinator calls a supplier. Forces:
         - present perfect ("we HAVE RECEIVED 3
           boxes")
         - conditionals ("IF you send tomorrow, we
           WILL pay Friday")
         - modals ("you SHOULD confirm by 2 PM")
       Cohort plays supplier (1 speaker, others
       observe).

     SCENE 3 — 14:00 AFTERNOON MEETING (~11 min)
       Coordinator presents to team. Forces:
         - comparatives ("plan B is FASTER than plan A")
         - passive ("the report WAS HANDED IN")
         - there is/are ("THERE ARE 3 risks open")
       Meeting closes with a 1-sentence agreed action.

     SCENE 4 — 17:00 END-OF-DAY REPORT (~11 min)
       Coordinator summarizes to manager. Forces:
         - past simple ("I FINISHED 3 tasks")
         - 3rd person -s ("the supplier CONFIRMS the
           date")
         - causative ("I HAD the printer FIXED")
       Manager asks 2 follow-ups: "What did you decide?"
       / "What will you do tomorrow?"

5. ESCOGE el primer coordinador (al azar). Roles claros
   a la cohorte: who is colleague / supplier / team /
   manager para cada escena.

6. CORRE Scene 1 (~11 min). CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si
   un error aparece 3 veces en la misma escena -> board
   -> coro 2x -> sigue escena.

7. ROTA coordinador. CORRE Scene 2 (~11 min). Flag
   wrong prepositions y missing causative participles.

8. ROTA coordinador. CORRE Scene 3 (~11 min). Flag
   missing back-shifts en reporting y "If I will" errors.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante la simulacion: si alguien
   recupera automaticamente un cluster correcto bajo
   presion de la escena, DI suave: "That is temperance
   in action. Measured."

10. ROTA coordinador. CORRE Scene 4 (~11 min). PUSH
    speed — report en 2 min cubriendo 5 clusters
    minimo, despues 2 follow-ups del manager.

11. [ALERTA] si una escena se atasca 15+ segundos en
    silencio, DROP UN PROMPT ("dictate the first line of
    the email to your colleague" / "ask the supplier about
    delivery") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el
    silencio, NO tomes el rol del coordinador.

12. CIERRA con UN coordinador modelo (el mas fuerte) —
    60 segundos: 1 frase por cluster, 9 frases en 60 seg.
    COHORTE cuenta cuantos clusters cazaron correctos.
    Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si 2 coordinadores consecutivos solo logran 4-5 clusters en su dia, no rotes uno mas — RESET: pide a cohorte que en parejas resuelvan rapido (90 seg) los 4-5 clusters faltantes, despues retoma el siguiente coordinador. Mejor cubrir los 9 con calidad que rush rotations.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 4 escenas corridas + UNA demo modelo al frente + 9 clusters tocados al menos 1 vez.

   RETROSPECTIVA COHORT — CIERRE DEL BLOQUE TEMPLANZA
