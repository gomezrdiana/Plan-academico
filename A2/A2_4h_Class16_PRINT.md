## 8. LIBRO M34 — HOW MUCH DOES IT COST? (00:54–01:16, 22 min) — SENTADO

**OBJETIVO PARA EL ESTUDIANTE:**
> *"Open Module 34 — 'How Much Does It Cost?'. Two ways to ask about prices: with the verb COST, or with BE."*

**Setup (2 min):** *"Open Module 34. Read with partner. 7 min."* Páginas 293-297 del libro.

**Lectura (7 min):** parejas leen en silencio.

**Explicación dirigida (10 min)** — los estudiantes te explican:

1. ¿Dos formas de preguntar por un precio?
   - Con COST: *"How much does the car cost?"* → *"It costs $5,000."*
   - Con BE: *"How much is the car?"* → *"It's $5,000."*
2. Para plural cambian a *"How much DO the cars cost?"* → *"They cost $5,000."*
3. Para plural con BE: *"How much ARE the cars?"* → *"They're $5,000."*
4. ¿Qué significa "each"? → "cada uno / por unidad". *"The sandwiches cost $2 EACH."*
5. ¿Error común? → decir *"How much costs the car?"* (incorrecto). Debe ser *"How much DOES the car cost?"*

**Speed Drill (3 min):** profesor lanza producto + precio → estudiante construye pregunta + respuesta:
- *"laptop / $1,200"* → *"How much does the laptop cost? — It costs $1,200."* o *"How much is the laptop? — It's $1,200."*
- *"sandwiches / $2 each"* → *"How much do the sandwiches cost? — They cost $2 each."*

> Consolidación: si dudan, repite hasta que salga sin pensar.

CLOTHING:
shirt / T-shirt    jeans      pants      belt       jacket
shoes              sandals    sneakers   sweater    underwear
