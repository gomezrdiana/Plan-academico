## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT M3 ORDINALS + DATES (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE 3 columnas grandes en board:

   ORDINALS              DATES PATTERN          READING DATES
   1st  first            American:              "May 25th" =
   2nd  second             month + ordinal        "May twenty-fifth"
   3rd  third              "May 5th"            "January 1st, 2016" =
   4th  fourth             "December 1st"        "January first,
   5th  fifth              "October 23rd"         twenty sixteen"
   9th  ninth            British:               PREP = ON
   10th tenth              the + ordinal          ON May 5th
   11th eleventh           + of + month           ON the 5th of May
   12th twelfth            "the 5th of May"
   20th twentieth          "the 23rd of         NEVER:
   21st twenty-first        October"             X "in May 5th"
   30th thirtieth                                X "May five"
   31st thirty-first                             X "the 3 of June"

2. SEÑALA columna 1. DI: "Most ordinals = number + 'th'.
   Fourth, fifth, sixth, seventh, tenth, twentieth.
   IRREGULARS — only five: first, second, third, ninth,
   twelfth. Memorize these five."

3. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "This is
   what the phrase says — the ordinal is the form
   prudence wears when it names the day. Pick it BEFORE
   you say the date."

4. PRONUNCIATION drill (coro 2x cada uno):
     fifth     = /fɪfθ/   (NOT "fiveth")
     twelfth   = /twelfθ/ (NOT "twelveth")
     ninth     = /naɪnθ/  (drop the 'e' from "nine")
     thirtieth = /THER-ti-eth/

5. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "fiveth" -> TACHA, ESCRIBE "FIFTH".
   COHORTE repite 2 veces en coro.

6. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "twelveth" -> TACHA, ESCRIBE
   "TWELFTH". COHORTE repite 2 veces.

7. PIDELES en parejas (3 min): "Take turns. Say all
   ordinals 1st to 15th, then 20th, 21st, 22nd, 23rd,
   30th, 31st. Partner corrects pronunciation."

8. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca
   en 2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota errores
   con NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

9. SEÑALA columna 2 (dates pattern). DI: "American =
   month first + ordinal. British = 'the' + ordinal +
   'of' + month. Both correct. Pick one and stay
   consistent in one sentence."

10. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "May five" -> TACHA "five",
    ESCRIBE "May FIFTH" (or "May 5th"). DI: "Never the
    cardinal alone for a date. Always the ordinal."
    COHORTE repite 2 veces.

11. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "the 3 of June" -> TACHA, ESCRIBE
    "June 3rd" / "the 3rd of June". DI: "Ordinal, not
    cardinal." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

12. PIDELES en parejas (4 min): "5 dates each — say
    them American style first, then British style.
    Mix months. Partner counts errors silently."

13. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca
    2 veces en parejas distintas.

14. SEÑALA columna 3 (reading + preposition ON). DI:
    "When you READ a date aloud, the year follows the
    day. 'March third, twenty eleven'. The preposition
    for a specific date is ON — same as days of the
    week from Cl 1. ON May 5th. ON Monday."

15. [VIRTUD] activation: si alguien titubea con el
    ordinal, DI suave: "Prudence. Pick the ordinal
    before the date. Plan before speaking."

16. [ERROR TIPICO #5] "the twenty-one of August" ->
    TACHA "twenty-one", ESCRIBE "the TWENTY-FIRST of
    August" / "August 21st". COHORTE repite 2 veces.

17. PIDELES en parejas (6 min): "Combine WILL from Cl 1
    + days from Cl 2 + ordinal today. 6 sentences each.
    Pattern: 'I will + verb + on + day + the + ordinal'.
    Example: 'I will meet you on Monday the fifth'."

18. CIRCULA, CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo construya WILL
    + day + ordinal sin titubear. DI: "Stand up — we
    go to the simulation."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 30 min y aun dudan en ordinals basicos (fifth, twelfth, twentieth), NO AVANCES a fechas completas con WILL. Reduce a ordinals + dates simples hoy, deja WILL combinado para Cl 4. Mejor LAND 2 bien que rush 3 shaky.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas construyendo "I will + verb + on + day + the + ordinal" sin pedir ayuda.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: SCHEDULING THE NEXT MONTH (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "SCHEDULING THE NEXT MONTH"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. You are two colleagues
   planning the next 30 days at work. One is the
   ORGANIZER — proposes meetings, deadlines, events.
   The other is the COWORKER — accepts, declines,
   counter-proposes. In the whole conversation, each
   pair must use AT LEAST 5 dates ALOUD + 3 dates
   WRITTEN in a notebook + 2 WILL constructions like
   'I will meet you on May 5th'. Then you rotate."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This call is the phrase
   in action — pick the ordinal BEFORE you say the
   date. Prudence wears the form of an ordinal when it
   names the day."

4. ESCRIBE 2 mini-scenarios en board:

   SCENARIO 1 — Project deadlines
     Organizer proposes 4 deadlines for next month
     (kickoff, draft, review, final). Coworker
     negotiates dates. Pick days + ordinals.
   SCENARIO 2 — Team events
     Organizer proposes training, lunch, client visit,
     team meeting across next month. Coworker checks
     calendar and confirms / pushes dates.

5. PIDELES round 1 (8 min): pareja escoge un scenario.
   Roles claros. Organizer arranca. Cada uno anota 3
   dates en su cuaderno mientras hablan.

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces
   en la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven
   a parejas.

7. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, switch to
   Spanish, think 5 seconds, come back to English.
   Don't freeze."

8. ROTA parejas + cambia roles. Round 2 (8 min):
   cambien de scenario tambien.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante la simulacion: si
   alguien suelta "May five" o "the 3 of June", DI
   suave: "Prudence. Ordinal first."

10. ROTA parejas otra vez. Round 3 (7 min): mismo
    formato, push para que aparezcan WILL + day +
    ordinal en cada turno.

11. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces
    en parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu
    cuaderno.

12. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente al
    grupo (organizer + coworker, 3 propuestas + 3
    respuestas, todas con fecha completa). COHORTE
    cuenta cuantas fechas + cuantos WILL cazaron
    correctos. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("Propose a kickoff meeting for the first week" / "Confirm a deadline on the twenty-first") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 3 rotaciones + UNA demo al frente + cada cuaderno con 3 fechas escritas.
