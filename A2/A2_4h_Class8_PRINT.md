### 3. 8:13-8:23 | ROMPEHIELO: "DID YOU OR HAVE YOU?" (10 min)

**Que es:** Activacion del contraste entre past simple y present perfect. Crean la necesidad antes de leer la regla.

Escribe en el tablero las 2 columnas:
```
PAST SIMPLE (closed)         PRESENT PERFECT (open)
Yesterday I went...          I have gone...
Last week I ate...           I have eaten...
In 2020 I lived...           I have lived...
```

Modela: "YESTERDAY I went to the store. (closed -- yesterday is over.) But: I HAVE GONE to the store this morning. (open -- this morning is still today.)"

**Round (8 min):** Senala estudiantes. Da un periodo. "Last weekend?" (Closed = past simple.) "This week?" (Open = present perfect.) "In December?" (Closed.) "Today?" (Open.)

Cada estudiante: 1 oracion con el periodo correcto.

### 4. 8:23-8:38 | LIBRO: MODULO 17 -- PAST SIMPLE vs PRESENT PERFECT (15 min)

Di: "Open Module 17. Read Guide 1 with your partner. 5 minutes."

**Primeros 5 min:** Leen.

**Siguientes 10 min:** Te explican (tu NO):
1. ¿Cuando se usa Past Simple? -> Accion terminada en punto especifico del pasado. ("I studied at 2:00.")
2. ¿Cuando se usa Present Perfect? -> 3 casos:
   - Periodo desconocido o no especificado.
   - Accion empezo en el pasado y NO ha terminado.
   - Accion termino, pero en un periodo que SIGUE ABIERTO ("today," "this week").
3. ¿Cual es la clave? -> El PERIODO. Cerrado = past simple. Abierto = present perfect.

### 5. 8:38-8:50 | EJERCICIOS + RESPUESTAS (12 min)

**8 ejercicios. Conjugar en past simple O present perfect:**
```
1. (I + be) ___ in Madrid last week.       -> WAS (last week = closed)
2. (you + travel) ___ to Canada?            -> Have you traveled (no time = open)
3. (Kyle + drive + neg) ___ his car today.  -> hasn't driven (today = open)
4. (the boys + play) ___ in the park at 3:00. -> played (3:00 = closed)
5. (Jessica + wake up) ___ this morning?    -> Has Jessica woken up (this morning = open)
6. (Paul + go + never) ___ to Bogota.       -> has never gone (no time / experience)
7. (the girls + go to bed) ___ yesterday.   -> went to bed (yesterday = closed)
8. (the girls + go to bed) ___ today.       -> have gone to bed (today = open)
```

**Siguientes 10 min:** Te explican:
