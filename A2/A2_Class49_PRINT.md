## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: WORKPLACE DECISION MEETING (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "WORKPLACE DECISION MEETING"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. You are colleagues in a
   workplace decision meeting. You receive ONE decision
   scenario. Pair must argue BOTH sides: each speaker
   uses AT LEAST 3 CONDITIONALS (1 Cond 0, 1 Cond 1,
   1 Cond 2) and AT LEAST 3 MODALS (mix of can / could /
   should / must / might). Pair then agrees on a
   recommendation in 1 sentence. Three rounds, rotate
   partners. Every student speaks at least 6 minutes."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This meeting is the phrase
   in action — match the IF, match the modal. Choose the
   right pair, every time."

4. ESCRIBE 3 scenarios en board:

   SCENARIO 1 — Release product Friday OR postpone next
                month. Argue both sides.
   SCENARIO 2 — Budget for ONE new hire: engineer OR
                designer. Argue both sides.
   SCENARIO 3 — Fully remote OR stay hybrid. Argue
                both sides.

5. PIDELES round 1 (13 min): pareja escoge un scenario.
   Cada hablante argumenta su lado ~5 min usando 3
   conditionals + 3 modals minimo. Pareja acuerda
   recomendacion en 1 frase.

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces en
   la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven a parejas.
   Flag inmediato "If I will" y "can to" en el momento.

7. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, switch to
   Spanish, think 5 seconds, come back to English. Don't
   freeze."

8. ROTA parejas + cambia scenario. Round 2 (13 min):
   push para que aparezca WERE ("If I WERE the boss...")
   y MIGHT (weak possibility). La mayoria default a
   WILL/WOULD — fuerza variedad.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante la simulacion: si alguien
   suelta "If I will" o "I can to", DI suave: "Measure.
   Pair the IF to its tense. Pair the modal to bare verb."

10. ROTA parejas otra vez. Round 3 (13 min): push para
    SPEED + VARIETY. Cada hablante debe usar al menos 4
    modales distintos en el round (asi la cohorte no se
    queda pegada en CAN).

11. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces en
    parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu cuaderno.

12. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente al
    grupo (1 Cond 1 + 1 Cond 2 + 1 modal cada uno + la
    recomendacion conjunta). COHORTE cuenta cuantos
    conditionals y modals cazaron correctos. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("If the client approved today, we would ship tomorrow" / "You should double-check the invoice before sending it") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol.
