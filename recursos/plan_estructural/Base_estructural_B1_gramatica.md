**B1 GRAMMAR & WRITING STRATEGY**

Complete guide for the grammar & writing teacher (Tue-Thu)

*A1/A2 Review + Writing Skills Development + B2 Preparation*

# 1. STRATEGIC OVERVIEW

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Component**     **Details**
  ----------------- -----------------------------------------------------
  Schedule          Tuesday and Thursday

  Session length    2 hours (120 min) --- no break, format changes keep
                    energy up

  Max group size    16 students (same group as conversational class
                    Mon-Wed-Fri)

  Main objectives   1\) Review and consolidate A1/A2 grammar. 2) Develop
                    writing skills for B2 readiness. 3) Bridge gaps
                    identified in conversational classes.

  Golden rule #1    This class SUPPORTS the conversational class ---
                    grammar serves communication, not the other way
                    around.

  Golden rule #2    NO activity longer than 20 min. Change format
                    constantly.

  Golden rule #3    Writing is a SKILL, not punishment. Make it engaging,
                    progressive, and connected to real life.

  Books used        A1 Book (Modules 0-35) + A2 Book (Modules 1-44)

  Montessori tools  GoldList, Answer Keys for self-correction, Shadowing,
                    Exit Tickets, peer review

  Connection to     Every class starts by asking: \'What was difficult in
  conversational    your conversational class?\' Teacher uses this to
                    prioritize grammar review topics.

  Content creation  Grammar tip videos, student progress photos, writing
                    before/after samples, and grammar challenges for
                    social media are integrated as regular classroom
                    activities (see Section 7).
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

  **Frequency**   **Instrument**    **Description**
  --------------- ----------------- -------------------------------------------
  Every class     Exit Ticket       \'Write one sentence using today\'s
                                    grammar\' + \'What\'s still confusing?\'
                                    Quick diagnostic.

  Every class     Error observation Note 5-7 common grammar errors during
                                    practice for Error Fix block.

  Weekly          Writing sample    Collect one writing piece per week for the
                  collected         portfolio. Track progress.

  Weekly          GoldList check    Are students writing phrases? Reviewing?
                                    Notebooks checked.

  Weekly          Satisfaction      Scale 1-5: How do you feel about grammar
                  check-in          this week?

  Monthly         Individual        Review grammar progress, writing portfolio,
                  meeting (10 min)  address concerns.

  Quarterly       Writing           Compare current writing with month 1
                  comparison        writing. Tangible evidence of growth.

  Final           B2 readiness      Timed essay (150-200 words, 30 min).
                  writing test      Evaluated with B2 rubric. Shows preparation
                                    level.

  Final           Complete writing  All collected writing pieces throughout the
                  portfolio         program. Evidence of progression.
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
