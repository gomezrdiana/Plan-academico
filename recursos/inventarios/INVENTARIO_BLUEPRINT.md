## THE 14 METHODOLOGIES DISTRIBUTION

**Rule: Maximum 2 per class (1 primary + 1 support). Methodology should be INVISIBLE to student.** | Part 4

**Available for Integration Throughout All Levels:**

1. TBLT (Task-Based Language Teaching) — Every week; real communicative tasks first, analysis after
2. PBL (Project-Based Learning) — Biweekly projects (10 pre-designed per level)
3. LEGO Serious Play — Construction + narration in English to reduce anxiety
4. STEAM + Inglés — Science/Tech projects with English presentations
5. Gamificación Estructurada — Puntos, levels, Escape Rooms, Jeopardy, Kahoot weekly, Karaoke
6. Debate Club (Oxford Lite) — Random positions, fast thinking, tournaments every 2 months
7. Design Thinking — 5 innovation phases on real Bucaramanga problems
8. Dogme/Teaching Unplugged — Emergent conversation, 80% student talk
9. Translanguaging Estratégico — Spanish as momentary bridge (no frustration while maintaining immersion)
10. TPRS (Teaching Proficiency through Reading and Storytelling) — Ideal A1-A2: absurd co-constructed stories
11. Process Drama y Improvisación — "Yes And...", Hot Seating, Freeze/Unfreeze, Mantle of Expert
12. Diseño Instruccional Neurocientífico — Framework enhancing all others: Spaced Retrieval, Interleaving, Desirable Difficulties
13. Simulation-Based Learning — Real functional roles with consequences, 30-45 min short or 2-4 week extended
14. Extensive Reading — 15-20 min silent reading per class, ~$500-800 USD initial investment for graded readers library
