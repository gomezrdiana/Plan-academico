ANEXO HEIIU B2 — STAR QUESTION BANK
===

> **FOR THE TEACHER:** Use this bank during Day 1 of the Portfolio Build Cycle. Pick 2 random questions per class — one for pairs, one for the volunteer recording. Rotate so students get different categories over the cycle.

> **FOR THE STUDENT:** The first 10 questions are the **MOST COMMON** in real interviews. Memorize a STAR-format answer for each one before you graduate B2.

---

## STAR FORMAT REMINDER

```
S — Situation: When? Where? What was the context?
T — Task:      What was YOUR specific responsibility?
A — Action:    What did YOU do? (use "I", not "we")
R — Result:    What was the outcome? (numbers if possible)
```

---

## TIER 1 — THE TOP 10 (memorize these first)

These appear in 80% of professional interviews. Master them.

1. **Tell me about yourself.**
   *(60-second pitch — same structure as your video pitch)*

2. **Why do you want this job?**
   *(Connect what THEY offer to what YOU want next)*

3. **Tell me about a time you faced a difficult challenge at work and how you handled it.**

4. **Tell me about a time you had to work with a difficult team member.**

5. **Describe a situation where you had to meet a tight deadline. How did you handle it?**

6. **Give me an example of a time you took initiative without being asked.**

7. **Tell me about a mistake you made and what you learned from it.**

8. **Describe a time when you had to learn something new quickly.**

9. **Tell me about a time you led a project or a team.**

10. **Where do you see yourself in 5 years?**

---

## GROUND RULES (FOR THE TEACHER)

1. **Pick questions BEFORE class.** Don't decide in the moment — students sense uncertainty.
2. **Rotate categories.** Don't ask 5 leadership questions in a row.
3. **Time strictly.** 90 seconds per answer. STAR forces conciseness.
4. **Give pronunciation feedback ONLY.** Not strategy advice.
5. **If a student freezes:** "Just answer Situation. Then we'll add Task and Action next time."
6. **Encourage ALL languages of experience.** Every job, every internship, every volunteer experience counts. Even a wedding they organized counts as "led a team."

---

## WHAT YOU DO NOT DO

- ❌ Coach them on what to say in their answer
- ❌ Tell them their experience isn't impressive enough
- ❌ Suggest they invent or exaggerate
- ✅ Only model correct English form. The student decides the content.
