## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT 5-GROUP TABLE + CONTRAST WITH REGULARS (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE la tabla completa en board (toma 5 min,
   vale la pena):

   PAST SIMPLE IRREGULAR — 5 GROUPS

   GROUP 1 — SAME (no change)
      put -> put
      set -> set
      let -> let
      cut -> cut
      hit -> hit
      read -> read (SAME SPELLING, pron /rɛd/)
      Ex: "I put the file on the desk yesterday."
          "She read /rɛd/ the email this morning."
      *** READ trap: same letters, different mouth ***

   GROUP 2 — VOWEL CHANGE
      run -> ran        sit -> sat
      give -> gave      see -> saw
      come -> came      begin -> began
      write -> wrote    drink -> drank
      get -> got        win -> won
      stand -> stood    understand -> understood
      Ex: "I ran to the meeting and sat in front."

   GROUP 3 — -OUGHT / -AUGHT
      think -> thought
      bring -> brought
      buy -> bought
      catch -> caught
      teach -> taught
      fight -> fought
      Ex: "I thought about it and bought the laptop."

   GROUP 4 — -ELT / -ENT / -EPT / -EFT
      feel -> felt
      mean -> meant
      send -> sent
      spend -> spent
      keep -> kept
      sleep -> slept
      leave -> left
      lose -> lost
      pay -> paid
      meet -> met
      Ex: "I felt tired so I left early."

   GROUP 5 — TOTALLY DIFFERENT (TOP HIGH FREQUENCY)
      go -> went
      be -> was / were
      have -> had
      do -> did
      say -> said
      make -> made
      see -> saw
      find -> found
      take -> took
      know -> knew
      Ex: "I went, I had, I did, I said, I made."

   CONTRAST WITH CL 11 REGULARS (callback)
      worked / studied / stopped / traveled /
      played / helped / called / wanted / asked /
      needed
      Mixed example:
        "I WORKED until 6, WENT home, HAD dinner,
         STUDIED for an hour, THOUGHT about today."
      *** Regular + Irregular live in the same
          sentence. The verb decides the form. ***

2. SEÑALA Group 5. DI: "Top 10 most frequent verbs.
   WENT. HAD. DID. SAID. MADE. SAW. TOOK. FOUND.
   KNEW. WAS/WERE. You will use these every single
   day. Own them first, the rest follow the pattern."

3. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "Group
   it, memorize it, write it true. Each verb owns
   its past. No ED can fake it. That is justice on
   paper."

4. SEÑALA Group 1. DI: "Six verbs, same spelling
   past and present. PUT-PUT. SET-SET. LET-LET.
   CUT-CUT. HIT-HIT. And the trap — READ-READ:
   spelled the same, pronounced different. /riːd/
   today, /rɛd/ yesterday. Mouth changes, letters
   don't."

5. SEÑALA Group 2. DI: "Vowel changes. RUN-RAN,
   SIT-SAT, GIVE-GAVE. The consonants stay, the
   vowel moves. Listen to the music — i/a, i/a,
   i/a."

6. SEÑALA Group 3. DI: "-OUGHT and -AUGHT family.
   Five verbs share the sound /ɔːt/. THINK-THOUGHT,
   BRING-BROUGHT, BUY-BOUGHT, CATCH-CAUGHT,
   TEACH-TAUGHT. Same music."

7. SEÑALA Group 4. DI: "-ELT, -ENT, -EPT, -EFT.
   Short endings. FELT, MEANT, SENT, SPENT, KEPT,
   SLEPT, LEFT. Crisp. Final consonant cluster."

8. SEÑALA Group 5. DI: "These don't follow any
   pattern you can guess. Memorize them as words,
   not as patterns. GO-WENT — totally different
   word. BE-WAS/WERE — two forms. HAVE-HAD."

9. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "I goed to the office" -> TACHA
   "goed", ESCRIBE "I WENT to the office." COHORTE
   repite 2 veces en coro.

10. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "She thinked about it" -> TACHA
    "thinked", ESCRIBE "She THOUGHT about it." DI:
    "Group 3 — -OUGHT family." COHORTE repite 2
    veces.

11. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "We getted there" -> TACHA
    "getted", ESCRIBE "We GOT there." DI: "Group 2
    — vowel change. Like sit-sat, run-ran."  COHORTE
    repite 2 veces.

12. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "He bringed his laptop" ->
    TACHA "bringed", ESCRIBE "He BROUGHT his laptop."
    DI: "Group 3 again — bought, thought, brought,
    caught. Same -OUGHT family." COHORTE repite 2
    veces.

13. [ERROR TIPICO #5] "I readed the email" (pron
    /riːdɪd/) -> TACHA "readed", ESCRIBE "I READ
    the email" + ESCRIBE encima /rɛd/. DI: "Group
    1 — same spelling. But READ in past pronounces
    /rɛd/. Letters don't change, the mouth does."
    COHORTE repite 2 veces: 'I read /riːd/ books.
    I read /rɛd/ the email yesterday.'

14. [VIRTUD] activation: cuando alguien titubee
    entre regular y irregular, DI suave: "Justice.
    That verb has its own past form. Find its group.
    Write it true."

15. PIDELES en parejas: "Transform 8 sentences. I
    dictate the present, you write the past. Each
    in a different group." 6 min. DICTA estos 8:
       a. "I go to the office every day."
          -> "I WENT to the office yesterday." (G5)
       b. "She thinks it's a good idea."
          -> "She THOUGHT it WAS a good idea." (G3+G5)
       c. "We put the files on the desk."
          -> "We PUT the files on the desk
              yesterday." (G1 — same)
       d. "He brings his laptop daily."
          -> "He BROUGHT his laptop yesterday." (G3)
       e. "I read /riːd/ the news every morning."
          -> "I READ /rɛd/ the news this morning."
              (G1 — SAME spelling, /rɛd/ pron)
       f. "They feel tired."
          -> "They FELT tired." (G4)
       g. "She runs to the bus."
          -> "She RAN to the bus." (G2)
       h. "We work until 6 and have dinner."
          -> "We WORKED until 6 and HAD dinner."
              (regular + G5 mixed — callback Cl 11)
    CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error
    aparezca 2 veces.

16. PODIUM (7 min). DOS volunteers al frente.
    Tu dictas un verbo base, ellos escriben el
    pasado + dicen el grupo. Cohorte verifica en
    coro: "Group N — confirmed!"

    12 verbos para llamar:
       go     -> went (G5)
       have   -> had (G5)
       think  -> thought (G3)
       take   -> took (G2)
       put    -> put (G1)
       send   -> sent (G4)
       buy    -> bought (G3)
       read   -> read /rɛd/ (G1)
       run    -> ran (G2)
       leave  -> left (G4)
       say    -> said (G5)
       bring  -> brought (G3)

17. Aplauso al final. CIERRA cuando 80% construya
    pasados correctos + identifique el grupo sin
    titubear. DI: "Stand. Push chairs to the walls.
    We go to the live application."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 35 min y aun reemplazan irregulares por -ED, NO AVANCES al podium. Reduce: solo Group 5 + Group 2 + Group 3 hoy, deja Group 1 + Group 4 para Cl 13 opener. Mejor LAND 3 patterns bien que rush 5 shaky.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: IRREGULAR PAST SPRINT (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "IRREGULAR PAST SPRINT
   — 25 VERBS + 8 TRANSFORMATIONS"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. Paper ready. Two phases.
   PHASE A — I dictate 25 base verbs at speed. You
   write the past form + the group number (1-5). 1
   minute per 5 verbs. Observer marks errors. PHASE
   B — I read 8 mixed sentences (regular + irregular
   present tense). You transform to past on paper.
   Then we share, then we rotate partners."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This sprint IS the
   phrase — group it, memorize it, write it true.
   25 verbs, 5 groups, no ED on the irregulars."

4. ESCRIBE en board los 25 verbos para Phase A
   (lista oculta del estudiante hasta que dicta):

   PHASE A — 25 BASE VERBS TO TRANSFORM:
     1 go      2 have     3 think    4 take    5 put
     6 see     7 give     8 come     9 find    10 tell
     11 feel  12 become  13 leave   14 keep   15 begin
     16 hear  17 run     18 bring   19 write  20 sit
     21 lose  22 pay     23 meet    24 spend  25 grow
     (extras if fast: spoke / bought / sent / built /
      fell / cut / read /rɛd/)

5. PHASE A — DICTATION (15 min):
   a. PIDE: "Paper in 3 columns: VERB / PAST /
      GROUP. I dictate 1 by 1, you write all 3
      columns at speed. No talking."
   b. DICTA en bloques de 5. Pausa 30 seg cada
      bloque para que cierren.
   c. CIERRA cada bloque escribiendo las respuestas
      en board:
        Block 1 (1-5): went/G5 · had/G5 · thought/G3 ·
          took/G2 · put/G1
        Block 2 (6-10): saw/G2 · gave/G2 · came/G2 ·
          found/G2 · told/G4
        Block 3 (11-15): felt/G4 · became/G2 ·
          left/G4 · kept/G4 · began/G2
        Block 4 (16-20): heard/G4 · ran/G2 ·
          brought/G3 · wrote/G2 · sat/G2
        Block 5 (21-25): lost/G4 · paid/G4 · met/G4 ·
          spent/G4 · grew/G2
   d. COHORTE cuenta su score. NO public reading.
      Cada uno sabe su propio numero.

6. PHASE B — TRANSFORMATION (20 min):
   a. PIDE: "Paper turn over. New section. 8
      sentences. I read in present, you write in
      past. Use the right group form. No ED on
      irregulars. Use regulars correctly too."
   b. DICTA estas 8:
       1. "I go to the office and work until 6."
          -> "I went to the office and worked
              until 6." (G5 + reg)
       2. "She thinks it costs too much and stops."
          -> "She thought it cost too much and
              stopped." (G3 + G1 + reg)
       3. "We have dinner and watch a movie."
          -> "We had dinner and watched a movie."
              (G5 + reg)
       4. "He reads /riːd/ the email and replies."
          -> "He read /rɛd/ the email and replied."
              (G1 — pron trap + reg)
       5. "They take the bus and arrive late."
          -> "They took the bus and arrived late."
              (G2 + reg)
       6. "I feel sick and leave early."
          -> "I felt sick and left early." (G4 + G4)
       7. "She runs to the meeting and apologizes."
          -> "She ran to the meeting and apologized."
              (G2 + reg)
       8. "We bring the laptop and study together."
          -> "We brought the laptop and studied
              together." (G3 + reg)

7. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3
   veces en la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x ->
   vuelven a pares.

8. ROTA parejas. Mini round 3 (10 min): pareja
   inventa 4 oraciones propias (no de la lista) que
   mezclen 2 irregulares + 2 regulares. Una persona
   dicta, la otra escribe. Switch.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante el sprint: si alguien
   escriba "goed" o "thinked" o "readed", DI suave:
   "Justice. Find the group. Write the form true."

10. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2
    veces en pares distintos. Anota con NOMBRE en
    tu cuaderno.

11. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente
    al grupo. Pareja escribe en board 4 pasados
    (uno por grupo 1-2-3-4 + el bonus 5) en menos
    de 2 min. COHORTE cuenta y confirma. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos durante Phase B, DROP UN PROMPT ("Yesterday — I went to / had / thought about / took") y RETIRATE. NO completes la oracion. NO escribas por ellos.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 25-verb dictation completo + 8 transformations completas + UNA demo al frente con los 5 grupos visibles.

## BLOCK 4 — CIERRE (~15 min)

```

7. PASE Grammar -> Conv entregada ANTES de salir del
   aula (ver siguiente seccion).

8. DI: "Next class Cl 13: past simple NEGATIVES and
   QUESTIONS — did + base verb / didn't + base verb.
   'I didn't GO' — not 'I didn't went'. The irregular
   collapses to base after DID. Cl 13 continues
   JUSTICE day 3 — same virtue, new shape. Today
   we wrote 25 irregulars true. Real B1 sounds like

   coffee, I read /rɛd/ the email, I left at 6.'
   Five verbs, five groups, zero ED. Any question
```
