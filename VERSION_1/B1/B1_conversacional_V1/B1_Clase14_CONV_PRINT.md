## 7. SIMULACIÓN — "THE PROCESS WALKTHROUGH" (00:42–01:02, 20 min) — DE PIE

**OBJETIVO PARA LA ESTUDIANTE:**
> *"Stand up. You are training a new employee. You explain HOW a process works at your job — step by step — using passive voice. You describe the ACTION, not the person."*

### Setup (3 min)

**Estructura sugerida (Tablero):**

```
"THE PROCESS WALKTHROUGH" — 3-4 min each

You are training a new hire. Explain ONE real process from your
job in 5-7 steps. Use PASSIVE VOICE for each step:

STEP 1: "First, the request is received..."
STEP 2: "Then, the data is checked..."
STEP 3: "After that, the form is approved by..."
STEP 4: "The product is prepared..."
STEP 5: "Finally, the client is notified..."

USE: 6+ passive voice forms / 2+ "is done / are sent / was
     approved" / 1+ sequence word (first, then, after that,
     finally) / professional context ONLY
```

### Modela (3 min)

> *"Let me train you. At my work, English classes are designed before the term starts. First, the level is assessed. Then, the curriculum is built around the book. The guides are written and reviewed by coordination. Each lesson is delivered in two blocks. At the end, the students are evaluated — but that part is handled only by coordination, never by us. The work is shared. That's how justice is done in a team."*

### Round 1 — Parejas (10 min)

Cada pareja: 5 min cada una. Persona A es el trainer, B es el nuevo empleado + cuenta cuántas formas pasivas usó A.

### Cierre (4 min)

2 voluntarias hacen su walkthrough frente al grupo. Cohorte cuenta formas pasivas.

> Anota errores con NOMBRE en libreta personal.
