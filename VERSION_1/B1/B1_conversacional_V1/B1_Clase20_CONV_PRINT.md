## 7. SIMULACIÓN — "THE PRODUCT SPECS INTERVIEW" (00:42–01:02, 20 min) — DE PIE

**OBJETIVO PARA LA ESTUDIANTE:**
> *"Stand up. You are a buyer interviewing a vendor (or a manager interviewing a supplier) about the features of a real product or service. You ask with 'How + adjective' and they answer with an intensifier — really / very / pretty / kinda / sorta / not very + adjective."*

### Setup (3 min)

**Estructura sugerida (Tablero):**

```
"THE PRODUCT SPECS INTERVIEW" — 4-5 min each pair

BUYER (you):  ask 6 questions with "How + adjective"
VENDOR (your partner): answer 6 with an intensifier
              + adjective

POINT 1: "How new is the model?"
         -> "It is pretty new. It is the 2024 version."
POINT 2: "How reliable is the system?"
         -> "It is very reliable. 99% uptime last year."
POINT 3: "How expensive is the premium plan?"
         -> "It is kinda expensive, but worth it."
POINT 4: "How complicated is the installation?"
         -> "It is not very complicated. Forty minutes."
POINT 5: "How fast is the support response?"
         -> "It is really fast. Under one hour."
POINT 6: "How important is the warranty?"
         -> "It is very important. Five years included."

USE: 6+ "How + adjective" questions / 6+ intensifier
     answers / professional context ONLY / honest
     measure (don't oversell, don't undersell)
```

### Modela (3 min)

> *"Let me interview the vendor. How new is the model? — Pretty new, the 2024 version. How reliable is the system? — Very reliable, we have 99% uptime. How expensive is the maintenance plan? — It is kinda expensive, around four hundred a month. How complicated is the installation? — It is not very complicated, about forty minutes if you follow the manual. How long is the warranty? — It is pretty long, five years included. One thing: if a teacher asks anything about evaluations, midterms or finals, you do not handle that — it goes only to coordination, never to us. Back to the interview: I won't oversell or undersell. The honest measure of a quality is what makes a buyer trust the answer — that's the strength on day five of FORTALEZA."*

### Round 1 — Parejas (10 min)

Cada pareja: 5 min cada una. Persona A es el buyer (6 preguntas "How + adjective"), B es el vendor (6 respuestas con intensificador + adjective) — luego invierten. Ambos cuentan cuántas preguntas/respuestas usaron correctamente y si la respuesta fue honesta (¿exageró? ¿escondió un problema real?).

### Cierre (4 min)
