## 7. SIMULACIÓN — "MY ENGLISH STORY" (00:45–01:02, 17 min) — DE PIE

**OBJETIVO PARA LA ESTUDIANTE:**
> *"Mini-presentation. Inspired by the text you just read. Tell YOUR English journey in 3-4 min using all the tenses we've seen this week. You just read someone else's story — now tell yours."*

### Setup (3 min)

**Estructura sugerida (Tablero):**

```
"MY ENGLISH STORY" — 4 min mini-presentation

PARAGRAPH 1 (Past Simple): When you STARTED learning English.
  "I started learning English in [year]. I was [age]."

PARAGRAPH 2 (Past Continuous + Past Simple): What was happening
  while you struggled.
  "While I was studying at night, I often felt..."

PARAGRAPH 3 (Present Perfect): What HAS shaped you since.
  "I have learned that... I have stopped..."

PARAGRAPH 4 (Present Perfect + Present Continuous): Where you are
  now and where you are going.
  "I have built [X]. Now I am becoming [Y]."

USE: 5+ Present Perfect / 3+ Past Simple / 2+ Past Continuous /
     1+ "while" / 1+ "already" or "still" or "until"
```

### Modela (3 min)

> *"I started learning English in 2010. I was 22 and working as a translator. While I was studying linguistics at university, I often struggled with tenses. I had already given up twice — once in 2008, once in 2009. But during those years, something quiet was happening. I have built confidence slowly. I have stopped expecting fluency to come fast. Now I am becoming the teacher I once needed."*

### Round 1 — Parejas (10 min)

Cada pareja: 5 min cada una. Persona A presenta, B escucha + cuenta tiempos usados.

### Cierre (4 min)

2 voluntarias presentan frente al grupo. Cohorte cuenta cuántas usaron Present Perfect.

> Anota errores con NOMBRE.

## 11. ERROR PAPER + FRASE DEL DÍA CIERRE + PASE + TAREA (01:43–01:50, 7 min) — DE PIE

### Error Paper Live (2 min)

**4 errores típicos:**

```
1. ❌ "What I has done shaped me."        → ✅ "What I HAVE done HAS shaped me." (3rd person + auxiliary)
2. ❌ "I have build confidence."           → ✅ "I have BUILT confidence." (built irregular)
3. ❌ "I am becoming a person who..."      → ✅ correct — verify natural use
4. ❌ "Since 5 years I have worked..."    → ✅ "I have worked FOR 5 years." (for + duration)
```
