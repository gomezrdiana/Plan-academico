# 🔧 YOUR ROLE — FACILITIES MANAGER

**B1 MASTERY — Cl 11 Grammar — VIP Hotel Hospitality Activity (13/05/2026)**

---

## YOUR POSITION
You handle **all technical failures** in the hotel:
- HVAC (heating, ventilation, air conditioning)
- Plumbing
- Electrical
- Maintenance and repairs

You manage a team of technicians. You decide priority order. You give realistic timelines.

## YOUR OBJECTIVE IN THE SCENE
1. **Acknowledge the failure** without minimizing it.
2. **Dispatch a technician** immediately — and explain ETA clearly.
3. **Give realistic timelines** — never promise what you can't deliver.
4. **Offer alternatives** — move the guest to another suite if repair will take too long.
5. **Follow up** — ensure the guest knows when the issue is resolved.

## REQUIRED VOCABULARY — use at least 5
- out of order
- maintenance request
- work order
- on-call technician
- HVAC
- deep clean
- prompt resolution
- service interruption
- emergency protocol
- dispatch

## REQUIRED GRAMMAR — you MUST produce both at least once

### ✅ M12 — Past Simple + Past Continuous
**Example:** *"While the technician was repairing another suite, your AC failed — we received the alert at 6:15."*

### ✅ M13 — Already / Still / Until — use at least 2
**Example:** *"I have already dispatched our on-call technician. He is still finishing the previous job. He won't be at your suite until 9:30."*

## TONE REGISTER (B2)
- Operational + clear timelines
- Calm and competent
- Never overpromise — under-promise and over-deliver

**Sample phrases:**
- *"Rest assured, we'll have this sorted by 10 PM at the latest."*
- *"I'll personally oversee the repair."*
- *"Allow me to offer you a temporary alternative suite while we resolve this."*
- *"You won't be charged for tonight as a goodwill gesture."*
- *"Please bear with us — this is our utmost priority."*
