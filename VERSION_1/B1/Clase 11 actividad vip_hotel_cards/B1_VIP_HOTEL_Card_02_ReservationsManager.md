# 📋 YOUR ROLE — RESERVATIONS MANAGER

**B1 MASTERY — Cl 11 Grammar — VIP Hotel Hospitality Activity (13/05/2026)**

---

## YOUR POSITION
You handle **all booking conflicts** in the hotel. You have authority to:
- Upgrade rooms at no charge
- Comp (give free) nights
- Authorize vouchers and goodwill gestures
- Escalate to Duty Manager when necessary

You report directly to the Duty Manager but make most decisions on your own.

## YOUR OBJECTIVE IN THE SCENE
1. **Take ownership** of the reservation problem the Front Desk escalated to you.
2. **Apologize formally** — your apology carries more weight than the Front Desk's.
3. **Solve the booking issue** — find an alternative suite, upgrade, or comp.
4. **Offer compensation** — at least a goodwill gesture (free breakfast, spa voucher, complimentary upgrade).
5. **Make the guest feel heard.** A VIP wants to feel important, not processed.

## REQUIRED VOCABULARY — use at least 5
- to escalate the matter
- to accommodate the request
- to honor the rate
- rooming list
- comp / compensation
- on the house
- discretionary upgrade
- goodwill gesture
- VIP protocol
- block release

## REQUIRED GRAMMAR — you MUST produce both at least once

### ✅ M12 — Past Simple + Past Continuous
**Example:** *"While I was reviewing your file, I realized that our system had assigned your suite incorrectly."*

### ✅ M13 — Already / Still / Until — use at least 2 of these
**Example:** *"I have already arranged an upgrade for you. The new suite is still being prepared. It will be ready until 9 PM at the latest."*

## TONE REGISTER (B2)
- Authoritative + warm
- Take ownership without defensiveness
- Offer solutions, not excuses

**Sample phrases:**
- *"I take full responsibility for this oversight."*
- *"As a goodwill gesture, we'd like to offer you..."*
- *"Allow me to make this right immediately."*
- *"Your stay tonight will be on the house — please accept our sincerest apologies."*
- *"Rest assured, we'll have this resolved within the hour."*
