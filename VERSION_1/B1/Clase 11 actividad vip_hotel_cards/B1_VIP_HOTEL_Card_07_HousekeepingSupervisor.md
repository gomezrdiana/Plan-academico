# 🧹 YOUR ROLE — HOUSEKEEPING SUPERVISOR

**B1 MASTERY — Cl 11 Grammar — VIP Hotel Hospitality Activity (13/05/2026)**

---

## YOUR POSITION
You manage **all room preparation and cleaning** in the hotel. You:
- Coordinate the cleaning team
- Prepare alternative suites quickly when needed
- Stock minibars, change linens, set turn-down service
- Handle lost and found items

When a VIP needs a room ready FAST, you are the one who makes it happen.

## YOUR OBJECTIVE IN THE SCENE
1. **Acknowledge the urgency** — VIPs don't wait long.
2. **Communicate clear timelines** — how long until the alternative suite is ready?
3. **Coordinate quickly** with your team without panicking.
4. **Add a personal touch** — flowers, a welcome note, restocked minibar at no charge.
5. **Make the guest feel cared for** — the room is the most personal space they will occupy.

## REQUIRED VOCABULARY — use at least 5
- turn-down service
- linen change
- deep clean
- restock the minibar
- DND (Do Not Disturb)
- express clean
- standby suite
- lost and found
- coordinate with
- prompt resolution

## REQUIRED GRAMMAR — you MUST produce both at least once

### ✅ M12 — Past Simple + Past Continuous
**Example:** *"While my team was preparing your alternative suite, we found that the previous guest had left some belongings behind."*

### ✅ M13 — Already / Still / Until — use at least 2
**Example:** *"The suite is still being prepared. It will be ready until 9 PM. We have already restocked the minibar with your preferences."*

## TONE REGISTER (B2)
- Operational + reassuring
- Detail-oriented
- Warm without being intrusive

**Sample phrases:**
- *"Bear with us, please — my team is working as fast as we can."*
- *"I've personally inspected the alternative suite — it's ready for you."*
- *"As a goodwill gesture, we've added a complimentary turn-down service."*
- *"Rest assured, your privacy is our priority."*
