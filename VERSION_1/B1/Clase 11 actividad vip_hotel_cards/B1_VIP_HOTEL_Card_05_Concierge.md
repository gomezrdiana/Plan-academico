# 🛎️ YOUR ROLE — CONCIERGE

**B1 MASTERY — Cl 11 Grammar — VIP Hotel Hospitality Activity (13/05/2026)**

---

## YOUR POSITION
You are the **personal assistant** of every VIP guest at the hotel. You:
- Anticipate guest needs before they ask
- Coordinate with ALL departments (Front Desk, Chef, Facilities, Housekeeping)
- Arrange anything the guest needs — transportation, tickets, restaurant reservations, personal shopping
- Provide a personalized itinerary upon request

The concierge is the **face of personalized service** — what separates a 4-star from a 5-star hotel.

## YOUR OBJECTIVE IN THE SCENE
1. **Anticipate problems** before they escalate.
2. **Liaise between departments** — you are the connector.
3. **Offer personalized solutions** — not generic compensation.
4. **Be proactive** — propose things the guest didn't ask for.
5. **Make the guest feel known** — a VIP wants to feel they are not just another reservation.

## REQUIRED VOCABULARY — use at least 5
- liaise with
- arrange transportation
- secure tickets
- personalized itinerary
- restock the minibar
- DND (Do Not Disturb)
- turn-down service
- lost and found
- on-site
- complimentary

## REQUIRED GRAMMAR — you MUST produce both at least once

### ✅ M12 — Past Simple + Past Continuous
**Example:** *"While you were in your meeting, we were preparing an alternative suite for your return."*

### ✅ M13 — Already / Still / Until
**Example:** *"Everything has already been arranged for your departure. The car will be waiting until 11 AM."*

## TONE REGISTER (B2)
- Helpful + proactive — never reactive
- Confident in your suggestions
- Personal warmth without being familiar

**Sample phrases:**
- *"May I suggest...?"*
- *"I've taken the liberty of arranging..."*
- *"Allow me to liaise with the kitchen on your behalf."*
- *"If I may — would you prefer X or Y?"*
- *"I've already secured a reservation for you at..."*
