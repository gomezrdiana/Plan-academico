### ☐ 00:13–00:28 | LIBRO: MÓDULO 14 — WHILE, DURING, AND AGAIN (15 min)

**⚠️ MÁXIMO 15 MINUTOS. 5 de lectura + 10 de explicación. Si no alcanza, corta. NO te pases.**

Di: "Abran su libro A2 en el Módulo 14 — 'We Studied During the Game Again!' Lean la Guía con su compañero. Tienen 5 minutos."

**Primeros 5 min:** Leen en parejas. Camina. A los 5 minutos EXACTOS: Di: "Tiempo. Dejen de leer. Pongan atención."

**Siguientes 10 min:** Siéntate con cada pareja. ELLOS te explican:

1. ¿Qué significa "while"? →
   - "While" = "mientras." Conecta DOS FRASES COMPLETAS (dos acciones).
   - "I studied while you worked." = "Estudié mientras tú trabajaste."
   - "I was studying while you were working."
   - Necesita SUJETO + VERBO en ambos lados.
2. ¿Qué significa "during"? →
   - "During" = "durante." Conecta una frase con un EVENTO O PERÍODO (NO otra frase completa).
   - "I studied during the party." = "Estudié durante la fiesta."
   - "I won't study during the night."
   - Después de "during" va un SUSTANTIVO, no un verbo.
3. ¿Cuál es la diferencia clave entre while y during? →
   - WHILE + frase completa: "I studied while you WORKED."
   - DURING + sustantivo/evento: "I studied during the PARTY."
   - WHILE necesita sujeto + verbo. DURING necesita sustantivo.
4. ¿Qué significa "again"? →
   - "Again" = "otra vez / de nuevo." Se coloca generalmente al FINAL de la frase.
   - "I ate again at 2:00." = "Comí otra vez a las 2:00."
   - "Are you working again today?" = "¿Estás trabajando otra vez hoy?"

Ahora dales situaciones. Di cada una y ellos construyen la frase:

**Situación 1:** Di: "Estudié mientras tú trabajaste."
→ "I studied while you worked."

**Situación 2:** Di: "Viví en Canadá durante la guerra."
→ "I lived in Canada during the war."

**Situación 3:** Di: "Estuve en el parque otra vez el lunes."
→ "I was in the park again on Monday."

**Situación 4:** Di: "Las chicas almorzaron durante la tarde."
→ "The girls ate lunch during the afternoon."

**Situación 5:** Di: "¿Te vas a duchar mientras yo me visto?"
→ "Will you shower while I get dressed?"

**Situación 6:** Di: "¿Cuándo vas a visitar Canadá otra vez?"
→ "When will you visit Canada again?"

Si está mal: "No. Listen." + versión correcta + repiten 2 veces.

**Escribe en el tablero:**
```
WHILE = MIENTRAS (connects two FULL sentences)
DURING = DURANTE (connects to a NOUN/EVENT)
AGAIN = OTRA VEZ (usually at the END)

WHILE + subject + verb:
I studied WHILE you worked.
She was cooking WHILE he was cleaning.
I'll read WHILE you sleep.

DURING + noun/event:
I studied DURING the party.
We played DURING the break.
He slept DURING the movie.

⚠️ KEY DIFFERENCE:
WHILE you WORKED ✓ (full sentence)
DURING the PARTY ✓ (noun/event)
WHILE the party ✗ (needs a verb!)
DURING you worked ✗ (needs a noun!)

AGAIN = otra vez (end of sentence):
I was in the park AGAIN on Monday.
Are you working AGAIN today?
When will you visit Canada AGAIN?
```

Di cada frase. La clase repite 2 veces.

### ☐ 01:08–01:18 | HISTORIA INTERACTIVA: EL SÁBADO DE ANDRÉS (10 min)

**Qué es:** Cuentas una historia sobre Andrés, que intenta hacer muchas cosas el sábado pero siempre está haciendo algo al mismo tiempo que pasa otra cosa. La historia usa while, during y again de forma natural.

Para. Di: "Say: 'During the game, it started raining.' 'They played while it was raining.' What happened?" Clase: "He scored!"

### ☐ 01:18–01:28 | COMPETENCIA: ¿WHILE, DURING O AGAIN? (10 min)

**Qué es:** 2 equipos. El profesor da una frase incompleta. Los equipos eligen la palabra correcta (while, during, o again) y completan la frase.

Divide en 2 equipos de 8. De pie.

Di: "Voy a dar frases incompletas. Ustedes deciden: ¿es WHILE (mientras + frase), DURING (durante + sustantivo), o AGAIN (otra vez)? Completen la frase. 2 puntos por respuesta correcta."

**Las 12 frases (una a la vez):**

1. "I studied ___ the party."
→ DURING → "I studied during the party."

2. "I was cooking ___ you were sleeping."
→ WHILE → "I was cooking while you were sleeping."

3. "Are you working ___ today?"
→ AGAIN → "Are you working again today?"

4. "The children swam ___ the morning."
→ DURING → "The children swam during the morning."

5. "Sarah cleaned her room ___ we were studying."
→ WHILE → "Sarah cleaned her room while we were studying."

6. "My dad met the doctor ___ yesterday."
→ AGAIN → "My dad met the doctor again yesterday."

7. "I won't clean ___ you are speaking on the phone."
→ WHILE → "I won't clean while you are speaking on the phone."

8. "Did Karol want a cookie ___ the class?"
→ DURING → "Did Karol want a cookie during the class?"

9. "My friends worked ___ at 7:30."
→ AGAIN → "My friends worked again at 7:30."

10. "Wilson will be at the mall ___ the break."
→ DURING → "Wilson will be at the mall during the break."

11. "Beatriz was finishing the project ___ I was sleeping."
→ WHILE → "Beatriz was finishing the project while I was sleeping."

12. "When will you visit Canada ___?"
→ AGAIN → "When will you visit Canada again?"

2 puntos por respuesta correcta. Si confunden while y during: "WHILE + sujeto + verbo (frase completa). DURING + sustantivo (evento/período). ¿Hay verbo después? WHILE. ¿Hay sustantivo después? DURING."

Errores que buscar:
- "During you worked" → "WHILE you worked" (during no va con frases completas)
- "While the party" → "DURING the party" (while necesita sujeto + verbo)
- "I again worked" → "I worked AGAIN" (again al final)

Equipo ganador celebra. Puntajes en el tablero.

> De pie. Rápido. Ruidoso. Distinguir while, during y again es la habilidad clave de esta clase.
