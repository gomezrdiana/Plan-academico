### 3. 8:13-8:23 | ROMPEHIELO: "I'm SMARTER than..." (10 min)

Escribe en tablero:
```
UNEQUAL COMPARATIVES — bigger / more important / better than

REGLAS:
1. Adjetivo CORTO (1 sílaba) → + ER + than
   big → bigger     fast → faster     tall → taller

2. Adjetivo CORTO terminado en -y → -y → -IER + than
   happy → happier  busy → busier     easy → easier

3. Adjetivo LARGO (2+ sílabas) → MORE + adj + than
   beautiful → MORE BEAUTIFUL than
   intelligent → MORE INTELLIGENT than
   important → MORE IMPORTANT than

4. IRREGULARES (memoria):
   good → BETTER than      bad → WORSE than
   far → FARTHER than      little → LESS than
   much/many → MORE than
```

**Round (8 min):** Señala estudiantes. Da prompt en español:
- "Más alto que mi papá" → I'm taller than my dad.
- "Más inteligente que su hermano" → He's more intelligent than his brother.
- "Mejor que el café" → Better than coffee.
- "Más feliz que ayer" → Happier than yesterday.
- "Peor que ayer" → Worse than yesterday.
- "Más caro que pensé" → More expensive than I thought.

Si dicen mal: "No. Listen." + correcto + 2x.

### 13. 10:20-10:25 | RECONNECT VIRTUD + RE-ANCLAJE (5 min)

"Welcome back. Same JUSTICE. New tool: SUPERLATIVE."

```
THE SUPERLATIVE — el MÁS / EL MEJOR de un grupo

PATRÓN:
1. Adjetivo CORTO → THE + adj + EST
   tall → THE TALLEST    big → THE BIGGEST    happy → THE HAPPIEST

2. Adjetivo LARGO → THE MOST + adj
   intelligent → THE MOST INTELLIGENT
   beautiful → THE MOST BEAUTIFUL

3. IRREGULARES:
   good → THE BEST       bad → THE WORST
   far → THE FARTHEST    little → THE LEAST
   much/many → THE MOST

DIFERENCIA con M27 (comparativo):
- M27: "He's TALLER THAN her."     (compara 2)
- M28: "He's THE TALLEST in family." (el #1 del grupo)
```

3 estudiantes ejemplo: "I'm the oldest in my family." / "Pizza is the best food." / "Math is the hardest subject."
