### ☐ 00:13–00:28 | LIBRO: MÓDULO 12 — PAST CONTINUOUS + PAST SIMPLE (15 min)

**⚠️ MÁXIMO 15 MINUTOS. 5 de lectura + 10 de explicación. Si no alcanza, corta. NO te pases.**

Di: "Abran su libro A2 en el Módulo 12 — 'I Was Playing when My Mom Called.' Lean la Guía con su compañero. Tienen 5 minutos."

**Primeros 5 min:** Leen en parejas. Camina. A los 5 minutos EXACTOS: Di: "Tiempo. Dejen de leer. Pongan atención."

**Siguientes 10 min:** Siéntate con cada pareja. ELLOS te explican:

1. ¿Cuál es la diferencia entre past simple y past continuous? →
   - Past simple: una acción TERMINADA en un momento específico. "I played yesterday."
   - Past continuous: una acción con DURACIÓN en el pasado. "I was playing for three hours."
2. ¿Qué pasa cuando se combinan? →
   - La acción LARGA estaba pasando (past continuous) y la acción CORTA la INTERRUMPIÓ (past simple).
   - "I was playing when my mom called." → Estaba jugando (largo) y mamá llamó (corto, interrumpió).
   - "I was eating pasta when my brother arrived."
   - "We were walking in the park when Jeff saw a dog."
3. ¿Cuál es la estructura? →
   - ACCIÓN LARGA: was/were + verb-ing (past continuous)
   - ACCIÓN CORTA: past simple
   - "I was studying when the phone rang."
   - "She was sleeping when the alarm went off."
4. ¿Se puede usar "while" con dos acciones largas? →
   - Sí: "I was studying while she was cooking."
   - "What were you doing when I was studying?"

Ahora dales situaciones. Di cada una y ellos construyen la frase:

**Situación 1:** Di: "Estaba comiendo cuando mi hermano llegó."
→ "I was eating when my brother arrived."

**Situación 2:** Di: "Ella estaba estudiando cuando el teléfono sonó."
→ "She was studying when the phone rang."

**Situación 3:** Di: "Estábamos caminando en el parque cuando vimos un perro."
→ "We were walking in the park when we saw a dog."

**Situación 4:** Di: "Mi papá estaba trabajando cuando yo almorcé."
→ "My dad was working when I ate lunch."

**Situación 5:** Di: "La familia estaba viendo una película cuando Sebastián se fue."
→ "The family was watching a movie when Sebastián left."

**Situación 6:** Di: "¿Qué estabas haciendo cuando yo estaba estudiando?"
→ "What were you doing when I was studying?"

Si está mal: "No. Listen." + versión correcta + repiten 2 veces.

**Escribe en el tablero:**
```
PAST CONTINUOUS + PAST SIMPLE = INTERRUPTION

LONG ACTION (was/were + -ing) + SHORT ACTION (past simple)

I was playing when my mom called.
She was studying when the phone rang.
We were walking when Jeff saw a dog.

STRUCTURE:
Subject + was/were + verb-ing + WHEN + subject + past simple

"was playing" = estaba jugando (LONG)
"called" = llamó (SHORT — interrupts!)

TWO LONG ACTIONS = WHILE:
I was studying while she was cooking.

NEGATIVE:
I wasn't driving when the accident happened.
The girls weren't studying when the teacher arrived.

QUESTIONS:
What were you doing when I called?
Were you sleeping when the alarm rang?

⚠️ "WHEN" = acción corta que interrumpe
⚠️ "WHILE" = dos acciones largas al mismo tiempo
```

Di cada frase. La clase repite 2 veces.

### ☐ 00:38–00:58 | SIMULACIÓN: ¿QUÉ ESTABAS HACIENDO CUANDO...? (20 min)

**Qué es:** Los estudiantes reconstruyen momentos memorables usando past continuous + past simple. Practican describir qué estaban haciendo cuando algo importante pasó.

**Paso 1 — Tablero (3 min).** Escribe en el tablero ANTES de empezar:

```
🕐 WHAT WERE YOU DOING WHEN...?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
MEMORABLE MOMENTS:

1. ...the last earthquake happened?
2. ...you heard your favorite song for the first time?
3. ...you got your first phone?
4. ...you met your best friend?
5. ...the last World Cup final started?
6. ...your last birthday began?
7. ...you woke up this morning?
8. ...you arrived at the academy today?

ANSWER WITH:
"I was ___-ing when ___."
"When ___, I was ___-ing."
"I wasn't ___-ing when ___."
```

**Paso 2 — Modelo (2 min).** Di: "Yo primero. Escuchen."

Di: "What was I doing when the last earthquake happened?"
→ Responde tú mismo: "I was sleeping when the last earthquake happened. I was dreaming. When I felt the earthquake, I was lying in my bed."

Di: "What was I doing when I woke up this morning?"
→ "My alarm rang when I was sleeping. I was having a nice dream when the alarm interrupted me."

Repite con 1 estudiante. Di: "What were you doing when you arrived at the academy today?" El estudiante responde.

**Paso 3 — Parejas (10 min).** Di: "Parejas. Pregunten y respondan TODAS las 8 situaciones del tablero. Cada persona responde con MÍNIMO 2 frases — qué estaba haciendo y qué lo interrumpió."

Preguntas que deben practicar:
- "What were you doing when the earthquake happened?"
- "Were you sleeping when the alarm rang?"
- "What was your mom doing when you left the house?"

Respuestas que deben usar:
- "I was watching TV when the earthquake happened."
- "I was eating breakfast when my friend called."
- "I wasn't studying when the teacher arrived."
- "My mom was cooking when I left the house."

A los 5 minutos: Di: "Switch. Cambien roles."

Camina con papel de errores. Errores comunes:
- "I was play when..." → debe ser "I was PLAYING when..." (was/were + -ing)
- "I was eating when my mom was called" → debe ser "when my mom CALLED" (past simple para la interrupción)
- "When I was arriving" para una acción corta → "When I ARRIVED" (llegar es puntual, no continuo)

Si escuchas un error: "No. Listen." + versión correcta. Repiten 2 veces.

**Paso 4 — Presentar (5 min).** Di: "4 parejas van a compartir su momento más interesante. Cada persona comparte 1 situación con 2 frases."

4 parejas pasan al frente. Después de cada presentación: clase aplaude. Corrige si es necesario.

> Camina con papel de errores durante Pasos 3-4. Error más común: olvidar "-ing" en el past continuous o usar past continuous para la acción corta.

### ☐ 01:08–01:18 | HISTORIA INTERACTIVA: EL DÍA LOCO DE VALENTINA (10 min)

**Qué es:** Cuentas una historia sobre Valentina, a quien todo le sale mal porque cada vez que está haciendo algo, algo la interrumpe. Toda la historia usa past continuous + past simple.

Di: "Les voy a contar la historia de Valentina. Valentina tuvo el peor día de su vida. Todo le salió mal."

**Cuenta la historia. LENTO. Pausa para que repitan y participen.**

"Valentina was sleeping peacefully. She was having a beautiful dream. Suddenly — RING! Her alarm went off. It was 5:30 AM!"

Para. Di: "Say it: 'Valentina was sleeping when her alarm went off.'" Repiten.

"She got up and went to the kitchen. She was making coffee when — CRASH! — she dropped the cup. Coffee everywhere!"

Para. Di: "Say: 'She was making coffee when she dropped the cup.' What happened to the coffee?" Clase: "It fell!" Di: "It was an interruption!"

"Valentina cleaned the mess and got dressed. She was walking to the bus stop when it started raining. She didn't have an umbrella."

Para. Di: "Say: 'She was walking to the bus stop when it started raining.' Did she have an umbrella?" Clase: "No!"

"She was waiting for the bus when her phone rang. It was her boss. He said: 'Valentina, today is Saturday. We don't work on Saturdays.'"

Para. La clase se ríe. Di: "Say: 'She was waiting for the bus when her boss called.'" Repiten.

"Valentina was standing in the rain when she realized: it was Saturday! She was so angry! She was walking back home when she saw her neighbor's dog running in the street."

Para. Di: "Say: 'She was walking home when she saw a dog.' What did the dog do?" Estudiantes adivinan.

"The dog was running toward Valentina when — SPLASH! — it jumped into a puddle. Valentina's clothes were completely wet!"

Para. Di: "Say: 'The dog was running when it jumped into a puddle.'" Repiten.

"Finally, Valentina arrived home. She was opening the door when she heard music inside. Her roommate was having a party — at 6 AM on a Saturday!"

Para. Di: "Say: 'She was opening the door when she heard music. Her roommate was having a party.'" Repiten.

"Valentina looked at her roommate and said: 'I was sleeping. I was making coffee. I was walking in the rain. I was standing at the bus stop. And now THIS?!' Her roommate said: 'Surprise! Happy birthday, Valentina!'"

Para. La clase se ríe. Di: "Was it really the worst day? What was happening?" Estudiantes responden.

Señala 3-4 parejas: "Tell me 2 things that happened to Valentina. Use 'She was ___-ing when ___.'" La pareja produce frases.

> La historia usa past continuous interrumpido por past simple en CADA escena. was + -ing (largo) + past simple (corto/interrupción).

### ☐ 01:18–01:28 | COMPETENCIA: CONSTRUYE LA ESCENA (10 min)

**Qué es:** 2 equipos. El profesor da 2 elementos (una acción larga y una interrupción). Los equipos construyen la frase completa con past continuous + past simple.

Divide en 2 equipos de 8. De pie.

Di: "Voy a dar 2 elementos. Ustedes construyen la frase usando past continuous y past simple. Acción larga = was/were + -ing. Interrupción = past simple. El primer equipo correcto gana 1 punto."

**Los 12 pares (uno a la vez):**

1. "Estudiar / teléfono sonar"
→ "I was studying when the phone rang."

2. "Cocinar / luz apagarse"
→ "She was cooking when the lights went off."

3. "Dormir / perro ladrar"
→ "We were sleeping when the dog barked."

4. "Manejar / llover"
→ "He was driving when it started raining."

5. "Caminar al parque / ver un amigo"
→ "I was walking to the park when I saw a friend."

6. "Ver una película / alguien tocar la puerta"
→ "They were watching a movie when someone knocked on the door."

7. "Ducharse / agua fría"
→ "She was showering when the water turned cold."

8. "Comer almuerzo / jefe llamar"
→ "I was eating lunch when my boss called."

9. "Correr en el parque / empezar a llover"
→ "We were running in the park when it started to rain."

10. "Hablar por teléfono / bebé llorar"
→ "She was talking on the phone when the baby cried."

11. "Hacer tarea / mamá llegar"
→ "I was doing my homework when my mom arrived."

12. "Jugar fútbol / lastimarse"
→ "He was playing soccer when he got hurt."

1 punto por frase correcta. Si usan past simple para la acción larga: "No. Listen. That action was happening for a LONG time — was/were + -ing." Repiten.

Errores que buscar:
- "I was study" → "I was STUDYING" (falta -ing)
- "when the phone was ringing" → "when the phone RANG" (interrupción = past simple)
- "I studied when" → "I WAS STUDYING when" (acción larga = continuous)

Equipo ganador celebra. Puntajes en el tablero.

> De pie. Rápido. Ruidoso. Construir la frase correcta con los 2 tiempos juntos es la habilidad clave.
