## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT CLUSTER 5 (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE 3 columnas en board grandes:

   PREPOSITIONS         ARTICLES              QUANTIFIERS
   TIME                 A / AN  first         MANY + countable
     IN month / year      "an office"           "many chairs"
     ON day / date      THE  specific /       MUCH + uncountable
     AT exact hour        already said          "much money"
   PLACE                  "THE manager"       A LOT OF both
     IN enclosed        Ø  general /         FEW / A FEW count
     ON surface           uncountable        LITTLE / A LITTLE
     AT specific point    "Ø coffee"           uncount
                                              SOME affirm
                                              ANY neg + Q
                                              THERE IS uncount
                                              THERE ARE plural

2. SEÑALA columna 1 (prepositions). DI: "Three rules for time.
   Month = IN. Day = ON. Hour = AT. Three rules for place.
   Enclosed = IN. Surface = ON. Specific point = AT."

3. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "This is what
   the phrase says — IN the month, ON the day, AT the time.
   Measure the small word."

4. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "In Monday" -> TACHA "In", ESCRIBE "ON
   Monday." COHORTE repite 2 veces en coro.

5. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "I work on office" -> TACHA "on",
   ESCRIBE "I work IN the office." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

6. PIDELES en parejas: "5 sentences with IN / ON / AT —
   mix time and place. Real work or home situations."
   3 minutos.

7. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca en
   2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota errores con
   NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

8. [VIRTUD] activation: si alguien titubea con la preposicion,
   DI suave: "Measure. Pick the right small word. That is
   temperance."

9. SEÑALA columna 2 (articles). DI: "A or AN — first time
   you mention a singular countable. AN before vowel sound.
   THE — specific, or already mentioned, or unique. Nothing
   — general uncountable, general plural."

10. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "I work in office" -> TACHA, ESCRIBE
    "I work in AN office." Despues escribe debajo: "THE
    office is on the second floor." DI: "AN first time, THE
    second time. That is the flow."

11. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "I drink the water every day" -> TACHA
    "the", ESCRIBE "I drink Ø water every day." DI: "General
    = nothing." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

12. PIDELES en parejas: "5 mini-dialogues with A/AN first,
    then THE second mention, then Ø for general." 4 minutos.

13. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca
    2 veces.

14. SEÑALA columna 3 (quantifiers). DI: "MANY for things
    you count. MUCH for things you can't count. A LOT OF
    for both. SOME for affirmative. ANY for negative and
    questions."

15. [ERROR TIPICO #5] "Much friends" -> TACHA "Much",
    ESCRIBE "MANY friends." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

16. [ERROR TIPICO #6] "I have any questions" (afirmativa)
    -> TACHA "any", ESCRIBE "I have SOME questions."
    COHORTE repite 2 veces.

17. PIDELES en parejas: "6 sentences mixing quantifiers in
    a workplace context — emails, meetings, time, paper,
    people, money." 5 minutos.

18. CIRCULA, CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo construya en las
    3 categorias sin titubear. DI: "Stand up — we go to
    the simulation."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 30 min y aun dudan en prepositions, NO AVANCES a articles. Reduce a 2 categorias hoy, deja quantifiers para Cl 47. Mejor LAND 2 bien que rush 3 shaky.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas construyendo en las 3 categorias sin pedir ayuda.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: SCHEDULING & INVENTORY CALL (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "SCHEDULING & INVENTORY CALL"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. One is the COORDINATOR — asks
   about schedule and inventory. The other is the SUPPLIER
   — answers. You must use, in the whole conversation, AT
   LEAST 4 prepositions (IN/ON/AT mix of time and place),
   5 articles (a/an, the, nothing), and 4 quantifiers
   (many/much/a lot of/some/any/a few/a little). Then you
   rotate."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This call is the phrase in
   action — IN the month, ON the day, AT the time. A few
   details, not too many. Pick the right small word."

4. ESCRIBE 2 mini-scenarios en board:

   SCENARIO 1 — Office supply order
     Coordinator asks about paper, pens, printers, delivery
     day, time, location.
   SCENARIO 2 — Meeting setup
     Coordinator asks about day, time, room, attendees,
     materials needed.

5. PIDELES round 1 (5 min): pareja escoge un scenario.
   Roles claros. Coordinator arranca.

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces en
   la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven a parejas.

7. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, switch to
   Spanish, think 5 seconds, come back to English. Don't
   freeze."

8. ROTA parejas + cambia roles. Round 2 (5 min): cambien
   de scenario tambien.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante la simulacion: si alguien
   suelta detalles de mas o se enreda, DI suave: "Measure.
   A few details, not too many. Temperance."

10. ROTA parejas otra vez. Round 3 (5 min): mismo formato,
    push para que aparezcan las 3 categorias en cada turno.

11. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces en
    parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu cuaderno.

12. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente al
    grupo (coordinator + supplier, 3 preguntas + 3 respuestas).
    COHORTE cuenta cuantas prepositions / articles /
    quantifiers cazaron correctas. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("schedule a meeting for next Friday at 3 PM in the office" / "How many boxes of paper do we have in the warehouse?") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol.
