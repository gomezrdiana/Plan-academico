### ☐ 00:13–00:28 | LIBRO: MÓDULO 10 — FIRST CONDITIONAL (15 min)

**⚠️ MÁXIMO 15 MINUTOS. 5 de lectura + 10 de explicación. Si no alcanza, corta. NO te pases.**

Di: "Abran su libro A2 en el Módulo 10 — 'I'll Buy Lunch!' Lean la Guía con su compañero. Tienen 5 minutos."

**Primeros 5 min:** Leen en parejas. Camina. A los 5 minutos EXACTOS: Di: "Tiempo. Dejen de leer. Pongan atención."

**Siguientes 10 min:** Siéntate con cada pareja. ELLOS te explican:

1. ¿Qué es el first conditional? →
   - Habla de situaciones PROBABLES o POSIBLES en el futuro.
   - Si es PROBABLE que pase → first conditional.
   - "If it rains, I will stay home." (Es posible que llueva → es probable.)
2. ¿Cuál es la estructura? →
   - IF + present simple, WILL + verbo base.
   - "If I have money, I will buy a car."
   - "If she studies, she will pass."
   - "If we leave now, we will arrive on time."
3. ¿Se puede cambiar el orden? →
   - SÍ. Se puede poner "will" primero:
   - "I will buy a car if I have money."
   - Cuando "if" va primero, se usa coma. Cuando "will" va primero, NO se usa coma.
4. ¿Cómo se hace negativo? →
   - Negativo en la cláusula "if": "If I DON'T have money, I will stay home."
   - Negativo en la cláusula "will": "If it rains, I WON'T go out."
   - Negativo en AMBAS: "If she doesn't study, she won't pass."
5. ¿Cómo se hacen preguntas? →
   - "What will you do if it rains?"
   - "Will you go if she invites you?"
   - "If you have time, will you help me?"

Ahora dales situaciones. Di cada una y ellos construyen la frase:

**Situación 1:** Di: "Si tengo dinero, compraré un carro."
→ "If I have money, I will buy a car."

**Situación 2:** Di: "Si llueve, no saldré."
→ "If it rains, I won't go out."

**Situación 3:** Di: "Si ella estudia, pasará el examen."
→ "If she studies, she will pass the test."

**Situación 4:** Di: "¿Qué harás si no tienes trabajo?"
→ "What will you do if you don't have a job?"

**Situación 5:** Di: "Si no nos apuramos, llegaremos tarde."
→ "If we don't hurry, we will be late."

**Situación 6:** Di: "Compraré el almuerzo si tú compras el café."
→ "I will buy lunch if you buy coffee."

Si está mal: "No. Listen." + versión correcta + repiten 2 veces.

**Escribe en el tablero:**
```
FIRST CONDITIONAL = PROBABLE / POSSIBLE FUTURE

IF + PRESENT SIMPLE, WILL + VERB

If I have money, I will buy a car.
If she studies, she will pass.
If it rains, I will stay home.
If we leave now, we will arrive on time.

NEGATIVE:
If I DON'T have money, I will stay home.
If it rains, I WON'T go out.
If she doesn't study, she won't pass.

QUESTIONS:
What will you do if it rains?
Will you go if she invites you?

ORDER:
If + present, will + verb. ← COMMA
Will + verb + if + present. ← NO COMMA

⚠️ IF + PRESENT (NOT "will"):
"If I have" ✓ NOT "If I will have" ✗
```

Di cada frase. La clase repite 2 veces.

### ☐ 00:38–00:58 | SIMULACIÓN: ¿QUÉ HARÁS SI...? — CADENA DE CONSECUENCIAS (20 min)

**Qué es:** Los estudiantes juegan un juego en cadena donde cada consecuencia lleva a una nueva pregunta con "if."

**Paso 1 — Tablero (3 min).** Escribe en el tablero ANTES de empezar (o prepáralo durante la actividad anterior):

```
🔗 CHAIN GAME — WHAT WILL YOU DO IF...?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STARTER QUESTIONS:

1. What will you do if it rains tomorrow?
2. What will you do if you lose your phone?
3. What will you do if you don't pass the test?
4. What will you do if your friend cancels plans?
5. What will you do if you get a raise?
6. What will you do if you have free time this weekend?
7. What will you do if you wake up late?
8. What will you do if you forget your homework?

USEFUL PHRASES:
"If ___, I will ___."
"I will ___ if ___."
"If ___, I won't ___."
```

**Paso 2 — Modelo (2 min).** Di: "Yo empiezo. Escuchen cómo funciona la cadena."

Di: "What will you do if it rains tomorrow?"
→ Responde tú mismo: "If it rains tomorrow, I will stay home."

Di: "OK. Now the CHAIN — my answer becomes the NEXT question: What will you do if you stay home?"
→ "If I stay home, I will watch a movie."

Di: "And what will you do if you watch a movie?"
→ "If I watch a movie, I will eat popcorn."

Di: "See? Each answer creates the NEXT question! That's the chain."

**Paso 3 — Cadena en grupo (10 min).** Di: "Vamos a hacer esto juntos. Yo empiezo con la primera pregunta. El que contesta, su respuesta se convierte en la siguiente pregunta. Vamos en orden."

**Cadena 1 — empieza tú:**
Di: "What will you do if you lose your phone?"

Estudiante 1: "If I lose my phone, I will buy a new one."
Di al Estudiante 2: "What will you do if you buy a new phone?"
Estudiante 2: "If I buy a new phone, I will take many photos."
Di al Estudiante 3: "What will you do if you take many photos?"
Estudiante 3: "If I take many photos, I will post them online."

Continúa hasta que se rompa la cadena o pasen 8 estudiantes. Luego empieza otra cadena.

**Cadena 2:** Di: "What will you do if you don't pass the test?"
**Cadena 3:** Di: "What will you do if you get a raise?"

A los 5 minutos: Di: "Now, PAIRS. Make your OWN chains. Start with any question from the board. Minimum 5 links in the chain."

Parejas practican (5 min). Camina con papel de errores.

Errores comunes:
- "If I will have" → debe ser "If I have" (present simple después de "if," NO "will")
- "If it rains, I stay" → debe ser "If it rains, I WILL stay" (falta "will" en la consecuencia)
- "If she don't study" → debe ser "If she DOESN'T study"

Si escuchas un error: "No. Listen." + versión correcta. Repiten 2 veces.

**Paso 4 — Presentar (5 min).** Di: "3 parejas van a presentar su cadena frente a la clase. Mínimo 4 eslabones."

3 parejas pasan al frente. Presentan su cadena completa. Después de cada cadena: clase aplaude. Corrige si es necesario.

> Camina con papel de errores durante Pasos 3-4. Error más común: "will" después de "if."

### ☐ 01:08–01:18 | HISTORIA INTERACTIVA: EL EFECTO DOMINÓ (10 min)

**Qué es:** Cuentas una historia donde CADA decisión del personaje tiene una consecuencia, y cada consecuencia lleva a otra — todo con first conditional. Los estudiantes repiten, responden preguntas y participan.

Di: "Les voy a contar la historia de Valentina. Hoy Valentina tiene que tomar MUCHAS decisiones. Cada decisión cambia TODO."

**Cuenta la historia. LENTO. Pausa para que repitan y participen.**

"Valentina wakes up. It's Monday morning. She looks at her phone. Her alarm didn't go off. She thinks: 'If I don't hurry, I will be late for work.'"

Para. Di: "Say it: 'If I don't hurry, I will be late for work.'" Repiten.

"She runs to the kitchen. There is no coffee. She thinks: 'If I don't have coffee, I won't survive this day. But if I stop at the café, I will be EVEN MORE late.'"

Para. Di: "Say: 'If I don't have coffee, I won't survive this day.' What should she do? Stop for coffee or go to work?" La clase vota. Di: "Let's see!"

"Valentina decides to stop at the café. She thinks: 'If I buy coffee now, I will feel better. And if I feel better, I will work faster.'"

Para. Di: "Say: 'If I buy coffee now, I will feel better. If I feel better, I will work faster.' Is that a good plan?" Clase: "Yes!" / "Maybe!"

"At the café, there is a LONG line. Valentina thinks: 'If I wait in this line, I will lose 15 minutes. If I lose 15 minutes, my boss will be angry.'"

Para. Di: "Say: 'If I wait in this line, I will lose 15 minutes. If I lose 15 minutes, my boss will be angry.' What will she do?" Clase responde.

"She waits. She gets her coffee. She arrives at work 20 minutes late. Her boss says: 'Valentina, if you are late again, I will talk to HR.'"

Para. Di: "Say: 'If you are late again, I will talk to HR.' Uh oh! Is she in trouble?" Clase: "Yes!"

"Valentina sits at her desk. Her coworker Carlos says: 'Valentina, if you finish the report before lunch, I will help you with the presentation.' Valentina says: 'If you help me with the presentation, I will buy you lunch!'"

Para. Di: "Say: 'If you finish the report, I will help you.' 'If you help me, I will buy you lunch!' See the chain?" Repiten ambas frases.

"Valentina works VERY fast. She finishes the report at 11:45 AM. Carlos helps her with the presentation. The boss sees the presentation. He says: 'This is excellent! Valentina, if you keep working like this, I will give you a promotion!'"

Para. Di: "Say: 'If you keep working like this, I will give you a promotion!' From late to promotion — all because of coffee!" La clase se ríe.

"Valentina goes home happy. She tells her mom: 'If I hadn't stopped for coffee, none of this would have happened.' Her mom says: 'If you set your alarm, you won't be late tomorrow.' Valentina laughs: 'You're right. If I set my alarm, everything will be fine.'"

Para. Di: "Good advice! Say: 'If I set my alarm, everything will be fine.'" Repiten.

Ahora señala una pareja: Di: "It's Tuesday. Valentina wakes up. What happens? Use 'If..., will...' to continue the story." La pareja inventa. Pasa a 3-4 parejas más. Cada una agrega un capítulo.

Ejemplos que podrían dar:
- "If she wakes up on time, she will have time for breakfast."
- "If she has breakfast, she won't need to stop at the café."
- "If she arrives early, her boss will be happy."
- "If her boss is happy, she will get the promotion."

Di: "¿Valentina va a poner su alarma?" La clase vota.

> La historia usa first conditional en CADA situación. If + present, will + verb. Negativas con won't y don't. La cadena de consecuencias hace que la estructura se repita naturalmente.

### ☐ 01:18–01:28 | COMPETENCIA: COMPLETA LA FRASE (10 min)

**Las 12 frases (una a la vez):**

1. "If it rains tomorrow, ___."
→ Cualquier respuesta correcta con "will": "I will stay home." "We will take an umbrella." etc.
