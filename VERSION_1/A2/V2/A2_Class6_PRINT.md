## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT M7 MIGHT (~52 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande el titulo y las REGLAS del libro
   (M7, p.51-52), tal cual:

   THE MODAL "MIGHT" — possibility / "es posible que..."

   1) "might" works like "should", "will" — a modal.
   2) Means an UNCERTAIN future: "it's possible that..."
   3) NO short form — not in positive, not in negative.
      (NOT "mightn't" in this course — write it full.)
   4) We do NOT usually make questions with "might".
      We use it to ANSWER a future question.
   5) Short answer = just "might" or "might not".

   STRUCTURE (write big):
      SUBJECT + MIGHT + VERB (base form)
      "I might GO."      "He might WORK."
      "I might NOT go."  "She might NOT come."
      NO 'to'.  NO '-s'.  NO '-ing'.  Base verb only.

2. SEÑALA la regla 2. DI: "Might = possible, not sure. 'I
   WILL go' = sure. 'I MIGHT go' = maybe, it's possible.
   That is temperance — you say only what you know."

3. ESCRIBE los EJEMPLOS del libro (p.53), tal cual:

      Q: Where are you going to go tomorrow?
         A: I might go to Piedecuesta.
      Q: What time is Steve going to work?
         A: He might work at 4:15.
      Q: What time does your flight arrive?
         A: It might arrive at 7:00.
      Q: Are you waking up early tomorrow?
         A: I might not. I'm very tired.
      Q: Where are Frank and Joe?
         A: They might be at the mall.
      Q: Where is Diego?
         A: He might be sick.

4. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "This is the
   phrase — 'a calm mind says I might.' It names the
   possible. It does not promise the certain. Stay steady."

5. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "I might TO go" -> TACHA "to", ESCRIBE
   "I might GO." DI: "No 'to' after might. Base verb."
   COHORTE repite 2 veces.

6. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "He mightS work" / "He might workS"
   -> TACHA la S, ESCRIBE "He might WORK." DI: "No S after
   a modal. Might is the same for I, he, she, they."
   COHORTE repite 2 veces.

7. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "I might GOING" -> TACHA "going",
   ESCRIBE "I might GO." DI: "No -ing. Base verb after
   might." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

8. PRACTICA dirigida (oral, rapido): TU haces una pregunta
   futura, el grupo responde con "might". Usa el patron del
   libro:
      "What will you do this weekend?"  -> "I might ___."
      "Where will you travel this year?" -> "I might ___."
      "What time will the bus arrive?"   -> "It might ___."
   8-10 vueltas, distintos estudiantes, sin nombres en board.

9. [VIRTUD] activation: si alguien fuerza "I WILL go" cuando
   claramente no esta seguro, DI suave: "Temperance. You
   don't know yet. Don't over-promise. 'I MIGHT go.'"

10. PIDELES en parejas: "Student A asks a FUTURE question
    (What/Where/What time + will). Student B answers with
    MIGHT or MIGHT NOT. 6 exchanges, then switch. Base verb,
    no 'to', no S, no -ing. 5 minutes."

11. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca en
    2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota errores con
    NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

12. ESCRIBE las RESPUESTAS CORTAS del libro (regla 5):
       Q: Are you going to play soccer?
          A: I might.  /  I might not.
    DI: "Short answer = just 'might' or 'might not'. Calm.
    No extra words." Practica 4-5 vueltas rapidas en coro.

13. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "I will might go" -> TACHA "will",
    ESCRIBE "I MIGHT go." DI: "One modal only. Not 'will
    might'. Just 'might'." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

14. PIDELES en parejas round 2: "Now MIX. 6 exchanges — at
    least 2 SHORT answers ('I might' / 'I might not') and at
    least 1 NEGATIVE ('I might not go'). Same theme — your
    plans for next week. 5 minutes."

15. CIRCULA. CORRIGE cuando el mismo error aparezca 2 veces
    en parejas distintas.

16. CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo responda preguntas futuras
    con "might + base verb" sin titubear. DI: "Stand up —
    we go to the simulation. Team planning call."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 32 min y aun ponen "to" / S / -ing despues de might, NO AVANCES al mix. Quedate solo en afirmativo ("I might go") hasta que salga limpio; deja negativo y short answer para el opener de Cl 7. Mejor LAND uno bien que rush dos shaky.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas respondiendo con might + verbo base, sin 'to', sin S, sin -ing.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: TEAM PLANNING CALL (~36 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "TEAM PLANNING CALL"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. One is the TEAM LEAD asking
   about NEXT WEEK's plan — but nothing is confirmed yet.
   The other is a TEAM MEMBER who is NOT sure, so every
   answer uses MIGHT or MIGHT NOT. You must use, in the
   whole conversation, AT LEAST:
     - 5 answers with MIGHT / MIGHT NOT (base verb)
     - 2 SHORT answers ('I might' / 'I might not')
     - 3 question words (What / Where / What time / Who)
   Then rotate."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This call is the phrase in
   action. A calm mind says 'I might.' Nothing is confirmed.
   You stay steady. You name the possible."

4. ESCRIBE 2 mini-scenarios en board:

   SCENARIO 1 — Planning next week's client visit
     Lead asks: arrival time, who presents, where you meet,
     what you bring. Member is unsure — answers with might:
     "The client might arrive at 10." "I might present the
     numbers." "We might meet in room 3."

   SCENARIO 2 — Planning a team shift / project deadline
     Lead asks: who covers Saturday, what time the report
     is ready, where the team works. Member unsure:
     "I might cover Saturday." "It might be ready by 5."
     "We might work from the office."

5. PIDELES round 1 (5 min): pareja escoge un scenario.
   Roles claros. Lead arranca con una pregunta futura.

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces en
   la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven a parejas.

7. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, switch to
   Spanish, think 5 seconds, come back to English with
   'might'. Don't freeze."

8. ROTA parejas + cambia roles. Round 2 (5 min): cambien
   de scenario tambien.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante la simulacion: si alguien
   suelta "I will" para algo no confirmado, DI suave:
   "Temperance. Not confirmed. 'I MIGHT.' Stay calm with
   not knowing."

10. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces en
    parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu cuaderno.

11. OBSERVER COUNT (ligero): en round 2, asigna a 2
    estudiantes como observers — cuentan en silencio
    cuantos "MIGHT" y cuantos "MIGHT NOT" oyeron en la
    pareja vecina. Al final reportan: "Pair X — 5 might,
    1 might not." No correccion, solo conteo.

12. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente al
    grupo (lead + member, 3 preguntas + 3 respuestas con
    might). COHORTE cuenta cuantos "might" correctos
    cazaron. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("What time might the client arrive?" / "Who might cover the Saturday shift?") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 2 rotaciones + UNA demo al frente + observer count reportado.

```
4. HOMEWORK Cl 7 (~5 min). ESCRIBE en board mientras
   la dices:

      HOMEWORK CL 6 — DUE BEFORE CL 7, 7:00 PM
      TIME: 20-30 min (A2). NOT fragmented.

      (1) WRITING — 8 sentences with MIGHT (base verb,
          no 'to', no S, no -ing):

                "I might travel to Bogotá next month."
                "She might work at 4:00."
                "They might be at the office."
                "It might arrive at 7:00."

                "I might not go to the party."
                "He might not come tomorrow."

                "Are you going to study tonight? — I might."
                "Will they help? — They might not."

          Label: A2-6. Send to WhatsApp group before Cl 7.

      RULE: not fragmented. No "I didn't have time."

5. Cl 7 ANNOUNCE. DI: "Next class Cl 7: M8 — the relative
   pronoun 'that'. We join two ideas into one — 'I think
   THAT you are right.' Temperance day 2. See you next
   class — calm and steady."
```
