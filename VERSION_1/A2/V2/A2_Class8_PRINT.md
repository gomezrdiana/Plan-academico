## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT M10 1ST CONDITIONAL (~52 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board el titulo y las REGLAS del libro
   (M10, p.75-76), tal cual:

   THE FIRST CONDITIONAL — likely / certain events

   1) Like Spanish: an "IF" clause (si) + a CONDITIONAL
      clause.
   2) English uses the same 4 conditionals as Spanish —
      it depends how PROBABLE the event is.
   3) FIRST conditional = PROBABLE or CERTAIN events.

   STRUCTURE (write big, 2 columns):
      IF CLAUSE                 CONDITIONAL CLAUSE
      VERB IN PRESENT           VERB IN SIMPLE FUTURE (will)
      "If I HAVE money…"        "…I WILL buy a car."
      Si tengo plata…           …compraré un carro.

   4) Negative in BOTH clauses + question in the result:
        "If I DON'T have money, I WON'T buy a car."
        "If you have money, WILL you buy a car?"
        "If you have money, WHAT will you buy?"

2. SEÑALA las 2 columnas. DI: "Left = the condition, PRESENT.
   Right = the result, WILL. The 'if' side never takes
   'will'. Only the result does."

3. ESCRIBE EJEMPLOS del libro (p.77), tal cual:

      If I play, I will play soccer.
      If we travel, we will fly to Paris.
      If you eat lunch, where will you eat?
      If I wake up late, I won't shower in the morning.
      If I have a car, I will drive to Bogotá.
      If the girls are hungry, where will they eat?
      If there is an emergency, who will you call?

4. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "The phrase
   shows it — 'If I keep my balance, I WILL choose well.'
   Condition present, result with will. Steady."

5. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "If I WILL have money, I will buy a
   car" -> TACHA el primer "will", ESCRIBE "If I HAVE money,
   I will buy a car." DI: "No 'will' in the 'if' side.
   Present there." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

6. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "If I have money, I buy a car" -> AÑADE
   "will", ESCRIBE "...I WILL buy a car." DI: "The result
   needs 'will'." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

7. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "If I will be late..." -> TACHA "will
   be", ESCRIBE "If I AM late..." DI: "Present in the 'if'.
   'If I am late, I will take a taxi.'" COHORTE repite 2 veces.

8. PRACTICA dirigida (oral): TU das la mitad "If...", el
   grupo completa con "will":
        "If it rains, ___."   "If I have time, ___."
        "If you study, ___."  "If we win, ___."
   8-10 vueltas, distintos estudiantes.

9. [VIRTUD] activation: si alguien mete "will" en el "if",
   DI suave: "Temperance. Slow down. Present on the 'if'
   side. Only the result takes 'will'."

10. PIDELES en parejas: "Student A says an 'IF' condition
    (present). Student B finishes with the result (will).
    Then switch. 6 conditionals, real situations — work,
    money, travel, study. 5 minutes."

11. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca en
    2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota errores con
    NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

12. ESCRIBE la regla 4 en accion (negativo + pregunta):
        "If they have no money, they won't go to the store."
        "If you want a cookie, where will you buy it?"
        "If you work in London, who will you live with?"
    Practica 4-5 vueltas rapidas en coro.

13. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "If I won't have money..." -> TACHA,
    ESCRIBE "If I DON'T have money..." DI: "Negative on the
    'if' side = don't/doesn't (present). 'Won't' is for the
    result." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

14. PIDELES en parejas round 2: "Now MIX. 6 conditionals
    total — 4 affirmative, 1 negative ('If I don't... I
    won't...'), 1 question ('If..., what will you...?').
    Theme: your work or money decisions. 5 minutes."

15. CIRCULA. CORRIGE cuando el mismo error aparezca 2 veces
    en parejas distintas.

16. CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo forme "If + present, ... +
    will" sin meter will en el if. DI: "Stand up — we go to
    the simulation. Customer service deal desk."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 32 min y aun meten "will" en la clausula del "if", NO AVANCES al mix. Quedate solo en afirmativo ("If I have..., I will...") hasta que salga limpio; deja negativo y pregunta para el opener de Cl 9. Mejor LAND uno bien que rush dos shaky.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas formando first conditional con presente en el "if" y will en el resultado.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: CUSTOMER SERVICE / DEAL DESK (~36 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "CUSTOMER SERVICE / DEAL DESK"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. One is the AGENT, one is the
   CUSTOMER negotiating a likely deal. Every offer is a
   first conditional — a real condition + a result with
   will. You must use, in the whole conversation, AT LEAST:
     - 5 first conditionals ('If you pay today, I will ship
       tomorrow')
     - 1 NEGATIVE ('If you don't sign, we won't start')
     - 2 QUESTIONS ('If you order 10, what will you pay?')
   Then rotate."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This deal desk is the phrase in
   action. 'If I keep my balance, I will choose well.' Real
   condition, steady result. No rush."

4. ESCRIBE 2 mini-scenarios en board:

   SCENARIO 1 — Closing a sale / service contract
     Agent offers conditions, customer asks results:
     "If you buy today, I will give a discount." "If you
     pay in cash, what will you charge?" "If the product
     fails, we will replace it."

   SCENARIO 2 — Booking / rescheduling at a front desk
     Customer asks conditions, agent answers:
     "If I arrive late, will you keep my room?" "If you
     cancel, you won't pay the fee." "If the flight is
     delayed, what will the hotel do?"

5. PIDELES round 1 (5 min): pareja escoge un scenario.
   Roles claros. El cliente o el agente arranca con un "if".

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces en
   la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven a parejas.

7. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, switch to
   Spanish, think 5 seconds, come back to English with
   'if + present, will'. Don't freeze."

8. ROTA parejas + cambia roles. Round 2 (5 min): cambien
   de scenario tambien.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante la simulacion: si alguien
   mete "will" en el "if", DI suave: "Temperance. Present
   on the 'if' side. Only the result takes 'will'."

10. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces en
    parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu cuaderno.

11. OBSERVER COUNT (ligero): en round 2, asigna a 2
    estudiantes como observers — cuentan en silencio
    cuantos first conditionals CORRECTOS (present + will)
    oyeron en la pareja vecina. Reportan: "Pair X — 5
    correct conditionals." No correccion, solo conteo.

12. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente al
    grupo (3 ofertas condicionales + respuestas). COHORTE
    cuenta cuantos first conditionals correctos cazaron.
    Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("If the customer pays today, what will you do?" / "If you book two nights, will the hotel give breakfast?") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol.
