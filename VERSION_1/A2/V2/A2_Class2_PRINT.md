## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT M2 MONTHS + YEARS + IN (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE 3 columnas grandes en board:

   MONTHS                  YEARS                   PREPOSITION
   January   (Jan)         2026 =                  IN + month
   February  (Feb)          "twenty twenty-six"    IN + year
   March     (Mar)           OR                    IN + month + year
   April     (Apr)          "two thousand
   May                       twenty-six"           "IN March"
   June      (Jun)                                 "IN 2026"
   July      (Jul)         "twenty twenty-six"     "IN March 2026"
   August    (Aug)          more common today
   September (Sept/Sep)                            NEVER:
   October   (Oct)         Abbreviated:            X "ON March"
   November  (Nov)          "26'" informal         X "AT March"
   December  (Dec)                                 X "in the 2026"

   ALL MONTHS CAPITALIZED — always.

2. SEÑALA columna 1. DI: "Twelve months. All
   capitalized. Not like Spanish — English ALWAYS
   capitalizes the month."

3. PRONUNCIATION drill (coro 2x cada uno):
     February  = /FEB-roo-er-ee/ (4 silabas, segunda
                  'r' suave; NO sobrepronunciar)
     August    = /AW-gust/ (NO "Augusto")
     September = /sep-TEM-ber/ (acento en TEM)
     March     = /mɑːrtʃ/ (NO como Spanish "marcha")

4. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "january" (sin mayuscula) -> TACHA
   la 'j' minuscula. ESCRIBE "January" (J grande).
   COHORTE repite 2 veces.

5. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "FE-BRU-A-RI" (sobrepronunciado)
   -> TACHA. ESCRIBE "FEB-ru-ar-y" (4 silabas).
   COHORTE repite 2 veces en coro.

6. PIDELES en parejas (3 min): "Take turns. Say all
   12 months in order, then random. Partner corrects
   pronunciation. Listen for February, August, March."

7. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca
   en 2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota errores
   con NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

8. SEÑALA columna 2 (years). DI: "Two ways to say a
   year from 2000 onward. 'Twenty twenty-six' is now
   more common in workplace English. 'Two thousand
   twenty-six' is also correct. Pick one in one
   sentence and stay consistent."

9. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "This is
   what the phrase says — the small preposition IN
   decides if the listener trusts the date. Measure
   before you speak."

10. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "two thousand AND twenty-six"
    (with AND, British style not standard here) ->
    TACHA "and". ESCRIBE "two thousand twenty-six".
    DI: "American workplace English — drop the 'and'."
    COHORTE repite 2 veces.

11. PIDELES en parejas (3 min): "5 years each — say
    them aloud both ways. Mix decades: 1998, 2005,
    2019, 2026, 2030. Partner counts errors silently."

12. CIRCULA. CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca en 2
    parejas distintas.

13. SEÑALA columna 3 (preposition IN). DI: "For
    months, for years, for month+year — always IN.
    NEVER ON. NEVER AT. NEVER 'in the' before a
    year."

14. [VIRTUD] activation: si alguien titubea con la
    preposicion, DI suave: "Prudence. Pick the right
    preposition. Capitalize the month."

15. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "on March" -> TACHA "on".
    ESCRIBE "IN March". COHORTE repite 2 veces.

16. [ERROR TIPICO #5] "at June" -> TACHA "at".
    ESCRIBE "IN June". COHORTE repite 2 veces.

17. [ERROR TIPICO #6] "in the 2026" -> TACHA "the".
    ESCRIBE "in 2026". COHORTE repite 2 veces.

18. PIDELES en parejas (6 min): "Combine WILL from
    Cl 1 + month + year from today. 6 sentences each.
    Pattern: 'I will + verb + IN + month' or 'I will
    + verb + IN + month + year'. Example: 'I will
    travel IN July twenty twenty-six'."

19. CIRCULA, CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo construya
    WILL + IN + month sin titubear. DI: "Stand up —
    we go to the simulation."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 30 min y aun confunden ON/IN o se les escapa "january" sin mayuscula, NO AVANCES a combinacion con WILL. Reduce a meses + preposicion IN simples hoy, deja WILL combinado para Block 3 con menos exigencia. Mejor LAND 2 bien que rush 3 shaky.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas construyendo "I will + verb + IN + month (+ year)" sin pedir ayuda.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: BIRTHDAY & ANNIVERSARY EXCHANGE (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "BIRTHDAY & ANNIVERSARY
   EXCHANGE — important dates in your life."

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. You interview each other
   about important dates. Birthdays, anniversaries,
   past vacations, future trips. Each pair must use
   AT LEAST 5 months ALOUD + 3 years ALOUD + 5
   prepositions IN + 2 WILL constructions for the
   future like 'I'll travel IN July'. Then you
   rotate."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This call is the phrase
   in action — measure the month before you name it.
   The small preposition IN decides if the listener
   trusts your date."

4. ESCRIBE 4 question prompts en board:

   - When is your birthday?
   - When is your wedding / family anniversary?
   - When was your last vacation? Where?
   - When WILL your next trip be? Where?

5. PIDELES round 1 (8 min): pareja se entrevista.
   Cada uno hace las 4 preguntas + 2 follow-ups.
   Cada uno anota en su cuaderno 3 dates que oye
   de su pareja.

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3
   veces en la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x ->
   vuelven a parejas.

7. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, switch
   to Spanish, think 5 seconds, come back to English.
   Don't freeze."

8. ROTA parejas + cambia roles. Round 2 (8 min):
   nuevas parejas, mismas 4 preguntas + push para
   add 1 nueva pregunta personal.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante la simulacion: si
   alguien suelta "on March" o "in the 2026", DI
   suave: "Prudence. IN, not ON. Drop 'the' before
   the year."

10. ROTA parejas otra vez. Round 3 (7 min): mismo
    formato, push para que aparezcan WILL + IN +
    month + year en cada turno futuro.

11. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2
    veces en parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en
    tu cuaderno.

12. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente
    al grupo (interviewer + interviewee, 3 preguntas
    + 3 respuestas, todas con month + IN). COHORTE
    cuenta cuantos months + cuantos years + cuantos
    WILL cazaron correctos. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("Ask about her birthday month" / "Ask about a vacation in 2025") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 3 rotaciones + UNA demo al frente + cada cuaderno con 3 dates escritas.

              "I will travel IN July twenty
