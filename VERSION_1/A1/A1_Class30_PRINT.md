## ⚠️ NOTA SOBRE EL CONTENIDO — M33 THE PRESENT CONTINUOUS

**M33 oficial (libro pág 375):** "What Are You Doing? — The Continuous"

Estructura clave: **BE (am / is / are) + VERBO + "-ing"** — acciones que ocurren EN ESTE MOMENTO.

```
EL PRESENTE CONTINUO — BE + VERBO + "-ing"

Se forma con "be" (estar) de auxiliar + el verbo con "-ing"
("-ing" = el equivalente de "-ando / -iendo").

I         → am          I am playing.
You/We/They → are        You are playing.
He/She/It → is           He is playing.

(El presente SIMPLE = acciones habituales: "I play soccer."
 El presente CONTINUO = acción AHORA: "I am playing golf today.")

AFIRMATIVO / NEGATIVO:
- I am (not) playing soccer.        → I'm (not) playing.
- You are (not) playing soccer.     → You're (not) / aren't playing.
- He is (not) playing soccer.       → He's (not) / isn't playing.
- We are (not) playing soccer.      → We're (not) / aren't playing.
- They are (not) playing soccer.    → They're (not) / aren't playing.

INTERROGATIVO:
- Am I playing soccer?
- Are you playing soccer?
- Is he playing soccer?
- Are we playing soccer?
- Are they playing soccer?

REGLAS DE ORTOGRAFÍA:
- Verbo termina en "e" → se quita la "e": like → liking.
- En algunos casos se duplica la última consonante: run → running.

RESPUESTA CORTA: mismas reglas de "be" ya aprendidas.
- "Are you playing today?" → "Yes, I am. / No, I'm not."
```

Vocabulario base del módulo (libro pág 376) — **verbos de acción**: **take a bus, take a taxi, drive, walk, watch, clean**. (+ verbos ya conocidos para combinar: play, eat, study, work, make, talk, visit, live, swim.)

### Concepto (10 min)

**Tablero:**

```
THE PRESENT CONTINUOUS — BE + VERB -ING

I            → am        I am playing.
You/We/They  → are       You are playing.
He/She/It    → is        He is playing.

              AFFIRMATIVE        NEGATIVE              QUESTION
I             I'm playing.       I'm not playing.      Am I playing?
You           You're playing.    You aren't playing.   Are you playing?
He / She / It  He's playing.      He isn't playing.     Is he playing?
We            We're playing.     We aren't playing.    Are we playing?
They          They're playing.   They aren't playing.  Are they playing?

KEY RULES:
- Simple = habit ("I play soccer"). Continuous = NOW ("I'm playing golf today").
- Verb ends in "e" → drop the "e": like → liking.
- Sometimes double the last consonant: run → running.

SHORT ANSWERS:
- "Are you playing today?" → "Yes, I am. / No, I'm not."
- "Is he working?" → "Yes, he is. / No, he isn't."
```

**Ejemplos modelados del libro pág 378** (usar Frase del Día — inserción #1):
- *"Are you taking a bus today?"* → *"No, I'm taking a taxi."*
- *"Where is Maria walking?"* → *"She is walking in the park."*
- *"What are the girls watching?"* → *"They're watching a movie."*
- *"What are you cleaning?"* → *"I am cleaning my house — I'm not rushing."*

## 8. SIMULACIÓN — "THE LIVE UPDATE CALL" (00:58–01:23, 25 min) — DE PIE

**OBJETIVO:** *"Real-life scene. You call your team to give a live update — what is happening right now at the site, who is working, what is not happening yet — calmly, at a steady pace so nobody panics. That's temperance."*

### Setup (3 min)

**Escenario:** Un empleado llama a su equipo / supervisor para dar un reporte EN VIVO: qué está pasando ahora mismo (quién está trabajando, qué se está haciendo, qué NO se está haciendo todavía). Mantiene un tono calmado y un ritmo moderado — informa sin acelerarse ni alarmar.

**Roles:** empleado en el sitio (reporta lo que pasa ahora) + supervisor al teléfono (pregunta + anota el update).

### Vocabulario forzado

- Mínimo 5 preguntas con *"What are you doing? / Is...? / Are...?"* + verbo -ing
- Mínimo 5 respuestas con *"I'm...ing / We're...ing / He isn't...ing / They're not...ing"*
- Estructura: *"What are you doing right now?"* → *"I'm checking the orders. We're not closing yet."* / *"Is the team working?"* → *"Yes, they are. They're working calmly."*

### Modela (3 min)

> *Supervisor: "Hi — what's happening right now?"*
> *Empleado: "I'm checking the floor. The team is working, but we aren't finishing yet."*
> *Supervisor: "Is everything calm?"*
> *Empleado: "Yes. We're moving at a steady pace — not too fast, not too slow."*

### Round 1 (9 min) — Grupos de 4

Cada grupo tiene una "escena en vivo" (papelitos: opening / closing / cleaning / driving / a customer waiting / two people not here / everything calm). El supervisor pregunta, el empleado responde con be + verbo -ing y mantienen el tono moderado.

### Round 2 (8 min) — Rotación de roles

Nuevo supervisor. Nueva escena en vivo.

### Cierre (2 min)

1 grupo representa frente al curso. Aplauso. Anotas errores con NOMBRE en tu libreta personal.
