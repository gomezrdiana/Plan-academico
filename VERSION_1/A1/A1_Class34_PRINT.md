## 10. DICTADO INVERSO (01:28–01:38, 10 min) — DE PIE

**OBJETIVO:** *"Reverse roles. YOU dictate. I write on the board. I'm going to make mistakes on purpose — you catch them. Reinforces grammar with energy."*

### Setup (1 min)

Te paras en el tablero con marcador. *"Dictate me sentences from YOUR letter — one sentence at a time. I'll write what I hear. If I make a mistake — YOU correct me out loud."*

### Drill (8 min)

5-7 estudiantes dictan una oración cada uno (de su carta). Tú escribes — y deliberadamente metes errores cada 2da oración:

- Estudiante: *"I'm going to call my brother on Sunday."*
- Tú escribes: *"I going to calls my brother on Sunday."*
- Grupo: *"NO teacher — I'M going to CALL. No -s, and you need I'M."*
- Tú: *"Right! Thank you."* (corriges visiblemente)

Alterna errores: falta de be, doble conjugación, verbo con -s después de going to, present continuous sin -ing, pasado regular sin -ed, comparativo mal formado.

### Cierre (1 min)

*"Notice — you fixed every mistake. You KNOW the grammar. The Final is just showing what you already have."*
