## 6. VISUAL REVIEW — TIMELINE M0–M35 — 20 min — SIT

> *"Open your notebook to a fresh page. Today we draw the WHOLE A1 map. One picture, every tense, every structure."*

### Setup — 2 min

Draw TIMELINE big on the board BEFORE class:

```
                ===  A1 TIMELINE — M0-M35  ===

PASADO              <-- PRESENTE -->                 FUTURO
--------            ----------------                 -------
was / were          am / is / are                    will + base
past simple -ed     present simple (-s 3rd)          going to + base
past simple irreg.  present continuous (-ing)        gonna (spoken)

      MODALES (top):    can | should | want to | need to

      OTROS  (bottom):  there is/are | some/any
                        adverbs of frequency
                        prepositions (in/on/at/under/next to)
```

### Collaborative build — 13 min

1. (2 min) Read TIMELINE aloud. Students copy to their notebook.
2. (11 min) For each block, call ONE student (rotating) for ONE affirmative + ONE negative + ONE question:
   - **Past simple:** *"I went to the gym yesterday." / "I didn't go." / "Did you go?"*
   - **Present simple 3rd:** *"She works in Bogotá." / "She doesn't work." / "Does she work?"*
   - **Present continuous:** *"They're studying right now." / "They aren't studying." / "Are they studying?"*
   - **Future will:** *"I'll help you tomorrow." / "I won't be late." / "Will you come?"*
   - **Future going to:** *"He's going to buy a phone." / "He isn't going to buy it." / "Is he going to buy it?"*
   - **Can:** *"I can swim." / "I can't drive." / "Can you cook?"*
   - **Should:** *"You should rest." / "You shouldn't smoke." / "Should I call her?"*
   - **There is / are:** *"There is a park near here." / "There aren't any taxis." / "Are there any restaurants?"*

If someone hesitates, the rest completes. NO correction aloud — note with NAME in personal notebook.

## 7. 10 RAPID EXAM ITEMS + AUTO-CORRECTION — 15 min — SIT

Hand out the sheet (1 per student):

```
A1 — RAPID 10 — EXAM FORMAT — CL 39
Name: _________________________

Choose the correct tense / form. 1 min per item.

1. Yesterday I _______ (go) to my grandmother's house.
2. She usually _______ (work) at 8 a.m.
3. Right now we _______ (study) for the final.
4. I _______ (not eat) meat. I'm vegetarian.
5. _______ you _______ (live) in Bucaramanga? (question, present)
6. They _______ (visit) Cartagena next month. (future plan)
7. _______ (can) you swim? — Yes, I _______.
8. There _______ (be) three chairs and one table in the room.
9. I _______ (not see) him last Sunday.
10. You _______ (should) rest more — you look tired.
```

### Procedure — 15 min

1. (1 min) Read instructions.
2. (10 min) Work alone. Visible stopwatch: 1 min per item. Announce every minute.
3. (4 min) Swap sheets with partner. Read answers aloud:
   - 1) went | 2) works | 3) are studying | 4) don't eat | 5) Do … live | 6) are going to visit | 7) Can … can | 8) are | 9) didn't see | 10) should

Public count: *"How many got 8+? Hand up. 5–7? 0–4?"* Note levels in your personal notebook for coordination (don't say names aloud).

**Insertion #2 of Phrase of the Day:** *"If you got fewer than 5 right — listen: 'I'm going to keep showing up even when it's hard.' That's the answer."*

## 8. MINI MOCK EXAM — 15 MIXED ITEMS — 20 min — SIT

> *"This is exactly what the Final looks like. 18 minutes, 15 items, mixed sections. No talking. Then auto-correct."*

Hand out the sheet (1 per student):

```
A1 — MINI MOCK EXAM — CL 39
Name: _________________________   Time: 18 min

SECTION A — Grammar (5 items, 1 pt each)
1. _______ (be) you at the gym yesterday?
2. My sister _______ (not like) coffee.
3. We _______ (travel) to Cali next December.
4. They _______ (watch) TV right now.
5. _______ (be) there any milk in the fridge?

SECTION B — Vocabulary (3 items, 1 pt each)
6. Translate: "siempre" = _______
7. Translate: "el sábado pasado" = _______
8. Translate: "debería" = _______

SECTION C — Translation ESP > ENG (3 items, 2 pts each)
9. Yo voy a llamar a mi mamá mañana.
10. ¿Puedes manejar un carro?
11. Hay tres parques cerca de mi casa.

SECTION D — Free writing (2 items, 3 pts each)
12. Write 1 sentence about something you DID last weekend.
13. Write 1 sentence about a PLAN for next weekend (use going to).

SECTION E — Reading (2 items, 2 pts each)
"Maria works in a hospital. She wakes up at 5 a.m. every day.
Yesterday she didn't go to work because she was sick. Today
she is feeling better and she is going to return tomorrow."

14. When did Maria stay home? _______________
15. What is Maria going to do tomorrow? _______________

TOTAL: ____ / 25
```

### Procedure — 20 min

1. (1 min) Read instructions. Full silence. Stopwatch up.
2. (15 min) Work ALONE. Walk in silence. NO help, NO correction. Just presence.
3. (4 min) Guided auto-correction:
   - 1) Were | 2) doesn't like | 3) are going to travel | 4) are watching | 5) Is
   - 6) always | 7) last Saturday | 8) should
   - 9) I'm going to call my mom tomorrow. | 10) Can you drive (a car)? | 11) There are three parks near my house.
   - 12-13) accept any well-formed sentence
   - 14) Yesterday | 15) She is going to return (to work)

Students note their score. Collect sheets — input for coordination (do NOT communicate scores).
