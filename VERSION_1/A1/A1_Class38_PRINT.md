## 6. A1 8-STRUCTURES CHECKLIST + MODELING — 8 min — SIT

Write on the board (before class):

```
MINIPROJECT — A1 STRUCTURE CHECKLIST

[ ] PRESENT SIMPLE      "I work / My family lives..."
[ ] PRESENT CONTINUOUS  "I'm studying English right now..."
[ ] PAST SIMPLE         "Yesterday I went / Last month I..."
[ ] GOING TO / WILL     "I'm going to / I will..."
[ ] CAN / SHOULD        "I can speak / You should visit..."
[ ] THERE IS / ARE      "There is a park / There are 3..."
[ ] SOME / ANY          "I have some / There isn't any..."
[ ] PREPOSITIONS        "in / on / at / next to / between"
```

> *"Your miniproject MUST contain these 8 structures. Not one. Not two. ALL of them. Before you practice, here's a model."*

Model out loud (cohort counts how many structures you use — target 8/8):

> *"Hi, my name is [name] and I work at [place]. Right now I'm preparing my final presentation. Yesterday I practiced for 20 minutes with my brother. This weekend I'm going to record myself and review it. I can speak about my routine in English, and you should visit Bucaramanga — there are amazing mountains, and there is a beautiful park in the center, between two avenues."*

**Insertion #1 of Phrase of the Day:** *"Like our phrase — 'I'm going to practice my project today.' That's PRESENT CONTINUOUS plan with GOING TO. Already 2 structures."*

## 7. MINIPROJECT PRACTICE IN PAIRS — 30 min — STAND UP

> *"Present YOUR miniproject to a partner. Partner times you 3–4 min and gives 3-line feedback. We rotate 3 times — three different partners, three improvements."*

### Setup — 3 min

Each student takes out their 5-keyword card (max). NO full text. NO phone. Just the 5-word card.

Write on the board (partner feedback frame):

```
PARTNER FEEDBACK — 3 LINES ONLY

(1) "I understood ___" / "I didn't understand ___"
(2) "Add more about ___"
(3) "Use ___ structure — I didn't hear it"
    (look at the 8-structures checklist)
```

### Round 1 — 8 min (4 min present + 4 min feedback, then swap)

Pairs: A presents to B. B times + marks which structures from the checklist they heard. End: 3 feedback lines. Swap. B presents to A. Same feedback.

You walk with personal notebook. Note 3 errors with NAMES. **No interruption during presentation.** Only listen.

### Round 2 — 10 min (new partners)

New pairs. Same format. PRESENTER must incorporate 1 improvement from Round 1 feedback (more structures, more detail, slower, etc).

**Insertion #2 of Phrase of the Day:** *"Between rounds — 'I'm going to practice my project today.' This IS practicing. Each round is fortitude."*

### Round 3 — 8 min (final rotation + self-check)

Third rotation. By now each presenter should sound more confident. Close of Round 3 (1 min individual silence): each student asks *"How many of the 8 structures did I really use? Which one is missing?"* and notes it on their card for home practice.

### Block close — 1 min

> *"Hand up — who feels more confident than at minute 31? Good. That's fortitude — not magic, just repetition."*

## 8. TIME CAPSULE — LETTER TO FUTURE SELF — 18 min — SIT

> *"A letter to your future self in English. Written BY HAND, IN CLASS, RIGHT NOW. Sealed in an envelope with your name. Opened when you finish A2 — about 12 weeks. This is fortitude on paper."*

### Setup — 3 min

Hand each student: 1 blank A4 sheet + pen. The 6 white envelopes (pre-labeled with names) wait at the front.

Write on the board (rules):

```
TIME CAPSULE — RULES

[1] BY HAND. No phones. No typing. No AI.
[2] IN ENGLISH only. (If you don't know a word,
    write it in Spanish in parentheses and continue.)
[3] 12-15 sentences minimum.
[4] Use AT LEAST 5 of the 8 structures
    from the miniproject checklist.
[5] You write it. I seal it. I keep it.
    You open it the day you finish A2.
```

### Prompt on the board — 1 min read

```
DEAR FUTURE ME (in 12 weeks),

Today is ___/___/2026.
I'm in the middle of A1 — class 38 of 45.

Right now I can ___ (what you can do today).
Right now I cannot yet ___ (be honest).
This week I'm going to ___ (concrete plan).
My miniproject is about ___ (which one).
My oral final topic is ___ (which of the 5).

Something that's hard for me in English: ___
Something I'm proud of: ___

When you read this, you will already be in A2.
Remember the person who wrote this. They were
trying. They were tired. They didn't give up.

One question for the future you:
"Did you finish what you started?"

With fortitude,
[your name]
```

### Writing — 12 min — SILENCE

Each student writes their letter, by hand, on their sheet. You walk in silence. **Verify visually it's the student's handwriting** (no screen-copy, no pre-written paper). If someone finishes early: re-read and add 2 sentences.

**Do NOT correct grammar during Time Capsule.** It's a personal document — the future self understands the present self's mistakes.

### Fold + envelope — 2 min

Each student folds their letter, puts it in their envelope (with their name). You pass with tape — seal each envelope in front of the student. Envelopes stay with you (folder labeled *"A1 Time Capsule — open at end of A2"*).

**Insertion #3 of Phrase of the Day:** *"That letter — 'the person I'm becoming' reads it in 12 weeks. That's fortitude written down."*

## 10. ERROR PAPER LIVE + PHRASE CLOSE + ENVELOPE VERIFICATION + HOMEWORK + CLOSE — 16 min — STAND UP

### Error Paper Live — 4 min

Write on the board (anonymous; NAMES go ONLY in your personal notebook for coordination):

```
1. ❌ "I going to talk about my city."     → ✅ "I'm going to talk about my city."
2. ❌ "Yesterday I go to my brother house."→ ✅ "Yesterday I went to my brother's house."
3. ❌ "There is many parks in Bucaramanga."→ ✅ "There ARE many parks in Bucaramanga."
4. ❌ "You can to visit Chicamocha."        → ✅ "You can visit Chicamocha."
5. ❌ "My family is lives in Floridablanca."→ ✅ "My family lives in Floridablanca."
```

Read. Students correct in chorus 2×.
