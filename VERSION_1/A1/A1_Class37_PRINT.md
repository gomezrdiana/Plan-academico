## 6. SPEED DATING 5 RONDAS (00:23–00:43, 20 min) — DE PIE

**OBJETIVO:** *"Two lines facing each other. 5 rounds. Each round is 2 minutes. Different tense each round. When I clap — rotate. Speak. Don't pause. Don't translate. If you don't know a word, point or describe."*

### Setup (3 min)

Tablero — escribe ANTES de clase las 5 preguntas/rondas:

```
SPEED DATING — 5 ROUNDS — 2 min each

R1 CAN       → "What can you do? What can your family do?"
R2 SHOULD    → "What should I visit in Bucaramanga? What should I eat?"
R3 PAST      → "What did you do yesterday? Where did you go last weekend?"
R4 CONTINUOUS→ "What are you studying right now? Who is sitting beside you?"
R5 GOING TO  → "What are you going to do this weekend? Where are you going?"

RULE: 2 min per round. Clap = rotate. No silence. Profe walks + listens.
```

Acomoda 2 filas enfrentadas (si son 6 → 3 vs 3; si son 4 → 2 vs 2; si son 5 → trío rotativo en un extremo). Lee las 5 rondas en voz alta. *"This is your A1 mouth. All your verb tenses, back to back."*

### Rondas 1-5 (15 min — 2 min × 5 + 5 min de transiciones)

- **Palmada inicia** la ronda. *"R1 — CAN. Go!"*
- **2 min** — caminas, escuchas, anotas errores en libreta personal (con NOMBRE).
- **Palmada** → *"Rotate! Next person. R2 — SHOULD. Go!"*
- Continúa hasta R5.

Si alguien se queda callado: dale la pregunta más fácil directamente (*"Can you cook? Yes or no, then why."*). NUNCA corrijas durante. Errores van a libreta personal.

### Cierre Speed Dating (2 min)

*"Sit. In one word — which round was HARDEST? Which felt EASIEST?"* — 2-3 voces. Anotas para Cl 38.
