## E3. ORAL EXAM A1 — 25 min — YES (presenter) / SIT (waiting)

> **CONTENT = COORDINATION.** Per-student order assigned by coordination (list received before class). Each student presents their cohort miniproject close (~3 min) + answers 1 coord question (~1 min). Guide describes ONLY the structure.

### Setup (1 min)

- Cohort waits at desks in silence — eyes on own paper or hands on lap. NO phones (still at front). NO whispering.
- Teacher calls in order of coordination's list.
- Each student goes to the front (~4 min per student × 6 = 24 min — 1 min buffer).

### Per-student structure (~4 min)

| Time | Block | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:15 | Student stands at front | brief pause to breathe (the "Temperance to breathe" line from the Phrase) |
| 0:15–3:00 | Free production | ~3 min on the miniproject close prepared since Cl 43; teacher listens, does NOT interrupt |
| 3:00–3:45 | 1 teacher question | from coord list (simple A1 English: past / present / future / opinion) |
| 3:45–4:00 | Brief close | *"Thank you. Archived for coordination. Next student."* |

### During the oral — teacher

- SILENT annotations on coordination sheet (say nothing to the student during their turn).
- Do NOT comment performance. NO feedback. NO *"good / better / nice / that was wrong / try again"*. Only *"Thank you. Archived for coordination. Next."*
- If the student stumbles or pauses: ONE soft cue — *"Take a breath. Continue when you're ready."* — then silence. NO rescuing. NO finishing the sentence.
- If the student blanks completely: *"That's enough. Thank you. Archived for coordination. Next."* — no shame, no extension.
- No-show in cohort (absent today): mark NOT PRESENTED, hand to coordination. Do NOT reassign time. Do NOT comment in class.
- If minutes free because cohort is < 6: waiting students sit silent with no material; teacher reviews list / annotations.
