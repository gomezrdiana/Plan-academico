## 6. LAST WRITTEN MINI-MOCK (FINAL FORMAT) + AUTO-CORRECTION — 25 min — SIT

> *"This is your LAST written practice. Same format as the Final. 15 items. 18 minutes to write. 5 minutes to self-correct with the KEY. 2 minutes for one urgent question per person. After today, you've SEEN the format twice."*

### Setup — 2 min

Hand out the mini-mock sheet. Read instructions aloud:

```
MINI-MOCK (FINAL FORMAT) — 15 ITEMS — 18 min

PART A: GRAMMAR (5 items, 1 pt each)
  Complete with the correct tense (past / present /
  present continuous / going to / should / can).

PART B: VOCABULARY (3 items, 1 pt each)
  Translate the underlined word from English to Spanish
  (or write a synonym in English).

PART C: TRANSLATION (3 items, 1 pt each)
  Spanish to English. Short sentences.

PART D: SHORT WRITING (2 items, 2 pts each)
  - 1 sentence about YESTERDAY (past simple).
  - 1 sentence about NEXT WEEK (going to).

PART E: READING COMPREHENSION (2 items, 1.5 pts each)
  Read the short text + answer 2 questions in English.

TOTAL: 15 items / 18 pts. NO talking. NO looking at
your notebook. This is your real Final muscle.
```

### Write in silence — 18 min

Visible stopwatch. If anyone finishes early: review their own exam. **No help. No translation. No correction.** Let them feel the silence of the exam.

### Auto-correction with KEY — 5 min

Hand out the KEY sheet (answers + 1 explanation line per item). Each student corrects their own. They write their score in the corner (for them only — NOT collected, NOT communicated).

### Resolve urgent doubts — 1–2 min

*"ONE urgent question per person, only if it's still unclear. No discussion of grades."* Note recurring doubts in your personal notebook for coordination.
