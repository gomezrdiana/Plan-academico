## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT REFINEMENT + SET #25 PRECISION + PAIR REHEARSAL (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board el arco del monologo:

   MONOLOGUE ARC — 5 to 7 MIN
   [HOOK 20 sec] -> [PILLAR 1 · PILLAR 2 · PILLAR 3] -> [CLOSE 15 sec]
      (Cl 34)         MIDDLE = today's work               (Cl 34)
                      ~1 min each pillar
                      SHAPE drift lives here
                      Set #25 verb sharpens

2. DI (micro-talk ~3 min, de pie en board): "A 5-7 minute
   monologue has a HOOK, three PILLARS (the middle, ~1 min
   each — today's work), and a CLOSE. The hook and close
   carry attention; the pillars carry the ARGUMENT. The
   middle is where SHAPE drifts — where you mean X and say
   a safer version of X because X can be challenged.
   Today we sharpen ONE 60-sec spot per student."

3. DI: "Inside each pillar there is usually ONE 60-sec
   spot that drifts — generic instead of specific, safer
   instead of meant. That drift is what the external
   evaluator on Cl 36 will read as fluent-but-shapeless.
   Justice day 3 says: name what you actually mean."

4. ESCRIBE Set #25 en board (pre-escrito, revelado ahora):

   ACADEMIC B2 SET #25 (CL 33) — HOLDING THE TURN WITH PRECISION
   Cumulativo: 30 palabras en sistema.

   1. ELABORATE -- desarrollar / desplegar con detalle
      "Let me elaborate -- there are three layers
       to this point."

   2. CLARIFY -- precisar lo que si se quiso decir
      "To clarify -- I'm not saying X, I'm saying Y."

   3. QUALIFY -- matizar sin retirarse de la posicion
      "I'd qualify that -- it holds in most cases, not all."

   4. NUANCE (v.) -- añadir matiz fino sin perder el core
      "Let me nuance the claim -- the core stands,
       the edges shift."

   5. SPECIFY -- nombrar exactamente lo que se quiere decir
      "Let me specify -- by 'cultural', I mean the
       daily-life layer."

   6. CONTEXTUALIZE -- anclar el punto en su marco
      "Let me contextualize -- this claim sits inside
       post-conflict reality, not abstract theory."

5. Por palabra: pronuncia 2x → cohorte 2x → defincion +
   ejemplo. ~50 seg cada una = ~5 min embebidos. NO se
   drillea en isolation aqui — se usa DENTRO del spot
   afilado en pasos 6-12.

6. [FRASE] insertion 2: DI: "What is owed is the SHAPE.
   These six verbs ARE the shape language. Pick one. Own
   it. Use it inside the 60-sec spot you sharpen in the
   next ten minutes."

7. CRAFT INDIVIDUAL (~15 min) - SENTADOS, cuaderno abierto:
   DI: "Sit. Notebook open. Your monologue draft out.
   Fifteen minutes. By the end: ONE 60-second spot
   selected from inside pillars 1-3 + REWRITTEN by hand
   using ONE Set #25 verb as precision anchor. The spot
   must be a place where you currently say something fluent
   but generic, OR where a listener could ask 'what do you
   actually mean?' and you wouldn't have a sharp answer."

8. CIRCULA continuamente. ~5 min de coaching one-on-one
   por estudiante en whispers. Usa 2-3 de estos moves
   segun lo que escribieron:

   - "Read me the spot. Stop. What did you actually mean
     -- the unsafer version? Write that next to the safe
     version."
   - "Your spot says 'X is important' -- that's the
     generic. SPECIFY: 'Let me specify -- by X I mean...'"
   - "Your spot drifts at second 30 -- between the claim
     and the example. Insert ELABORATE: 'Let me elaborate
     -- there are two layers here.'"
   - "Your claim is true everywhere -- and that's the
     problem. QUALIFY: 'I'd qualify that -- it holds in
     the cities, not the rural zones.' The qualification
     makes the core SHARPER, not weaker."
   - "You hedged with 'I think maybe.' Strip the hedge.
     CONTEXTUALIZE: 'Let me contextualize -- this sits
     inside [their topic frame], not abstract theory.'"

9. ANOTA en TU cuaderno: por estudiante, QUE verbo Set #25
   escogio + QUE spot (pillar # + la frase safer que reemplaza).
   Sirve para Cl 34.

10. [FRASE] insertion 3 (whispered a quien este atascado):
    "Justice day 3 -- name what you actually mean, not
    what is safer to say. The safer version is what the
    external evaluator hears as shapeless fluency."

11. PAIR REHEARSAL (~20 min) - DE PIE, pares rotando:
    DI: "Stand up. Pair up. You rehearse ONLY the
    60-second spot you sharpened -- not the full pillar,
    not the full monologue -- just the one minute that
    now carries a Set #25 verb. Three times each per pair.
    Partner feedback after every delivery: ONE question
    + two checks. ONE: did the SHAPE fit -- did the
    specific replace the generic? Y/N. TWO: did you hear
    the Set #25 verb? Y/N. Rotate pairs every 6-7 min."

12. ROTA pares cada 6-7 min (3 rotaciones). Asegura que
    cada estudiante reciba feedback de 2 peers distintos
    + UNA pasada contigo. Regla estricta para el partner:
    NO "good job". Literal: "Shape fit? Y/N. Verb heard?
    The verb was ___. The specific was ___."

13. [VIRTUD] activation: cuando un estudiante de respuesta
    vaga, DI: "Justice. Specify. Contextualize. The room
    can't trust what you don't shape."

14. CIERRA (~30 seg de pie): "You each rehearsed the
    sharpened spot 9 times. Block 3 -- Q&A INTENSIVE,
    3 rondas, 3 preguntas cada una. Stay standing."
```

**[ALERTA]** si en el paso 7 alguien no encuentra spot (responde "all my pillars are fine") → DI: "Then pick the pillar your hook depends on most. The most-load pillar is the one to sharpen." NO aceptes "no tengo spot débil".
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** cada estudiante rehearseo su spot 9 veces y recibio feedback binario de 2 peers + ti.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: Q&A INTENSIVO HARDER (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande:

   Q&A INTENSIVO — 3 RONDAS x 3 PREGUNTAS
     Round 1: explain to SKEPTIC (deeper than B1 simplify)
     Round 2: COUNTER-ARGUMENT to your own position
     Round 3: PANEL questions (cohort generates)

   RHYTHM: question lands -> 3 sec start -> 80 sec answer
           -> 5 sec teacher note -> next.

   Set #24 to ENTER. Set #25 to HOLD with precision.

2. DI las reglas: "Nine questions total. Each of you
   answers ALL nine across rotation. Each answer ~80 sec.
   3 seconds to start. You stand to answer; the others
   sit and take notes. I do NOT stop you in the middle.
   Between answers I take 5 sec to note. Only on the
   worst gap I use 30 sec for cohort coaching."

3. DI: "This is HARDER than Cl 32. Round 1 you explain
   to a SKEPTIC who already heard your hook -- you can't
   replay the script. Round 2 you give ONE counter-argument
   to your own position -- self-skepticism. Round 3 the
   cohort generates Q&A panel questions for each other."

4. [PROMPT] cronometro on. ~80 seg cap por respuesta.

5. RONDA 1 - SKEPTIC POST-HOOK (~14 min)
   3 preguntas, rotadas (cada estudiante UNA):

   (a) "Someone heard your hook and says: 'I'm not
       convinced -- explain it again without repeating
       what you just said.' 80 sec. Set #25 SPECIFY o
       CLARIFY required."
   (b) "A skeptic says: 'Your central claim is fluent
       but I don't know what you actually mean.' Push
       back with SHAPE. Set #25 ELABORATE o NUANCE."
   (c) "Someone says: 'Your hook landed but you didn't
       deliver. Where is the substance?' Answer with
       CONTEXTUALIZE -- anchor the claim in real frame."

   Coaching solo si necesario (30 seg max una vez por
   ronda): "You replayed the hook. SPECIFY -- new layer.
   Try the second half again with: 'Let me specify by
   layer two -- ...'"

6. RONDA 2 - COUNTER-ARGUMENT A TI MISMO (~14 min)
   Rota desplazado (quien respondio R1-Q1 ahora responde
   R2-Q2, etc):

   (a) "Give me ONE counter-argument to your own central
       claim -- the strongest one a skeptic would raise.
       Then defend against it. 80 sec. Set #25 QUALIFY."
   (b) "Where does your position have its biggest weakness?
       Name it. Then say why the core still holds. Set #23
       hold + Set #25 NUANCE. Don't collapse."
   (c) "If you were the panelist, what's the question you
       would ask yourself to expose the weakest pillar?
       Ask it -- then answer it. Set #25 CONTEXTUALIZE."

   Coaching: si alguien EVITA la pregunta o da un fake
   counter-argument (uno que ya tiene respuesta pulida)
   → "That was a fake counter. The real counter is the
   one that makes you uncomfortable. SPECIFY the real
   weakness. Try again."

7. [FRASE] insertion 4 (entre Ronda 2 y 3, ~10 seg):
   "Precision is the form your respect takes. Round 3
   the cohort builds the questions -- you receive what
   they actually want to know."

8. RONDA 3 - PANEL DEL COHORTE (~14 min)
   Los OTROS 2 estudiantes generan UNA pregunta cada uno
   para el que responde. Rota.

   DI: "The two of you who are not answering generate
   ONE Q&A panel question for the one who IS. Make it
   the question a real panel would ask -- not a softball.
   The answerer has 3 sec to start, 80 sec to answer.
   Set #24 to enter even with half-formed answer + Set
   #25 to shape it as you go."

   Para cada respondedor: 2 preguntas de peers (una de
   cada uno) + 1 tuya como cierre cubriendo el gap mas
   doloroso que viste en R1+R2 de esa estudiante.

   Coaching: si bajo presion el reflejo Q&A degrada a
   5-seg silencio → "3 sec. Use Set #24 to ENTER even
   when the answer is half-formed, then Set #25 to shape
   it. Try again."

9. CIERRE BLOQUE 3 (~3 min, de pie en semicirculo):
   DI: "Nine answers each. Three rounds. More shape-
   pressure in 45 minutes than Cl 36 will give you in
   a single session. Each in ONE sentence: which ROUND
   today exposed your weakest move + what's the ONE
   thing you drill before Cl 34."

   3 x ~30 seg. Sin coaching aqui. Si alguien deflecta
   ("they were all hard"), anotalo en cuaderno -- se
   nombra en Cl 34.

10. DI: "Sit. Block 4 closes the class."
```

**[ERROR TIPICO] 5 marcadores**

```
1. Respuesta vaga ("It's important...")
   → DI: "Specify. WHO benefits? HOW? Use SPECIFY,
     not 'important'."

2. Repite material del hook en la respuesta
   → DI: "QUALIFY differently. Don't replay the
     script -- the skeptic already heard it."

3. Evita la pregunta
   → DI: "CLARIFY what you understood from the
     question first, then answer the actual question."

4. Set #25 usado mal (ej. "I'd elaborate that..." como
   bridge en vez de expansion)
   → DI: "Set #25 EXPANDS what you already said. Set
     #22 BRIDGES to something new. Different jobs."

5. Reflejo Q&A degrada a 5-seg silencio bajo presion
   → DI: "3 sec. Use Set #24 to ENTER even when the
     answer is half-formed, then Set #25 to SHAPE it
     as you talk. Silence is the collapse."
```

**[ALERTA]** si una ronda colapsa (todos dan respuestas vagas seguidas), PARA, ESCRIBE en board "SPECIFY. CONTEXTUALIZE." y reanuda con UN ejemplo modelo dicho por ti (15 seg max) → siguiente pregunta. NO te quedes en explicacion.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 9 preguntas + cohorte nombro su ronda mas debil + 3 anotaciones en tu cuaderno con nombres.
