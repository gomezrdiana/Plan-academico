## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT SET #26 + CLOSE CRAFT (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande titulo:
   "SET #26 — CLOSING THE TURN CLEANLY (6 words)"

2. ESCRIBE las 6 palabras en columna (revelalas una por
   una al pronunciarlas):

   1. LAND      — aterrizar el punto con peso
                  "Let me LAND this: [takeaway]."
   2. WRAP      — cerrar limpio sin colgar
                  "To WRAP — three pillars, one frame."
   3. SEAL      — sellar con autoridad
                  "Let me SEAL: if you keep the frame..."
   4. HAND OFF  — pasar la palabra con intencion
                  "I'll HAND OFF — what would you push on?"
   5. YIELD     — ceder cuando ya cerraste
                  "I'll YIELD — case made; floor is open."
   6. PUNCTUATE — enfasis final que se recuerda
                  "Let me PUNCTUATE: not X — Y."

3. Por palabra: pronuncia 2x -> cohorte coro 2x -> NOMBRA
   definicion + lee ejemplo workplace. ~50 sec cada una
   = ~5 min embebidos en este bloque.

4. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "These six
   verbs ARE the last words. Pick ONE in the next ten
   minutes. Own it. Use it in YOUR close."

5. DI micro-arco del monologue (al board, dibuja 3
   bloques):

   [HOOK 20 sec] -> [3 PILLARS ~1 min cada] -> [CLOSE 15 sec]
      imagen           anchor + example + bridge       Set #26 verb
      anti-thesis                                       (CTA o eco hook)

6. DI: "5-7 min monologue = 3 load-bearing structures.
   HOOK 20 sec + CLOSE 15 sec carry 80% del load. La
   audiencia recuerda los primeros 20 y los ULTIMOS 15.
   Hoy sharpen los 15 — that is the LAST word — that
   is justice."

7. PIDELES (rewrite individual, ~17 min): "Sit. Notebook
   open. Saca tu topic sheet. Rewrite BY HAND el OPENING
   20 sec + CLOSING 15 sec. CLOSE anchor en UN Set #26
   verb. HOOK = imagen/sorpresa, NO thesis. Yo circulo,
   coaches en whisper one-on-one, NO desde el frente."

8. CIRCULA continuamente. Por estudiante, 5-6 min coaching
   one-on-one. Movimientos (usa 2-3 segun lo que escribio):

   - "Read me your hook. Image or thesis? If thesis,
     what's the image underneath?"
   - "Close ends 'thank you' — strip it. LAND if settle.
     PUNCTUATE if contrast."
   - "Close mirrors hook — sharpen the echo: take the
     strongest noun from hook, place it as LAST WORD."
   - "SEAL fits a frame argument. 'Let me seal — if you
     keep the frame...'"
   - "'In conclusion' = rehearsed. WRAP or LAND replaces."

9. [FRASE] insertion 3 (whispered a quien lucha): "Justice
   day 4 — what is owed is the LAST word. First word
   rewritten Cl 35; last word sharpened TODAY."

10. PIDELES (pair rehearsal HOOK + CLOSE, ~22 min):
    "Stand. Pair up. Rehearse ONLY hook (20 sec) + close
    (15 sec) — 3x cada uno. Partner feedback LITERAL:
    'Did close LAND? Y/N. Set #26 verb heard? Y/N. Verb
    was ___.' Binary signal + verb name. No 'good job.'
    If verb not heard -> speaker drills close UNA vez
    mas antes de seguir."

11. ROTA pares cada ~7 min (3 rotaciones). Cada estudiante
    rehearses con cada otro + contigo en una rotacion.

12. [VIRTUD] activation durante rotaciones: si oyes a
    alguien fade al final ("...so yeah...") -> DI suave:
    "Justice. LAND it. Set #26. Punctuate." -> sigue.

13. ANOTA con NOMBRE en TU cuaderno: que Set #26 verb
    escogio cada estudiante + si su CLOSE landed o
    drifted en la rotacion final.

14. CIERRA bloque (~30 sec, todos de pie): "You each
    rehearsed hook + close NINE times. Block 3 next —
    full monologue, 5-7 min FROM MEMORY, hook and close
    you just sharpened sitting inside the whole talk.
    No notes. No paper. Stand where you are. Block 3
    begins as soon as I draw the order."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 25 min y la rewrite individual no progresa, REDUCE pair rehearsal a 2 rotaciones (no 3) — mejor que TODOS rewriten el close que rusheen rehearsal vacio. La rewrite es el material de bloque 3.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** cada estudiante tiene SU Set #26 verb escogido + close rehearsed 6+ veces -> PASA a Block 3.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: FULL MONOLOGUE FROM MEMORY (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "FULL MONOLOGUE FROM MEMORY"

2. DI las reglas (~3 min setup):

   - "FULL 5-7 min monologue FROM MEMORY. No notes. No
     paper. No phone. Anti-fraud — the talk is yours."
   - "Hook + close de bloque 2 sit INSIDE the full talk."
   - "After each monologue: SILENCE 3 sec — let it land."
   - "Peer Q&A: 2 questions B1-frame (real curiosity,
     non-expert phrasing — NOT a B2 trap)."
   - "I — as B1 audience — ask ONE critical question."
   - "Recovery: 30 sec, water, sit. Next up."
   - "I do NOT stop you mid-monologue. If you blank, you
     recover — that recovery is exactly what the Cl 36
     external evaluator will read."

3. ADAPTACION COHORTE:
   - 3 estudiantes: TODOS full (~13 min cada = ~40 min) +
     ~3 min cierre.
   - 5+ estudiantes: 3 full hoy + resto 60-sec PREVIEW
     (hook+close). Cl 35 DRY-RUN cierra pendientes.
     ANOTA en cuaderno quienes hicieron full vs preview.

4. DRAW ORDER al azar (~30 sec — nombres random).
   Cronometro en mano, cap 7 min por monologue.

5. POR CADA ESTUDIANTE FULL (~13 min cada uno):

   (a) MONOLOGUE 5-7 min from memory:
       - Estudiante de pie. Otros anotan Q&A.
       - TU anotas (interno): hook imagen no thesis?
         Set #26 verb landed? "um"/"I think" donde?
         Blank y como recovered? Tiempo: 5-7 ok,
         <4 underprepared, >8 rambling.
       - Stopwatch off al final.

   (b) 3 SEC SILENCIO INTENCIONAL — primera señal del
       close LANDED o no.

   (c) 2 PEER QUESTIONS B1-FRAME (~80 sec cada una):
       Peer 1 + Peer 2 preguntan. Tu observas: Set #25
       (clarify/specify) entry? Set #26 LAND/WRAP close
       o fade?

   (d) 1 TEACHER CRITICAL Q&A (~80 sec):
       Tu como audiencia B1 critica — UNA pregunta
       (slight confusion + push for reason). Ejemplos:
       - "I'm B1, didn't fully get pillar 2 — say it
         again for someone who's never been there. Use
         a Set #25 word."
       - "You said 'X is asked the wrong question' —
         what IS the right question, in ONE sentence?"
       - "Hook strong, close strong, middle rehearsed.
         Which pillar is your weakest? Re-anchor NOW."

       Estudiante responde ~80 sec. Observas: shape-fit?
       LAND con Set #26? Collapse / fluff / defend?

   (e) ~30 SEC RECOVERY: estudiante se sienta. Silencio
       breve. Anota interno: UN gap especifico + UN win
       especifico.

6. [VIRTUD] activation entre monologues: si peer questions
   se ablandan a partir del 2do estudiante -> DI: "Peer
   questions softened. Bring the sharp question now."
   -> sigue.

7. [FRASE] insertion 4 entre monologue 2 y 3: "Justice in
   the close. The room remembers the last line, not the
   words that drifted into silence. Last monologue up —
   listen for the LAST word."

8. CIERRE BLOQUE 3 (~3 min, semicirculo de pie):
   "Each in ONE sentence: what LANDED cleanest in YOUR
   monologue today + what needs more reps before Cl 36."
   3 estudiantes x ~30 sec. NO coaches aqui. Solo
   self-report. Si alguien sustituye un gap comodo por
   el real (patron fake-gap de Cl 33), ANOTA en cuaderno
   — lo nombras en bloque 4, NO ahora.
```

**[ALERTA]** si estudiante usa notas escondidas o saca el celular -> DI: "Stop. Restart from memory. The Final is no notes." -> reset cronometro -> sigue. NO regañes mas alla de 5 segundos.
**[ALERTA]** si monologue cae bajo 4 min (undershoot) -> en bloque 4 anotas con NOMBRE para Cl 35 DRY-RUN.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** todos los que hacian full hoy entregaron + Q&A completo + recovery -> PASA a Block 4.

## BLOCK 4 — CIERRE (~15 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board: "ERRORES DE HOY (anonimos)"

2. ESCRIBE los 3-5 errores tipicos (sin nombres — NOMBRES
   van en TU cuaderno):

   X "Closed with 'and that's it'"
     -> Set #26 LAND with substance. Restate takeaway.
   X "Voice dropped on the last word"
     -> Set #26 PUNCTUATE — voice forward.
   X "...so yeah, that's my project"
     -> Set #26 SEAL with the line that captures the WHY.
   X "Q&A reflex 5+ sec"
     -> 3 sec. #24 enter -> #25 hold -> #26 close.

3. COHORTE lee silencioso (~30 sec). Cada estudiante sabe
   cual es el SUYO porque lo vivio ~20 min atras.

4. [FRASE] close: UN estudiante (al azar) dice la frase
   de memoria + LA PARAFRASEA en sus propias palabras
   para audiencia B1 imaginaria. Aceptas cualquier
   parafraseo que mantenga "what you say LAST is what
   the room takes home."

5. [VIRTUD] cierre: PREGUNTA: "Which close did you LAND
   clean today?" — 2 respuestas maximo, rapido, sin
   discusion.

6. OTRO estudiante: "The Set #26 verb I commit to using
   in the next interaction is ___." Confirma — proceso,
   sin nota.

7. ESCRIBE HOMEWORK en board (mientras la dices):

      HOMEWORK CL 34 — DUE BEFORE CL 35, 7:00 PM
      TIME: 30-45 min (B2). NOT fragmented.

      (1) WRITING (10-12 min, HANDWRITTEN antifraude):
          Final close (15 sec) en 3 versiones con 3
          Set #26 verbs distintos. Marca la mas fuerte
          + WHY en 1 sentence. Trae Cl 35.

      (2) AUDIO eyes closed (15-18 min, 5-7 min audio):
          FULL monologue FROM MEMORY, no script, eyes
          closed para los ULTIMOS 15 SEC (Set #26 LANDS
          sin soporte visual). Label B2-CL34-FROMMEMORY.
          WhatsApp.

      (3) LISTENING (10 min): YouTube TED talk —
          5 CLOSE MOMENTS que LANDED. Sentence literal
          + timestamp + Set #26 verb identificado.
          5 lineas papel.

      RULE: not fragmented. The Final is in ~4h de clase.

8. DI Cl 35 announce: "Cl 35 next — FULL DRY-RUN under
   Final conditions + cohort retrospective + Set #27
   ENTERING WITH AUTHORITY. JUSTICIA day 5 CLOSES the
   block. Cl 36 = FINAL with EXTERNAL EVALUATOR from
   coordinacion — I am NOT the proctor."
```
