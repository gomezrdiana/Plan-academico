## 9. MICRO-DRILL "WHO HAS AN EXAMPLE?" — FIRST HAND UP IN 3 SEC — 20 min — STAND UP / ROTATION

> *"Cl 33 Friday — united class. The single highest-risk moment in any united B1+B2 class is when I — Amina — say 'who has an example?' B1 hesitate. B2 has 3 seconds. If no B2 hand goes up, a B1 fills it weakly OR silence colonizes. Either way you LOST role-model space. Today we kill that moment with drill."*

**Format (~20 min, 6 reps):**

Each rep = ~3 min. Amina says EXACTLY: *"Who has an example?"* Wait 3 seconds, no more. Whoever raises hand first takes ~30 sec with Set #24 entry + B2 answer. Then the OTHER TWO build with Set #24 "building on what she said." Each builds ~30 sec.

The 6 prompts (rotate the topic so no one gets a free turn):
- *"Who has an example of a sentence using 'jump in' naturally?"*
- *"Who has an example of an argument for Colombia's coffee that doesn't sound like a tourism brochure?"*
- *"Who has an example of how to disagree with a teacher politely in B2?"*
- *"Who has an example of a Colombian custom that foreigners misunderstand?"*
- *"Who has an example of a hard Q&A question you fear at the Final?"*
- *"Who has an example of a phrase from Sets #22-#24 you've actually used outside class?"*

**Strict rule: 3 seconds. If no hand up, Amina names: *"Three seconds gone. Restart."* Same prompt. No skipping forward. The whole point is killing the wait reflex.**

**Tracking (internal notebook):** for each rep, write the NAME of the student who raised hand first, AND the names of who built after. Pattern read at the end:
- If one student dominated all 6 first-up: Cl 33 Friday strategy is to PRIME the other two pre-class — quick word with them privately to take floor on the first 2 prompts.
- If rotation natural: Cl 33 Friday strategy is just to trust the drill held.
- If no one took first across multiple reps: extra reps + explicit assignment of WHO goes first on Friday.

Close (~1 min): *"This is the muscle. Friday in united class, when I say 'who has an example?', you don't have to think. The hand goes up in 3 seconds because we drilled it 6 times today. The 'B1 fills with weak English' moment dies because YOU filled first with B2."*
