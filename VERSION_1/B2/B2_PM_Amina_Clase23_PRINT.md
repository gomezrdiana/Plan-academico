> *"Pillar two of your monologue, ~1 minute, from memory, no paper. You will say it many times today — to yourself, to a partner, to the group — until your mouth knows it. ### Initial refresh chaining HOOK + PILLAR 1 + start PILLAR 2 (3 min)

- All standing, in circle.
- You say: *"In ~1 min each, no paper: HOOK → PILLAR 1 → the first sentence of PILLAR 2 (the empalme). 2 students only, at random."*
- 2 students AT RANDOM chain HOOK + P1 + the first sentence of P2 (this last one can be tentative — first worked today). Rest observe the EMPALME.
- *"That's the bridge we are building today. From here on, pillar two is the work."*

### (A) GUIDED MEMORIZATION (10 min) — SIT

- Each student gets her FULL script (HOOK + 3 PILLARS + CTA).
- 3 min: silent reading of PILLAR 2 only, 3 times.
- 3 min: in absolute silence, mark by hand: (1) the CONNECTOR joining P1; (2) the 2-3 internal ideas of P2; (3) 1 Set #16 word she will use; (4) the final line that closes P2.
- 4 min: close the script. *"Script closed for the rest of this block. Whisper-rehearse PILLAR 2 in your seat 3 times, no paper, eyes closed if it helps."*

### (B) INDIVIDUAL REHEARSAL — low voice (10 min) — SIT / STAND BY CHOICE

- 10 min of individual rehearsal at low voice, 5 reps minimum, NO PAPER.
- You walk: if you see anyone with script open in hand → *"Paper face down. Restart this rep from the empalme."*
- At 5 min: *"Two more rep cycles. By the end of this block your pillar two should feel like a 1-minute piece that begins, develops, and lands, not 1 sentence and silence."*

### (C) PAIRS (10 min) — STAND UP IN PAIRS, not at table

- Assign 2 pairs + 1 trio (if N=5).
- Partner A says PILLAR 2 to Partner B (~1 min). B listens without interrupting.
- B gives feedback in 30 sec, using ONLY these 3 questions:
  - *"Did the empalme from pillar one land or was it cold?"*
  - *"Did pillar two sound different from pillar one or like a remix?"*
  - *"Did you hear 1 SET #16 word used (not crammed)?"*
- Swap. Repeat 2 times (each says P2 ~2 times).
- (In the trio: A → B (feedback), B → C (feedback), C → A (feedback), 2 rounds).
- You circulate, DO NOT interrupt a rep in progress. Note in your notebook: name + 1 thing you heard in the empalme.

### (D) MINI-AUDIENCE — PILLAR 2 solo (12 min) — 1 STANDING / 4 SITTING OBSERVING

- Per student (~1 min + 30 sec transition):
  - Student STANDING at front, no paper.
  - Says PILLAR 2 (~1 min). Group listens in silence, hands down.
  - At close: you say *"Next."* — NO Q&A or feedback here (reserved for 2nd pass in block 11). This is DENSITY, not dialogue.
- 5 students × ~1 min + transitions = ~7-8 min.
