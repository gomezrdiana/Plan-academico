## 8. 🆕 DISCOURSE MARKERS + INTONATION — LAYER 5 (19:21–19:38, 17 min) — SENTADAS + voz alta

**OBJETIVO PARA LA ESTUDIANTE:**
> *"This is the layer that makes you sound like a B2 SPEAKER, not a B2 student. Two things: HOW you connect ideas (discourse markers) + the MUSIC of your voice (intonation)."*

### Parte A — Discourse Markers (8 min)

**Tablero:**

```
DISCOURSE MARKERS (diferentes de connectors — estos manejan
el FLUJO del discurso completo, no solo una oración)

OPENING:    "To begin with..." / "First and foremost..."
ADDING:     "What's more..." / "On top of that..."
CONTRASTING:"Having said that..." / "That being said..." / "Then again..."
GENERALIZING:"By and large..." / "On the whole..." / "More often than not..."
CONCEDING:  "Admittedly..." / "Granted..." / "To be fair..."
CONCLUDING: "All things considered..." / "At the end of the day..." / "Ultimately..."
BLUNT:      "To put it bluntly..." / "Let's be honest..."
```

Drill (4 min): tú das una función (opening / contrasting / concluding), cohorte da 1 discourse marker en coro. Luego 2 estudiantes encadenan 3 ideas usando 3 discourse markers distintos.

### Parte B — Intonation (9 min)

**Tablero:**

```
INTONATION — la música de la voz

1. RISING ↗ = question, uncertainty, list continues
   "Are you sure?↗"  "We have coffee,↗ tea,↗ and water.↘"

2. FALLING ↘ = statement, certainty, list ends
   "This is final.↘"

3. CONTRASTIVE STRESS = emphasize the word that matters
   "I didn't say SHE did it." (vs "I didn't SAY she did it.")

4. PAUSE for weight
   "The answer is... [pause] ...not what you expect."
```

**Drill (5 min):**
- (2 min) Tú dices una oración plana. Cohorte la repite CON intonación correcta.
- (3 min) Parejas: cada una dice 3 oraciones de SU tema aplicando: 1 con rising, 1 con falling, 1 con contrastive stress.

> Inserción #2 Frase del Día con intonación exagerada: *"Having said that ↗ ... the evidence is, by and large ↘ , far more NUANCED than it appears."*
