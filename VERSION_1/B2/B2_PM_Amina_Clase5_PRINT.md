## 6. VOCABULARIO B2-TIER PARA BODY DEL MONÓLOGO (20:00–20:15, 15 min)

**Setup (2 min):** "The HOOK and CLOSE you already have phrases for. Today: phrases for the BODY (the 3 points). 10 phrases that connect ideas like a B2."

```
BODY PHRASES (B2-tier connectors):

To list points:
1. "First, ..."
2. "Building on that, ..."
3. "Equally important, ..."

To give examples:
4. "For instance, ..."
5. "Take, for example, ..."

To contrast:
6. "On the other hand, ..."
7. "However, what's interesting is ..."

To emphasize:
8. "What's worth highlighting is ..."
9. "The crucial point here is ..."

To transition:
10. "This brings me to my next point: ..."
```

**Práctica (12 min):** Cada estudiante elige 3 connectors y los integra en su esqueleto. Lo dice en voz alta a su compañera. Cambian.

> +1 EP a quien use 3 connectors B2-tier en una sola frase.
