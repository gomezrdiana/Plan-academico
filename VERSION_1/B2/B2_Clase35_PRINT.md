## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT SET #27 + DRY-RUN SETUP (~35 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande titulo:
   "SET #27 — ENTERING WITH AUTHORITY (6 words)"

2. ESCRIBE las 6 palabras en columna (revelalas una por
   una al pronunciarlas):

   1. OPEN     — abrir limpio sin pedir permiso
                 "OPEN — three pillars on Why Colombia."
   2. STEP IN  — entrar al espacio fisicamente
                 "STEP IN — let me start where it matters."
   3. CLAIM    — reclamar el piso con derecho
                 "I CLAIM ten minutes for this case."
   4. ASSERT   — afirmar la tesis sin disculpa
                 "I ASSERT — Colombia is the bet."
   5. PROJECT  — proyectar la voz al fondo del cuarto
                 "Let me PROJECT this so the back hears."
   6. COMMAND  — comandar atencion con peso
                 "COMMAND your eyes for sixty seconds."

3. Por palabra: pronuncia 2x -> cohorte coro 2x -> NOMBRA
   definicion + lee ejemplo workplace. ~45 sec cada una
   = ~5 min embebidos.

4. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "These six
   verbs ARE the first words. The first word the evaluator
   hears tomorrow. Set #21-26 carried you through the
   middle. Set #27 carries the OPENING. Pick ONE — own it."

5. PIDELES: "Each one, ONE sentence using ONE word of
   Set #27 as YOUR opening for the Final tomorrow. Go."
   ESCUCHA. CORRIGE si la palabra se usa fuera de su
   sentido (ej. "OPEN the door" — no — "OPEN the case").

6. ESCRIBE DRY-RUN RULES en board (grande, numeradas):

      DRY-RUN RULES
      (1) 5-7 min monologue FROM MEMORY
          (no notes, no paper, no phone)
      (2) OPEN with AUTHORITY (Set #27 first word)
      (3) 2-3 min Q&A from teacher (evaluator-simulated)
      (4) NO coaching during, NO peer interruption
      (5) Cronometro audible — signal 5 min, 6 min,
          7 min cut

7. DI: "Same rules as the Final. I take notes silently.
   No coaching. No rescue. If you blank, you recover —
   that recovery is exactly what the external evaluator
   will read tomorrow."

8. PIDELES (first-word drilling en pares, ~12 min):
   "Stand. Pair up. Drill THE FIRST 3 WORDS of your
   monologue — 5 reps each. Partner gives feedback on
   AUTHORITY: (a) voice forward Y/N, (b) posture
   anchored Y/N, (c) eye contact held Y/N. Binary signal.
   No 'good job.'"

9. CIRCULA. CORRIGE postura y volumen cuando flagee.
   Movimientos (usa segun lo que oyes):

   - "Set #27 first word — say it. Don't announce it."
   - "Voice projects forward. Drop the chest, lift the
     air."
   - "Eyes on the evaluator, not the floor. Hold 3 sec."
   - "STEP IN means physically — feet move. Show me."

10. DI: "Now the room. No more rehearsal. Block 3 is the
    dry-run."
```

**[ALERTA]** si pasa de 35 min -> SALTA "first-word drilling" round 5 y entra al dry-run. El dry-run es el corazon de la sesion.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** cada estudiante ha dicho 1 sentence con Set #27 + practico primeras 3 palabras al menos 3 veces -> PASA a Block 3.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: FULL DRY-RUN (~55 min)

```
1. SETUP (~3 min):
   - DRAW order al azar (sobre con nombres o numero
     random).
   - DI: "Same rules as the Final. I take notes silently.
     No coaching. No rescue. Cronometro signals at 5, 6,
     7 cut. After the monologue, 3 sec silence, then 2-3
     min Q&A from me as evaluator. Then 30 sec recovery.
     Next student steps up."

2. [FRASE] insertion 3: SEÑALA la frase antes del primer
   turno. DI: "The dry-run is the form's rehearsal.
   Open clean, step in, claim the floor."

3. POR CADA ESTUDIANTE (~10-12 min):

   (a) ENTRADA (~1 min): estudiante de pie al frente.
       Respira. Empieza cuando este listo. Cronometro
       arranca al PRIMER WORD.

   (b) FULL MONOLOGUE 5-7 min from memory:
       - TU anotas (interno, silencio total): Set #27
         opening landed? hook claro? 3 pillars? Set #26
         close clean? Blanks y como recovered? Tiempo:
         5-7 ok, <4 underprepared, >8 rambling.
       - NO coaches durante. NO peer interrupcion.

   (c) 2-3 MIN Q&A teacher-as-evaluator:
       - UNA pregunta directa + UNA pregunta de
       seguimiento. Ejemplos:
         - "You said 'X is the bet' — defend that with one
           data point."
         - "Pillar 2 — what is the ONE risk you did NOT
           mention?"
         - "If the evaluator pushed back on this, what is
           your one-sentence reply?"
       - Estudiante responde. Observas: Set #25 (clarify/
         specify) entry? Set #26 (LAND/WRAP/SEAL) close?
         Collapse / fluff / defend?

   (d) ~1 MIN CUT + RECOVERY: DI: "Thank you." Estudiante
       se sienta. 30 sec silencio. Anota interno: 1 win
       + 1 sharpen (literal, breve — feed para bloque 4).

4. ADAPTACION COHORTE:
   - 3 estudiantes: TODOS full dry-run (~12 min cada =
     ~36 min) + setup 3 min + cierre Block 3 ~3 min.
   - 5+ estudiantes: 3 full dry-run + resto 90-sec
     PREVIEW (only first 3 words + last 15 sec close,
     NO middle, NO Q&A pace-Final). FLAG a coordinacion
     los que NO hicieron full — necesitan extra prep
     antes del Final.

5. CIERRE BLOQUE 3 (~2-3 min, semicirculo de pie):
   "Each in ONE word: how did the dry-run FEEL?" 1 word
   por estudiante. NO discusion. NO coaches aqui.
```

**[ALERTA]** si estudiante se traba mid-monologue: NO RESCATAR. Si para 10+ seg, DI: "Take a breath. Continue from where you remember." Si 30+ seg stuck, DI: "Sit. We continue tomorrow." No drama.
**[ALERTA]** si estudiante usa notas escondidas o saca el celular -> DI: "Stop. Sit. The Final is no notes." -> sigue con el siguiente. NO regañes mas alla de 5 segundos.
**[ALERTA]** cohorte 5+: pick 3 students who need it most for full + resto 90-sec preview (first-words + last-15-sec close ONLY). Flag a coordinacion los que NO hicieron full.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** cada estudiante completo al menos una vez la entrada-monologue-Q&A bajo cronometro -> PASA a Block 4.

## BLOCK 4 — CIERRE (~20 min)

```
1. FEEDBACK ESTRUCTURADO (~5 min):
   Por estudiante: 1 cosa que landed clean + 1 cosa
   para pulir en las proximas 24h. Sin debate. Sin
   defensa. Estudiante anota en cuaderno personal.
   Ejemplos del wording:

   - "Landed clean: opening with COMMAND — voice
     forward. Sharpen: pillar 2 — anchor with one
     concrete number."
   - "Landed clean: Q&A 3-sec entry. Sharpen: close
     fade — Set #26 PUNCTUATE, voice up on last word."

2. [FRASE] close (~2 min): UN estudiante (al azar) dice
   la frase de memoria + LA PARAFRASEA en sus propias
   palabras. [FRASE] insertion 4. Aceptas cualquier
   parafraseo que mantenga "the dry-run rehearsed,
   the Final delivered."

3. COHORT RETROSPECTIVA — CIERRA BLOQUE JUSTICIA (~5 min):
   DI: "One word that captures JUSTICIA esta semana.
   30 seg cada uno. SIN discusion, SIN comentarios.
   Just the word + one short sentence why. Anotenlo
   en cuaderno personal."

   Cada estudiante: 1 palabra + 1 frase. Tu anotas
   las 5-6 palabras en TU cuaderno (no en board).
   NO comentarios entre turnos. NO devolucion grupal.

4. DESPEDIDA SOBRIA + CL 36 ANNOUNCE (~5 min):

   DI con tono firme, sin emocion:
   - "Today is the last time we see each other as a
     cohort with me. Cl 36 — the Final — is administered
     by an external evaluator from coordinacion. I am
     NOT the proctor. The Final is your room, not mine."
   - "Tonight: sleep. Tomorrow: arrive 10 min early,
     water, pen, ID. Open with authority. Deliver what
     you've practiced."
   - "Coordination contacts each of you with results.
     I do not communicate criteria, rubric, or scores."

5. PRE-FINAL ROUTINE (~3 min, NO homework tradicional):
   DI: "No homework tonight. Personal pre-Final routine:
   sleep + breathing + arrive ready. Do NOT rehearse
   the monologue past 9 PM — over-rehearsal kills
   freshness. Trust the reps. That is the rule."
```

**[ALERTA]** tono sobrio, NO emotivo, NO terapeutico. Diana feedback firme: no psychology. Si alguien pide "consejo personal", DI: "Sleep, water, arrive ready. That is the consejo." No mas.
**[ALERTA]** NO comuniques nada sobre evaluacion del Final. Solo: "El evaluador externo de coordinacion aplica Cl 36 — yo no soy el proctor. Coordinacion comunica resultados." Coordinacion maneja criterios + comunicacion + rubrica.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** retrospectiva completada + Cl 36 anunciado 3 veces (opener + setup dry-run + cierre) + cohorte sale con la rutina personal clara.

## 5 [ERROR TIPICO] OBSERVABLES EN BLOQUE 3

```
   X "Today I'm going to talk about..."
     -> Set #27. CLAIM the room. Don't ANNOUNCE — START.
        Real fix: replace with one of OPEN/ASSERT/COMMAND
        used as a verb to the audience, not as preface.

   X Mid-monologue stops (10+ sec silence)
     -> Silent prompt at 10 sec: "Take a breath.
        Continue from where you remember." No rescue.
        At 30 sec stuck: "Sit. We continue tomorrow."
        No drama. Anotas en cuaderno + flag coordinacion.

   X Q&A reflex 5+ sec
     -> "3 seconds. #24 enter, #25 hold, #26 close.
        The chain." Hour-glass timing — first reply in
        3 sec or the evaluator reads hesitation as
        absence of position.

   X Voice fades at end ("...so yeah...", "...that's it...")
     -> "Set #26 SEAL. Voice forward on the last word."
        Real fix: the LAST word carries air. If voice
        falls, the close is lost. Drill the close-line
        twice with voice rising, not falling.

   X Eyes on floor / hands during monologue
     -> "Eyes on the room. Address the audience."
        Physical reset: feet planted, hands relaxed at
        sides, eyes scan three points (left / center /
        right) every 20 sec. STEP IN = body too, not
        only voice.
```

## ANEXO TECNICO — SIMULACION EVALUADOR EXTERNO

```
El Q&A de bloque 3 simula al evaluador externo de Cl 36.
Reglas para el profesor regular al hacer la pregunta:

  - UNA pregunta directa + UNA pregunta de seguimiento.
    No tres, no cuatro. El Final es 2-3 min Q&A maximo.

  - Tone: NEUTRAL, no calido. El evaluador externo no
    conoce al estudiante. No sonrisas excesivas, no
    "muy bien." Solo la pregunta + escucha + cut.

  - Tipos de pregunta (rota — no uses dos del mismo
    tipo en la misma sesion):
      (a) Defensa de tesis: "Defend that with one
          data point."
      (b) Riesgo no mencionado: "What is the ONE risk
          you did NOT mention?"
      (c) Reformulacion B1: "Say pillar 2 again for
          someone who has never been to Colombia."
      (d) Contra-argumento: "If the evaluator pushed
          back on this, what is your one-sentence reply?"
      (e) Comparativa: "Why Colombia and not [country]?
          One sentence."

  - Cut en 2-3 min total. NO sigas conversacion.
    DI: "Thank you." Estudiante se sienta.
```
