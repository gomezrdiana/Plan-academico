## BLOQUE 2 — REPORTED SPEECH ON PAPER (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone reports say/tell/ask sentences with the inner verb correctly stepped back.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M8, Guides 2-3):
```
REPORTED SPEECH (B1 Book M8, p.83-96)
SAY  = no person needed ("to" optional):  He SAID (that) he was busy.
TELL = needs a person (no "to"):          He TOLD me (that) he was busy.
ASK  = needs a person / IF-WHETHER:       She ASKED me IF I was busy.
BACK-SHIFT (p.93): "the control verb moves one step into the
past -> all dependent verbs move one step into the past."
  is/are   -> was/were        will -> would
  plays    -> played          can  -> could
  played / has played -> had played
  is playing -> was playing   must -> had to
BOOK (p.85): "Daniella said that she was happy /
 told me where she lives / asked me if I was sick."
```
2. Señala: *"You already stepped verbs back for the third conditional. Same move — one foot into the past."*
3. **Conveyor chain (10', DE PIE, circulo):** tu lanzas una frase directa real de oficina ("The client says the invoice is wrong"); estudiante 1 la reporta con SAID + back-shift, 2 la vuelve con TOLD + persona, 3 la vuelve pregunta con ASKED IF ("She asked if the invoice was wrong"), 4 lanza la siguiente. Velocidad creciente.
4. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):**
   - "He said me..." -> "He TOLD me..." / "He SAID (that)..."
   - "She told that..." -> "She told ME that..." (TELL needs a person)
   - "He said he IS busy" -> "he WAS busy" (back-shift)
   - "asked that I was free" -> "asked IF I was free" (yes/no -> IF)
5. **Zone walk (12', DE PIE):** board `SAY | TELL | ASK-IF | BACK-SHIFT`. Tu cantas una frase directa; parejas caminan a la zona del reporting verb correcto Y producen la version reportada con el verbo atras. Si zona o forma mal, el grupo dice "again" y re-caminan. 12 llamados.
6. Cierre del bloque: uso libre — cada uno reporta 2 cosas verdaderas que le dijeron hoy (1 con TELL + persona, 1 con ASK IF).

## ANEXO — VERBATIM DEL LIBRO + PLAN B (no leer literal en clase)

**M8 (p.83-96) — reglas y ejemplos citados verbatim:**
- **Guide 2 (p.89):** SAY (no persona; "to" opcional). TELL (persona requerida, sin preposicion). ASK (persona; si/no con IF/WHETHER). Libro p.85: *"Daniella said that she was happy / Daniella told me where she lives / Daniella asked me if I was sick."*
- **Guide 3 (p.93):** *"When the control verb moves one step into the past, all dependent verbs also move one step into the past."* Ejemplo del libro: *"Mark: 'I am happy.' -> Mark said that he was happy."* Tabla back-shift: is->was, plays->played, played/has played->had played, is playing->was playing, will->would, can->could, must->had to.
- El libro cubre back-shift cuando el reporting verb esta en pasado; NO extender hoy a reglas de tiempo/lugar mas alla de now->then / today->that day / here->there (que ya salen de los ejemplos).

**PLAN B por bloque:**
- B1: si flojea la deduccion, drilea solo 3 back-shifts (is->was, will->would, can->could) de pie antes de M8.
- B2: si la conveyor chain se atasca, baja la velocidad y haz solo SAY->TELL->ASK IF con una sola frase base; luego acelera.
- B3: grupo <=4 -> 2 rotaciones + mas cadena a velocidad; el WITNESS puede leer 4 citas en vez de 6.
- B4: si falta tiempo, el ticket baja a 3 frases (1 por reporting verb), pero SIEMPRE se recoge.
