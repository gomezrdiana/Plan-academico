## BLOQUE 2 — EL PRONOMBRE POSESIVO (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds whose-questions and possessive-pronoun answers on paper — no "my" before "mine".**

1. Board — cita del libro (M39, Guia 1 p.319-320):
```
POSSESSIVE PRONOUNS AS OBJECTS (A2 Book M39, p.318-320)
Whose ...? = "de quien...?"  (whose, NOT who's)
  Mío=Mine · Tuyo/Suyo=Yours · de él=His · de ella=Hers
  Nuestro=Ours · de ellos=Theirs
Apostrofe 's = de:  "Peter's dog" -> "It's Peter's."
  varios dueños: "my brothers' dog" -> "It's my brothers'."
BOOK EXAMPLES (p.320):
  "Whose keys are those?"        "They're mine."
  "Whose car is this?"           "It's Frank's."
  "Is this Sarah's marker?"      "Yes, it's hers."
  "Is this Marie and Celeste's house?"  "Yes, it's theirs."
  "Whose hair is longer?"        "Mine is."  (subject use)
  "Whose father is taller?"      "Sam's is."
```
2. **Meta de tarea comunicativa (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, this board holds 12 real exchanges — 6 whose-questions and 6 answers with a possessive pronoun or 's."*
3. **Preparacion en parejas (7', DE PIE junto al board):** cada pareja arma 2 preguntas "Whose ___ is this/are these?" + respuestas con mine/yours/his/hers/ours/theirs, reales de su trabajo.
4. **Ejecucion (12'):** cada pareja ESCRIBE su intercambio en el board, lo dice, y el grupo verifica: ¿"mine" (no "my")? ¿"whose" (no "who's")? ¿"'s" bien puesto? Meta: 12+ limpias.
5. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):**

   - "the report of Peter" -> "Peter's report / it's Peter's"
