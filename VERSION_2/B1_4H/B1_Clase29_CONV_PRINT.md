## BLOQUE 3 — THE EVENT COORDINATION CALL (35')

Turn on the sound system before they arrive. Look up the guest list and print it. Take the old banners down."*); otro estudiante rota como el asistente que confirma y pregunta (*"So I pick him up at 8? Should I turn them off after?"*); el resto = **OBSERVERS ACTIVOS** (2 errores, 1 acierto, 1 pregunta). Tu = COACH desde afuera — NUNCA juegas guest.
