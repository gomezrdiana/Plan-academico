## BLOQUE 2 — LAS TRES FORMAS EN PAPEL (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone forms affirmative, negative and question with correct -s, fast and clean.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M8-M9, p.99-113):
```
THE SIMPLE PRESENT (A1 Book M8 p.99-102 / M9 p.111-113)
Uso: acciones habituales, cosas que no cambian.
Auxiliar:  I / you / we / they -> DO      he / she / it -> DOES
AFFIRMATIVE  (se OMITE el auxiliar, M9):
   I / you / we / they + VERB        "I work at 8."
   he / she / it + VERB-S            "She works at 9."  (la -s corre al verbo)
NEGATIVE:  don't / doesn't + BASE    "I don't work Sundays." / "She doesn't work weekends."
QUESTION:  Do / Does + subj + BASE?  "Do you work Mondays?" / "Does she work Mondays?"
have -> HAS (M17 p.210):  "He HAS a car."  (nunca "haves")
Sonido -s/-es (M9): kisses/washes/watches = "-ez"; eats = "-s"; plays = "-z".
```
2. Señala la logica: *"Only he/she/it takes the -s. In the affirmative the -s runs onto the verb. In the negative and the question the auxiliary DOESN'T/DOES carries it — the main verb stays BASE. Two common errors: 'he doesn't works' and 'does she works' — both double the mark. Never double it."*
3. **Drill en circulo (10', DE PIE):** cadena con contenido real de trabajo: tu lanzas sujeto + verbo ("she / check the report") -> estudiante 1 afirmativa, 2 negativa, 3 pregunta, 4 respuesta corta ("Yes, she does."); el 5 lanza el siguiente sujeto+verbo. Velocidad creciente.
4. **Errores tipicos al board (coro 2x cada correccion):**
   - "she work" -> "she WORKS" (3a persona, -s)
   - "he doesn't works" -> "he doesn't WORK" (auxiliar ya lleva la marca)
   - "does she works?" -> "DOES she WORK?" (verbo base tras does)
   - "he don't" -> "he DOESN'T"
5. **Zone walk (12', DE PIE):** tablero dividido `AFFIRMATIVE | NEGATIVE | QUESTION`. Tu dices una frase base + nombras una zona ("she checks email" -> QUESTION); las parejas CAMINAN a la zona y PRODUCEN la frase transformada en voz alta ("Does she check email?"). El que llega, justifica en 1 linea la regla.
6. Cierre del bloque: produccion libre de pie — cada uno: 2 frases verdaderas sobre su rutina (1 afirmativa con -s de un colega, 1 pregunta). El drill nunca cierra el bloque — cierra el uso libre.

```
"Los auxiliares do/does tiene una propiedad especial: en el caso positivo
 (NUNCA en el negativo o de la pregunta) generalmente omitimos el do/does.
```
