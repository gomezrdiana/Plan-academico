## BLOQUE 2 — POSSESSIVE PRONOUNS + PARTICIPLE ADJECTIVES (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds ownership and feeling sentences on paper — mine/whose and -ing/-ed.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M39 + M41, verbatim compacto):
```
OWNERSHIP & FEELINGS (A2 Book M39 + M41)

   mine · yours · his · hers · ours · theirs (se usan solos, sin sustantivo).
   BOOK: "Whose keys are those?" - "They're mine."
         "Whose car is this?" - "It's Frank's."  "Whose hair is longer?" - "Mine is."

         "He is amazing. She is amazed."  (irregular: "We are lost.")
```
2. **Meta de tarea comunicativa (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, the board holds 12 real lines — 6 ownership (mine/whose), 6 feelings (3 -ing, 3 -ed)."*
3. **Preparacion en parejas (7', DE PIE junto al board):** cada pareja arma 2 lineas de posesion (whose + pronombre) y 2 de sentimiento (1 -ing, 1 -ed) reales de su trabajo.
4. **Ejecucion (12'):** cada pareja ESCRIBE sus frases en el board, las dice, y el grupo verifica: ¿pronombre solo (mine, no "my")? ¿-ing para la cosa, -ed para la persona? Meta: 12+ limpias.
5. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):**
   - "it's my" (solo) -> "it's mine"
   - "I am boring" (queriendo decir aburrido) -> "I am bored"
   - "the class was tired" -> "the class was tiring"

6. **Producto verificable:** lectura coral del board; feedback primero sobre el logro, luego la forma.
