## BLOQUE 2 — EL SUPERLATIVO + LOS INTENSIFICADORES (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds superlatives on paper (the -est / the most / the best-worst) and one honest "how X?" answer.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M28 Guia 1 p.254 + M29 Guia 1 p.262):
```
SUPERLATIVE (A2 Book M28, p.253-254)
Regla: "X es el/la MAS (descriptor)."
  a. 1 silaba: "the" + "-est"   -> "I am the oldest."
  b. 2 silabas en -y: "-iest"   -> "I am the prettiest."
  c. 2+ silabas: "the most ..." -> "I am the most intelligent."
  d. Irregulares: good -> THE BEST ; bad -> THE WORST
BOOK EXAMPLES (p.254):
  "Donald is the richest."  "The cat is the ugliest."
  "My father is the most serious."  "Diana is the nicest."

INTENSIFIERS (A2 Book M29, p.262-263)
"How + descriptor?" = "¿Que tan ___?"  -> "How tall are you?"
  really / very / pretty / kinda / sorta / not very
BOOK EXAMPLES (p.263):
  "How new is your car? — It's pretty new."
  "How old is your dad? — He's not very old. He's 38."
  "How intelligent is Sally? — She's kinda intelligent."
```
2. **Meta de tarea comunicativa (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, this board holds 8 real superlatives — short with -est, long with the most, plus the best/worst — and 4 honest 'how X?' answers."*
3. **Preparacion en parejas (7', DE PIE junto al board):** cada pareja arma 1 superlativo corto (the -est), 1 largo (the most), 1 irregular (the best/worst) — reales de su trabajo — + 1 pregunta "How X is it?" con respuesta honesta.
4. **Ejecucion (12'):** cada pareja ESCRIBE sus frases en el board, las dice, y el grupo verifica: ¿-est o the most segun silabas? ¿"the" presente? ¿"how + adj" sin "much"? Meta: 8 superlativos + 4 intensificadores limpios.
5. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):**
   - "the most best" -> "the best" (irregular, sin doble)
   - "the intelligentest" -> "the most intelligent" (largo = the most)
   - "the goodest / the baddest" -> "the best / the worst"
   - "how much good is it?" -> "how good is it?" (how + adj, sin much)
6. **Producto verificable:** lectura coral del board; feedback primero sobre el logro, luego la forma.
