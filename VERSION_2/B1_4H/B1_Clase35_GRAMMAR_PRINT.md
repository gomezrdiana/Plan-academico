## BLOQUE 2 — HOW MUCH? + PRICES (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds "how much?" (uncountable) and price sentences on paper.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M33 Guia 1 p.284 + M34 Guia 1 p.293-294):
```
UNCOUNTABLE + PRICES (A2 Book M33 p.283-284 / M34 p.293-294)
M33: "How much + uncountable?" = "¿Cuanto/a ___?"
  "How much milk do you have?"  "How much milk is there?"
  "I have 1 liter of milk."  "I have 300 pesos." (plata=incontable)
  a lot of / lots of = mucho ; a bit of = un poco de
M34 PRICES: two ways to ask/give a price —
  a) COST: "How much does it cost? — It costs $5."
           "How much do they cost? — They cost $5."
  b) BE:   "How much is it? — It's $5."
           "How much are they? — They're $5."
  "each" = cada uno: "The markers cost $2 each."
```
2. **Meta de tarea comunicativa (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, this board holds 6 'how much?' (uncountable) exchanges + 6 fair prices — with cost, is, and each."*

4. **Ejecucion (12'):** cada pareja ESCRIBE sus frases en el board, las dice, y el grupo verifica: ¿"how much" (no "many")? ¿"it costs $" con -s? ¿"a bit OF / a lot OF"? Meta: 12+ limpias.
5. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):**

   - "how much costs it?" -> "how much does it cost?"
