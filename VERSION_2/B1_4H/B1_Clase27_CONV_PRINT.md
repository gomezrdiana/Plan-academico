## BLOQUE 1 — APERTURA (20')

**Objective: reactivate the passive process language and the past narrative chain, then aim at double verbs.**

1. **Recuperacion (10', oral, DE PIE, cadena, sin cuaderno).** Disparadores (Cl 24 y Cl 20); errores anonimos al board; 1 min re-produccion:
   - "Say one work process in 3 passive steps: 'the order is received, it is checked, it is sent.'" (Cl 24)
   - "Fix it: 'The report receives every week.'" (Cl 24)
   - "Chain a mini past story: used to / was -ing / when + past simple / had + participle." (Cl 20)
   - "Which form? 'By then she had already signed.'" (Cl 20)
   - "First / then / after that / finally — order a real task, all past." (Cl 20/24)
2. **Chequeo publico de portafolio (3'):** conteo en voz alta del audio de ayer; no-envios a tu cuaderno.
3. **Frase del Dia (4').** Board antes; 2 lecturas + coro + 1 solo; 3+ inserciones. Señala: *"Hear the two shapes? I want TO grow (not started yet). I like learnING (already started). Same idea, two forms — that's today."*
4. **Revision tarea (3'):** "My work day in routines" (8 lineas, preposicion + -ing de Cl 26) sobre la mesa; recoge. **PASE de Grammar (Cl 26)** leido: hoy giramos a lo que se quiere / gusta / decide hacer.

## BLOQUE 2 — WHAT I LOVE, WANT, AND STOPPED DOING (40')

**Objective: everyone has stated three real work choices — one they want to start, one they love doing, one they decided to stop.**

1. Meta en 1 linea (arriba). **Avance de proyecto hoy: una linea para MY STORY — "what I love doing, and what I decided to stop doing".**
2. Board — el molde oral (Grammar lo formaliza hoy mismo con M22; aqui NO se enseña la regla):
```
TWO VERBS, TWO SHAPES
"I want to learn a new skill this year."   (to + verb)
"I decided to change teams."               (to + verb)
"I love solving problems with my team."    (verb + ing)
"I stopped checking email at night."       (verb + ing)
(want/need/decide/forget = to ... ; like/love/start/stop/finish = -ing)
```
3. Modela 45 seg con 3 elecciones tuyas reales (una "want to", una "love -ing", una "stopped -ing"). Señala el contraste.
4. Preparacion en parejas (7'): cada uno arma sus 3 elecciones reales de trabajo/estudio (notas minimas) + 1 razon.
5. Ejecucion (15'): mini-dialogos en parejas: A dice "I want to..." / "I love -ing..." / "I stopped -ing..."; B responde con curiosidad y su propia eleccion usando el mismo molde ("I decided to... too, but I never stopped -ing"). Cambio de pareja a mitad.
6. Producto verificable (10'): vuelta completa — cada estudiante dice al grupo LA eleccion de la que esta mas orgulloso (20 seg). Tu solo cuentas en el board cuantos moldes limpios salieron (to + verb / verb + ing).
7. Feedback: logro primero, forma despues. Errores 2+ parejas -> board anonimo. Nombres a tu cuaderno.

## BLOQUE 3 — THE CAREER CONVERSATION (35')

**Objective: hold a realistic one-on-one about goals — what to start, keep doing, and stop.**

1. Board: `CAREER CONVERSATION — "WHAT DO YOU WANT TO DO NEXT?" — 3 ROTATIONS`
2. Escenario profesional realista (solo tablero y sala): conversacion de desarrollo con un jefe o mentor. **1 guest = empleado** que expone sus metas con el molde (*"I want to lead a project. I love mentoring new hires. I decided to stop taking on extra shifts."*); otro estudiante rota como el jefe que pregunta (*"What do you want to learn? What should you stop doing?"*); el resto = **OBSERVERS ACTIVOS** (2 errores, 1 acierto, 1 pregunta). Tu = COACH desde afuera — NUNCA juegas guest.
3. 3 rotaciones (~9'). Debrief por TIPOS (anonimo): moldes limpios vs "I want learn" / "I love to solve" (forma cruzada).
4. **Cierre TEMPLANZA (3'):** *"Temperance day two — name one thing you decided to STOP doing at work, and how stopping helped."* — 2 respuestas maximo. *"Knowing when to stop is self-control."*
