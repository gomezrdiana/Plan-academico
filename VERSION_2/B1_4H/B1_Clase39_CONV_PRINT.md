## BLOQUE 2 — BORING vs BORED (40')

**Objective: everyone describes real work situations (-ing) and their own reaction (-ed) correctly.**

1. Meta en 1 linea: *"By the end of this block, each person has described one real work situation and their honest feeling about it, with the right -ing / -ed."*

3. Board — el molde oral (Grammar formaliza hoy con M41; aqui NO se enseña la regla):
```

```
Guia 1 (p.328): el -ing (participio presente) = para quien PRODUCE
