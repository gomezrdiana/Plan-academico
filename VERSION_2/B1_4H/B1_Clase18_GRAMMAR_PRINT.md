## BLOQUE 2 — THE 4-LAYER STORY ON PAPER (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone writes/produces the 4 layers in order, correct forms, no present leak.**

1. Board — el molde de 4 capas:
```
THE STORY MACHINE ON PAPER — one past, no present leak
(1) SCENE      used to + base  /  was/were + -ing
(2) TURNING PT when + past simple
(3) STRUGGLE   past simple + past continuous mixed
(4) SEQUENCE   first / then / after that / while / finally
Anchor (A2 Book M12, p.99-100): "I was working WHEN the client called."
Form check per layer: is the background -ing? is the strike bare past?
                       is used to + bare? no present verb anywhere?
```
2. Señala la logica: *"Each layer has ONE job and ONE form. You already own all four — today they run in a row."*
3. **Story-build sprint (14', DE PIE):** en el board, historia colaborativa — cada pareja agrega UNA clausula en la capa correcta (scene, then turning point, then struggle, then sequence). Apuntas la capa antes de cada aporte. Meta: 18+ builds sin fuga al presente.
4. **Errores tipicos al board (coro 2x cada correccion):**
   - "I was work when he calls" -> "I was WORKING when he CALLED" (mezcla de tiempos y presente)
   - "I used to worked alone" -> "I used to WORK alone" (bare)
   - background en simple donde debia ser -ing / secuencia sin conectores
5. **Zone walk (8', DE PIE):** tablero con las 4 capas como zonas. Dices una linea suelta; las parejas CAMINAN a la capa que le corresponde Y la reescriben con la forma correcta en voz alta.
6. Cierre del bloque: cada uno produce de pie una micro-historia de 4 lineas verdadera. El uso libre cierra el bloque.
