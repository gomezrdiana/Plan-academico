## BLOQUE 2 — TIEMPO Y ORDEN DEL PASADO (40')

**Objective: by the end, everyone anchors past sentences with the right time word and sequences a real story.**

1. Board (uso oral — Grammar formaliza la regla hoy mismo; ejemplos del libro):
```
TIME & ORDER OF THE PAST (A2 Book M12, p.99-102 — verbatim)
Book: "I ate a sandwich AT 8:00." · "Steve woke up AT 6:30 today."
      "I was living in Bucaramanga IN 2019."
AT + clock   ON + day/date   IN + year/month   (no prep: yesterday, this morning)
LAST + period (NO "the", NO "in"):  last week, last Monday
___ AGO (after the period):  two days ago, three weeks ago
POSITION: time word at the END (or front + comma). Never mid-verb.
ORDER: First, / Then, / After that, / Before ..., / Finally,
```
