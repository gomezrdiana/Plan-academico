## BLOQUE 2 — FREQUENCY + A + PERIOD EN PAPEL (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds the expression correctly and places it at the end, fast.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M13, p.165-169):
```
EXPRESSIONS OF FREQUENCY (A1 Book M13, p.165-169)
Forma:  FREQUENCY + A(N) + PERIOD
   once / twice / three times / four times  +  a  +  day / week / month / year
   every + day / week / month / year   (every = "cada")
POSITION: al FIN de la frase (igual que en español).
   "We eat dinner in the restaurant TWICE A WEEK."
   "I swim in the pool EVERY DAY."
   "Miguel works in the office THREE TIMES A YEAR."
```
2. Señala la logica: *"Number + 'a' + period. Not 'to the week' — 'a week'. And it goes at the END. 'Every' means 'cada' — 'every day', no 'a'. Watch: 'every days' is wrong — every + singular."*
3. **Drill en circulo (10', DE PIE):** tu lanzas verbo + numero + periodo ("send the report / 1 / week") -> estudiante produce "I send the report once a week"; el siguiente lanza el proximo. Velocidad creciente.
4. **Errores tipicos al board (coro 2x):**
   - "three times to the week" -> "three times A WEEK"
   - "every days" -> "every DAY"
   - "I twice a week go" -> "I go... TWICE A WEEK" (al final)
   - "I go two times a week" -> "TWICE a week" (natural)
5. **Zone walk (12', DE PIE):** tablero `EVERY + PERIOD | NUMBER + A + PERIOD`. Tu dices una idea ("check email / daily" o "gym / 3x week"); las parejas CAMINAN a la zona correcta Y producen la frase completa con la expresion al final. El que llega justifica.
6. Cierre del bloque: produccion libre de pie — cada uno 2 frases verdaderas de su semana con expresion al final.
