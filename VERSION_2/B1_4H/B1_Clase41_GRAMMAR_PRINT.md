## BLOQUE 2 — ADVERBIOS DE MANERA (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds adverb-of-manner sentences on paper — adverb after the verb/object, irregulars right.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M43, Guia 1 p.342):
```
ADVERBS OF MANNER (A2 Book M43, p.340-345) — responden "how?"
  Regla: adjetivo + "-ly"  (sad->sadly ; happy->happily ->
    "-y" pasa a "-ily" ; careful->carefully)
  Irregulares: fast -> fast (NO 'fastly') · good -> WELL
  Posicion: despues del verbo principal o del objeto directo.
    "I play well." "He plays soccer well." "She writes beautifully."
  Frecuencia + manera: "I always plan carefully."
BOOK EXAMPLES (p.343): "He plays badly." "William studies
  intelligently." "Philip drives fast." "Sebastian worked
  carefully." "Mike speaks English correctly."
```
2. **Meta (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, this board holds 10 sentences describing how you do real tasks — adverb in the right place, well and fast handled correctly."*
3. **Preparacion en parejas (7', DE PIE junto al board):** cada pareja arma 3 frases: 2 con adverbio en "-ly" + 1 con irregular (well/fast/hard), reales de su trabajo.
4. **Ejecucion (10'):** cada pareja ESCRIBE y dice; el grupo verifica: ¿"-ly" bien formado? ¿"well" no "goodly"? ¿adverbio despues del verbo? Meta: 10+ limpias.
5. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):** "I speak good" -> "I speak well" · "goodly / fastly" -> "well / fast" · "I carefully work" -> "I work carefully" · "happyly" -> "happily".
