## BLOQUE 2 — EL COMPARATIVO IGUAL (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds equal-comparison sentences on paper — as ... as, not as ... as, almost as ... as.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M26, Guia 1 p.242-243):
```
EQUAL COMPARATIVES (A2 Book M26, p.241-244)
Regla: para comparar grados usamos "tan ... como" = "as ... as".
  "I am as tall as Carlos."            (igual)
  "I am not as tall as Carlos."        (menos)
  "I am almost as tall as Carlos."     (casi igual)
BOOK EXAMPLES (Ejemplos p.243-244):
  "I'm as nice as Ana."
  "My car isn't as new as your car."
  "Your father is almost as old as my father."
  "Jack is as good as Sam."
  "Dan isn't as bad as Vern."
  "My house isn't as new as your house."
VOCAB (p.241-242): nice · new · old · good · bad ·
  smart / intelligent
```
2. **Meta de tarea comunicativa (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, this board holds 12 real work comparisons — with as ... as, not as ... as, and almost as ... as."*
3. **Preparacion en parejas (7', DE PIE junto al board):** cada pareja arma 2 comparaciones reales (herramientas, turnos, equipos) con "as ... as" y una con "not/almost".
4. **Ejecucion (12'):** cada pareja ESCRIBE sus frases en el board, las dice, y el grupo verifica: ¿los dos "as"? ¿"than" ausente? Meta: 12+ limpias.
5. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):**
   - "as good than the other" -> "as good AS the other" (igualdad usa "as", no "than")
   - "as good like the other" -> "as good AS the other"
   - "as tall as Carlos" con un solo "as" ("tall as Carlos") -> restaurar el primer "as"
6. **Producto verificable:** lectura coral del board; feedback primero sobre el logro, luego la forma.

## BLOQUE 3 — DRILL + THE FAIR REVIEW (35') — DE PIE 100%

**Objective: produce equal comparisons at speed, then hold them in a realistic fair review of two options.**

1. **Cadena de conversion (12', circulo, DE PIE):** tu cantas dos elementos + descriptor ("this laptop / that laptop / fast"); estudiante 1 los iguala ("This laptop is as fast as that one"), 2 lo matiza ("but it's not as light as that one"), 3 lanza el siguiente par. Velocidad creciente; ultimo giro: solo pares reales de SU trabajo.
2. **The fair review (18'):** escenario profesional realista: comparar dos candidatos, planes o herramientas con equidad antes de recomendar. **1 guest = evaluador** que compara con el molde (*"Both options are strong. Is it as fast as the current tool?"*); el resto = **OBSERVERS ACTIVOS** (2 errores, 1 acierto, 1 pregunta). Tu = COACH afuera, nunca guest. 3 rotaciones.
