## BLOQUE 2 — EL COMPARATIVO DESIGUAL (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds unequal-comparison sentences on paper — -er/-ier/more ... than + better/worse.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M27, Guia 1 p.248-249):
```
UNEQUAL COMPARATIVES (A2 Book M27, p.247-249)
Regla: "X es mas (descriptor) que Y" — la forma depende del
       adjetivo. "than" = "que".
  a. 1 silaba: + "-er ... than"
       "I am older than Carlos."
  b. 2 silabas en "-y": "-y" -> "-ier ... than"
       "I am prettier than Anita."
  c. 2+ silabas: "more ... than"
       "I am more intelligent than Carlos."
  d. Irregulares: good -> BETTER than ; bad -> WORSE than
       "I am better than Carlos."  "I am worse than Carlos."
BOOK EXAMPLES (Ejemplos p.249):
  "I'm richer than Donald Duck."
  "Sarah is prettier than Clara."
  "The cat is uglier than the dog."
  "My father is more serious than my mother."
  "Spanish is more complicated than English."
  "Einstein is more intelligent than me."
VOCAB (p.247-248): rich · pretty · ugly · serious ·
  early · late · complicated
```
2. **Meta de tarea comunicativa (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, this board holds 12 real work comparisons — short adjectives with -er, long ones with more, plus better/worse — all with 'than'."*
3. **Preparacion en parejas (7', DE PIE junto al board):** cada pareja arma 1 comparacion con adjetivo corto (-er), 1 con largo (more), 1 con better/worse — reales de su trabajo.
4. **Ejecucion (12'):** cada pareja ESCRIBE sus frases en el board, las dice, y el grupo verifica: ¿-er o more segun la silaba? ¿"than" presente? Meta: 12+ limpias.
5. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):**
   - "more faster" -> "faster" (no doble comparativo)
   - "more intelligenter" -> "more intelligent" (largo = solo "more")
   - "gooder" / "more good" -> "better" (irregular)
   - "smarter that Steve" -> "smarter THAN Steve"
6. **Producto verificable:** lectura coral del board; feedback primero sobre el logro, luego la forma.
