## BLOQUE 2 — COUNTING FAIRLY (40')

**Objective: everyone asks and answers "how many?" about real countable things at work — many, a few, a lot of.**

1. Meta en 1 linea (arriba). **Avance de proyecto hoy: una linea para MY STORY — "how many steps it took: a few big decisions, a lot of small ones."**
2. Board — el molde oral (Grammar lo formaliza hoy mismo con M32; aqui NO se enseña la regla):
```
COUNTING THE COUNTABLE (people, tasks, clients, hours)
"How many people are there in your team? — There are a few."
"How many clients do you have? — I have a lot of clients."
"How many meetings are there today? — There are many."
"We only have a few hours — let's use them fairly."
(how many + countable? ; many / a lot of / lots of ; a few = 3-5)
```
3. Modela 45 seg: pregunta y responde "how many?" sobre cosas reales contables tuyas (people, tasks, hours).
4. Preparacion en parejas (7'): cada uno prepara 3 preguntas "How many ___?" reales de su trabajo + su respuesta honesta (many / a few / a lot of / un numero).
5. Ejecucion (15'): mini-entrevistas en parejas: A pregunta ("How many people report to you?"); B responde con justicia ("A few — three."); cambian roles. Se busca el conteo REAL, no el impresionante. Cambio de pareja a mitad.
6. Producto verificable (10'): vuelta completa — cada estudiante hace al grupo UNA pregunta "How many?" y responde honesto (20 seg). Tu cuentas moldes limpios en el board.
7. Feedback: logro primero (¿el conteo es justo y claro?), forma despues. Errores 2+ parejas -> board anonimo. Nombres a tu cuaderno.
