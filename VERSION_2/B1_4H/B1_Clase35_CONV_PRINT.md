## BLOQUE 2 — PRICING FAIRLY (40')

**Objective: everyone asks "how much?" about uncountables AND states a fair price with cost / it's / each.**

1. Meta en 1 linea (arriba). **Avance de proyecto hoy: una linea para MY STORY — "how much it cost me to get here — and it was worth it."**
2. Board — el molde oral (Grammar lo formaliza hoy mismo con M33+M34; aqui NO se enseña la regla):
```
MEASURING & PRICING FAIRLY (uncountable + price)
"How much money do we have? — A bit of budget left."
"How much time is there? — A lot of time, if we start now."
"How much does the service cost? — It costs $50."
"How much is the plan? — It's $30 a month."
"The licenses cost $12 each — that's fair."
(how much + uncountable? ; a lot of / a bit of ;
 how much does it cost? — it costs $ ; how much is it? — it's $ ; each)
```
3. Modela 45 seg: pregunta "how much?" sobre algo incontable real (money, time, water) y da un precio justo (it costs / it's / each).
4. Preparacion en parejas (7'): cada uno prepara 2 preguntas "How much ___?" reales (dinero, tiempo, material) + 1 precio de algo de su trabajo con "cost" o "is".
5. Ejecucion (15'): mini-negociaciones en parejas: A pregunta el precio ("How much does it cost?"); B responde justo ("It's $40 each — fair for the quality"); A matiza ("That's a bit high, but the time we save is worth it"). Cambio de pareja a mitad.
