## BLOQUE 2 — THE FAIR RECOMMENDATION (40')

**Objective: everyone has recommended one real choice at work using -er/more ... than, with an honest reason.**

1. Meta en 1 linea (arriba). **Avance de proyecto hoy: una linea para MY STORY — the turning point: "I'm better than I was, because..." (the fair comparison that defines my growth).**
2. Board — el molde oral (Grammar lo formaliza hoy mismo con M27; aqui NO se enseña la regla):
```
NAMING THE DIFFERENCE FAIRLY
"The new process is faster than the old one."
"This supplier is more reliable than the last one."
"Working in the morning is better than working at night — for me."
"This option is more complicated, but more honest, than the shortcut."
(short adj + -er + than ; long adj + more + than ; better/worse)
```
3. Modela 45 seg recomendando 1 opcion real tuya con una razon justa. Señala: corto = "-er than"; largo = "more ... than"; irregulares "better/worse".
4. Preparacion en parejas (7'): cada uno elige 1 decision real de su trabajo (dos opciones) + por que una es mejor, con razon honesta (no exagerada).
5. Ejecucion (15'): mini-debates en parejas: A recomienda ("Option A is more efficient than B"); B contrasta justamente ("True, but B is cheaper and almost as fast"). Se busca la recomendacion JUSTA, no ganar. Cambio de pareja a mitad.
6. Producto verificable (10'): vuelta completa — cada estudiante da al grupo UNA recomendacion con "-er/more ... than" + 1 razon honesta (20 seg). Tu cuentas moldes limpios en el board.
7. Feedback: logro primero (¿la recomendacion es justa y clara?), forma despues. Errores 2+ parejas -> board anonimo. Nombres a tu cuaderno.
