## BLOQUE 2 — ROUTINES AND GOALS AROUND A MOMENT (40')

**Objective: everyone describes real work routines (before/after/without + -ing) AND real work goals (want to / love -ing), mixing both.**

1. Meta en 1 linea (arriba). **Avance de proyecto hoy: una linea para MY STORY — "what I always do before a key moment, and what I decided to start doing at work".**
2. Board — el molde oral (Grammar lo formaliza hoy con M21 + M22; aqui NO se enseña la regla):
```

"After finishing a call, I write two notes."     (prep + -ing)
"I want to lead a small project this year."       (want to)

(before/after/without + -ing  ·  want/decide TO ...  ·  love/start/stop ...-ING)
```

3. Modela 45 seg: 1 rutina (prep + -ing) + 1 meta (want to) + 1 cosa que amas/dejaste (love/stop -ing).

5. Ejecucion (15'): en parejas, A cuenta rutina + meta; B escucha con tarea (¿la accion tras la preposicion es -ing? ¿el primer verbo pide "to" o "-ing"?) y RE-CUENTA una de A ("Before leaving, he checks... and he wants to lead..."). Cambio de pareja a mitad.
