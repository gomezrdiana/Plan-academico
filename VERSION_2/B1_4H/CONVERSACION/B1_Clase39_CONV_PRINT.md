## ANEXO — VERBATIM DEL LIBRO + PLAN B (no leer literal en clase)

**Modulo ancla: B1 Book M9 "More About Perfect Tenses" (p.97-116).** Conv lo APLICA oralmente; Grammar lo formaliza hoy. NO enseñar la tabla en Conv.

Reglas del libro (referencia; el molde oral sale de aqui):
- **Present perfect (have/has + participio):** periodo abierto / no completo / muy reciente; con for/since/already/yet/just. "I have lived here since 2014." "I have just finished." "Have you ever tried it?"
- **Past perfect (had + participio):** accion antes de otra pasada. "When we moved, I had learned Spanish." "I had already left when she called."
- **Future perfect (will have + participio):** completado para un limite de tiempo. "I will have finished by 5pm."
- **Perfect continuous (has/have/had/will have + been + V-ing):** duracion de una accion en curso. "I have been working all day."
- **already / yet:** ALREADY -> afirmativo, antes del tiempo referido; YET -> negativo o pregunta, al final. "I haven't finished YET." "Have you sent it YET?"

**PLAN B por bloque:**
- B1: si flojean los modales perfectos, drilea 4 participios en el aire (do->done, send->sent, write->written, finish->finished) antes de M9.
