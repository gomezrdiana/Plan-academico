## BLOQUE 2 — HOW OFTEN, EN VOZ ALTA (40')

**Objective: by the end, everyone describes their week using 5 frequency adverbs in the correct position.**

1. Meta en 1 linea (arriba). **Producto: cada estudiante DICE 8 frases sobre su semana usando los 5 adverbios, cada uno antes del verbo principal.**
2. Board (uso solo oral — Grammar formaliza hoy mismo):
```
HOW OFTEN?  always -> usually -> often -> sometimes -> never
POSITION: right BEFORE the main verb.
   "I ALWAYS confirm by email."   "She USUALLY arrives at 9."
   "He OFTEN calls before lunch."  "We SOMETIMES work Saturdays."
   "They NEVER close on time."
With BE, the adverb goes AFTER:  "She is USUALLY on time."
Question:  "HOW OFTEN do you check email?" -> "I check it every morning."
```
3. Modela 60 seg: tu semana real con los 5 adverbios. Señala: *"The adverb sits right before the main verb — 'I ALWAYS confirm', not 'I confirm always'. Only with BE does it move after — 'she is usually calm'."*
4. Preparacion en parejas (8'): cada uno arma 8 frases (los 5 adverbios + 3 preguntas "How often do you...?").
5. Ejecucion (18'): entrevista de pareja — A pregunta "How often do you...?" (6 preguntas), B responde en frase completa con adverbio; luego cambian. Segunda ronda, nueva pareja, mas fluida.
6. Producto verificable: TODOS respondieron 6 preguntas con adverbio en posicion. Feedback sobre el LOGRO ("could I picture your week?"), luego la forma.
7. Corrige en board errores en 2+ parejas (anonimos). Nombres a tu cuaderno.
