## BLOQUE 2 — EL PASADO REGULAR EN VOZ (40')

**Objective: by the end, everyone speaks past regular with the right spelling family, the right sound and a time marker.**

1. **Texto Heiiu — "First-Time Experiences That Have Shaped Us" (ESTUDIANTE, se reparte) (10').** Parejas leen. Subrayan cada verbo en pasado simple regular ("interviewed, shaped, planned, enrolled, rebuilt-NO"). Cada estudiante da una frase oral: *"One first time that changed me was when I ___ (past regular)."* El texto es exposicion rica; el foco de hoy es el pasado regular.
2. Board (uso oral — Grammar formaliza la tabla hoy mismo):
```
4 SPELLING FAMILIES            3 SOUNDS
work -> worked   (base+ED)     /t/  worked, asked, helped, finished
live -> lived    (E+D)         /d/  lived, played, called, arrived
study-> studied  (cons+Y=IED)  /ɪd/ wanted, needed, started (extra syllable!)
stop -> stopped  (CVC double)  TIME: yesterday · last week · 2 days ago · in 2020
```
3. Modela 60 seg: cuenta 4 cosas reales que hiciste ayer en el trabajo/estudio, una de cada familia, con time marker. Marca el sonido de cada verbo.
4. **Meta del bloque (P6):** *"By the end, each of you tells a partner 5 true things you did yesterday or last week — every sentence with a time marker."* Preparacion en parejas (6', notas minimas), luego cada uno DICE sus 5 frases; la pareja verifica con checklist oral: familia de spelling + sonido + time marker. Cambio de pareja, segunda ronda mas fluida.
5. **Avance MY STORY (P7):** cada uno agrega a su proyecto 2 lineas en pasado — *"a first time that shaped me"* — usando pasado regular.
