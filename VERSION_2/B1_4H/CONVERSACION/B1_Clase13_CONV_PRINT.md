## BLOQUE 2 — PREGUNTAR Y NEGAR EL PASADO (40')

**Objective: by the end, everyone asks and answers about the past and turns statements into clean negatives.**

1. **Texto Heiiu — "What Time Has Taught Me About English" (ESTUDIANTE, se reparte) (12').** Parejas leen. (a) Subrayan 6 verbos en pasado simple. (b) Convierten 4 de esas frases afirmativas en PREGUNTA (Did + subj + base?) y 2 en NEGATIVO (didn't + base) — oral. Ej.: *"I started in 2018" -> "Did you start in 2018?" / "I didn't start in 2018."* El texto trae ademas un ejercicio de identificar tiempos: uselo como cierre rapido del bloque si sobra tiempo.
2. Board (uso oral — Grammar formaliza la regla hoy mismo):
```
THE ASKING / DENYING PAST (A2 Book M12, p.99-102)
(-)  subj + DIDN'T + BASE:   "I didn't work." / "She didn't go."  (NOT "didn't went")
(?)  DID + subj + BASE?:      "Did you work?" / "Did she go?"
     Short: Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.  (never repeat the whole verb)
WH?  WH + DID + subj + BASE:  "What did you do?" / "Where did she go?"
WHO/WHAT as SUBJECT = NO DID: "WHO called?"  "WHAT happened?"  (keeps past form)
```
3. Modela 60 seg: entrevista relampago a un estudiante con 3 preguntas Did/WH sobre su semana; el responde con short answers y frases completas.
4. **Meta del bloque (P6):** *"By the end, each pair runs a 6-question past interview about last week — every question with DID, one WHO-question, one denial."* Preparacion (5'), ejecucion en parejas (rotando), la pareja verifica: base verb tras DID, short answers correctas.
5. **Avance MY STORY (P7):** cada uno escribe 2 preguntas que un entrevistador le haria sobre su historia + 1 negacion honesta ("I didn't speak English at first").
6. Corrige en board solo el error que aparezca en 2+ parejas (anonimo). Nombres a tu cuaderno.
