# B1 MASTERY — Cl 44/44 — CONV (UNICO BLOQUE CON GUIA) — HOJA DE RUTA (CIERRE)

**Sesion 44/44 · 110 min · Bloques: B1 15' + B2 45' + B3 35' + B4 15' = 110**
**Focus (1 line):** Consolidate every B1 family in a board-station circuit (whole level: A2 reviewed + B1 book M1-M12), then return and read the Letter to My Future Self written in Cl 1 — the closing ritual of the level.
**Virtud:** SINTESIS — dia 4 de 4 · **las 4 virtudes integradas (PRUDENCIA + TEMPLANZA + JUSTICIA + FORTALEZA)**
**Frase del Dia:** *"Four virtues, one language, mine now: prudence to plan what's next, temperance to keep the habit, justice to thank WHO helped me, fortitude to keep going — I read the letter I HAVE carried since Cl 1, and I choose what I take forward."*

> **CIERRE del nivel, no clase de modulo.** Repaso integral (todo el libro B1) + ritual de la Carta. Sin PASE, sin tarea. **PROHIBIDO mencionar el examen o su contenido** — el profe no conoce el instrumento.

## BLOQUE 1 — APERTURA (15')

**Objective: open the closing with an integral warm-up across the whole level.**

1. **Chequeo publico de portafolio (3'):** ¿quien grabo su reflexion (B1-43-REFLECTION)? conteo a tu cuaderno — el ultimo del nivel.
2. **Frase del Dia (4').** Board antes; 2 lecturas + coro + 1 solo; 3+ inserciones. Señala: *"Prudence plans the road ahead; temperance walks it steadily; justice thanks who helped; fortitude keeps going. If you had to keep ONE virtue for life — which? Hold that question today."*
3. **Recuperacion integral (8', oral, DE PIE, cadena, sin cuaderno) — repaso de TODO el nivel, no solo N-3/N-7.** Un disparador por familia del libro B1; errores anonimos al board:
   - "Report it: 'She SAID she WAS ready; he ASKED IF I had finished.'" (M8)
   - "Timeline: 'I HAVE learned a lot; I HAD started before; by next year I WILL HAVE improved.'" (M9)
   - "Delegate: 'I got it done, I had the report sent.'" (M10 causative)
   - "Describe: 'a well-organized, hard-working, never careless teammate.'" (M11)
   - "Who does what: 'Everyone IS proud; I did it by myself; we helped each other.'" (M12)
   - "Confirm + compare: 'You have grown, haven't you? I'm better THAN I was.'" (M1 tag · comparatives)

> [VIRTUD] Board: "SYNTHESIS — DAY 4 · THE FOUR VIRTUES". *"The last day is not for new rules — it is for choosing what you carry. Prudence to plan what's next, temperance to keep the habit, justice to thank who helped, fortitude to keep going."*

## BLOQUE 2 — CIRCUITO DE ESTACIONES — TODO EL LIBRO B1 (45')

**Objective: every student produces one real, correct line at each station — proof they own every family of the level.**

1. **Meta (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, each of you has produced one true sentence at every station — the whole B1 book, in your own words."*
2. Board dividido en 6 ESTACIONES (una familia por estacion; solo titulo + 1 molde, el estudiante produce):
```
STATION CIRCUIT — THE WHOLE B1 BOOK (produce, don't copy)
1. REPORTED SPEECH (M8): "She SAID/TOLD/ASKED (IF) ___."
2. PERFECT TENSES (M9): "I HAVE ___ / I HAD ___ / I WILL HAVE ___."
3. CAUSATIVE (M10):     "I had/got ___ + past participle."
4. ADVANCED ADJ (M11):  "a ___-organized, ___ teammate; it is ___ to ___."
5. PRONOUNS (M12):      "Everyone IS ___; I did it by MYSELF; each other."
6. RELATIVE+TAG+COMPARE: "The client WHO ___ ; ..., didn't you? ;
                          better/stronger THAN ___."
```
3. **Circuito de movimiento (30', DE PIE, por estaciones):** en grupos rotan por las 6 estaciones (~5 min c/u). En cada una, cada estudiante DICE en voz alta 1 frase real de su vida/trabajo con esa estructura; el grupo verifica limpio; se anota al board 1 ejemplo modelo por estacion. Velocidad tranquila (templanza), sin competir.
4. **Vuelta de honor (10'):** cada estudiante elige SU mejor frase del circuito y la dice al grupo (20 seg). Tu cuentas familias dominadas al board (anonimo).
5. Feedback: logro primero (¿la frase es real y clara?), forma despues. Errores comunes -> board anonimo; nombres a tu cuaderno.

## BLOQUE 3 — LA CARTA A MI YO DEL FUTURO (35')

**Objective: return and read the Letter to My Future Self written in Cl 1 — the closing ritual of the level.**

1. **Entrega de las Cartas (5'):** las Cartas escritas en Cl 1 las trae COORDINACION (no el profe) — reparte cada carta a su autor. (Ver Plan B si falta alguna.)
2. **Lectura en silencio (7'):** cada estudiante lee en silencio lo que se escribio a si mismo al empezar el nivel. Templanza: en calma, sin apuro.
3. **Compartir (15', voluntario, en circulo):** quien quiera lee en voz alta 1-2 frases de su carta + responde: *"Did the person in this letter become who you are now? What did you plan then (prudence), and what do you keep now?"* Nadie obligado a leer; todos escuchan con respeto (justicia).
4. **Cierre SINTESIS dia 4 (8'):** canon VATS: *"If you had to live with ONE virtue for the rest of your life — which one, and why?"* Cada estudiante dice su virtud en 1 linea. *"Four virtues, one language — all yours now."*

## BLOQUE 4 — CIERRE DEL NIVEL (15')

1. **Ticket final (7', sentados):** cada estudiante escribe 4-6 frases: la estructura del curso B1 que mas domina + la virtud que elige llevarse + una meta concreta para seguir (prudencia) + una linea a su yo del futuro (el proximo). Nombre en el papel. Se recogen TODOS; sin evaluar — el ultimo ticket del nivel.
2. **Error paper** anonimo cerrado (ultimo del nivel); nombres/citas al reporte de coordinacion.
3. **Sin tarea** (cierre del nivel).
4. **Foto/video de grupo (opcional):** cierre del portafolio; entregable estandar, con el reframe ya trabajado (grabarse = fortaleza + servicio).
5. **Anuncio proxima sesion (textual, sin nada mas):** *"Next block: a closing session with a special guest. Come as you are."* **NO menciones examen, formato ni contenido.** *"Everything about what comes next goes through coordination."*

**Checklist salida:** ☐ Reporte firmado ☐ Error paper recogido ☐ Tickets finales recogidos (TODOS) ☐ Cartas del Futuro entregadas y leidas ☐ Fotos/videos ☐ Conteo portafolio anotado ☐ Ningun contenido de examen mencionado
