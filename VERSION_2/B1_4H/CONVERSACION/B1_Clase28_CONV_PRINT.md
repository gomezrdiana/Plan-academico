## BLOQUE 2 — COMPARING MY OPTIONS AT WORK (40')

**Objective: everyone compares real work options three ways — equal, unequal, and superlative.**

1. Meta en 1 linea (arriba). **Avance de proyecto hoy: una linea para MY STORY — "the best decision I made at work, and why it was better than the alternative".**
2. Board — el molde oral (Grammar lo formaliza hoy con M26+M27+M28; aqui NO se enseña la regla):
```
THREE FAIR WAYS TO COMPARE
"The new tool is as good as the old one."          (equal)
"Working remotely is better than commuting."       (unequal)
"Email is the most reliable channel we have."      (superlative)
"This supplier isn't as cheap as that one, but the service is the best."
(as ... as  ·  ...-er / more ... than  ·  the ...est / the most)
```
3. Modela 45 seg comparando 3 cosas reales tuyas: 1 igual, 1 mejor/peor, 1 la mejor/peor.
4. Preparacion en parejas (7'): cada uno elige 3 comparaciones reales de su trabajo (herramientas, opciones, decisiones) — una igual, una desigual, una superlativa — con notas minimas.
5. Ejecucion (15'): en parejas, A compara sus opciones; B escucha con tarea (¿usa as...as? ¿-er/more...than? ¿the ...est?) y RE-CUENTA una de A ("For him, the app is better than email, but the phone is the best"). Cambio de pareja a mitad.
6. Producto verificable (10'): vuelta completa — cada estudiante da al grupo SU mejor comparacion (una con superlativo, 20 seg). Tu cuentas moldes limpios de cada tipo en el board.
