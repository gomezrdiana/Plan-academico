## BLOQUE 2 — LA VOZ PASIVA EN PAPEL (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone converts active to passive with to be + participle, with and without the agent.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M18, Guia 1 p.174):
```
THE PASSIVE VOICE (A2 Book M18, p.173-175)
Activa: el sujeto es el actor y el enfoque.
Pasiva: el enfoque pasa al que RECIBE la accion; el actor
        puede no importar o ser desconocido.
  "Juan prepares the lunch at 12:00."   (activa)
  "The lunch is prepared at 12:00."     (pasiva)
FORM:  Sujeto (paciente) + TO BE + PARTICIPIO + (by + agente)
  The lunch  is  prepared  by Juan.
  El agente se puede ELIMINAR totalmente:
  The lunch  is  prepared.
BOOK PAIRS (p.175):
  "Sebastian plays the game."  -> "The game is played by Sebastian."
  "Mariana studies English."   -> "English is studied by Mariana."
  "We help the teacher."       -> "The teacher is helped by us."
  "The girls clean the house." -> "The house is cleaned by the girls."
```
2. Señala: *"Same -ed participle from M15. You built it for HAVE; today it works for TO BE."*
3. **Conveyor chain (10', DE PIE, circulo):** tu lanzas una activa real de oficina ("the team checks the reports"); estudiante 1 la pasa a pasiva CON agente, 2 ELIMINA el agente, 3 la vuelve pregunta ("Are the reports checked?"), 4 lanza la siguiente activa. Velocidad creciente.
4. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):**
   - "the lunch is prepare" -> "is preparED" (participio obligatorio)
   - "is prepared FOR Juan" -> "BY Juan" (agente = by)
   - "the reports are check" -> "are checkED"
   - "the order receives at 9" -> "the order IS RECEIVED at 9"
5. **Zone walk (12', DE PIE):** board `ACTOR MATTERS -> ACTIVE | ACTOR UNKNOWN / UNIMPORTANT -> PASSIVE (no agent)`. Tu cantas situaciones ("who cooked? — we don't know / the chef is famous" · "who cleans the office? — nobody cares / the new guy did it great"); parejas caminan a la zona Y producen la frase correcta. Justifican en 1 linea.
6. Cierre del bloque: uso libre — cada uno 2 frases pasivas verdaderas de su trabajo, 1 con agente y 1 sin.
