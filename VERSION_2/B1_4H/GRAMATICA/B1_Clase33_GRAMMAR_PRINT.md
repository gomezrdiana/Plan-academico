## BLOQUE 2 — GET / ADVERBS OF MANNER / ACTIVE CAUSATIVE (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds change, manner and volition sentences on paper — across the three closing modules.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M42-M44, verbatim compacto):
```
CLOSING THE A2 BOOK (A2 Book M42-M44, p.333-350)
M42 GET (Guia p.334-335): "to get" = cambio de estado (cualquier tiempo).
   "I get sick." "She gets angry." "I got lost." "The class got quiet."
   con participios: "She got interested in the class."
M43 ADVERBS (Guia p.341-342): adjetivo + "-ly" = "-mente" (how).
   "He plays well." "Sebastian worked carefully." "Philip drives fast."
   irregular: good -> well ; fast -> fast (NO "fastly"). Va tras el verbo/objeto.
M44 CAUSATIVE (Guia p.347-348) - ULTIMO MODULO:
   Subject + want/need/would like + PERSON + to + verb.
   "I want you to get a job." "Connie needs you to talk to her."
   NO: "I want that you go." (mas de esto en el nivel B1.)
```
2. **Meta de tarea comunicativa (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, the board holds 12 real work lines — 4 with GET, 4 with an adverb of manner, 4 with 'want/need + person + to'."*
3. **Preparacion en parejas (7', DE PIE junto al board):** cada pareja arma 1 cambio con GET, 1 frase con adverbio de manera, 1 peticion con "want/need + person + to" — reales de su trabajo.
4. **Ejecucion (12'):** cada pareja ESCRIBE sus frases en el board, las dice, y el grupo verifica: ¿get + adjetivo? ¿adverbio tras el verbo (well, no "good")? ¿persona + to + verbo (NO "that you...")? Meta: 12+ limpias.
5. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):**
   - "I want that you send it" -> "I want you to send it"
   - "we worked careful" -> "we worked carefully"
   - "he plays good" -> "he plays well"
   - "I got tiring" -> "I got tired"
6. **Producto verificable:** lectura coral del board; feedback primero sobre el logro, luego la forma.

## BLOQUE 3 — DRILL + THE DEBRIEF & DELEGATION (35') — DE PIE 100%
