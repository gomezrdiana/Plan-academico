## ANEXO — VERBATIM DEL LIBRO + PLAN B + NOTA INTERNA

### M7 — Relative Pronouns & Embedded Questions (Guias 1-5, p.63-81)
Guia 1 (Simple RP) p.63: *"A relative pronoun connects two ideas... 'I know where you play' / 'when you play' / 'why you play' / 'how you play' / 'who you play with' / 'what you play'. 'what' -> 'lo que', not 'that'. Sometimes we use the infinitive: 'I know where to play.'"*
Guia 2 (Restrictive) p.67: *"gives information necessary for identification. Almost always use 'that' for 'que'. 'The car that I bought is blue.'"*
Guia 3 (Non-Restrictive) p.71: *"gives information NOT necessary for identification... separated by commas... almost always use 'which'. 'My house, which is big, is white.'"*
Guia 4 (Omitting) p.75: *"When the relative pronoun is the OBJECT, it can be omitted ('a person I love'); when it is the SUBJECT, it cannot ('a person who loves me')."*
Guia 5 (Embedded) p.79: *"'Embedded' = 'encapsulado'... the relative clause uses the AFFIRMATIVE ORGANIZATION, not the question: 'Can you tell me what you need?' (NOT 'what do you need'). No question word -> 'if' / 'whether'."*
Ejemplos p.64/68/72/80: *"My sister knows how to swim."* · *"The people who I live with are very friendly."* · *"Bucaramanga, where we live, is beautiful."* · *"I need to know why you left the school."* · *"Could you tell me if I can call my mother?"*
