## BLOQUE 2 — TALLER DE PRECISION DEL PITCH (45') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone corrects the five pitch structures on the board, from real rehearsal errors, citing the course modules.**

```

   -> "she SAID she WAS busy" ; "he said me" -> "he TOLD me" ;
   "asked that I was free" -> "asked IF I was free".
2. PERFECT TENSES (M9): "I have work here 3 years" -> "I HAVE

   ("before she called, I HAD finished").
```

## ANEXO — Plan B + referencia (no leer en clase)

**Formas correctas citadas (para el profe):**
```
M8 Reported (p.93): back-shift is->was, will->would, can->could,
  has done->had done. SAY (no persona) / TELL (persona) / ASK IF.
M9 Perfect (p.97-116): have/has + participle (open), had +
  participle (before a past), will have + participle (by a deadline).
M7 Relative: WHO (people), WHICH/THAT (things); embedded keeps
  affirmative order ("where it is", not "where is it").
M1 Tags: positive clause -> negative tag ("..., didn't you?");
  negative clause -> positive tag ("..., is she?").
Comparatives: -er / more ... THAN ; better/worse (irregular).
```
