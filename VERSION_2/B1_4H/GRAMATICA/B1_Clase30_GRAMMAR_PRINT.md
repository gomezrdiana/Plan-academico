## BLOQUE 2 — HOW MANY / HOW MUCH / PRICES (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds counting, measuring and pricing sentences on paper — across the three modules, fast.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M32-M34, verbatim compacto):
```
QUANTITIES & PRICES (A2 Book M32-M34, p.273-297)
M32 COUNTABLE (Guia p.275): "how many...?" = cuantos (contable)
   "How many dogs do you have?"  "How many dogs are there?"
   many / a lot of / lots of = muchos ; a few = unos (3-5)
M33 UNCOUNTABLE (Guia p.285): "how much...?" = cuanto (incontable)
   "How much milk is there?"   "I have a liter of milk / 300 pesos."
   a lot of / lots of = mucho ; a bit of = un poco de
M34 PRICE (Guia p.293-294): two ways ->
   be:   "How much is the car?" "It's $5." / "They're $5."
   cost: "How much does the car cost?" "It costs $5." / "They cost $5."
   each = por unidad -> "The sandwiches cost $2 each."
```
2. **Meta de tarea comunicativa (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, the board holds 12 real work lines — 4 how many, 4 how much, 4 prices (with 'each' at least twice)."*
