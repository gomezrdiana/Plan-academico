           "I had already left when she called."

           "I will have finished by 5pm."

## ANEXO — VERBATIM DEL LIBRO + PLAN B (no leer literal en clase)

**M9 (p.97-116) — reglas y ejemplos citados verbatim:**
- **Present perfect (have/has + participio):** periodo abierto / no completo / muy reciente; for/since/already/yet/just. "I have lived here since 2014." "I have just finished." "Have you ever tried it?"
- **Past perfect (had + participio):** accion antes de otra pasada. "I had already left when she called."
- **Future perfect (will have + participio):** completado para un limite de tiempo. "I will have finished by 5pm."
- **Perfect continuous (has/have/had/will have + been + V-ing):** duracion de una accion en curso. "I have been working all day."
- **already / yet:** ALREADY -> afirmativo; YET -> negativo/pregunta, al final. "I haven't finished YET." "Have you sent it YET?"
