## BLOQUE 2 — LAS TRES COMPARACIONES, RAPIDO (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: reactivate M26, M27 and M28 in ~5 min each, then everyone MIXES all three on paper about real work.**

```
  "Your father is almost as old as my father."  (almost as ... as)
```
2. **Activacion M27 — DESIGUAL (5', board compacto — cita libro p.247-252):**
```
UNEQUAL COMPARATIVE (M27, p.247-252): ...-er / more ... + THAN
  1 syllable: +er   "I'm richer than Donald Duck."
  2 syll -y -> -ier: "Sarah is prettier than Clara."
  2+ syll: more:    "My father is more serious than my mother."
  Irregular: good->better, bad->worse
     "The restaurant is better than the school."
```
3. **Activacion M28 — SUPERLATIVO (5', board compacto — cita libro p.253-260):**
```
SUPERLATIVE (M28, p.253-260): the ...-est / the most
  1 syllable: the ...est   "Donald is the richest."
  2 syll -y -> -iest:      "Sarah is the prettiest."
  2+ syll: the most:       "Spanish is the most complicated."
  Irregular: the best / the worst   "You're the best!"
```
Cada activacion: coro 2x + cada estudiante 1 frase real de trabajo con esa forma.

4. **Meta de tarea comunicativa (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, this board holds 12 real work comparisons — some equal, some unequal, some superlative."*

6. **Ejecucion (12'):** cada pareja ESCRIBE sus 3 frases en el board (una zona por pareja), las dice, y el grupo verifica: igual -> ¿as...as? desigual -> ¿-er/more + than? superlativo -> ¿the ...est/the most? Meta: 12+ limpias.
7. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):**
   - "as good than" -> "as good AS" · "more better" -> "better" (nunca doble)

8. **Producto verificable:** lectura coral del board; feedback logro primero, forma despues.

## ANEXO — REFERENCIAS COMPACTAS DEL LIBRO (no leer en clase; consulta rapida)

**M26 "I'm as Big as My Dad" — Equal Comparatives (p.241; Vocab p.242; Guia p.243; Ejemplos p.244).** Regla: "as (descriptor) as" = tan...como; "not as...as" (no tan), "almost as...as" (casi tan). Ej. libro: "I'm as nice as Ana" · "My car isn't as new as your car" · "Your father is almost as old as my father" · "Jack is as good as Sam" · "Dan isn't as bad as Vern." Vocab p.242: nice · new · old · good · bad · smart/intelligent.

**M27 "I'm Smarter than Steve" — Unequal Comparatives (p.247; Vocab p.248; Guia p.249; Ejemplos p.250).** Regla: 1 sil +er / 2 sil-y -> -ier / 2+ sil "more" + THAN; irregulares good->better, bad->worse. Ej. libro: "I'm richer than Donald Duck" · "Sarah is prettier than Clara" · "The cat is uglier than the dog" · "My father is more serious than my mother" · "Spanish is more complicated than English" · "Diana is nicer than Walter." Vocab p.248: rich · pretty · ugly · serious · early · late · complicated.

**M28 "You're the Best!" — The Superlative (p.253; Guia p.254; Ejemplos p.255).** Regla: 1 sil "the ...est" / 2 sil-y "the ...iest" / 2+ sil "the most"; irregulares the best / the worst. Ej. libro: "Donald is the richest" · "Sarah is the prettiest" · "The cat is the ugliest" · "My father is the most serious" · "Diego is the tallest" · "Spanish is the most complicated" · "Einstein is the most intelligent."
