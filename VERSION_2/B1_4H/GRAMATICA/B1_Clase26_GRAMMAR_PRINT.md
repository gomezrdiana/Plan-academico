## BLOQUE 2 — DOS FORMAS DEL GERUNDIO, RAPIDO (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: reactivate M21 and M22 in ~7 min each, then everyone MIXES both on paper about real work.**

1. **Activacion M21 (7', board compacto — cita libro p.197-200):**
```
GERUND AFTER A PREPOSITION (M21, p.197-200)
Regla: verbo justo DESPUES de preposicion = -ing.
  before / after / without / about + accion-ING
  "I never eat before swimming."  "I never swim after eating."
  "I never speak without thinking."  "You should ask before using the car."
```
Coro 2x + cada estudiante 1 rutina real de trabajo en voz alta.

2. **Activacion M22 (7', board compacto — cita libro p.207-209):**
```
DOUBLE VERB: "to" or "-ing" (M22, p.207-209)
Regla: el PRIMER verbo decide la forma del segundo.
  TO (aun NO empezado):  want/need/decide/forget/have to
     "I want to play."  "We decide to travel tomorrow."
  -ING (YA empezado):    start/stop/finish/like/love
     "I love eating."  "The girls stop fighting."  "I start studying."
```
Coro 2x + cada estudiante 1 meta real ("I want to..." / "I love ...-ing").

3. **Meta de tarea comunicativa (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, this board holds 12 real work lines that MIX both shapes — a preposition + -ing AND a first verb + to/-ing."*
4. **Preparacion en parejas (7', DE PIE junto al board):** cada pareja arma 2 lineas que combinan ambos moldes, ej.: *"Before deciding to change teams, I always think."* / *"I love finishing a report without rushing."*
5. **Ejecucion (12'):** cada pareja ESCRIBE su mejor linea mixta en el board (una zona por pareja), la dice, y el grupo verifica: ¿accion en -ing tras la preposicion? ¿"to" o "-ing" correcto tras el primer verbo? Meta: 12+ lineas mixtas limpias.
6. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):**
   - "after eat lunch" -> "after eatING lunch" · "before to leave" -> "before leavING"
   - "I want learn" -> "I want TO learn" · "I love to solve" -> "I love solvING"
   - "I stopped to check" (=me detuve) -> "I stopped checkING" (=dejar de)
7. **Producto verificable:** lectura coral del board ("our 12 mixed lines"); feedback logro primero, forma despues.

## ANEXO — REFERENCIAS COMPACTAS DEL LIBRO (no leer en clase; consulta rapida)

**M21 "We Shouldn't Swim After Eating" — Gerunds after Prepositions (p.197; Vocab p.198; Guia p.198-199; Ejemplos p.199-200).** Regla: verbo tras preposicion = -ing. before/after/without/about + accion-ING. Ej. libro: "I never eat before swimming" · "I never swim after eating" · "I never speak without thinking" · "I read about playing golf" · "You should ask before using the car" · "He always showers after eating breakfast." **M22 "I Love Swimming!" — Gerunds with Double Verbs (p.207; Vocab p.207-208; Guia p.208-209; Ejemplos p.209).** Regla: con dos verbos, el primero decide. TO (no empezado): decide/forget/need/want/have to. -ING (ya empezado): start/finish/stop/like/love. Ej. libro: "I want to wake up early" · "She doesn't have to eat" · "He forgets to say hello" · "We decide to travel tomorrow" · "I start studying" · "We finish talking" · "I love eating ice cream" · "The girls stop fighting." Vocab p.207-208: decide · forget · start · stop.
