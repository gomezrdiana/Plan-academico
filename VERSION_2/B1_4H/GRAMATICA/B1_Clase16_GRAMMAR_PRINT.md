## BLOQUE 2 — WAS -ING + WHEN/WHILE EN PAPEL (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone forms the interruption both orders, plus the two-long-actions pattern, fast and clean.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M12, Guia 1 p.99-100):
```
PAST SIMPLE vs PAST CONTINUOUS (A2 Book M12, p.99-102)
Past simple  = evento terminado en tiempo especifico  "I played yesterday."
Past continuous = accion con duracion en el pasado     "I was playing for three hours."
Interruption (short cuts long):
  was/were + -ing  ...  WHEN + past simple
  "I was playing WHEN my mom called."
  "My dad was working WHEN I ate lunch."
  "We were walking in the park, WHEN Jeff saw a dog."
Two long actions at once:  WHILE + was -ing ... was -ing
  "I studied WHILE you worked." / "I was studying WHILE you were working."
Question:  "What were you doing WHEN I was studying?"
Book models (p.100-102): "I was living in Bucaramanga in 2019."
  "Eliana was studying for her exams last week." "Where was Joe working last year?"
```
2. Señala la logica: *"was/were carries the tense; the verb ALWAYS wears -ing. The strike is bare past. WHEN + simple, WHILE + -ing."*
3. **Drill en circulo (10', DE PIE):** cadena — tu lanzas un par ("work / phone ring") -> estudiante 1 interrupcion con WHEN, 2 la reordena WHILE-first, 3 la vuelve pregunta, 4 short answer; el 5 lanza el siguiente par. Velocidad creciente.
4. **Errores tipicos al board (coro 2x cada correccion):**
   - "I was play when he called" -> "I was PLAYING when he called" (-ing obligatorio)
   - "While he called I was working" -> "WHILE I was working, he called" / "I was working WHEN he called" (while + long, when + strike)
   - "What you were doing?" -> "What WERE YOU doing?" (orden de pregunta)
5. **Zone walk (12', DE PIE):** tablero dividido `WAS -ING (long) | WHEN + SIMPLE (strike)`. Tu dices media frase ("the system crashed" / "we were finishing the report"); las parejas CAMINAN a la zona correcta Y producen la frase completa en voz alta. El que llega, justifica en 1 linea.
6. Cierre del bloque: produccion libre de pie — cada uno 2 frases verdaderas de interrupcion sobre su semana de trabajo. El uso libre cierra el bloque, no el drill.
