## BLOQUE 2 — -ING vs -ED (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds thing(-ing)/person(-ed) sentences on paper, affirmative-negative-question.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M41, Guia 1 p.328):
```
PARTICIPLE ADJECTIVES (A2 Book M41, p.326-330)
Regla (Guia 1 p.328):
  -ING = quien PRODUCE el efecto (la cosa / causa)
  -ED  = quien SIENTE el efecto (la persona)
  "The teacher is boring."  "The student is bored."
  "He is amazing."          "She is amazed."
  Irregulares del participio: lose->lost "We are lost."
BOOK EXAMPLES (p.329):
  "This is a really boring book."
  "Mark was sleeping in class. He was really bored."
  "The soccer game was very exciting!"
  "I am excited to visit New York next year."
  "The teacher is annoyed that Juan didn't study."
  "Santiago is very interested in the English class."
VOCAB pairs (p.327): boring/bored · exciting/excited ·
  confusing/confused · tiring/tired · frustrating/frustrated ·
  interesting/interested · surprising/surprised · annoying/annoyed
```
2. **Meta de tarea comunicativa (1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, this board holds 12 lines — 6 things (-ing) and 6 feelings (-ed) about real work — no mixing."*
3. **Preparacion en parejas (7', DE PIE junto al board):** cada pareja arma 2 pares: la cosa (-ing) + la persona (-ed), reales de su trabajo; incluye 1 pregunta ("Are you interested in ...?").
4. **Ejecucion (12'):** cada pareja ESCRIBE su par en el board, lo dice, y el grupo verifica: ¿la cosa lleva -ing? ¿la persona -ed? ¿la pregunta bien formada? Meta: 12+ limpias.
5. **Errores tipicos (board, coro 2x):**
   - "I am boring" -> "I am bored" (a menos que de verdad aburras a otros)
   - "the class is bored" -> "the class is boring"
   - "interesting in" -> "interested in"
   - "I am confuse / I am tire" -> "confused / tired"
6. **Producto verificable:** lectura coral del board; feedback primero sobre el logro, luego la forma.

## BLOQUE 3 — DRILL + THE FEEDBACK STANDUP (35') — DE PIE 100%

**Objective: flip cause<->feeler at speed, then hold it in a realistic performance chat.**

1. **Cadena de conversion (12', circulo, DE PIE):** tu cantas un verbo/vocab ("bore", "excite", "confuse"); estudiante 1 dice la cosa ("The report is boring"), 2 dice la persona ("I am bored"), 3 lanza el siguiente verbo. Velocidad creciente; ultimo giro: solo situaciones reales de SU trabajo, incluye 1 negativa ("I am not bored, I am motivated") y 1 pregunta.
2. **The performance standup (18'):** escenario profesional realista: reunion de seguimiento donde cada quien reporta como esta el trabajo (-ing) y como se siente (-ed). **1 guest = quien reporta** ("The onboarding is confusing and the volume is tiring, so I get frustrated — but the goal is exciting and I stay interested."); otro rota preguntando ("Is the tool annoying? Are you worried or motivated?"); el resto = **OBSERVERS ACTIVOS** (2 errores, 1 acierto, 1 pregunta). Tu = COACH afuera, nunca guest. 3 rotaciones.
3. **Cierre FORTALEZA (5'):** *"Fortitude day four — name one frustrating thing you refuse to let leave you defeated: 'It's frustrating, but I am not giving up.'"* — 2 respuestas maximo.
