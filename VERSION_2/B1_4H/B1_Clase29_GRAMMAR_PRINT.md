## BLOQUE 2 — EL PHRASAL VERB QUE SI SE SEPARA (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds separable phrasal-verb sentences on paper, pronoun in the middle.**

1. Board — cita del libro (M24, Guia 1 p.234-235):
```
SEPARABLE PHRASAL VERBS (A2 Book M24, p.233-236)
Regla 1: la mayoria de los phrasal verbs son SEPARABLES.
  a. El objeto directo puede ir DESPUES del phrasal o EN MEDIO:
       "I pick up Amy."   /   "I pick Amy up."   (ambas OK)
  b. Con un PRONOMBRE objeto, DEBE ir EN MEDIO:
       "I pick up her."  (X)   ->   "I pick her up."  (OK)
BOOK EXAMPLES (Ejemplos p.235):
  "Did you turn on the TV? — Yes, I turned it on."
  "Will you turn off your phone in the class? — Yes, I will turn it off."
  "Did you put on your hat? — Yes, I put it on."
  "Will Alvaro pick up his sister? — Yes, he will pick her up."
  "Did you look up Shakira on Google? — Yes, I looked her up."
VOCAB (p.233-234): turn on · turn off · put on (put on) ·
  take off (took off) · pick up · look up ·
  a radio · a TV · a computer · a light
```
