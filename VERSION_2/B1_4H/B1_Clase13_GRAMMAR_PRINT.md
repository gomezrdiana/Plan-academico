## BLOQUE 2 — DID + VERBO BASE EN PAPEL (40') — DE PIE ~90%

**Objective: everyone builds negatives, yes/no and WH questions with DID + bare verb, and short answers, fast and clean.**

1. Board — cita del libro + regla:
```
NEGATIVES & QUESTIONS — PAST SIMPLE (A2 Book M12, p.99-102)
Book examples (verbatim): "Karol didn't want a cookie."
                          "Did Simon eat breakfast before 6:00?"
                          "What time did you get dressed?"
(-)  subj + DIDN'T + BASE      "I didn't go."   (NOT "didn't went")
(?)  DID + subj + BASE?        "Did you go?"    Short: Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.
WH?  WH + DID + subj + BASE    "Where did you go?" · "What did you do?"
SUBJECT Q (WHO/WHAT = doer) = NO DID:  "WHO called?"  "WHAT happened?"
```
2. Señala la logica: *"DID already carries the past. The verb must NOT carry it again. 'Did you WENT' is the past twice — unfair to the verb."*
3. **Drill en circulo (10', DE PIE):** cadena — tu das una afirmativa ("She finished the report") → estudiante 1 la niega → 2 la vuelve pregunta yes/no + short answer → 3 la vuelve WH-question → 4 lanza la siguiente afirmativa. Velocidad creciente.
4. **Errores tipicos al board (coro 2x cada correccion):**
   - "Did you worked?" → "Did you WORK?"
   - "She didn't went" → "She DIDN'T GO"
   - "Where did she went?" → "Where did she GO?"
   - "Yes, I worked yesterday" (short answer larga) → "Yes, I did."
   - "Who did call?" → "WHO CALLED?" (subject question, sin DID)
5. **Transform walk (12', DE PIE):** tablero en 3 zonas `NEGATIVE | YES/NO Q | WH Q`. Dictas una afirmativa; la pareja CAMINA a una zona (rotando) y produce la transformacion en voz alta + la escribe. Reta 2 subject-questions (WHO/WHAT) sin DID.
6. Cierre del bloque: produccion libre de pie — cada uno hace 2 preguntas reales en pasado a un compañero, con short answers. El drill no cierra el bloque — cierra el uso libre.
