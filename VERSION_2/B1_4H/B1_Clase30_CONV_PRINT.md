## BLOQUE 2 — HOW MANY / HOW MUCH / HOW MUCH IS IT? (40')

**Objective: everyone asks and answers about real work quantities and prices, fast, across the three functions.**

1. Meta en 1 linea (arriba). **Avance de proyecto hoy: una linea para MY STORY — "how much I've grown: I didn't have many words before; now I have a lot."**
2. Board — el molde oral (Grammar lo formaliza hoy mismo con M32-M34; aqui NO se enseña la regla):
```
COUNTING & PRICING AT WORK
COUNTABLE:   "How many clients are there?" - "There are a few / a lot of."
UNCOUNTABLE: "How much coffee is there?"   - "There's a bit of / a lot of."
PRICE (be):  "How much is the printer?"    - "It's $95."  / "They're $6 each."
PRICE (cost):"How much does it cost?"      - "It costs $95." / "They cost $6 each."
(how many = countable · how much = uncountable & price · each = por unidad)
```
3. Modela 45 seg con 2 cosas reales de tu trabajo: una contable ("How many reports...?") y una incontable o precio ("How much does the license cost?").
4. Preparacion en parejas (6'): cada uno arma 2 pares reales de su trabajo — una pregunta HOW MANY y una HOW MUCH / precio.
5. Ejecucion rapida (14'): mini-rondas en parejas: A pregunta ("How many boxes are there?"), B responde con cantidad + a few / a lot of; luego B pregunta un precio, A responde con "It's ___" o "It costs ___ each". Cambio de pareja 2 veces.
6. Producto verificable (10'): vuelta completa — cada estudiante hace al grupo UNA pregunta de cantidad Y una de precio (20 seg). Tu cuentas moldes limpios en el board.
7. Feedback: logro primero, forma despues. Errores 2+ parejas -> board anonimo. Nombres a tu cuaderno.
