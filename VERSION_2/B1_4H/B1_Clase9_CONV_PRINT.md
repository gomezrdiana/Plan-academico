**A1 Book — M15 "I Love My Parents / The Possessive Adjectives" (p.187-196) + M30 "When Is Juan's Birthday? / The Possessive Case" (p.333-336):**
```
ADJETIVOS POSESIVOS (M15):  My / Your / His / Her / Its / Our / Their + sustantivo.
   "She likes HER team."   "He lives in HIS house."   "It woke up with ITS mother."
CASO POSESIVO 's (M30):  se coloca el DUENO primero + ('s) + la propiedad.
   "The dog of my brother" -> "My brother's dog."
   "The dog of Peter" -> "Peter's dog."
   Dueños plurales: apostrofe DESPUES de la s -> "my brothers' dog".
   Dueño termina en s/z: apostrofe + sonido "-es" -> "Chris' dog".
   Ejemplos: "your mother's car", "your brother's team", "Bella's friend".
```
