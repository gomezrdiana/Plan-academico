## BLOQUE 2 — TU RITMO EN NUMEROS (40')

**Objective: by the end, everyone describes their real rhythm using 6+ frequency expressions at the end of the sentence.**

1. Meta en 1 linea (arriba). **Producto: cada estudiante DICE su semana con expresiones (once/twice/three times a week/month/year + every day).**
2. Board (uso solo oral — Grammar formaliza hoy):
```
EXPRESSIONS OF FREQUENCY  = FREQUENCY + A(N) + PERIOD
   once / twice / three times   +   a   +   day / week / month / year
   every + day / week / month
PLACE THEM AT THE END:
   "I check email every morning."   "We meet twice a week."
   "She travels for work three times a year."
   "I visit my family once a month."
Contrast:  weekdays vs weekends -> "On weekdays I start at 7; on weekends I never work."
```
3. Modela 60 seg: tu semana real con 6 expresiones al final. Señala: *"Frequency + a + period. And it goes at the END, like in Spanish — 'twice a week', at the end."*
