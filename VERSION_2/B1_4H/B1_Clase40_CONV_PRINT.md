## BLOQUE 2 — GETTING THERE (40')

**Objective: everyone narrates a real work change using GET in present, past, future and continuous.**

1. Meta en 1 linea: *"By the end of this block, each person has told one real story of change at work using GET in at least three tenses."*
2. **Avance de proyecto hoy: una linea para MY STORY — the change: "I got started with almost no English; I got better every month; soon I will get certified."**
3. Board — el molde oral (Grammar formaliza hoy con M42; aqui NO se enseña la regla):
```
CHANGE OF STATE WITH GET (say the change)
  Present:    "I get tired after long shifts."
  Past:       "I got promoted last year."
  Future:     "The client will get angry if we're late."
  Continuous: "The project is getting complicated."
```
