## BLOQUE 2 (43') — M28: el SUPERLATIVO (the -est / the most)

**Vocabulario del modulo:** el libro NO trae vocabulario nuevo aqui — REUSA los descriptores de M26-M27 (nice, new, old, good, bad, smart, rich, pretty, ugly, serious, early, late, complicated). Repasalos en coro 1 min.

**Tablero verbatim del libro (M28, Guia 1, p.253-254):**
1. El superlativo: *X es el/la mas (descriptor)*.
2. La forma depende del adjetivo, muy parecido al comparativo desigual, pero con **the**:
   - (a) 1 silaba → **the** + adjetivo + **-est**: *"I am the oldest."*
   - (b) 2 silabas terminando en **-y** → **the** + -y por **-iest**: *"I am the prettiest."*
   - (c) 2 silabas o mas → **the most** + adjetivo: *"I am the most intelligent."*
   - (d) Irregulares: *good → the best* · *bad → the worst*.

**Ejemplos del libro (p.254):** *Donald is the richest. · Sarah is the prettiest. · The cat is the ugliest. · My father is the most serious. · The mass is the earliest. · Diego is the tallest. · Spanish is the most complicated. · Diana is the nicest. · Einstein is the most intelligent.*

**Actividad (meta en 1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, each of you will have named the number ONE in a real group — the best, the most serious, the earliest — using the right superlative form."*
1. Preparacion en parejas (6'): cada uno arma 5 superlativos reales sobre grupos que conoce (su familia, su equipo de trabajo, su barrio, tres restaurantes); obliga variedad — al menos 1 con *the -est*, 1 con *the -iest*, 1 con *the most*, 1 irregular (*the best / the worst*).
2. Ejecucion: se los dicen; la pareja verifica la forma segun las silabas (+ *the*) y pregunta *"Why?"*.
3. Producto: 4-5 estudiantes dicen al grupo su superlativo mas util (*"My manager is the most serious person on the team, but she is the best."*).
4. Feedback: logro primero, forma despues.

**Errores tipicos (tachalos, coro 2x):** ~~the most old~~ → the OLDEST · ~~the most best~~ → the BEST · ~~most pretty~~ → the PRETTIEST.

## ANEXO — M28 verbatim + Plan B (no leer en clase)

**Libro A2, Module 28 — "You're the Best!" — The Superlative (p.253-260). Habilidad objetiva del libro:** "Sin dudar, formar el superlativo." (El modulo NO trae seccion de vocabulario propia: reusa los descriptores de M26-M27.)

**Guia 1 (p.253-254), reglas tal cual:**
1. El superlativo tiene la forma *X es el/la mas (descriptor)*.
2. La forma depende del adjetivo, de una manera muy parecida al comparativo desigual:
   - (a) Adjetivos de UNA silaba → *the* + sufijo **-est**. *Soy el mas viejo. = I am the oldest.*
   - (b) Adjetivos de DOS silabas que terminan en **-y** → *the* + -y por **-iest**. *Soy la mas bonita. = I am the prettiest.*
   - (c) Adjetivos de DOS silabas o mas → **the most** antes del adjetivo. *Soy el mas inteligente. = I am the most intelligent.*
   - (d) Irregulares: good (bueno) → *I am the best*. bad (malo) → *I am the worst*.

**Ejemplos completos (p.254):** *Donald es el mas rico. = Donald is the richest. · Sarah es la mas bonita. = Sarah is the prettiest. · El gato es el mas feo. = The cat is the ugliest. · Mi padre es el mas serio. = My father is the most serious. · La misa es la mas temprana. = The mass is the earliest. · Diego es el mas alto. = Diego is the tallest. · El espanol es el mas complicado. = Spanish is the most complicated. · Diana es la mas amable. = Diana is the nicest. · Einstein es el mas inteligente. = Einstein is the most intelligent.*

**Practica del libro (Practica Verbal 1, p.257):** *Cecilia is the prettiest. · Monica is the richest. · The school is the best. · The dog is the ugliest. · The nurses are the happiest. · The engineer is the most serious. · The hamburger is the most expensive. · The woman is the earliest. · The student is the latest. · Spanish is the most complicated.*
**Mezcla igual/desigual/superlativo (Practica Verbal 2, p.259):** *The engineer is more complicated than the maid. · Monica is the richest. · The school is the best. · The dog is more expensive than the elephant. · The engineer is the most serious. · The restaurant is as old as the school. · The woman is the worst. · The student is later than the teacher.* — util para el debrief final del arco comparativo.
