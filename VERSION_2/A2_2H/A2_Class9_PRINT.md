## BLOQUE 2 (43') — M11: El segundo condicional (eventos imaginarios)

**Tablero verbatim del libro (M11, Guide 1, p.88):**
1. El SEGUNDO condicional se usa para eventos IMPROBABLES, IMPOSIBLES o IMAGINARIOS.
2. Describe no lo que HAREMOS, sino lo que HARIAMOS bajo circunstancias diferentes. *"Si yo tuviera plata... compraria un carro."* (en realidad no tengo plata, y por eso no comprare uno).
3. Como el subjuntivo ya casi esta obsoleto en ingles, se usa el PASADO SIMPLE en su lugar.

**Estructura (tablero, 2 columnas) + contraste con el 1st:**

```
IF CLAUSE (verbo en PASADO SIMPLE)   CONDITIONAL CLAUSE (WOULD + verbo base)
"If I HAD money..."                  "...I WOULD buy a car."

1ST (probable):  If I HAVE money, I WILL buy a car.
2ND (imaginado): If I HAD money,  I WOULD buy a car.
```

4. "would" corresponde a las terminaciones -ia / -ias / -iamos / -ian. Negativo = "would not" / "wouldn't". La negativa y la interrogativa se tratan igual que en el primer condicional (pasado + would).

**Ejemplos del libro (p.89)** — escribelos:

```
If I played, I would play soccer.
If we traveled, we would fly to Paris.
If I had a car, I would drive to Bogota.
If the girls were hungry, where would they eat?
If we didn't watch the game, what would we do?
```

**Actividad (meta en 1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, each of you will have imagined 5 dreams with the second conditional — a different life, a different job."*
1. Preparacion en parejas (6'): A imagina una condicion (PASADO: *"If I had... / If I were..."*); B la termina con el resultado (WOULD). Notas minimas.
2. Ejecucion: 6 condicionales imaginarios y cambian; la pareja verifica pasado en el "if" y would en el resultado, distinguiendolo del first (will/present).
3. Producto: 4-5 estudiantes dicen al grupo su sueño mas grande (*"If I had a year free, I would travel the world."*). El bloque no termina sin esto.
4. Feedback: primero el logro (imaginaron con calma), despues la forma.

**Errores tipicos (tachalos, coro 2x):** ~~If I would have money, I would buy~~ → If I HAD money, I would buy · ~~If I had money, I will buy~~ → ...I WOULD buy · ~~If I didn't had money~~ → If I DIDN'T HAVE money.

## ANEXO — M11 verbatim + Plan B (no leer en clase)

**Libro A2, Module 11 — "I'd Eat a Cookie." — The Second Conditional (p.87-98). Objetivo del libro:** expresar eventos improbables o imaginarios con "if" + pasado simple / resultado + would.

**Guide 1 (p.88), reglas tal cual:**
1. "El segundo condicional" se usa para eventos improbables, imposibles o imaginarios.
2. Se usa para describir no lo que haremos, sino lo que hariamos bajo circunstancias diferentes. "Si yo tuviera plata... compraria un carro." (implica que en realidad no tengo plata y por tal razon no comprare uno; pero al tener plata, yo compraria uno).
3. Como el subjuntivo ya casi esta obsoleto en ingles, se usa el pasado simple en su lugar. Clausula con "if" = VERBO EN PASADO SIMPLE / Clausula condicional = con auxiliar "WOULD". E.g. "If I had money... I would buy a car."
4. El auxiliar "would" corresponde a las terminaciones -ia, -ias, -iamos, -ian.
5. Su forma negativa es "would not" o "wouldn't".
6. En sus formas negativa e interrogativa, el segundo condicional se trata igual al primero.

**Ejemplos completos (p.89):** *If I played, I would play soccer. · If we traveled, we would fly to Paris. · If you ate lunch, where would you eat? · If I woke up late, I wouldn't shower in the morning. · If you worked in London, who would you live with? · If we didn't watch the game, what would we do? · If I had a car, I would drive to Bogota. · If I drove to Bogota, I would stay in a hotel.*
