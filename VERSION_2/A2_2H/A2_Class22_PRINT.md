## BLOQUE 2 (43') — M26: comparaciones IGUALES (as ... as)

**Vocabulario del modulo (tablero, p.242):** Nice (bonito de objeto / amable de persona) · New (nuevo) · Old (viejo) · Good (bueno) · Bad (malo) · Smart / Intelligent (inteligente). Pronunciacion coral + individual.

**Tablero verbatim del libro (M26, Guia 1, p.243):**
1. El comparativo IGUAL: *X es tan (descriptor) como Y* → **as ... as**. *"I am as tall as Carlos."*
2. Se puede modificar la frase basica:
   - Negativo (no tan como): *"I am NOT as tall as Carlos."*
   - Casi (almost as ... as): *"I am ALMOST as tall as Carlos."*

**Ejemplos del libro (p.243):** *I'm as nice as Ana. · My car isn't as new as your car. · Your father is almost as old as my father. · Jack is as good as Sam. · Dan isn't as bad as Vern. · Pedro is almost as nice as Cecilia. · My house isn't as new as your house.*

**Actividad (meta en 1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, each of you will have compared two real things — a job, a place, two people — using AS...AS, NOT AS...AS and ALMOST AS...AS."*
1. Preparacion en parejas (6'): cada uno arma 5 comparaciones reales (dos trabajos, dos barrios, dos telefonos, dos personas) con los 6 descriptores del modulo; minimo 1 negativa (*not as ... as*) y 1 con *almost*.
2. Ejecucion: se las dicen; la pareja verifica la estructura [A] + is/are + (not/almost) + as + descriptor + as + [B], y responde si esta de acuerdo.
3. Producto: 4-5 estudiantes dicen al grupo su comparacion mas util (*"This phone is almost as good as that one, but it isn't as expensive."* — expensive ya conocido del nivel).
4. Feedback: logro primero, forma despues.

**Errores tipicos (tachalos, coro 2x):** ~~as tall than~~ → as tall AS · ~~I am as good like Sam~~ → as good AS Sam · ~~is not as new that~~ → is not as new AS.

**Guia 1 (p.243), reglas tal cual:**
1. Usamos tres formas para comparar los grados o niveles de un descriptor. El comparativo IGUAL: *X es tan (descriptor) como Y*. El "tan...como" → "as...as". Se puede modificar la frase basica.
- *Yo soy tan alto como Carlos. = I am as tall as Carlos.*
- *Yo NO soy tan alto como Carlos. = I am NOT as tall as Carlos.*
- *Yo soy CASI tan alto como Carlos. = I am ALMOST as tall as Carlos.*

**Vocabulario (p.242):** nice (bonito de objeto / amable de persona o animal) · new (nuevo) · old (viejo) · good (bueno) · bad (malo) · smart / intelligent (inteligente).

**Ejemplos completos (p.243):** *Yo soy tan amable como Ana. = I'm as nice as Ana. · Mi carro no es tan nuevo como tu carro. = My car isn't as new as your car. · Tu padre es casi tan viejo como mi padre. = Your father is almost as old as my father. · Jack es tan bueno como Sam. = Jack is as good as Sam. · Dan no es tan malo como Vern. = Dan isn't as bad as Vern. · Pedro es casi tan amable como Cecilia. = Pedro is almost as nice as Cecilia. · Mi casa no es tan nueva como tu casa. = My house isn't as new as your house.*

**Practica del libro (Practica Verbal 1, p.245):** *Alex isn't as smart as Sara. · The school isn't as new as the restaurant. · The dog is as old as the elephant. · The doctors are as happy as the nurses. · The engineer is as good as the maid. · The hamburger is as expensive as the sandwich. · The woman isn't as bad as the man. · Spanish isn't as easy as English. · The teacher is as sick as the student.*
