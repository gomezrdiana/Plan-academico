## BLOQUE 2 (43') — M37: Describir personas (to be / to have + very / somewhat)

**Vocabulario del modulo (tablero, p.308-309):** PARTES: hair · face · eyes · nose · mouth · ears · arms · hands · legs · feet · a beard · a mustache · freckles. ADJETIVOS: tall · short · long · young · old · strong · big / small · thin / slender / fat · straight · curly · wavy · blond · pale/olive/dark (skin) · average-height. Pronunciacion coral + individual.

**Tablero verbatim del libro (M37, Guia 1, p.310):**
1. La preposicion *like* tiene usos idiomaticos: *"What are you like?"* = ¿Como eres tu? (personalidad) · *"What do you look like?"* = ¿Como te ves? (apariencia). Para el animo NO se usa like: *"How are you?"*
2. Para describirnos usamos *to be* o *to have*: *"I have a round face." · "I am tall."*
3. Usamos *very* (muy) para amplificar y *somewhat* (algo) para minimizar: *"I have a very round face." · "I am somewhat tall."*

**Ejemplos del libro (p.311):** *The man is average height and average weight. He has long, strong arms and short legs. His eyes are round and his nose is big. He has pale skin. He has brown, curly hair. He has a beard and a mustache. · The woman is young and tall and slender. She has short, straight black hair. · The man is old and average height. He has a big nose and gray hair.*

**Actividad (meta en 1 linea):** *"By the end of this block, each of you will have described a real person (a classmate or someone you know) using BOTH 'to be' and 'to have', with 'very' or 'somewhat'."*
1. Preparacion en parejas (6'): cada uno arma 4 frases sobre una persona real — 2 con *to have* (*"He has short curly hair."*), 2 con *to be* (*"She is somewhat tall."*). Al menos 1 con *very* y 1 con *somewhat*.
2. Ejecucion: se las dicen; la pareja verifica que *to have* describa lo que la persona TIENE (pelo, ojos, nariz) y *to be* lo que ES (tall/young/strong).
3. Producto: 4-5 estudiantes describen al grupo a una persona (sin nombrarla) con 4+ rasgos; el grupo pregunta *"What does he/she look like?"*.
4. Feedback: logro primero, forma despues.

**Errores tipicos (tachalos, coro 2x):** ~~She is long hair~~ → She HAS long hair · ~~I have tall~~ → I AM tall · ~~a hair black~~ → BLACK hair.
