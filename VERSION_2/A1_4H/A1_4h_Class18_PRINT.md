## BLOQUE 2 (90') — M26 que profesion + M27 this/that + M28 donde esta

- **Puente (3'):** ya sabemos *"How are you?"* (emociones). Hoy: *"What are you?"* (rol), *"What is that?"* (objetos) y *"Where is it?"* (ubicacion).
- **M26 — vocab (p.298):** a man / men · a woman / women · a boy · a guy · a girl / a kid · a child / children · a group · a teacher · a student. **Regla (Guia 1, p.299):** el ingles usa *"what?"* con personas para preguntar profesion o rol; objeto singular lleva *"a"* antes (*a friend*), plural suma *"s"* (*friends*). Coro + 3 individuales (*"What are you? — I'm a student."*).
- **M27 — vocab (p.310):** a whiteboard · a book · a notebook · a folder · guides · a table · a desk · a door · a window. **Tablero verbatim (Guia 1, p.311):**

```
Cerca:  THIS is a book.   Are THESE books?  THESE are books.
Lejos:  THAT is a book.   Are THOSE books?  THOSE are books.
        (THAT's a book.)  What is it? -> It's a book.
```
Siempre se incluye el pronombre; si no es masc./fem. → *it* (*"It is a car."*). Tambien se usa para personas.

- **M28 — vocab (p.320-321):** comida: tomato · bread · juice · cheese · egg · meat · chicken · milk. Preposiciones: over/above (sobre) · on (encima de) · under (debajo de) · in front of (delante de) · behind (detras de) · in (en) · beside (al lado de) · between (entre). **Regla (Guia 1, p.321):** la preposicion describe la ubicacion; *be* igual (*"I'm behind the house."*); la preposicion inglesa ya incluye el "de".
- **Historia interactiva (25'):** "el salon al reves." Narras 2-3 frases mezclando los tres (*"What is that? That's a whiteboard! Who is she? She's a teacher. And where is the book? It's UNDER the table, and the cheese is BEHIND the door!"*) → PAUSA → coro repite → un estudiante AGREGA que es un objeto / quien es una persona / donde esta algo y TU lo tejes. NUNCA huecos. Cierre: 2-3 recuentan.
- **Meta en 1 linea:** *"By the end of this block, each of you will say what 2 people are (What are you? I'm a...), point at 2 objects (this/that), and say where 2 things are (prepositions)."* Preparacion en parejas (6', oral) → ejecucion → feedback: logro primero, forma despues.

## BLOQUE 3 (85') — Drill de pie + Simulacion

- **Drill de movimiento (35'), TODOS DE PIE:**
  - Ronda 1 — *what/who/how*: tu senalas a alguien y preguntas (*"Who is she? What is she? How is she?"*); la cadena responde (*"She's Ana. She's a student. She's busy."*).
  - Ronda 2 — this/that/these/those: tocas o senalas un objeto real de la sala y la cadena lo nombra (*"This is a door. Those are windows."*); si esta bien, cambia de lugar.
  - Ronda 3 — preposiciones: tu pones un objeto en un lugar (o lo dibujas) y preguntas *"Where is it?"*; la cadena responde (*"It's on the desk. It's between the books."*). Cierre: 2 min de produccion libre.
- **Simulacion (50'): THE OFFICE TOUR — FIRST DAY.** Escenario profesional/cotidiano solo con tablero y sala: alguien recorre una oficina/salon nuevo; un companero le muestra el lugar. Tablero: lista de personas (*manager, teacher, student, group*) + objetos (*whiteboard, folder, table, door*) + preposiciones.
  - Al frente: **1 estudiante es la PERSONA NUEVA (guest)** + 1 el GUIA. El guia senala (*"That is the manager. This is your desk. Your folder is under the table."*); la persona nueva pregunta (*"What is that? Where is the whiteboard? Who are they?"*).
  - El RESTO son OBSERVERS ACTIVOS: 2 errores (sobre todo *this/that* o preposicion), 1 acierto, 1 pregunta.
  - TU eres COACH desde afuera: NUNCA juegas persona nueva ni guia. Congelados 15+ seg → UN prompt y te retiras.
  - Minimos por ronda: 3 con *what/who* (*What is that? Who is he?*) + 2 con demostrativos + 2 con preposiciones.
  - Rotacion cada 4-5 min. Debrief (5'): observers reportan (anonimo); correccion clave — *"Where is the book? — It's IN front of the door,"* coro 2x.

## ANEXO — M26 + M27 + M28 verbatim + Plan B (no leer en clase)

**Module 26 — "What Are You? / 'Be' for Professions" (p.297-308). Objetivos:** usar *be* para el rol; diferenciar *what / who / how*. **Vocab (p.298):** a man / men · a woman / women · a boy · a guy · a girl / a kid · a child / children · a group · a teacher · a student. **Guia 1 (p.299):** (1) el ingles usa "¿que?" con personas para preguntar profesion/papel; (2) objeto singular requiere "a" antes; plural agrega "s". **Ejemplos (p.300):** *"Are you a woman? — No, I'm not. I'm a man." · "Are they parents? — No, they aren't. They're friends." · "WHO IS SHE? SHE'S CARLA. / WHAT IS SHE? SHE'S A WOMAN. / HOW ARE YOU? I'M HAPPY. / WHAT ARE YOU? I'M A FATHER."*

**Module 27 — "What Is It? / The Demonstrative Pronouns" (p.309-318). Objetivos:** usar *be* para identificar objetos; usar demostrativos. **Vocab (p.310):** a whiteboard · a book · a notebook · a folder · guides · a table · a desk · a door · a window. **Guia 1 (p.311):** (1) siempre se incluye el pronombre; si no es masc./fem. → *it* (*"Es un carro." → "It is a car." / "What is it?"*); (2) *It is (not) a table / It's (not) a table / Is it a table? / Yes, it is / No, it's not / No, it isn't*; (3) demostrativos por cantidad y distancia; (4) IMPORTANTE: tambien se usan para personas. **Tabla (p.311):** *Is THIS a robot? / Is THAT a robot? · THIS is (not) a robot / THAT is a robot (THAT'S a robot) · Are THESE robots? / Are THOSE robots? · THESE are robots / THOSE are robots.* **Practica (p.313-317):** *"THAT IS AN ERASER. / THOSE ARE BOOKS. / THAT'S JOHN. / WHO IS THAT? / WHAT ARE THOSE? / WHAT IS THIS?"*

**Module 28 — "Where Is It? / Prepositions of Location" (p.319-324). Objetivos:** vocab del modulo; usar preposiciones de ubicacion. **Vocab (p.320-321):** comida — tomato · bread · juice · cheese · egg · meat · chicken · milk; preposiciones — over/above (sobre) · on (encima de) · under (debajo de) · in front of (delante de) · behind (detras de) · in (en) · beside (al lado de) · between (entre). **Guia 1 (p.321-322):** (1) la preposicion describe la ubicacion, *be* igual (*"Estoy detras de la casa." → "I'm behind the house."*); (2) con pocas excepciones la preposicion inglesa ya incluye el "de" (*"He Is Behind The house."*). **Ejemplos (p.323-324):** *"Where is the cheese? — It's above the folders." · "Where are the folders? — They're under the cheese." · "Where is the egg? — It's between the meat and the fruit."*
