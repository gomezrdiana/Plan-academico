## BLOQUE 2 (85') — M22 be negativo + M23 be pregunta

- **Repaso rapido (5'):** *be* afirmativo de Cl 15 (I'm / you're / he's / she's / it's / we're / they're). Coro veloz.
- **Tablero verbatim — M22 (Guia 1, p.271):** negativo = agregar *not* despues de *be*; dos abreviaciones equivalentes:

```
Full:            I am not  · You are not · He is not  · We are not
Abrev. #1:       I'm not   · You're not  · He's not   · We're not
Abrev. #2:       (no hay)  · You aren't  · He isn't   · We aren't
```
(*I'm not* NO tiene segunda forma; *isn't* = "esunt", *aren't* = "arunt".)

- **Tablero verbatim — M23 (Guia 1, p.277):** pregunta = cambiar el orden de *verbo* y *sujeto* (como siempre):

```
Am I ...?   Are you ...?   Is he ...?   Is she ...?
Is it ...?  Are we ...?    Are they ...?
```
Palabras de pregunta antes: *"Who are you?" · "Where is he?" · "How are you?"*

- **Historia interactiva (22'):** "el dia del error de identidad." Narras 2-3 frases con negativo + pregunta (*"Are you the doctor? No, I'm not the doctor. I'm the pilot! Is she the nurse? She isn't the nurse — she's the engineer."*) → PAUSA → coro repite → un estudiante AGREGA una confusion de identidad/profesion y TU la tejes en pregunta + negativa. NUNCA huecos. Cierre: 2-3 recuentan.
- **Instalacion (12') DE PIE:** tu das una afirmativa (*"He is the engineer."*) → la cadena la vuelve negativa (dos formas: *"He's not / He isn't the engineer."*) y pregunta (*"Is he the engineer?"*). 5 individuales; ojo *is/are* en la pregunta.
- **Meta en 1 linea:** *"By the end of this block, each of you will ask 2 'be' questions (Are you...? Is he...?) and answer 2 in the negative about who you are and are NOT."* Preparacion en parejas (6', oral) → ejecucion → feedback: logro primero, forma despues.

## ANEXO — M22 + M23 verbatim + Plan B (no leer en clase)

**Libro A1, Module 22 — "You're Not Juan! — 'Be' in the Present Simple Negative" (p.269-274). Habilidad objetiva:** "Sin dudar, usar el verbo 'to be' en forma negativa en el presente sencillo." (Sin vocabulario nuevo.)

**Guia 1 M22 (p.271), reglas tal cual:**
1. Para la forma negativa, agregamos "not" despues de "be", con dos opciones de abreviacion; las dos son completamente equivalentes.
2. Abreviacion #1 = la del modulo anterior + "not": *I'm not · You're not · He's not · She's not · It's not · We're not · They're not.*
3. Abreviacion #2 usa "isn't" (esunt) / "aren't" (arunt): *You aren't · He isn't · She isn't · It isn't · We aren't · They aren't.* Nota: *"I am not"* solo abrevia a *"I'm not"* (no existe #2 para "I").

**Ejemplos M22 (p.272):** *I am not Pepito. → I'm not Pepito. · He is not John. → He's not John. / He isn't John. · She is not Pepita. → She's not Pepita. / She isn't Pepita. · We are not Pepito and Pepita. → We're not... / We aren't... · They are not John and Mike. → They're not... / They aren't...*

**Libro A1, Module 23 — "Are You Juan? — 'Be' in the Present Simple Interrogative" (p.275-282). Habilidad objetiva:** "Sin dudar, usar el verbo 'to be' en forma interrogativa en el presente sencillo." (Sin vocabulario nuevo.)

**Guia 1 M23 (p.277), reglas tal cual:**
1. Para la pregunta, simplemente cambiamos verbo y sujeto (como en toda pregunta): *Am I Pablo? · Are you Pablo? · Is he Pablo? · Is she Ana? · Is it Pablo? · Are we Pablo and Ana? · Are they Pablo and Ana?*
2. Podemos poner palabras de pregunta antes: *"Who are you?" → "¿Quien eres tu?"*

**Ejemplos M23 (p.278):** *Am I Pepito? · Are you Steven? · Is he John? · Is she Pepita? · Are we Pepito and Pepita? · Are they John and Mike?* (PV, tiempo: 25 seg por 10 preguntas al azar.)

**Plan B:**
- Si sale *"do be"* / *"you is...?"*: regla fisica — "BE flips, it never takes DO"; gira la mano (verbo↔sujeto) al hacer la pregunta; coro *"Are you...? Is he...?"* 2x.
- Si mezclan las dos abreviaciones negativas: elige UNA por hoy (recomendado *isn't/aren't*) y menciona la otra como opcion.
