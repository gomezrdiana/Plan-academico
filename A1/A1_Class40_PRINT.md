## 8. EXAM-FORMAT QUIZ — 15 ITEMS — 20 min — SIT

> *"15 questions, SAME format as the real exam. 30 seconds per question. Auto-correction immediately. This is calibration — you'll know exactly what to expect."*

### Setup — 2 min

NO communication of weight, score, or detailed format of the Final beyond *"same format as today's quiz"*. This is PRACTICE.

### Quiz — 15 min — 15 questions × ~60 sec (read + write)

Read each question aloud 2×. Students write the answer on their sheet.

```
EXAM-FORMAT QUIZ — CL 40 (15 ITEMS)

GRAMMAR (5):
1. Complete: "Yesterday I _____ (go) to the market."
2. Complete: "She _____ (not / like) coffee."
3. Complete: "What _____ (you / do) right now?"
4. Complete: "Next Monday I _____ (going to) call my doctor."
5. Complete: "_____ there any milk in the fridge?"

VOCABULARY (3):
6. Translate: "feliz / triste / cansado / enojado / preocupado"
7. Translate: "always / usually / sometimes / never"
8. Translate: "doctor / teacher / nurse / engineer / chef"

TRANSLATION ESP > ENG (3):
9. "Mi hermana puede cocinar muy bien."
10. "El año pasado fuimos a Cartagena."
11. "Este fin de semana voy a visitar a mi mamá."

WRITING (2):
12. Write 2 sentences about your routine (use present + frequency).
13. Write 2 sentences about your plans for next week (use going to).

READING COMPREHENSION (2):
"Maria wakes up at 6 am. She works in a hospital as a nurse.
Yesterday she helped 12 patients. Next weekend she's going
to visit her family in Cali."
14. What does Maria do?
15. What did Maria do yesterday? What is she going to do next weekend?

KEYS (auto-correction):
1.went  2.doesn't like  3.are you doing  4.am going to  5.Is
6. happy / sad / tired / angry / worried
7. siempre / usualmente / a veces / nunca
8. doctor / profesor / enfermera/o / ingeniero/a / chef
9. My sister can cook very well.
10. Last year we went to Cartagena.
11. This weekend I'm going to visit my mom.
14. She works in a hospital as a nurse.
15. She helped 12 patients. She's going to visit her family in Cali.
```

### Auto-correction + close — 3 min
