## 7. ROUND ROBIN — 6 ESTACIONES (00:48–01:08, 20 min) — DE PIE ROTANDO

**OBJETIVO:** *"6 stations around the room. Real-life questions only. Every 3 minutes you ROTATE. Everyone talks at every station — no exceptions."*

### Setup (2 min)

Coloca 6 tarjetas en 6 esquinas/mesas del salón (preparadas antes de clase):

```
E1 FAMILY (presente + -s 3ra persona)
  - How many siblings do you have?
  - What does your dad/mom do?
  - Who in your family makes you laugh the most?

E2 ROUTINE (presente simple + frecuencia)
  - What time do you wake up?
  - How often do you exercise?
  - What do you usually eat for lunch?

E3 YESTERDAY (pasado simple + irregulares)
  - What did you do yesterday?
  - Where did you go last weekend?
  - How did you feel after work yesterday?

E4 PLANS (going to / gonna / will)
  - What are you going to do tonight?
  - Are you gonna work this weekend?
  - Will you study English next month?

E5 FOOD (vocabulario + can + like)
  - What's your favorite food?
  - Can you cook? What can you cook?
  - Do you have any food you don't like?

E6 EMOTIONS (emociones + cuando + por qué)
  - How are you today, really?
  - What makes you happy?
  - When are you usually tired?
```

Forma grupos de 2 (si vienen 6) o de 1-2 (si vienen 4). Cada grupo arranca en una estación.

### Rotación (18 min)

- **3 min por estación.** Todos en el grupo deben responder las 3 preguntas (no se queda nadie callado).
- **Palmada del profesor = rotan a la siguiente estación** (E1→E2→E3→E4→E5→E6).
- Tú caminas con la libreta. Anotas errores con NOMBRE para coordinación (no corriges durante).
- Si un grupo termina antes = vuelven a preguntar variando la respuesta.

### Cierre Round Robin (al rotar de regreso a la estación inicial)

NO cierres — pasa directo al Quiz Mega. Energía alta.

## 8. QUIZ MEGA — 4 EQUIPOS, 20 PREGUNTAS MIXTAS (01:08–01:28, 20 min) — SENTADOS EN EQUIPO

**OBJETIVO:** *"Same 4 teams from Jeopardy. 20 questions. 30 seconds each. Write the answer on paper, lift it up. Right = 1 point. Wrong = 0. If another team finds an extra error in your sentence = bonus point for them."*

### Setup (2 min)

Cada equipo: 1 hoja en blanco + 1 marcador grueso (o lapicero + hoja grande). Sentados en grupo de equipo. Tú con el banco de 20 preguntas + cronómetro.

### Banco de 20 preguntas (16 min) — formato pre-final

```
GRAMÁTICA (5 preguntas) — completar
1. Complete: "She ___ (work) in a bank every day." → works
2. Complete: "Yesterday I ___ (eat) pasta." → ate
3. Complete: "Tomorrow I ___ (go to) call my mom." → 'm going to / am going to
4. Complete: "He ___ (not / can) swim." → can't / cannot
5. Complete: "We ___ (study) English right now." → are studying

VOCABULARIO (5 preguntas) — traducir / nombrar
6. Translate: "estoy cansado" → I'm tired / I am tired
7. Translate: "ella tiene hambre" → she's hungry / she is hungry
8. Name 3 days of the week. → Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday (any 3)
9. What's the opposite of HAPPY? → sad / unhappy
10. Translate: "vamos a comprar comida" → we're going to buy food / we are going to buy food

CORRECCIÓN (5 preguntas) — encontrar el error
11. "She don't likes coffee." → "She doesn't like coffee."
12. "Yesterday I go to the park." → "Yesterday I went to the park."
13. "I'm going to bought a car." → "I'm going to buy a car."
14. "He working in a bank." → "He is working in a bank."
15. "My sister have two kids." → "My sister has two kids."

PRODUCCIÓN (5 preguntas) — hacer la oración
16. Make a sentence with CAN about your family. (any correct)
17. Make a sentence with GOING TO about this weekend. (any correct)
18. Make a NEGATIVE sentence in past simple. (any correct)
19. Make a question about a partner's routine. (any correct)
20. Make a sentence with how you FEEL right now + why. (any correct)
```

### Mecánica

- Lees la pregunta 2x.
- 30 seg de cronómetro.
- *"PAPERS UP!"* — todos levantan al mismo tiempo.
- Cuentas puntos en tablero lateral.
- Si otro equipo grita *"extra error!"* y muestra una corrección válida → +1 bonus.
- **Inserta Frase del Día** en transición entre bloques: *"Same rules, equal chance — that's why this counts."*

### Cierre Quiz (2 min)

Equipo ganador = +15 puntos Heiiu. Aplauso. Foto del tablero con puntajes finales.
