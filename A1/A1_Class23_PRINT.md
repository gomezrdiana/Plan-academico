```
HOMEWORK MYSTERY CHECK — CLASE 23
```

## 4. PEER TEACHER SLOT (00:12–00:19, 7 min) — DE PIE

**OBJETIVO PARA EL ESTUDIANTE:**
> *"One of you teaches today. Teaching cements your own learning."*

**Pre-clase:** asigna a UN estudiante (rotación visible) 1-2 errores recurrentes vistos en clases anteriores o en el midterm (sin mencionar el midterm como tal).

**Errores sugeridos típicos A1 (escoge 1-2):**
```
1. "He go to school" → "He GOES to school" (3rd person -s)
2. "I no like coffee" → "I DON'T LIKE coffee" (negative form)
3. "She is study" → "She IS STUDYING" (continuous) o "She STUDIES" (present)
4. "I am 25 years" → "I am 25 years OLD"
5. "How old you are?" → "How old ARE you?" (verb position)
```

**En clase (7 min):**
1. (30 seg) *"Today's peer teacher is [name]. Floor is yours."*
2. (5 min) Estudiante: explica → corrige → cohorte repite 2x.
3. (1 min) Aplauso + *"Tomorrow it's [next name]."*
4. (30 seg) Anota cómo se sintió.

> Si peer teacher se traba, NO rescates. Dale 10 seg. Que produzca.
