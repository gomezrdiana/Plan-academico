## 6. FULL VISUAL REVIEW — TIMELINE M0–M35 + 10 TENSE ID — 25 min — SIT

> *"One picture. The whole A1 arc on ONE board. You're going to see everything you learned in 9 weeks."*

### Setup TIMELINE — 10 min — you draw, they copy

Draw a big horizontal timeline BEFORE class:

```
        PAST           |        PRESENT        |        FUTURE
   <-----------         |                       |          ----------->

PAST SIMPLE             PRESENT SIMPLE          GOING TO / GONNA
- was/were              - I/you/we/they + base  - am/is/are + going to
- regular -ed           - he/she/it + base + s  - + verb BASE
- irregular (go-went,   - do/does for ?/neg     - "gonna" = spoken only
  see-saw, eat-ate)
                        PRESENT CONTINUOUS      WILL
                        - am/is/are + verb-ING  - will + verb BASE
                        - NOW / right now       - decisions, promises,
                                                  predictions

           MODALS (top of board):
           - CAN (ability, permission)
           - SHOULD (advice, recommendation)
           - WANT TO / NEED TO

           OTHERS (bottom of board):
           - THERE IS (singular) / THERE ARE (plural)
           - SOME (+) / ANY (- / ?)
           - FREQUENCY: always, usually, often, sometimes, never
           - PREPOSITIONS: in (month/year), on (day/date), at (hour)
```

Read each section aloud. Students copy. This is NOT new — it's ONE look at the map.

### 10 tense-ID exercises — 15 min — board

Write 10 sentences on the board (mixed). Student at random says which tense AND why:

```
1. She is cooking dinner right now.
   → PRESENT CONTINUOUS (action happening NOW)

2. They went to the beach last weekend.
   → PAST SIMPLE (regular finished past)

3. I'm going to call my mother tonight.
   → GOING TO (planned future)

4. He can speak three languages.
   → MODAL CAN (ability)

5. There are five books on the table.
   → THERE ARE (plural existence)

6. We always wake up at six.
   → PRESENT SIMPLE + frequency (routine)

7. You should drink more water.
   → MODAL SHOULD (advice)

8. I will help you tomorrow.
   → WILL (promise / decision)

9. Did you eat breakfast?
   → PAST SIMPLE question (did + base)

10. She doesn't have any sisters.
    → PRESENT SIMPLE negative + ANY (negative existence)
```

Auto-correction. Anything that fails → goes to Top 10 errors poster (Block 10).

**Insertion #1 of Phrase of the Day:** *"And our phrase uses 4 tenses at once: 'I think' (present), 'I control' (present), 'I give' (present), 'I'm becoming' (continuous future-ish). Synthesis in grammar too."*
