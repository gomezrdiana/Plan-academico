## ⚠️ NOTA SOBRE EL CONTENIDO — M32 "BE" IN THE SIMPLE PAST

**M32 oficial (libro pág 359):** "Diego Was Sick Yesterday — 'Be' in the Simple Past"

Estructura clave: **WAS / WERE** (afirmativo, negativo, interrogativo) — el pasado del verbo "to be".

```
"BE" EN PASADO SIMPLE — WAS / WERE

I / He / She / It      → WAS
You / We / They        → WERE

(El inglés tiene UNA sola forma de pasado: no hay "fue / era / estaba"
 por separado — todo es was / were.)

AFIRMATIVO:
- "I was happy."          "He was sick."
- "You were happy."       "They were happy."
- "We were happy."

NEGATIVO (no hay abreviación afirmativa; negativo: wasn't / weren't):
- "I wasn't happy."   (was not — "wazunt")
- "You weren't happy." (were not — "wurent")

INTERROGATIVO:
- "Was I happy?"        → "Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't."
- "Were you happy?"     → "Yes, you were. / No, you weren't."
- "Was he sick?"        → "Yes, he was. / No, he wasn't."
- "Were they tired?"    → "Yes, they were. / No, they weren't."

RESPUESTA CORTA: se forma con was / were / wasn't / weren't (igual que en presente).
```

Vocabulario base del módulo (libro pág 360) — **marcadores de tiempo pasado**: **yesterday, last night, last week, last weekend, last month, last year, last class**. (+ estados de ánimo ya conocidos para combinar: happy, sad, sick, tired, busy, worried, in love, angry, ok, great.)

### Concepto (10 min)

**Tablero:**

```
"BE" IN THE SIMPLE PAST — WAS / WERE

I / He / She / It   → WAS
You / We / They     → WERE

              AFFIRMATIVE        NEGATIVE              QUESTION
I             I was happy.       I wasn't happy.       Was I happy?
You           You were happy.    You weren't happy.    Were you happy?
He / She / It  He was sick.       He wasn't sick.       Was he sick?
We            We were happy.     We weren't happy.     Were we happy?
They          They were tired.   They weren't tired.   Were they tired?

KEY RULES:
- English has ONE past form (no separate "fue / era / estaba").
- No affirmative short form. Negative only: wasn't / weren't.
- wasn't = "wazunt"   |   weren't = "wurent"

SHORT ANSWERS:
- "Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't."
- "Yes, they were. / No, they weren't."
```

**Ejemplos modelados del libro pág 361-374** (usar Frase del Día — inserción #1):
- *"Was she sad?"* → *"No, she wasn't. She was happy."*
- *"Were they busy?"* → *"Yes, they were. They were busy last week."*
- *"Was he sick?"* → *"Yes, he was. Diego was sick yesterday."*
- *"Where were you yesterday?"* → *"I was in the office yesterday. I was at home last night."*

### Ejercicios libro (5 min)

Libro pág 362, Ejercicios 1 ("To Be" en el pasado simple — completar pregunta + respuesta corta). 4-5 ítems. Auto-corrección en parejas.

## 8. SIMULACIÓN — "THE SHIFT REPORT" (00:58–01:23, 25 min) — DE PIE

**OBJETIVO:** *"Real-life scene. You arrive at work and report to your supervisor how yesterday's shift was — what was good, what wasn't, who was there, who wasn't — so the team improves with a steady, balanced plan. That's temperance."*

### Setup (3 min)

**Escenario:** Un empleado entrega el reporte de turno a su supervisor: cómo estuvo el día anterior (tranquilo / ocupado), quién estuvo / no estuvo, si todo estuvo bien o no. Juntos deciden un ajuste moderado para hoy (ni hacer de más ni de menos).

**Roles:** empleado (reporta el turno de ayer) + supervisor (pregunta + anota el reporte).

### Vocabulario forzado

- Mínimo 5 preguntas con *"Was...? / Were...?"* + marcador de pasado
- Mínimo 5 respuestas con *"It was... / It wasn't... / They were... / I wasn't..."*
- Estructura: *"Was the morning busy yesterday?"* → *"Yes, it was. It was very busy yesterday."* / *"Were the new people here last week?"* → *"No, they weren't here last week."*

### Modela (3 min)

> *Supervisor: "How was yesterday's shift?"*
> *Empleado: "It was busy in the morning, but it wasn't busy at night."*
> *Supervisor: "Were all the people here yesterday?"*
> *Empleado: "No, two weren't here. But the team was steady — not too fast, not too slow."*

### Round 1 (9 min) — Grupos de 4

Cada grupo tiene un "turno de ayer" (papelitos: morning / night / busy / calm / late / two people sick / everything ok). El supervisor pregunta, el empleado responde con was/were + marcador y deciden el ajuste moderado.

### Round 2 (8 min) — Rotación de roles

Nuevo supervisor. Nuevo turno.

### Cierre (2 min)

1 grupo representa frente al curso. Aplauso. Anotas errores con NOMBRE en tu libreta personal.
