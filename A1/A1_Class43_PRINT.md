## 7. MINIPROJECTS — 6 PRESENTATIONS — 52 min — presenter STAND / audience SIT

> *"This is your day. Six of you. Three to four minutes each. No reading. Five keywords. Mistakes OK. Audience: one short question in English per presenter."*

### Setup — 2 min

Stopwatch visible. Alarm at 3:30 (raised hand — soft signal). At 4:00: *"Bring it to a close."* Audience writes 1 short English question for each presenter on a small slip. **You:** stopwatch + personal notebook. **NO interruption. NO correction. NO translation. NO communication of scores.**

### Per presenter — ~8 min × 6 = 48 min

```
PER PRESENTER — ~8 MINUTES

[0:00–0:30] Set-up: presenter stands, shows 5-keyword card,
            audience closes laptops/phones, opens listening.

[0:30–4:30] PRESENTATION (3–4 min). No reading. Teacher
            silent with stopwatch. Notes errors with NAME
            + real time in personal notebook. Does NOT
            interrupt. 3:30 → raised hand. 4:00 → "Bring
            it to a close."

[4:30–5:00] APPLAUSE — full group, real.

[5:00–6:00] AUDIENCE Q (in English, 1 short question):
            "What is your favorite [place / memory / plan]?"
            "When did you [arrive / start / decide]?"
            "How long did you [practice / live / study]?"
            Presenter answers 30–45 sec, free, no judgment.

[6:00–8:00] TRANSITION: applause again, next presenter
            comes up, shows card, audience resets.
```

### Silent rules YOU apply

1. **NO interruption** — not to correct, not to praise, not to "help". Teacher silence = level respect.
2. **NO scores. NO criteria. NO "good job". NO "you missed X".** Only group applause + next. Technical feedback goes to coordination.
3. **Personal notebook:** literal errors (verbatim quote) + NAME + real presentation time + 1 strength observed per student. This goes to coordination at close.
4. **If someone freezes:** count 5 sec internally. If still frozen, ONE phrase: *"Take a breath. Next keyword."* Do NOT complete the sentence for them. Wait again.
5. **If someone overruns 4 min:** *"Bring it to a close"* at 4:00. At 4:30 → *"Thank you. Applause."* Clean cut.
6. **If someone reads from the card more than 3 seconds straight:** soft clap + *"Eyes up."* — once per presentation MAX.

**Insertion #3 of Phrase of the Day** (between presentation 3 and 4, if natural micro-pause): *"Halfway through. They are presenting themselves in English — all four virtues are living in these 4 minutes."*
