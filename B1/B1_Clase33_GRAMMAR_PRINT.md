## 7. WALKING TRANSFORMATION — 16 min — STAND UP

- **POSITION CORRECT** (SUBJ + verb + OBJ + TO/BASE + verb)
- **TRAP ZONE** ("I want THAT you..." / "She made me TO stop" / missing TO)
