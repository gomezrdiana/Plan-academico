## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT TABLE + WH + SUBJECT Q EXCEPTION (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE la tabla completa en board (toma 5 min,
   vale la pena):

   PAST SIMPLE — FOUR MOVES + EXCEPTION

   AFFIRMATIVE (Cl 11 + Cl 12 — already known)
      I worked. / She went. / We bought it.

   NEGATIVE — subj + DIDN'T + BARE verb
      I DIDN'T work.        (not "didn't worked")
      She DIDN'T go.        (not "didn't went")
      We DIDN'T buy it.     (not "didn't bought")
      They DIDN'T have it.  (not "didn't had")

   YES/NO QUESTION — DID + subj + BARE verb
      DID you work?
      DID she go?
      DID we buy it?
      DID they have it?

   SHORT ANSWER
      Yes, I did.    /  No, I didn't.
      Yes, she did.  /  No, she didn't.
      Yes, they did. /  No, they didn't.
      *** Never repeat the full verb. ***

   WH-QUESTION — WH + DID + subj + BARE verb
      What DID you do yesterday?
      Where DID she go?
      When DID they start?
      Why DID he leave?
      How DID you find the office?

   EXCEPTION — SUBJECT QUESTIONS (no DID)
      WHO called?       (not "Who did call?")
      WHO went?         (not "Who did go?")
      WHAT happened?    (not "What did happen?")
      *** When WHO/WHAT IS the subject, no DID. ***
      *** The verb keeps its past form. ***

2. SEÑALA bloque NEGATIVE. DI: "Subject + DIDN'T +
   BARE. The -ed leaves. The irregular form leaves.
   Just the base. 'I didn't WORK.' 'She didn't GO.'
   'We didn't BUY it.' One rule for regulars and
   irregulars."

3. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "DID +
   subj + bare asks it, DIDN'T + bare denies it, WH
   + DID + bare seeks it, and WHO + past keeps its
   form when WHO acts. Bare is the form's justice."

4. SEÑALA bloque YES/NO QUESTION. DI: "DID + subject
   + BARE verb. Same rule mirrored. 'DID you work?'
   'DID she go?' Never 'Did you worked?' Never 'Did
   she went?'."

5. SEÑALA SHORT ANSWER. DI: "Short and clean. DID
   or DIDN'T match the subject. You never repeat the
   verb. 'Yes, I did.' 'No, she didn't.' Done."

6. SEÑALA bloque WH-QUESTION. DI: "Same machine
   with a WH-word in front. WH + DID + subject +
   BARE. The verb is ALWAYS bare after DID — even
   when the question starts with WHAT, WHERE, WHEN,
   WHY, or HOW."

7. SEÑALA EXCEPTION. DI: "One exception. When WHO
   or WHAT IS the subject — the person or thing
   doing the action — no DID. The verb keeps its
   past form. 'Who called?' — not 'Who did call?'.
   'What happened?' — not 'What did happen?'. The
   rest of the time: DID + bare. Just this one
   exception."

8. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "Did you worked yesterday?"
   -> TACHA "worked", ESCRIBE "Did you WORK yesterday?"
   DI: "After DID = bare. Drop the -ed." COHORTE
   repite 2 veces en coro.

9. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "She didn't went home"
   -> TACHA "went", ESCRIBE "She DIDN'T GO home."
   DI: "After DIDN'T = bare. The irregular form
   leaves." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

10. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "Where did she went?"
    -> TACHA "went", ESCRIBE "Where did she GO?"
    DI: "WH + DID + subject + BARE. Same rule. No
    exception for WH-questions." COHORTE repite 2
    veces.

11. [VIRTUD] activation: cuando alguien titubee
    entre la forma marcada y la bare despues de
    DID/DIDN'T, DI suave: "Justice. After DID, the
    verb is bare. Drop the past form."

12. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "Did he wanted it?"
    -> TACHA "wanted", ESCRIBE "Did he WANT it?"
    DI: "Even with regulars — after DID, the -ed
    leaves. WANT, not wanted." COHORTE repite 2
    veces.

13. [ERROR TIPICO #5] "Who did call?"
    -> TACHA "did", ESCRIBE "WHO CALLED?"
    DI: "Subject question. No DID. The verb keeps
    its past form. Just the exception — everywhere
    else, DID + bare." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

14. PIDELES en parejas: "Transform 10 affirmative
    sentences. I dictate the source — half go to
    NEGATIVE, half go to YES/NO QUESTION." 6 min.
    DICTA estos 10:
       a. "She went to the meeting"      -> NEGATIVE: "She didn't GO to the meeting"
       b. "I worked late yesterday"      -> QUESTION: "DID you WORK late yesterday?"
       c. "He bought a new laptop"       -> NEGATIVE: "He didn't BUY a new laptop"
       d. "They had a long conversation" -> QUESTION: "DID they HAVE a long conversation?"
       e. "We saw the report"            -> NEGATIVE: "We didn't SEE the report"
       f. "She finished the project"     -> QUESTION: "DID she FINISH the project?"
       g. "I took the train"             -> NEGATIVE: "I didn't TAKE the train"
       h. "He called the client"         -> QUESTION: "DID he CALL the client?"
       i. "They studied for the exam"    -> NEGATIVE: "They didn't STUDY for the exam"
       j. "She wrote the email"          -> QUESTION: "DID she WRITE the email?"
    CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca
    2 veces.

15. PODIUM — WH-QUESTION SPRINT (7 min). DOS
    volunteers al frente. Tu das un kit (WH-word +
    SUBJECT + VERB) y construyen la pregunta completa
    en past simple, manteniendo BARE. Cohorte verifica
    en coro: "Confirmed — DID + bare!"

    8 kits para llamar:
       What  / you  / do      -> "What DID you DO yesterday?"
       Where / she  / go      -> "Where DID she GO?"
       When  / they / start   -> "When DID they START?"
       Why   / he   / leave   -> "Why DID he LEAVE?"
       How   / we   / find    -> "How DID we FIND the office?"
       What  / the boss / say -> "What DID the boss SAY?"
       Where / you  / study   -> "Where DID you STUDY?"
       When  / they / arrive  -> "When DID they ARRIVE?"

    Plus 2 subject question challenges:
       WHO   / call           -> "WHO CALLED?" (no DID!)
       WHAT  / happen         -> "WHAT HAPPENED?" (no DID!)

16. Aplauso al final. CIERRA cuando 80% construya
    DID + bare y respete la excepcion subject Q sin
    titubear. DI: "Stand. Push chairs to the walls.
    We go to the live drill."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 35 min y aun pegan -ed/irregular despues de DID/DIDN'T, NO AVANCES al podium. Reduce: solo negative + yes/no question hoy, deja WH + subject question para Cl 14 opener. Mejor LAND 2 bien que rush 4 shaky.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: DID/DIDN'T DRILL + WH-QUESTION SPRINT (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "DID/DIDN'T DRILL +
   WH-QUESTION SPRINT — 18 BUILDS, 3 ROUNDS"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. Two phases. PHASE A —
   10 transformation drills: I read an affirmative
   past sentence, your pair transforms it — half to
   NEGATIVE, half to YES/NO QUESTION, alternating.
   You say it OUT LOUD and then write it on the
   board grid I'm about to draw. PHASE B — 8
   WH-question builds: I give the WH-word + subject
   + verb, your pair builds the full WH-question in
   past simple, holding BARE after DID. Observer
   counts BARE verbs maintained vs -ed/irregular
   slips. Then you rotate roles."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This drill IS the
   phrase — DID + subj + bare asks, DIDN'T + bare
   denies, WH + DID + bare seeks, WHO + past keeps
   form. Bare is the form's justice."

4. DIBUJA en board un grid 2x10 vacio para PHASE A
   (cada par anota su transformacion en una celda):

     #   AFFIRMATIVE              -> TARGET FORM
     1   She finished the report     NEGATIVE
     2   I called the manager        QUESTION
     3   He went to the airport      NEGATIVE
     4   They had a meeting          QUESTION
     5   We saw the document         NEGATIVE
     6   She bought new equipment    QUESTION
     7   I took the early train      NEGATIVE
     8   He sent the invoice         QUESTION
     9   They studied the material   NEGATIVE
    10   We wrote the proposal       QUESTION

5. PIDELES round 1 — PHASE A (12 min): pareja toma
   transformaciones 1-5. Roles claros: A dicta /
   verifica, B transforma + escribe en el grid, luego
   switch en mid-pair. Observer cuenta slips.

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces
   en la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven
   a parejas.

7. ROTA parejas + cambia roles. Round 2 — PHASE A
   parte 2 (10 min): transformaciones 6-10. Mismo
   formato.

8. [VIRTUD] activation durante los rounds: si alguien
   escriba "didn't went" o "Did you worked" en el
   grid, DI suave: "Justice. After DID, the verb is
   bare. Drop the past form."

9. ROTA parejas otra vez. Round 3 — PHASE B
   (15 min): WH-QUESTION SPRINT. DICTA los 8 kits
   (WH-word + subj + verb), pareja construye la
   pregunta completa en past simple. Plus 2 subject
   question challenges al final — sin DID.

    8 WH-question kits:
       1. What  / you  / do       -> "What DID you DO yesterday?"
       2. Where / she  / go       -> "Where DID she GO?"
       3. When  / they / start    -> "When DID they START?"
       4. Why   / he   / leave    -> "Why DID he LEAVE?"
       5. How   / we   / find     -> "How DID we FIND the office?"
       6. What  / the manager / say -> "What DID the manager SAY?"
       7. Where / you  / study    -> "Where DID you STUDY before?"
       8. When  / they / arrive   -> "When DID they ARRIVE?"

    2 SUBJECT Q challenges:
       9. WHO  / call             -> "WHO CALLED?"
      10. WHAT / happen           -> "WHAT HAPPENED?"

10. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces
    en parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu
    cuaderno.

11. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente
    al grupo (4 transformations + 4 WH-questions in
    a row, alternating). COHORTE cuenta cuantas BARE
    verbs maintained. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("Transform: 'I worked late'") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 3 rounds completos (PHASE A x 10 + PHASE B x 8 + 2 subject Q) + UNA demo al frente con BARE verbs visibles.
