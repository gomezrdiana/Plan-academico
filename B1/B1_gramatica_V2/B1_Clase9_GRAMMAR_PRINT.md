## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT FULL TABLE (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE la tabla completa en board (toma 4 min,
   vale la pena):

   ADJECTIVES (before noun, NO article)
      I->MY  you->YOUR  he->HIS  she->HER
      it->ITS (NO apostrophe)  we->OUR  they->THEIR
      Ex: "my laptop / her report / our team"
      *** NEVER "the my book" — replaces article. ***

   's (people, animals)
      One owner       -> 's   "John's report / the boss's office"
                              "Charles's car" (keep 's)
      Plural ending s -> '    "the bosses' offices"
      Irregular plural -> 's  "the children's bags / the women's room"

   OF (things, long phrases)
      Inanimate -> the X OF the Y
      "the cover OF the book / the end OF the meeting"
      Long noun phrase: "the report OF the manager I met yesterday"

   WHOSE (question for owner) — H, NO apostrophe
      "Whose laptop is this? / Whose idea was that?"
      *** NEVER "Who's" — that is "who is". ***

   ITS vs IT'S
      ITS  = belonging to it: "The cat licks its paw."
      IT'S = it is:           "It's raining."

2. SEÑALA bloque ADJECTIVES. DI: "Replaces the article.
   Never 'the my'. Match the OWNER, not the object. Car
   belongs to a woman — HER car, even if 'car' is
   masculine in Spanish."

3. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "Her
   laptop for the owner, the boss's office for the
   person, the cover of the book for the thing, Whose
   with H. Four forms, no confusion."

4. SEÑALA bloque 's. DI: "People only. One owner — 's.
   Plural ending in s — apostrophe AFTER. Irregular
   plural — back to 's. Writing makes the distinction
   visible."

5. SEÑALA bloque OF. DI: "Things and long phrases.
   Books don't own. Meetings don't own. Use OF."

6. SEÑALA bloque WHOSE. DI: "H, no apostrophe. WHO'S
   with apostrophe = WHO IS. Two different words."

7. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "The my book" -> TACHA "the",
   ESCRIBE "MY book." COHORTE repite 2 veces en coro.

8. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "It's cover is blue" (about a
   book) -> TACHA "It's", ESCRIBE "ITS cover is blue."
   DI: "ITS no apostrophe = belongs to it. IT'S
   apostrophe = it is." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

9. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "The book's cover" -> TACHA,
   ESCRIBE "the COVER OF the book." DI: "Books don't
   own. Things use 'of'. People use 's." COHORTE
   repite 2 veces.

10. [VIRTUD] activation: cuando alguien titubee
    entre 's y of, DI suave: "Measure. Person or
    thing? Pick the right form. That is temperance."

11. PIDELES en parejas: "Transform 8 sentences. I
    dict the source, you say the alternative." 5 min.
    DICTA estos 8:
       a. "Anna's laptop"        -> of form: "the laptop of Anna" (heavier)
       b. "the office of the boss" -> 's form: "the boss's office"
       c. "the cover of the book" -> can 's? NO — thing. Keep 'of'.
       d. "Whose pen is this?"   -> adj: "Is this your/his pen?"
       e. "He has a car"         -> adj: "His car..."
       f. "She has a meeting"    -> adj: "Her meeting..."
       g. "The students have books" -> plural 's: "the students' books"
       h. "The children have bags" -> irreg pl 's: "the children's bags"
    CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca
    2 veces.

12. PODIUM (7 min). DOS volunteers al frente. Tu das
    un trio (OWNER + OBJECT + FORMAT) y producen la
    oracion en 10 segundos. Cohorte verifica en coro:
    "Confirmed — right shape!"

    8 trios para llamar (cubre 1 de cada forma):
       Anna / laptop / 's          -> "Anna's laptop"
       meeting / end / of          -> "the end OF the meeting"
       unknown / phone / WHOSE     -> "Whose phone is this?"
       team / report / adj         -> "their report"
       boss(f) / office / adj      -> "her office"
       children / bags / 's        -> "the children's bags"
       bosses(pl) / offices / 's   -> "the bosses' offices"
       book / cover / of           -> "the cover OF the book"

13. Aplauso al final. CIERRA cuando 80% construya los
    4 formatos sin titubear. DI: "Stand. Push chairs
    to the walls. We go to the live application."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 35 min y aun confunden 's con of, NO AVANCES al podium. Reduce: solo adjective + 's + of hoy, deja WHOSE para Cl 10 opener. Mejor LAND 3 bien que rush 4 shaky.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas + podium produciendo los 4 formatos sin pedir ayuda.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: POSSESSIVES INTERROGATIVE (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "POSSESSIVES INTERROGATIVE
   — 8 OWNERSHIP EXCHANGES"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. Office scene — you are
   two colleagues sorting out who owns what after a
   shared project. Each pair must produce 8 ownership
   exchanges using the 4 forms in rotation. Each
   exchange = one WHOSE question + one answer that
   uses ONE of the 4 forms (adjective / 's / of /
   pronoun). Then you rotate."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This drill IS the phrase
   — her laptop, the boss's office, the cover of the
   book, Whose. Four forms, in rotation, no confusion."

4. ESCRIBE en board los 8 exchanges (Q + answer
   format) — cada uno fuerza UNA forma distinta:

     1 WHOSE + adj (her):   "Whose charger?"  -> "HER charger."
     2 WHOSE + 's (one):    "Whose phone?"    -> "John's phone."
     3 WHOSE + of (thing):  "Whose part of the report?" -> "the intro OF the report."
     4 WHOSE + plural 's:   "Whose offices on 3rd floor?" -> "the managers' offices."
     5 WHOSE + irreg pl 's: "Whose bags?"     -> "the children's bags."
     6 WHOSE + ITS:         "What's on the printer?" -> "ITS lid."
     7 WHOSE + adj (his):   "Whose coffee cup?" -> "HIS — the new analyst."
     8 WHOSE + 's (extra-s): "Whose office key?" -> "the boss's office key."

5. PIDELES round 1 (15 min): pareja toma exchanges
   1-4. Roles claros: A pregunta, B responde, luego
   switch.

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces
   en la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven
   a parejas.

7. ROTA parejas + cambia roles. Round 2 (15 min):
   exchanges 5-8.

8. [VIRTUD] activation durante los rounds: si alguien
   pone 's en una cosa o usa "Who's" en lugar de
   "Whose", DI suave: "Measure. Person or thing? H
   or apostrophe? Pick the right form."

9. ROTA parejas otra vez. Round 3 (10 min): pareja
   inventa 4 exchanges propios (no de la lista) que
   cubran los 4 formatos. Push para mezclar todos.

10. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces
    en parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu
    cuaderno.

11. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente
    al grupo (4 exchanges in a row, uno de cada
    formato). COHORTE cuenta cuantas formas
    diferentes cazaron. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("Whose folder is on the boss's desk?" / "What's the title of the report Anna handed in?") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 3 rounds completos + UNA demo al frente con los 4 formatos visibles.

```

   que mantenga las 4 formas separadas.

      HOMEWORK CL 9 GRAMMAR — DUE BEFORE NEXT CLASS, 7:00 AM
      TIME: 30-45 min (B1). NOT fragmented.

      (1) WRITING — 12 sentences in 4 sets of 3,
          one set per format:
          - 3 with possessive ADJECTIVES (his/her/our/their):
              "Her report contains three sections."
          - 3 with 's for people (singular + plural-s + irregular):
              "The manager's office is being repainted."
              "The bosses' offices were redesigned."
              "The children's bags were stored safely."
          - 3 with OF for things:
              "The conclusion of the report surprised everyone."
          - 3 WHOSE questions + their answers:
              "Whose folder is on the desk? — That's John's folder."
          Hand in on paper next class.
          RULE: NEVER "the my book"; NEVER "Who's" for
          ownership; NEVER 's on a thing; ITS no apostrophe.

      (2) AUDIO eyes closed — 2-3 min — "Describe your
          colleague's workspace" — use AT LEAST 3
          possessive adjectives, 2 possessive 's, 1 of,
          and ask yourself 2 WHOSE questions aloud.
          Label: B1-GRAMMAR-9.

      (3) LISTENING — 10 min YouTube "office tour
          walkthrough English". Note 5 sentences with
          possessives — mark which of the 4 forms each
          uses. Paper.

      RULE: Not fragmented. No "I didn't have time."
      If pushback: "This is what B1 requires. 30-45
      min between classes is the minimum. We don't
      lower the bar — we raise your stamina."

7. DI: "Next class Cl 10: possessive PRONOUNS
   (mine / yours / his / hers / ours / theirs) —
   the form WITHOUT the noun. Temperance day 5 —
   closes the block. Real B1 sounds like: 'Her
   laptop, the boss's office, the cover of the book,
   Whose is this?' Four forms held cleanly. Any
   question about evaluations goes to coordination —
   not to me, not to the Conv Teacher."
```
