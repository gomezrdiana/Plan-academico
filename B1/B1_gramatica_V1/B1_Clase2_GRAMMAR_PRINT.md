## 6. GRAMMAR DEEP DIVE — PRESENT PERFECT vs PAST SIMPLE (01:23–01:35, 12 min)

**Por qué hoy:** El diagnóstico de ayer mostró confusión entre periodo cerrado (past simple) vs abierto (present perfect). Hoy reforzamos.

**Setup (1 min):** "Yesterday some of you confused these tenses. Quick fix today."

**En el tablero:**
```
PAST SIMPLE → tiempo CERRADO → yesterday, last year, in 2020
PRESENT PERFECT → tiempo ABIERTO → today, this year, since/for, ever/never
```

**5 oraciones para corregir (ellos identifican el error, 8 min):**
```
1. Yesterday I have eaten pizza.        → Yesterday I ATE pizza.
2. I have lived here since 5 years.     → I have lived here FOR 5 years. (since + fecha; for + duración)
3. She didn't see that movie this week. → She HASN'T SEEN that movie this week.
4. They have arrived at 3pm.            → They ARRIVED at 3pm.
5. Have you been to Paris last year?    → Did you GO to Paris last year? (last year = past simple)
```

**Cómo:** Pregunta una a una. ANTES de dar la respuesta, deja que 2-3 estudiantes opinen. Anchors van segundo. Si está mal: "No. Listen." + correcto + 2 estudiantes repiten.

**⚠️ ANTI-EXPLICACIÓN:** Si todos fallan en la misma, MODELA: "Listen. Yesterday I ATE pizza. Why? YESTERDAY = closed. Repeat." NO digas "porque la regla es..."

**Cierre (3 min):** "These 2 tenses you must master before midterm in week 4. Practice."

## 7. ERROR PAPER — INTRODUCCIÓN (01:35–01:45, 10 min)

**⚠️ AYER NO SE HIZO. Hoy lo establecemos como ritual permanente.**

**Setup (2 min):** "From now on, every class I write down errors I hear. We fix them together at the end of every Block 1 and Block 2. The errors are ANONYMOUS. I never say who said what."

**Escribe en el tablero 4 errores reales que anotaste durante el Speaking Circle de hoy:**
Ejemplos típicos:
- "I have learn English 5 years"  (debería ser "I have LEARNED English FOR 5 years")
- "Yesterday I have went to..."  (debería ser "Yesterday I WENT to...")
- "I lived here since 2020"  (debería ser "I HAVE LIVED here since 2020")
- "He don't understand"  (debería ser "He DOESN'T understand")

**Corrección colectiva (6 min):**
- "What's wrong with #1?" Espera. Deja silencio.
- Anchors van SEGUNDO (no monopolicen).
- Cuando alguien dice la corrección: "Yes. Repeat 2x. Everyone repeats."
- Sigues al siguiente.

**Cierre (2 min):** "Mistakes are normal. They are the road. The student who makes the mistake today is the student who fixes it tomorrow. NO shame, just learning."
