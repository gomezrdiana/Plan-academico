**Cita textual del A2 Book M27 (módulo pág 247; Vocabulario 1 pág 248; Guía 1 pág 249; Ejemplos/Ejercicios pág 250) — escribe el esquema en tablero ANTES de clase:**

```
   Serious (serio / grave), Early (temprano),

"1. El comparativo no igual tiene la forma 'X es mas

"2. La forma del comparativo depende del adjetivo"

PRACTICA VERBAL 1 — Respuestas (pag 252):

   The dog is uglier than the elephant.
   The engineer is more serious than the maid.
   The nurses are happier than the doctors.

   The student is later than the teacher.
   Spanish is more difficult than English.

KEY: 1 syllable -> adj + "-er than".
     2 syll. in "-y" -> "-y" becomes "-ier than".
```

> Di: *"One thing is more than another — change the adjective by its shape. Short word: add '-er'. Ends in '-y': '-y' becomes '-ier'. Long word: put 'more' in front. 'good' and 'bad' are irregular: 'better', 'worse'. And the joining word is always 'than' — that means 'que' — never 'that'."*

### BOARD RACE — unequal comparative (7 min) — DE PIE, 2 equipos

Dos columnas en el tablero. Dictas una frase mal formada (con "more" en adjetivo corto, con "-er" en adjetivo largo, con "that" en vez de "than", o con "more good"); el primer estudiante de cada fila corre al tablero y escribe la frase correcta. Cohorte verifica. Rota.

```
Round (dicta MAL; ellos escriben bien):
1. "I am more old than Carlos."        -> I am OLDER than Carlos.
2. "I am more good than Carlos."       -> I am BETTER than Carlos.
3. "Sarah is more pretty than Clara."  -> Sarah is PRETTIER than Clara.
4. "It is more complicated that English." -> It is more complicated THAN English.
5. "Dan is more bad than Vern."        -> Dan is WORSE than Vern.
6. "The class is laterer than golf."   -> The class is LATER than golf.
```

> **Inserción #2 Frase del Día:** *"Compare with our phrase — 'today is hard-ER THAN yesterday', 'I am strong-ER THAN my excuses', 'the goal is clos-ER THAN it was'. Short adjectives, '-er', 'than'. Phrase first, then the rule."*

## 7. WALKING TRANSFORMATION — M27 (00:34–00:50, 16 min) — DE PIE

**OBJETIVO PARA EL ESTUDIANTE:**
> *"You become the sentence. Wrong comparison on one side of the room, correct unequal comparative on the other. You walk the transformation."*

### Setup (2 min)

### Round 1 — Walk it (8 min)

Cada estudiante:

3. El cohorte (de pie) confirma con pulgar arriba/abajo.

**Tiras sugeridas (contexto profesional):**

```
"The new tool is more good than the old one."   -> The new tool is BETTER than the old one.
"This shift is more busy than the night one."    -> This shift is BUSIER than the night one.
"The paid plan is more fast than the free one."  -> The paid plan is FASTER than the free one.
"Supplier A is more cheap than Supplier B."       -> Supplier A is CHEAPER than Supplier B.
"My report is more bad than hers."                -> My report is WORSE than hers.
"This system is more complicated that the old one."-> This system is more complicated THAN the old one.
"Evaluations are handled by coordination, not by us." -> (limit reminder — leave correct as is)
```

Ahora al revés: parten de la forma CORRECTA, caminan a WRONG diciendo en voz alta por qué está mal ("'more good' is wrong: 'good' is irregular — 'better than', never 'more good'; and short adjectives take '-er', not 'more'"). Intercambia tiras entre estudiantes.

## 9. HUMAN-SENTENCE LINE-UP + STATION ROTATION (01:02–01:16, 14 min) — DE PIE

### Human-Sentence Line-up (4 min) — DE PIE

Reparte tarjetas grandes (1 palabra por estudiante: un sujeto, el verbo, el comparativo correcto, "than", el segundo elemento; y tarjetas sueltas con el adjetivo base + el sufijo "-er"/"-ier" o la palabra "more" para que el grupo decida cómo armarlo). Por grupos, los estudiantes se PARAN en fila físicamente para formar la oración correcta — y físicamente deben decidir si el adjetivo toma "-er", "-ier", "more" o es irregular ("better/worse"), y "than" debe aparecer SIEMPRE (nunca "that"). El grupo lee la oración en voz alta. 2-3 rondas, alternando 1 sílaba / "-y" / 2+ sílabas / irregular.

### Station Rotation (10 min) — DE PIE

3 estaciones rotuladas. Grupos rotan cada ~3 min (campana). Todos de pie.

```
STATION A — 1 SYLLABLE ("-er than")
Build 4 sentences about your job with 1-syllable adjectives
+ "-er than": fast, hard, cheap, slow, late, old.
"The new app is faster than the old one. Today was harder
 than Friday. This supplier is cheaper than that one. The
 morning report is later than the night one."

STATION B — "-Y" ("-ier than") + IRREGULAR ("better/worse than")
Build 4 sentences: 2 with "-y" adjectives (busy, happy,
easy) -> "-ier than"; 2 with "good/bad" -> "better/worse
than".
"This week is busier than last week. The team is happier
 than before. The new tool is better than the old one. My
 first draft was worse than this one."

STATION C — 2+ SYLLABLES ("more ... than")
Build 3 sentences with 2+-syllable adjectives + "more ...
than" — and say WHY they take "more" (not "-er").
"The premium plan is more expensive than the free one. This
 system is more reliable than the old one. The night shift
 is more complicated than the morning one."
```

Tú circulas entre estaciones, corriges en voz baja, anotas errores con NOMBRE.
