### Drill 2 — Measurement + quantity chain reciclado (4 min) — DE PIE

Tú das un objeto/concepto; el estudiante responde con UNA pregunta de medida (M29) Y UNA respuesta de cantidad (M30):

```
"your study time per week"
  -> Q: "How much time do you spend studying per week?"
  -> A: "I spend about six hours — not as many as I
         wanted, but enough to keep moving."

"the people in your team"
  -> Q: "How many people are there on your team?"
  -> A: "There are eight, and most of them speak some
         English."

"your weekly goals"
  -> Q: "How many goals do you set per week?"
  -> A: "I set three, and I finish about two — that's
         enough for now."
```

Refuerza: countable ("how many" / "many" / "a few") vs uncountable ("how much" / "much" / "a little / a lot of").
