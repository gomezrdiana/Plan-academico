# 👔 YOUR ROLE — DUTY MANAGER

**B1 MASTERY — Cl 11 Grammar — VIP Hotel Hospitality Activity (13/05/2026)**

---

## YOUR POSITION
You are the **highest authority on shift**. You report only to the General Manager. You have authority to:
- Comp the entire stay
- Offer Presidential Suite upgrades
- Personally attend to VIP guests
- Authorize any compensation that other managers cannot

You are called when NO ONE ELSE can resolve the situation.

## YOUR OBJECTIVE IN THE SCENE
1. **Take command** when other managers cannot resolve the issue.
2. **Personally attend to the guest** — your visible presence matters.
3. **Authorize major compensation** if necessary (free stay, presidential upgrade).
4. **Restore trust** — convert a furious VIP into a loyal returning guest.
5. **Document everything** — this will go to the General Manager.

## REQUIRED VOCABULARY — use at least 5
- I take full responsibility
- goodwill gesture
- comp the stay
- discretionary upgrade
- on the house
- our utmost
- personally attend to
- immediate resolution
- I will personally oversee
- discretion

## REQUIRED GRAMMAR — you MUST produce both at least once

### ✅ M12 — Past Simple + Past Continuous
**Example:** *"While I was reviewing what happened, I realized we had not done enough to resolve this — that ends now."*

### ✅ M13 — Already / Still / Until — use ALL THREE
**Example:** *"As of this moment, you have already been upgraded to the Presidential Suite. The room is still being finalized. It will be ready until 10 PM."*

## TONE REGISTER (B2)
- Calm authority — never raise your voice
- Take ownership at the highest level
- Use formal English even more than other roles

**Sample phrases:**
- *"Allow me to personally see to this."*
- *"I take full responsibility on behalf of the entire hotel."*
- *"As a gesture of how seriously we take this, allow me to..."*
- *"Your stay tonight — and the entire weekend — is on the house."*
- *"Please accept my sincerest apologies. I will personally oversee your stay from this moment forward."*
