# 🥂 YOUR ROLE — ROOM SERVICE SERVER / BUTLER

**B1 MASTERY — Cl 11 Grammar — VIP Hotel Hospitality Activity (13/05/2026)**

---

## YOUR POSITION
You **deliver food and beverages to guest rooms** and handle in-room service issues. You:
- Deliver orders professionally with silver service standards
- Communicate kitchen delays to guests
- Escalate complaints to the F&B Manager
- Anticipate small needs (extra napkins, condiments, water)

You are the **last person** the guest sees before they sit down to eat. Your professionalism shapes their experience.

## YOUR OBJECTIVE IN THE SCENE
1. **Apologize for any delay** as soon as you enter the room.
2. **Offer alternatives** if the meal is wrong, cold, or incomplete.
3. **Escalate to F&B Manager** immediately for complaints you can't resolve.
4. **Maintain silver service register** — even under stress.
5. **Don't take complaints personally** — you represent the hotel.

## REQUIRED VOCABULARY — use at least 5
- expedite the order
- course timing
- off-menu request
- plate / plating
- signature dish
- room service
- butler
- cart
- silver service
- discretion

## REQUIRED GRAMMAR — you MUST produce both at least once

### ✅ M12 — Past Simple + Past Continuous
**Example:** *"While the chef was plating your order, I noticed the time and called the manager immediately."*

### ✅ M13 — Already / Still / Until
**Example:** *"Your second course is still being prepared. It will arrive until approximately 6:45. The chef has already been informed of your timeline."*

## TONE REGISTER (B2)
- Service-oriented + apologetic
- Composed even when the guest is upset
- Brief and clear — guests don't want long explanations from servers

**Sample phrases:**
- *"I'm so sorry for the delay — allow me to..."*
- *"Please accept my apologies on behalf of the kitchen team."*
- *"May I bring you a complimentary drink while you wait?"*
- *"I will escalate this to our F&B Manager immediately."*
- *"Bear with me — I'll have an update within 5 minutes."*
