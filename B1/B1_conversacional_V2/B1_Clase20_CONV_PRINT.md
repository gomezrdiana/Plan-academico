## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT FIVE-FORM MASTERY (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande la CADENA COMPLETA (las 5
   formas, una debajo de otra):

   THE WHOLE PAST MACHINE — FIVE FORMS, ONE STORY

   (1) OLD HABIT / STATE
        used to + base   "I used to work alone."
        would + base     "Every morning I would arrive early."

   (2) BACKGROUND (in progress)
        was/were + -ing  "I was finishing a report..."

   (3) THE EVENT (it cut the background)
        when + past simple "...when my manager called."
        past simple        "She gave me a hard client."

   (4) THE EARLIER EVENT (already done before)  [Cl 19]
        had + participle  "By then she had already chosen me."
                          "I had never managed an account alone."

   (5) THE ORDER
        first / then / after that / while / finally

   *** Habit = used to.  Background = was -ing. ***
   *** Event = simple.  Earlier = had + participle. ***
   *** Order = connectors.  The whole thing = PAST. ***

2. SEÑALA cada capa, una por una, con UN ejemplo rapido
   de cada una. DI: "You own each of these. Today we don't
   re-teach them — we CHAIN them. Watch how one story uses
   all five."

3. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase en el board. DI:
   "The phrase IS the chain — 'used to, was -ing, when it
   happened, and what I had already done before. One whole
   past mastered.' Five forms, one story, no slip."

4. [ERROR TIPICO #1] (dropped layer) historia sin past
   perfect cuando claramente hay un evento anterior:
   "When I arrived, the meeting started" (pero ya habia
   empezado). -> ESCRIBE "...the meeting HAD already
   started." DI: "Don't drop the earlier layer — if it
   was already done, use had + participle." COHORTE
   repite 2 veces.

5. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "I used to worked there and I was
   prepare a report." -> CORRIGE las dos: "used to WORK"
   + "was PREPARING". DI: "After used to = base. Background
   = was + -ing." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

6. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "...then I am closing the deal."
   -> TACHA "am closing", ESCRIBE "...then I CLOSED the
   deal." DI: "Fortitude — the whole story stays in the
   past. No jump to the present." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

7. MODELA UNA historia completa en voz alta (75 seg),
   apuntando a cada una de las 5 capas mientras la dices:
   "I used to work in a small office. One morning I was
   finishing a report when my manager called me in. By
   then she had already chosen me for a difficult client,
   and I had never managed an account alone. First I
   panicked. Then I asked for help. After that I practiced
   every night. Finally I closed the deal." DI: "Count the
   five: used to — was -ing — when + simple — had already
   chosen / had never managed — first/then/finally. That
   is mastery."

8. PIDELES en parejas: "Each person tells a 6-line past
   story about a real challenge. Your partner has a
   CHECKLIST — they must hear all FIVE: (1) used to/would,
   (2) was -ing, (3) when + simple, (4) had + participle,
   (5) a connector. They tick each one they hear and tell
   you which is MISSING." 9 minutos.

9. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca en
   2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota errores con
   NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

10. [VIRTUD] activation: si alguien deje caer una capa o
    salte al presente, DI suave: "Fortitude. Finish the
    story. Hold all five — used to, was -ing, when it
    happened, had done, and the order."

11. ESCRIBE en board la SELF-CHECK CARD (5 preguntas):

    BEFORE YOU FINISH A STORY, ASK:
       1. Did I set an OLD habit? (used to / would)
       2. Did I set a BACKGROUND? (was -ing)
       3. Did I hit the EVENT? (when + past simple)
       4. Did I show an EARLIER event? (had + participle)
       5. Did I ORDER it? (first / then / finally)
       *** And did I stay in the PAST the whole time? ***

12. SEÑALA la self-check. DI: "This is your pre-midterm
    checklist. A complete past story answers YES to all
    five. If one is missing, the story is thinner. Today
    you make sure none is missing."

13. PIDELES en parejas (nueva): "Retell your story — but
    now hit ALL FIVE on purpose, and add the layer your
    partner said was missing last round. 6-8 lines.
    Partner runs the self-check card out loud at the end."
    8 minutos.

14. CIRCULA, CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo encadene las 5
    formas en una sola historia sin saltar al presente.
    DI: "Stand up — we go to the showcase."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 30 min y la cohorte aun deja caer 2+ capas o salta al presente, NO exijas las 5 a la vez. Reduce a 4 formas (used to + was -ing + when + simple + had + participle) y deja la cadena completa de connectors como callback en el showcase. Mejor UNA historia de 4 capas limpia que 5 capas rotas.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas narrando 6-8 lineas que tocan las 5 formas, manteniendose en pasado, con la self-check card corriendo sola.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: FULL-STORY SHOWCASE — "MY HARDEST CHALLENGE, START TO FINISH" (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "FULL-STORY SHOWCASE — MY
   HARDEST CHALLENGE, START TO FINISH — 3 ROTACIONES +
   FRONT DEMOS"

2. DI las reglas: "This is the dress rehearsal for your
   midterm story. In pairs. One TELLS a complete 7-8
   sentence past story about a real, hard professional or
   study challenge — start to finish, with all FIVE forms.
   The other is the SCORER with the self-check card: they
   listen and at the end report which of the five forms
   were present and which was missing. Then switch. Every
   round, the goal is FEWER missing layers and ZERO present
   slips."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This showcase IS the phrase
   — used to, was -ing, when it happened, and what I had
   already done before. One whole past mastered. You tell
   the entire story and you do not slip."

4. ESCRIBE en board el ESQUELETO (no un guion — un mapa):

     HABIT     -> "I used to..." / "I would..."
     SCENE     -> "One day I was ...-ing..."
     EVENT     -> "...when ... (past simple)"
     FLASHBACK -> "By then I had already..." / "...because
                   I had..."
     ORDER     -> "First... Then... After that... Finally..."
     CLOSE     -> "...and in the end I learned..."

5. PIDELES round 1 (12 min): pareja escoge roles. Teller
   gives the full 7-8 sentence story. Scorer runs the
   self-check at the end ("I heard used to, was -ing, when
   + simple, and a connector — you were MISSING the had +
   participle flashback"). SWITCH roles — different story.

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces en
   la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven a
   parejas.

7. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, plan the
   missing layer in Spanish for 5 seconds, then say it in
   English. Don't freeze and don't drop into the present."

8. ROTA parejas. Round 2 (12 min): same format, but now
   the teller must add the layer they were told was missing
   in Round 1 — every story must hit all FIVE. Scorer
   confirms aloud.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante el showcase: si alguien
   deje caer una capa o salte al presente, DI suave:
   "Fortitude. Finish the story. Hold all five."

10. ROTA parejas otra vez. Round 3 (12 min) — FRONT DEMO
    READY: cada pareja pule UNA historia para que suene
    como un mini-pitch (esto ES el ensayo del midterm).
    Luego 2-3 parejas dan una demo de 75-90 seg AL FRENTE;
    el resto corre la self-check card en silencio y al
    final UN observer reporta: "All five present, all
    past." Aplauso.

11. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces en
    parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu cuaderno.

12. CIERRA: DI: "That story you just polished — keep it.
    Next class opens the midterm prep, and that is exactly
    the shape your 'My Story, My Goals' pitch will take."
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("What did you USED TO do before this started?" / "What HAD you already tried?") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol del teller.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 3 rotaciones + 2-3 demos al frente con las 5 formas presentes y verificadas por la self-check card, todo en pasado.
