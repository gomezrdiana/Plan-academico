## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT NEGATIVES + QUESTIONS (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE 3 columnas en board grandes:

   AFFIRMATIVE         NEGATIVE              QUESTION
   I worked            I DIDN'T work         DID you work?
   She went            She DIDN'T go         DID she go?
   He had it           He DIDN'T have it     DID he have it?
   We saw them         We DIDN'T see them    DID we see them?
   They bought it      They DIDN'T buy it    DID they buy it?

   *** RULE: after DID / DIDN'T, the verb is BARE. ***
   *** NEVER "didn't worked", NEVER "didn't went". ***

2. SEÑALA columna NEGATIVE. DI: "Subject + DIDN'T +
   BARE verb. The -ed disappears. The irregular form
   disappears. Just the base. 'I didn't WORK.' 'She
   didn't GO.' 'He didn't BUY it.'"

3. SEÑALA columna QUESTION. DI: "DID + subject +
   BARE verb. Same rule. 'DID you work?' 'DID she
   go?' 'DID they buy it?' Never 'Did you worked?'
   Never 'Did she went?'."

4. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase en el board.
   DI: "This is what the phrase says — Did you work?
   She didn't go. Where did we meet? After DID the
   verb returns home. The form's justice is the bare
   base."

5. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "Did you worked yesterday?"
   -> TACHA "worked", ESCRIBE "Did you WORK yesterday?"
   DI: "After DID = bare. Drop the -ed." COHORTE
   repite 2 veces en coro.

6. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "She didn't went home"
   -> TACHA "went", ESCRIBE "She DIDN'T GO home."
   DI: "After DIDN'T = bare. The irregular form
   leaves." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

7. ESCRIBE una segunda seccion grande en board:

   SHORT ANSWERS

      Q: Did you work yesterday?
      A: Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.

      Q: Did she go to the meeting?
      A: Yes, she did. / No, she didn't.

      Q: Did they finish the report?
      A: Yes, they did. / No, they didn't.

   *** Never repeat the full verb in the answer. ***
   *** "Yes, I worked" sounds heavy. Just "Yes, I did". ***

8. SEÑALA short answers. DI: "Short and clean. DID
   or DIDN'T. Match the subject pronoun. You don't
   repeat the verb. 'Yes, I did' — done."

9. PIDELES en parejas: "5 yes/no questions each in
   past simple, plus the short answer. Use a mix of
   regulars and irregulars from Cl 11 and Cl 12 —
   work, study, eat, see, go, buy, have, take. About
   yesterday or last weekend." 5 minutos.

10. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca
    en 2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota errores
    con NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

11. [VIRTUD] activation: si alguien titubee con la
    forma del verbo despues de DID/DIDN'T, DI suave:
    "Justice. After DID, the verb is bare. Drop the
    past form."

12. ESCRIBE una tercera seccion grande en board:

    WH-QUESTIONS IN PAST SIMPLE

       WH-word + DID + subject + BARE verb

       What DID you do yesterday?
       Where DID she go?
       When DID they start?
       Why DID he leave?
       How DID you find the office?

       *** NEVER "What did you did?" -> "What did you DO?"
       *** NEVER "Where did she went?" -> "Where did she GO?"

13. SEÑALA WH-questions. DI: "Same machine. WH +
    DID + subject + BARE verb. The verb is ALWAYS
    bare after DID — even when the WH-word is in
    front."

14. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "Where did she went?"
    -> TACHA "went", ESCRIBE "Where did she GO?"
    DI: "WH + DID + subject + BARE. No exception."
    COHORTE repite 2 veces.

15. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "Did he wanted it?"
    -> TACHA "wanted", ESCRIBE "Did he WANT it?"
    DI: "Even with regulars — after DID, the -ed
    leaves. WANT, not wanted." COHORTE repite 2
    veces.

16. ESCRIBE la EXCEPTION en board (cuarto bloque):

    SUBJECT QUESTIONS (no DID needed)

       WHO called?         (NOT "Who did call?")
       WHO went?           (NOT "Who did go?")
       WHAT happened?      (NOT "What did happen?")

       *** When the WH-word IS the subject, no DID. ***
       *** The verb keeps its past form. ***

17. SEÑALA SUBJECT QUESTIONS. DI: "One exception.
    When WHO or WHAT is the SUBJECT — the person or
    thing doing the action — no DID. The verb keeps
    its past form. 'Who called?' — not 'Who did
    call?'. 'Who went?' — not 'Who did go?'. Just
    one exception. Keep it simple."

18. [ERROR TIPICO #5] "Who did call?"
    -> TACHA "did", ESCRIBE "WHO CALLED?"
    DI: "Subject question. No DID. The verb keeps
    its past form. Just the exception — everywhere
    else, DID + bare." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

19. PIDELES en parejas: "Each pair builds 6 WH-questions
    + 2 subject questions in past simple. Mix WH-words:
    what, where, when, why, how. For subject questions
    use WHO. About what happened in the office last
    week." 6 minutos.

20. CIRCULA, CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo construya
    DID + bare verb sin titubear y respete la excepcion
    subject question. DI: "Stand up — we go to the
    interview."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 30 min y aun pegan -ed/irregular despues de DID/DIDN'T, NO AVANCES a WH ni a subject question. Reduce a 2 estructuras hoy (negative + yes/no question), deja WH + subject question para callback en Cl 14. Mejor LAND 2 bien que rush 4 shaky.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas construyendo negatives + yes/no + WH + 1 subject question manteniendo BARE verb despues de DID/DIDN'T sin pedir ayuda.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: JOB INTERVIEW — PAST EXPERIENCE (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "JOB INTERVIEW — PAST
   EXPERIENCE — 3 ROTACIONES"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. One is the INTERVIEWER.
   One is the CANDIDATE. Interviewer asks 8 past
   simple questions about previous job — work history,
   education, why they left the last job, when they
   started, how they found this position. Candidate
   answers in past simple — affirmative mostly, with
   AT LEAST 2 negatives ('I didn't finish college' /
   'I didn't work in sales'). Every question must
   use DID + bare verb. Every answer must keep the
   bare rule when negative. Then you switch roles."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This interview IS the
   phrase in action — Did you work? She didn't go.
   Where did we meet? After DID the verb returns
   home. The form's justice is the bare base."

4. ESCRIBE en board los 8 standard interview questions
   (interviewer base):

     1. "Where DID you work before?"
     2. "Why DID you leave your last job?"
     3. "What DID you study?"
     4. "When DID you start your previous role?"
     5. "How DID you find this position?"
     6. "DID you manage a team there?"
     7. "DID you have any difficult moments?"
     8. "What DID you learn from that job?"

   Plus AT LEAST 2 negatives in the answers:
     "I DIDN'T finish college."
     "I DIDN'T work in sales — I worked in support."

5. PIDELES round 1 (12 min): pareja escoge roles.
   Interviewer asks 8 in order. Candidate answers
   each one. Then SWITCH roles, same 8 questions —
   different answers.

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces
   en la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven
   a parejas.

7. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, switch
   to Spanish, think 5 seconds, come back to English.
   Don't freeze."

8. ROTA parejas. Round 2 (12 min): same 8 questions
   pero ahora interviewer can ADD 2 follow-up questions
   improvisadas — usando WH-words (What / Where / When /
   Why / How) + DID + subject + BARE. Candidate keeps
   the bare rule + adds 2 negatives.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante la entrevista: si
   alguien diga "Did you worked there?" o "She didn't
   went" o "Where did he went?", DI suave: "Justice.
   After DID, the verb is bare. Drop the past form."

10. ROTA parejas otra vez. Round 3 (12 min): mismo
    formato, push para que en cada interview
    aparezcan AL MENOS 6 DID-questions + 2 DIDN'T-
    negatives + 1 WH-question + 1 short answer
    ("Yes, I did" / "No, I didn't"). Observer (third
    person of nearby pair) counts BARE verbs
    maintained.

11. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces
    en parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu
    cuaderno.

12. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente
    al grupo (1 mini-interview completa — interviewer
    + candidate, 4 questions + 1 negative). Observer
    voluntario reporta en voz alta: "I counted X
    DID-questions, Y DIDN'T-negatives, Z WH-questions
    — all with BARE verbs." Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("Did you work in retail before?" / "Where did you study?" / "Why did you leave?") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol de interviewer ni candidate.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 3 rotaciones + UNA demo al frente con DID + bare + 2 negatives + 1 WH + 1 short answer visibles.
