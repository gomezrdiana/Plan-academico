## 2. ICEBREAKER — "IF YOU HAVE EVER..." (00:07–00:20, 13 min)

**OBJETIVO:** Energizar, mover el cuerpo, practicar Present Perfect (tiempo de ayer).

**Setup (2 min):** "Stand up. Push your chairs back. Make space. I say sentences with HAVE YOU EVER. If TRUE for you, stay standing. If NOT, sit down."

**Round (10 min) — di una a una, 5 segundos entre cada una:**
- "If you have ever traveled outside Colombia..."
- "If you have ever cooked a meal for 10+ people..."
- "If you have ever performed in front of an audience..."
- "If you have ever broken a bone..."
- "If you have ever lost something important..."
- "If you have ever spoken English with a foreigner..."
- "If you have ever tried sushi..."
- "If you have ever lived alone..."
- "If you have ever read a book in English..."
- "If you have ever regretted a fast decision..." (conecta con prudencia)
- "If you have ever waited more than 1 hour for someone..."
- "If you have ever fallen in love..."

**Cierre (1 min):** "Sit. Notice — every sentence used 'HAVE YOU EVER + past participle.' That's PRESENT PERFECT for life experience. Use it today."

## 5. SHADOWING — DÍA 1 DE CICLO (01:00–01:15, 15 min) — ⚠️ NO SE PUEDE SALTAR

**⚠️ HOY ES DÍA 1 DEL PRIMER CICLO DE SHADOWING DEL B1.**

**Video:** *Penny Teaching the Guys to Catch Fish* (Big Bang Theory)
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AFVZ-U2rXW4
**Personajes:** ~3-4 (Penny + 2-3 chicos) — al Día 3 trabajarán en **tríos**

**Conexión:** El video tiene gerundios (catching, cooking), imperativos (clean it, hold it), conversación natural. Útil para B1.

**STEP 1 — Listen with focus (2 min):** "Watch. Don't repeat. Listen to the rhythm and HOW they speak."

**STEP 2 — Listen + read subtitles (2 min):** Activate English subtitles. "Read while listening."

**STEP 3 — Repeat in chunks (4 min):** Pause every 5-10 sec. Whole class repeats together after each chunk.

**STEP 4 — Shadow simultaneously (3 min):** "Speak AT THE SAME TIME as the video. Don't wait. Don't be perfect. Just keep up."

**STEP 5 — Pair role play (3 min):** "Pairs face each other STANDING. One says a chunk from memory, the other continues. Reconstruct from memory."

**Cierre (1 min):** "Tomorrow same video. Day 2. Try harder to memorize. Day 3 = presentation in trios."

> **REPORTE — auditable:** Anota en el reporte: "Shadowing Día 1 realizado. Video usado: BBT Penny fishing. 5 pasos completos sí/no."

## 6. PROJECT WORKSHOP — PAGE 1 DRAFT (01:15–01:35, 20 min)

**OBJETIVO:** Convertir el "My English Story" del Bloque 1 + brainstorm de ayer en el primer borrador de la presentación midterm.

**Setup (3 min):** "Take out your brainstorm from yesterday and your My English Story from this morning. Today we draft Page 1 of your project: WHO AM I?"

Estructura sugerida (escribe en tablero):
```
PAGE 1: WHO AM I?
- 3 sentences: name, where from, profession/role
- 2 sentences: 1 thing I have done in my life that I'm proud of
- 2 sentences: 1 challenge I have overcome
- 1 sentence: my biggest goal with English
```

**Trabajar (12 min):** "Sit. Quiet. Write Page 1. Use PRESENT PERFECT for what you've done. PAST SIMPLE for specific moments. 12 minutes."

Camina en silencio. Si alguien pregunta:
- "Is this right?" → "Read it to your partner next to you. Both decide."
- "What word for X?" → "Use simpler English. Describe it."

**Compartir parcial (5 min):** "Pairs. Read Page 1 to your partner. Partner gives ONE positive feedback + ONE thing to improve."

> **Soft skill spotlight:** Public Speaking (presentar oralmente lo escrito).

## 7. ERROR PAPER LIVE (01:35–01:45, 10 min)

**Setup (1 min):** "I heard 5 errors today. Anonymous. Let's fix them."

Escribe en tablero los 5 errores reales que anotaste hoy. Ejemplos típicos en B1:
- "I have studied English since 5 years" → since FIVE YEARS AGO o FOR 5 years
- "Yesterday I have eaten" → past simple
- "He don't speak" → doesn't
- "I'm boring" → BORED
- "Last year I have visited" → last year = past simple

**Corrección colectiva (8 min):**
- "What's wrong with #1?" Wait. Silence.
- Anchors van segundo.
- Cuando dicen la corrección: "Yes. Repeat 2x. Everyone repeats."

**Cierre (1 min):** "Mistakes are the road. The student who makes the mistake today is the student who fixes it tomorrow."
