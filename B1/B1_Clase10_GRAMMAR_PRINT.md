## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT TABLE + 2 ORDERS + EXCEPTION (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE la tabla completa en board (toma 4 min,
   vale la pena):

   OBJECT PRONOUNS (after verb, after preposition)
      SUBJ -> OBJ
      I    -> ME
      you  -> YOU (same form)
      he   -> HIM
      she  -> HER
      it   -> IT  (same form)
      we   -> US
      they -> THEM
      Ex: "He helped ME. / I called HIM. / They met US."
      *** NEVER "He helped I" — wrong slot. ***

   INDIRECT OBJECT — ORDER A (no TO)
      verb + IO (person) + DO (thing)
      "I gave HIM the report."
      "She sent US the invoice."
      "They told ME the news."
      Use when IO is a pronoun or short.

   INDIRECT OBJECT — ORDER B (with TO)
      verb + DO (thing) + TO + IO (person)
      "I gave the report TO HIM."
      "She sent the invoice TO US."
      Use when DO is a pronoun, or when IO is long.

   EXCEPTION: TELL / ASK take NO 'TO'
      "She TOLD ME the news."   (NOT "told to me")
      "He ASKED ME a question."  (NOT "asked to me")
      Other verbs that work like TELL: cost, charge,
      wish, owe.

   CALLBACK CL 9 (possessive vs object pronoun)
      HIS report  (before noun — possessive adjective)
      I helped HIM (after verb — object pronoun)
      Same person, two positions.

2. SEÑALA bloque OBJECT PRONOUNS. DI: "After the verb.
   After the preposition. NEVER in subject position.
   'He helped ME' — not 'me helped him' and not 'he
   helped I'. Position decides the form."

3. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase. DI: "Call
   him, send her the file, give them the report or
   give the report to them. The pronoun has its place
   after the verb. The indirect object has its order.
   TELL never takes TO."

4. SEÑALA bloque ORDER A. DI: "Verb + person + thing.
   No preposition. Default when the person is a
   pronoun. 'I gave HIM the report' — clean, short."

5. SEÑALA bloque ORDER B. DI: "Verb + thing + TO +
   person. Used when the thing is also a pronoun
   ('I gave it TO HIM') or when the person is a long
   phrase ('I gave the report to the manager I met
   yesterday'). Both orders are correct — but never
   mix them: NEVER 'I gave to him the report'."

6. SEÑALA bloque EXCEPTION. DI: "TELL and ASK take
   NO 'TO'. Always Order A. 'She told ME the news' —
   never 'told to me'. Same for cost, charge, wish,
   owe."

7. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "He helped I" -> TACHA "I",
   ESCRIBE "He helped ME." COHORTE repite 2 veces en
   coro.

8. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "Her called me" -> TACHA "Her",
   ESCRIBE "SHE called me." DI: "HER is the OBJECT
   form. After the verb. SHE is the SUBJECT form.
   Before the verb. Position decides."  COHORTE
   repite 2 veces.

9. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "I gave to him the report"
   -> TACHA "to", luego ESCRIBE 2 versiones limpias:
       "I gave HIM the report."
       "I gave the report TO HIM."
   DI: "Pick ONE order. Don't mix." COHORTE repite
   las 2 lineas 2 veces.

10. [VIRTUD] activation: cuando alguien titubee entre
    Order A y Order B, DI suave: "Measure. Where does
    the pronoun belong? Which order? Pick clean.
    Close the block."

11. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "She told to me the news"
    -> TACHA "to", ESCRIBE "She told ME the news."
    DI: "TELL and ASK — no TO. Period." COHORTE
    repite 2 veces.

12. [ERROR TIPICO #5] CALLBACK CL 9 vs HOY:
    ESCRIBE en board lado a lado:
       HIS report  (possessive — before noun)
       I helped HIM (object pronoun — after verb)
    DI: "Yesterday's rule lives next to today's rule.
    Same person, two positions. Don't fuse them."
    COHORTE repite las 2 lineas 2 veces.

13. PIDELES en parejas: "Transform 8 sentences. I
    dict the source, you say the alternative." 5 min.
    DICTA estos 8:
       a. "I gave HIM the report"      -> Order B: "I gave the report TO HIM"
       b. "She sent the invoice TO US" -> Order A: "She sent US the invoice"
       c. "He told ME the news"        -> try TO? NO — exception. Keep Order A.
       d. "They invited HER"           -> with subj? "SHE was invited" (passive — bonus)
       e. "I called HIM at 9"          -> Order A IO? "I called HIM about it" / no change needed (1 obj)
       f. "She asked ME a question"    -> try TO? NO — exception. Keep Order A.
       g. "We showed THEM the plans"   -> Order B: "We showed the plans TO THEM"
       h. "He helped US with the slides" -> NO IO transformation; mark "object pronoun only"
    CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca
    2 veces.

14. PODIUM (7 min). DOS volunteers al frente. Tu das
    un trio (VERB + PERSON-PRONOUN + THING) y producen
    AMBAS oraciones (Order A + Order B), o solo Order A
    si la verb es TELL/ASK. Cohorte verifica en coro:
    "Confirmed — both orders clean!"

    8 trios para llamar:
       give   / him  / the report      -> Order A + Order B
       send   / her  / the invoice     -> Order A + Order B
       tell   / me   / the truth       -> Order A ONLY (no TO)
       show   / them / the slides      -> Order A + Order B
       ask    / us   / the question    -> Order A ONLY (no TO)
       lend   / her  / the laptop      -> Order A + Order B
       write  / him  / a letter        -> Order A + Order B
       cost   / me   / 50 dollars      -> Order A ONLY (no TO)

15. Aplauso al final. CIERRA cuando 80% construya
    ambos ordenes + respete TELL/ASK sin titubear.
    DI: "Stand. Push chairs to the walls. We go to
    the live application."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 35 min y aun confunden Order A con Order B, NO AVANCES al podium. Reduce: solo object pronouns + Order A hoy, deja Order B + TELL/ASK exception para Cl 11 opener. Mejor LAND 2 bien que rush 4 shaky.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: PRONOUN POSITION DRILL (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "PRONOUN POSITION DRILL
   — 8 TASK EXCHANGES"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. Office scene — you are
   two colleagues coordinating tasks. Each pair must
   produce 8 task exchanges. Each exchange = one
   instruction or report that forces ONE specific
   position: direct object pronoun, OR indirect
   object Order A, OR indirect object Order B, OR
   TELL/ASK without TO. Observer marks position
   errors in 3 columns: SUBJ-OBJ confusion / Order
   mixed / TO with TELL-ASK. Then you rotate."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This drill IS the phrase
   — call him, send her the file, give them the
   report or give the report to them, and TELL never
   takes TO. Four positions, no confusion."

4. ESCRIBE en board los 8 exchanges (instruction +
   forced position):

     1 DO pronoun:        "Did you call the client?"  -> "Yes, I called HIM at 10."
     2 IO Order A:        "Did you send the invoice?" -> "Yes, I sent HER the invoice."
     3 IO Order B:        "Did the manager get the report?" -> "Yes, I gave the report TO HIM."
     4 TELL exception:    "Did you inform the team?"  -> "Yes, I told THEM the news."
     5 ASK exception:     "Did you talk to the boss?" -> "Yes, I asked HIM for an extension."
     6 DO + IO Order A:   "Show the new intern the deck." -> "OK, I'll show HIM the deck."
     7 DO + IO Order B:   "Send the proposal to her urgently." -> "OK, I'll send it TO HER."
     8 Mixed callback:    "Whose report is this and who helped?" -> "It's HIS report; I helped HIM with it."

5. PIDELES round 1 (15 min): pareja toma exchanges
   1-4. Roles claros: A pregunta, B responde, luego
   switch. Observer counts errors.

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces
   en la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven
   a parejas.

7. ROTA parejas + cambia roles. Round 2 (15 min):
   exchanges 5-8.

8. [VIRTUD] activation durante los rounds: si alguien
   diga "I gave to him the report" o "told to me",
   DI suave: "Measure. Which order? TELL takes TO?
   Pick clean. Close the block."

9. ROTA parejas otra vez. Round 3 (10 min): pareja
   inventa 4 exchanges propios (no de la lista) que
   cubran las 4 posiciones (DO / IO Order A / IO
   Order B / TELL or ASK). Observer verifica.

10. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces
    en parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu
    cuaderno.

11. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente
    al grupo (4 exchanges in a row, uno de cada
    posicion). COHORTE cuenta cuantas posiciones
    diferentes cazaron. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("Did you call the new manager?" / "Send the file to her now.") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 3 rounds completos + UNA demo al frente con las 4 posiciones visibles.
