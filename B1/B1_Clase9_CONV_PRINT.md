## BLOCK 2 — CONTENT POSSESSIVES (~50 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE 4 columnas en board grandes:

   ADJECTIVES         's (PEOPLE)         OF (THINGS)       WHOSE Q
   my                 the boss's          the cover OF      "Whose
   your                 office              the book         laptop
   his (man)          John's report       the end OF the     is this?"
   her (woman)        the children's        meeting         "Whose
   its (thing)          bags              the top OF the    keys did
   our                Anna's idea           page             we take?"
   their              after plural-s:                       NOT "Who's"
   + NOUN             the bosses'                            (= who is)
   no article!          offices

2. SEÑALA columna 1 (adjectives). DI: "Six adjectives
   plus IT. They go BEFORE the noun. They never take an
   article. NEVER 'the my book' — just 'my book'.
   Match the OWNER, not the object. 'Her car' even if
   the car is masculine in Spanish — the OWNER is a
   woman."

3. [FRASE] insertion 2: SEÑALA la frase en el board.
   DI: "This is what the phrase says — his laptop, her
   office, our team's report. Three of the four shapes
   already, in the phrase. Pick the right form."

4. [ERROR TIPICO #1] "The my book" -> TACHA "the",
   ESCRIBE "MY book." COHORTE repite 2 veces en coro.

5. [ERROR TIPICO #2] "His office" referido a una mujer
   -> TACHA "His", ESCRIBE "HER office." DI: "Match the
   OWNER. Not the object. Not Spanish gender of the noun."
   COHORTE repite 2 veces.

6. PIDELES en parejas: "5 sentences with possessive
   adjectives — mix all 7 forms. About your coworkers,
   your team, your office, your laptop." 4 minutos.

7. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca
   en 2 parejas distintas (sin nombres). Anota errores
   con NOMBRE en TU cuaderno.

8. SEÑALA columna 2 (possessive 's). DI: "For people
   and animals. ONE owner — apostrophe S. 'John's
   report. The boss's office. The children's bags.'
   Even if the owner's name ends in S — Charles's car.
   PLURAL owners ending in S — just apostrophe AFTER
   the s. 'The bosses' offices' — many bosses, many
   offices."

9. [ERROR TIPICO #3] "The office of John" -> TACHA,
   ESCRIBE "JOHN'S office." DI: "For people, prefer 's.
   'of' sounds heavy and translated." COHORTE repite
   2 veces.

10. [VIRTUD] activation: si alguien titubea entre 's y
    of, DI suave: "Measure. Whose is it? A person — use
    's. A thing — use of. Pick the right form."

11. SEÑALA columna 3 (of). DI: "For THINGS and for LONG
    phrases. 'The cover of the book.' 'The top of the
    page.' 'The end of the meeting.' Books and meetings
    don't own — they HAVE. Use 'of'."

12. [ERROR TIPICO #4] "The book's cover" -> TACHA,
    ESCRIBE "The COVER OF the book." DI: "Books don't
    own. Use 'of' for things." COHORTE repite 2 veces.

13. PIDELES en parejas: "4 sentences with 's for people,
    4 sentences with 'of' for things. Workplace context."
    5 minutos.

14. CIRCULA. CORRIGE en board cuando un error aparezca
    2 veces.

15. SEÑALA columna 4 (whose). DI: "WHOSE — with H, no
    apostrophe. Question for the owner. 'Whose laptop is
    this?' Different from 'WHO'S' with apostrophe — that
    is 'who is'. WHOSE = ownership. WHO'S = who is."

16. [ERROR TIPICO #5] "Who's laptop is this?" (in
    writing) -> TACHA "Who's", ESCRIBE "WHOSE laptop."
    DI: "Apostrophe = is. No apostrophe = ownership."
    COHORTE repite 2 veces.

17. PIDELES en parejas: "5 WHOSE questions about the
    objects in this room or in a typical office.
    Partner answers with 's or 'of' or possessive
    adjective." 5 minutos.

18. CIRCULA, CIERRA cuando 80% del grupo construya en
    los 4 formatos sin titubear. DI: "Stand up — we go
    to the simulation."
```

**[ALERTA]** si llegas a 30 min y aun dudan en 's vs of, NO AVANCES a whose. Reduce a 3 categorias hoy, deja whose para Cl 10. Mejor LAND 3 bien que rush 4 shaky.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** parejas construyendo en los 4 formatos sin pedir ayuda.

## BLOCK 3 — LIVE: OFFICE OBJECT OWNERSHIP DISPUTE (~45 min)

```
1. ESCRIBE en board grande: "OFFICE OBJECT OWNERSHIP
   DISPUTE"

2. DI las reglas: "In pairs. Office Monday morning —
   things are mixed up on the shared desk. One is the
   OWNER trying to recover their stuff; the other is
   the COLLEAGUE who picked things up by mistake. You
   must use, in the whole exchange, AT LEAST 4
   possessive adjectives (his/her/our/their), 3
   possessive 's (Anna's keys / the manager's folder /
   John's cup), 2 'of' constructions (the lid of the
   cup / the cover of the folder), and 5 WHOSE
   questions. Then you rotate."

3. [FRASE] insertion 3: DI: "This dispute IS the
   phrase in action — his laptop, her office, our
   team's report. Measure ownership. Claim nothing
   that isn't yours."

4. ESCRIBE 2 mini-scenarios en board:

   SCENARIO 1 — Monday morning desk mix-up
     Items on shared desk: laptop, charger, coffee
     cup, notebook, keys, folder, phone, headphones.
     OWNER: arrives, claims their items, names owners
     of others.
     COLLEAGUE: sorts items, asks WHOSE for each
     unclear one.

   SCENARIO 2 — Meeting room after a 6-person meeting
     Items left: marker, glasses, water bottle, USB,
     notebook, the cover OF a report.
     OWNER: returns to claim 1-2 items, names which
     belong to which colleague.
     COLLEAGUE: asks WHOSE for each item, then
     returns them.

5. PIDELES round 1 (5 min): pareja escoge un scenario.
   Roles claros. OWNER arranca: "This is my laptop.
   That's not my charger — whose is it? I think it's
   Anna's."

6. CIRCULA. INTERVEN solo si un error aparece 3 veces
   en la misma pareja -> board -> coro 2x -> vuelven
   a parejas.

7. [PROMPT] (translanguaging): DI: "If stuck, switch
   to Spanish, think 5 seconds, come back to English.
   Don't freeze."

8. ROTA parejas + cambia roles. Round 2 (5 min):
   cambien de scenario tambien.

9. [VIRTUD] activation durante la simulacion: si
   alguien reclama algo que claramente no es suyo
   ("My charger" cuando ya estableciste que es de
   otro), DI suave: "Measure. Claim nothing that
   isn't yours. Temperance."

10. ROTA parejas otra vez. Round 3 (5 min): mismo
    formato, push para que aparezcan los 4 formatos
    en cada turno (adjective + 's + of + whose).

11. CIRCULA, CORRIGE cuando un error aparezca 2 veces
    en parejas distintas. Anota con NOMBRE en tu
    cuaderno.

12. CIERRA con UNA pareja modelo: 90 segundos frente
    al grupo (owner + colleague, 3 WHOSE questions +
    3 claims). COHORTE cuenta cuantos possessives
    correctos cazaron en cada categoria. Aplauso.
```

**[ALERTA]** si una pareja se queda en silencio 15+ segundos, DROP UN PROMPT ("Whose phone is this — the one on Anna's chair?" / "I think the headphones belong to the new intern — what's his name again?") y RETIRATE. NO expliques, NO llenes el silencio, NO tomes el rol.
**[OK] SEÑAL DE AVANCE:** 3 rotaciones + UNA demo al frente con los 4 formatos producidos.
